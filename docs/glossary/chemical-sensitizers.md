# Chemical sensitizers

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Vulcanization accelerators (mercaptobenzothiazole, thiurams) and antioxidants are skin sensitizers that cause occupational dermatitis with repeated contact. Handle compounding ingredients with gloves. Minimize dust generation during weighing and mixing. MBT is also a suspected endocrine disruptor — EU classification as SVHC (Substance of Very High Concern).
