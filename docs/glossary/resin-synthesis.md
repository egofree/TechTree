# Resin synthesis

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Bisphenol A (BPA) + epichlorohydrin in molar ratio 1:2-10 (excess epichlorohydrin for higher molecular weight). NaOH (50% aqueous) added as both catalyst and to dehydrohalogenate the chlorohydrin intermediate. Reaction at 50-60°C for 2-4 hours. Product: DGEBA (diglycidyl ether of bisphenol A) — a viscous liquid (n ≈ 0.1-0.2, epoxy equivalent weight 180-190 g/eq). Higher molecular weight versions (n ≈ 2-30) are solid resins used in powder coatings.
