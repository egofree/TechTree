# Rotary decimal (most common)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: computing

Each digit is a wheel with 10 positions (0-9), readable through a window. Multiple wheels on a common shaft represent multi-digit numbers. Addition rotates wheels forward; subtraction rotates backward. Carry mechanisms propagate between adjacent digit wheels. Used in Pascaline, Arithmometer, and most desktop calculators.
