# Emissions

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

CO₂ from anode consumption (~1.5 t CO₂ per t Al), plus CO₂ from electricity generation (if fossil-fueled). Fluoride emissions (HF, CF₄, C₂F₆) controlled by dry scrubbing (alumina absorbs HF, then fed to cells — closing the fluoride loop). CF₄ and C₂F₆ are extremely potent greenhouse gases (GWP 6,620 and 11,100 respectively) — minimize anode effects.
