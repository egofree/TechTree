# Gate oxide

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

SiO₂ grown by thermal oxidation of silicon (dry O₂ at 900-1100°C or wet O₂/H₂O at 800-950°C). Thickness 5-50 nm depending on technology generation. Thinner oxide → stronger gate coupling → faster switching, but exponentially higher gate leakage current (quantum tunneling through oxide). Oxide quality is critical — even a single defect in the gate oxide can cause catastrophic breakdown (short circuit from gate to channel). Oxide integrity requires ultra-clean processing and careful handling.
