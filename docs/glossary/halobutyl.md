# Halobutyl

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

(chlorobutyl CIIR, bromobutyl BIIR): Butyl rubber halogenated with Cl₂ or Br₂ at the isoprene double bond. The halogen provides a second cross-linking site that enables covulcanization with general-purpose rubbers (NR, SBR). Regular butyl rubber cannot be covulcanized with NR/SBR — the different cure systems are incompatible.
