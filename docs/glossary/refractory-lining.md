# Refractory lining

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

The combustion and reduction zones of the gasifier operate at 800-1200°C, requiring refractory lining. Fireclay brick (Al₂O₃ 30-40%, service to 1300-1400°C) is adequate for most gasifier designs. Brick thickness: 50-100 mm on the inside of the steel shell, with an air gap or insulating blanket between the brick and shell.
