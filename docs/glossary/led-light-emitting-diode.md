# LED (light-emitting diode)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Forward-biased p-n junction where electron-hole recombination produces photons. Photon energy ≈ bandgap energy. GaAs: infrared (~870 nm). GaAsP: red (~630 nm). GaP: green (~555 nm). GaN: blue (~450 nm). External quantum efficiency: 1-30% depending on material and structure.
