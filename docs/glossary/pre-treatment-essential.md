# Pre-treatment essential

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Raw water must be coagulated and flocculated before rapid filtration. Add alum (aluminum sulfate, Al₂(SO₄)₃) at 10-50 mg/L depending on turbidity. Rapid mix (300-500 RPM, 30-60 seconds) disperses coagulant. Slow mix (20-40 RPM, 15-30 minutes) allows floc particles to grow and aggregate. Settling basin (2-4 hour retention) removes bulk of floc. Overflow enters rapid filter.
