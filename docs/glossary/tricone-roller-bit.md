# Tricone roller bit

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

(standard for large blast holes): - Three conical rollers, each studded with tungsten carbide buttons, rotate on bearings as the bit turns. The buttons crush and shear the rock under the combined effect of rotation and downward pressure (weight on bit: 5-30 tonnes depending on hole diameter and rock hardness). - Hole diameter: 150-380 mm.
