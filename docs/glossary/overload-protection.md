# Overload protection

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: computing

Thermal overload relay (bimetallic strip heated by motor current). If motor draws excessive current for too long, bimetallic strip bends and trips a mechanical latch that opens the contactor coil circuit, shutting down the motor. Protects motor from sustained overload without nuisance tripping on normal starting current surges.
