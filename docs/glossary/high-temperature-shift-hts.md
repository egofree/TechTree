# High-temperature shift (HTS)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

High-temperature shift (HTS): 350-450°C, Fe₂O₃/Cr₂O₃ catalyst. Converts bulk of CO. Exit: ~3% CO remaining. Iron-chrome catalyst is robust and tolerant of impurities. See: [Hydrogen & Silane Production](../chemistry/hydrogen-silane.md)
