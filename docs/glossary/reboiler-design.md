# Reboiler design

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Kettle reboiler (most common): liquid from the column bottom enters a shell-side pool heated by tube-side steam (or hot oil). Vapor rises back to the column, liquid product exits from the bottom. Steam pressure: 0.3-1.0 MPa (saturation temperature 144-184°C). Heat transfer coefficient: 800-1500 W/m²·K for boiling organic liquids.
