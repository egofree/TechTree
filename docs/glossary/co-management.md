# CO management

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Furnace off-gas contains 70-90% CO by volume. CO must be either flared (burned to CO₂ in an elevated flare stack with continuous pilot light) or recovered as fuel gas. CO recovery requires gas cleaning (remove dust, cool to ambient, compress) before use in boilers or gas turbines. CO has a heating value of ~10 MJ/m³, roughly one-third of natural gas.
