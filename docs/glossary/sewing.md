# Sewing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Sew through fold with linen thread onto hemp cords (sewing supports — 3-5 cords per spine). Kettle stitch at each end to lock. Sewing pattern: all-along (thread passes around each cord on every gathering) or skip-stitch (faster, slightly weaker). Tight, even sewing prevents loose pages.
