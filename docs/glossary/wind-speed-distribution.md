# Wind speed distribution

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Wind speed at any site follows a Weibull distribution (a skewed probability distribution). The mean wind speed is not the most common speed; the most probable speed is typically 70-80% of the mean. Typical inland mean speeds: 4-8 m/s. Coastal and ridge-top sites: 7-12 m/s. The Weibull shape parameter (k) describes the spread: k ≈ 2 (Rayleigh distribution) is typical for many inland sites.
