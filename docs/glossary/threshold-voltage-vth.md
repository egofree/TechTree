# Threshold voltage (Vth)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

0.5-2V for enhancement-mode. Vth = 2φF + (√(2εs·q·Na·(2φF)))/Cox, where φF is the Fermi potential, Na is substrate doping, and Cox is oxide capacitance per unit area. Vth increases with substrate doping and oxide thickness.
