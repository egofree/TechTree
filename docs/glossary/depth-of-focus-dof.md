# Depth of focus (DoF)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

The range of focus positions where CD remains within specification (typically ±10% of target). For i-line (365 nm) at NA 0.50: DoF ≈ ±1.0 μm. The DoF shrinks with increasing NA and decreasing wavelength, which is why shorter-wavelength lithography requires flatter wafers and better autofocus systems.
