# Porcelain

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

(highest-fire, 1260-1300°C+): - 50% kaolin (pure white clay, low plasticity), 25% feldspar (flux), 25% silica. Kaolin (Al₂Si₂O₅(OH)₄) fires pure white, unlike ball clay which contains iron impurities that darken the body. - Porcelain vitrifies completely at peak temperature, becoming translucent in thin sections (1-3 mm).
