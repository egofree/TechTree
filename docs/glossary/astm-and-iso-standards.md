# ASTM and ISO standards

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Key test methods include ASTM C133 (cold crushing strength), ASTM C20 (apparent porosity, water absorption, and bulk density by boiling water method), ASTM C1113 (thermal conductivity by hot wire), ASTM C583 (modulus of rupture at elevated temperature), ASTM C832 (thermal shock resistance by ribbon brick test), and ISO 10081 (classification of dense shaped refractory products by alumina and silica content).
