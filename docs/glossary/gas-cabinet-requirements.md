# Gas cabinet requirements

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Cylinders housed in ventilated gas cabinet with 200 fpm (1 m/s) face velocity exhaust, constructed from non-combustible materials with self-closing doors and continuous gas monitoring. Quantity limited to 1-2 cylinders per cabinet to limit potential release volume. Cabinet exhaust routed to the abatement system, not to general building exhaust.
