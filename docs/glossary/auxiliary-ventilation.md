# Auxiliary ventilation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Auxiliary ventilation (local supply to dead-end headings) — - Main ventilation cannot reach the end of a single-entry heading (dead end). A fan and duct supply air to the working face. - Forcing auxiliary fan: pushes fresh air through duct (300-600 mm diameter, flexible reinforced plastic) to the face. Air returns along the heading naturally. Face gets fresh air immediately upon fan start. - Exhausting auxiliary fan: pulls contaminated air from the face through duct.
