# Tank furnace

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Tank furnace (continuous, industrial) — A large refractory-lined tank, continuously charged with batch at one end and continuously withdrawn as molten glass at the other. Tank capacity: 1-500 tonnes. The glass flows through distinct zones: melting zone (1500-1600°C), fining zone (1500-1550°C), working zone (1100-1200°C).
