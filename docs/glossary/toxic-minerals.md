# Toxic minerals

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Some ore minerals contain arsenic (arsenopyrite), mercury (cinnabar), or lead (galena). Handle with gloves. Do not inhale dust from crushing test samples. Wash hands before eating. Arsenic compounds are tasteless and odorless — never eat, drink, or smoke while handling ore samples. Mercury vapor from cinnabar (HgS) accumulates in enclosed spaces — assay mercury ores only in well-ventilated areas with activated carbon traps.
