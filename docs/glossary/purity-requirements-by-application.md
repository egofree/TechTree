# Purity requirements by application

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Semiconductor-grade silane requires 6N+ (99.9999%) purity. The most critical contaminants are oxygen (as SiH₃OCH₃ or SiH₃OH, target <100 ppb), moisture (H₂O, target <50 ppb), higher silanes (Si₂H₆, Si₃H₈, target <10 ppm total), and chlorosilanes (SiH₃Cl, SiH₂Cl₂, target <1 ppm). Metallic contaminants (Fe, Cu, Ni from piping) are controlled to <1 ppb each by electropolished 316L stainless steel delivery systems.
