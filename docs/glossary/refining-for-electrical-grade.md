# Refining (for electrical grade)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

For submarine cable insulation and dental applications, higher purity is required. Dissolve crude gutta-percha in hot turpentine (80-100°C, ~1:3 gutta-percha to turpentine by weight) or carbon disulfide (CS₂, room temperature — more effective but highly toxic). Filter the hot solution through canvas or felt to remove insoluble debris. Cool to precipitate purified gutta-percha. Wash the precipitate with ethanol to remove residual turpentine.
