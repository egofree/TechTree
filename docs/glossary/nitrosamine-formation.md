# Nitrosamine formation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Certain rubber accelerators (particularly nitrosatable secondary amines like TMTD and DTDM) can form N-nitrosamines during vulcanization. Several nitrosamines are confirmed carcinogens (IARC Group 2A). Monitor airborne nitrosamine levels during mixing and curing operations. Use nitrosamine-safe accelerator systems (TBzTD, ZBEC) where possible — they generate non-carcinogenic nitrosamines.
