# Kiln design

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

The heart of cement production. A steel tube 50-80 m long, 3-5 m diameter, lined with refractory brick, rotating at 1-4 RPM (supported on rollers at 2-3 points), inclined 3-4° from horizontal. Feed enters the upper (cold) end, flame and combustion gases enter the lower (hot) end. Material moves through by gravity and rotation.
