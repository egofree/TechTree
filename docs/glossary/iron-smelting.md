# Iron smelting

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Bloomery and early blast furnaces consume 1-2 kg charcoal per kg of iron produced. A modest bloomery producing 10 kg iron per run needs 15-20 kg charcoal — one moderate mound kiln's output. A large blast furnace consumes 5-10 tonnes of charcoal per day.
