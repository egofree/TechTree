# Chemical hazards in colorimetry

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement

Photographic processing (for spectroscopic plates) involves developers containing hydroquinone (skin sensitizer, toxic by ingestion), stop baths (acetic acid, corrosive), and fixers (sodium thiosulfate, low toxicity but generates sulfur dioxide when acidified). Work in ventilated areas, wear nitrile gloves, and store chemicals in labeled containers away from food and drink.
