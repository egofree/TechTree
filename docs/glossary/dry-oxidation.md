# Dry oxidation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Si + O₂ → SiO₂ at 900-1200°C in pure O₂ atmosphere. Growth rate: ~1-5 nm/min in the linear regime, slowing as oxide thickens (parabolic regime). Produces the highest-quality oxide: dense, uniform, low defect density, breakdown field >10 MV/cm. Used exclusively for gate oxides (where quality is paramount) and thin screening oxides.
