# Aluminum wedge bonding

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Aluminum wedge bonding: 25-50 μm Al wire. Ultrasonic energy only (room temperature). Wedge tool presses wire against pad. Lower cost but slower. Used for power devices (thicker wire carries more current). See: [Semiconductor Packaging & Testing](../chemistry/packaging-testing.md)
