# Industrial epoxy coating

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

(two-component): Part A (resin): epoxy resin 60%, TiO₂ 15%, barium sulfate (extender) 10%, xylene/butanol solvent 15%. Part B (hardener): polyamide curing agent 70%, xylene solvent 30%. Mix ratio A:B typically 4:1 by volume. Pot life after mixing: 4-8 hours. Cure: touch dry 4-8 hours, full cure 7 days. Dry film thickness: 75-200 µm per coat.
