# Annealing schedule

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Heat to annealing point (~500°C for soda-lime glass). Hold until temperature is uniform throughout the piece (30 min per 6 mm thickness). Cool at 1-5°C/min through the strain point (~450°C). Below strain point, faster cooling is safe (5-10°C/min to room temperature).
