# Mechanical properties at room temperature

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Tensile strength 20-30 MPa. Elongation at break 3-8% (rigid, brittle). Hardness Shore D 60-80. Impact resistance low — cracks if struck sharply. When heated above the softening point: elongation increases dramatically to ~300-500%, tensile decreases to 1-5 MPa.
