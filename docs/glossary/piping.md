# Piping

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Electropolished 316L stainless steel tubing. Internal surface roughness <0.5 μm Ra (electropolishing removes micro-roughness where particles and contaminants could trap). Orbital-welded joints (automated TIG welding in inert atmosphere — no internal weld beads or discoloration). VCR-type face-seal fittings for connections to equipment. Minimum dead legs (no T-connections pointing down where gas stagnates).
