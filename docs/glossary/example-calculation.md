# Example calculation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

A heading with 10 workers and one 50 kW diesel loader requires 0.03 × 10 + 0.06 × 50 = 3.3 m³/s minimum. After blasting 20 kg of explosive, an additional 0.6-1.0 m³/s is needed for 15-30 minutes. Practical design provides 150-200% of minimum to account for leakage (10-30% of total airflow leaks through seals and old workings) and uneven distribution.
