# Stainless steel (304L / 316L)

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

The standard chamber and piping material for high and ultra-high vacuum. Both grades have low outgassing rates (<10⁻⁹ Torr·L/s·cm² after bake-out), good weldability (the "L" designation means low carbon, which prevents carbide precipitation and corrosion at weld seams), and adequate mechanical strength. 316L is preferred for corrosive gas applications (contains molybdenum for pitting resistance).
