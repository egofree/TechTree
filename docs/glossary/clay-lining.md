# Clay lining

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Where natural subsoil is permeable, spread a 15-30 cm layer of clay over the entire pond bottom and sides. Compact in 5 cm lifts using hand tampers or a heavy log dragged across the surface. The clay must be wet enough to bond (squeeze a handful; it should hold its shape without crumbling) but not so wet that it pumps underfoot. Bentonite clay, if available, seals at lower application rates (5-10 kg/m²) but is not essential.
