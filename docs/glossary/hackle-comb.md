# Hackle comb

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Drive iron nails (5-10 cm long, 2-3 mm diameter) through a hardwood board (15 × 30 cm × 2 cm thick) at 3-5 mm spacing, bent to a slight upward angle (~10-15°). Clinch the nail points on the back side by bending them over. Build three grades: coarse (10 cm nails, 5 mm spacing), medium (7 cm, 3 mm spacing), fine (5 cm, 2 mm spacing).
