# Fluid resuscitation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

(Parkland formula for burns >20% body surface area): 4 mL lactated Ringer's (or 0.9% NaCl) × kg body weight × %BSA burned. Give half in the first 8 hours from the time of burn, the remaining half over the next 16 hours. Monitor urine output (target 0.5-1.0 mL/kg/hour as indicator of adequate resuscitation).
