# Schedule

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics, glass

(1) Heat to annealing point. (2) Hold until temperature is uniform throughout the piece — allow 30 minutes per 6 mm of wall thickness. (3) Cool at 3-5°C/min through the strain point region. (4) Below the strain point, faster cooling is safe (5-10°C/min to room temperature).
