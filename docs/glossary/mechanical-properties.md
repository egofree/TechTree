# Mechanical properties

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals, glass

of soda-lime glass: - Compressive strength: 700-1000 MPa (very strong in compression — comparable to concrete). This is why glass supports heavy loads in structural applications when loaded in pure compression. - Tensile strength: 30-90 MPa nominally, but practical strength is much lower due to surface micro-cracks (Griffith flaws).
