# Scale of cultivation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Approximately 150,000 lac insects are required to produce 1 kg of sticklac (raw, unprocessed lac). Each female insect secretes roughly 0.5 mg of resin over her 6-month adult lifespan. The insects are tiny — adults measure 1-2 mm in length — but form dense colonies that encrust branches in resin layers 5-10 mm thick.
