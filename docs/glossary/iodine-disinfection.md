# Iodine disinfection

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Add 2-4 drops of 2% tincture of iodine per liter of clear water. Stir and wait 30 minutes before drinking. Iodine kills bacteria and viruses but is less effective against Cryptosporidium cysts. Not suitable for pregnant women or individuals with thyroid conditions. Water treated with iodine has a noticeable taste; adding vitamin C (ascorbic acid) after the 30-minute wait neutralizes both the taste and residual iodine.
