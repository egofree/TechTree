# Sheet rolling

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

(flat sheet from ingot): 1. Cast ingot (copper, brass, or steel) 20–40 mm thick. Soak at forging temperature 30–60 min. 2. Pass through rolls, reduce 2–3 mm per pass. Rotate 180° between passes for even reduction. 3. Reheat every 4–6 passes. Anneal at 500–700°C when reduction exceeds 40–50% from last anneal. 4. Target: copper sheet to 0.5 mm (hand mill) or 0.1 mm (powered mill).
