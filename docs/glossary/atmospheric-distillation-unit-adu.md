# Atmospheric distillation unit (ADU)

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

The first processing step in a petroleum refinery. Crude oil preheated to 350-370°C in a tube-still furnace (fuel-fired, 70-80% thermal efficiency) enters the atmospheric distillation column as a partially vaporized mixture.
