# Flatness grades

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement, optics

Flatness grades (per ISO 8512) — grade AA plates — flat to ±2.5 μm per 1000 mm, used as laboratory reference standards. Grade A — flat to ±5 μm per 1000 mm, used for precision inspection work. Grade B — flat to ±10 μm per 1000 mm, used for general shop inspection. Most workshop surface plates are Grade B; calibration laboratories maintain Grade AA plates as references.
