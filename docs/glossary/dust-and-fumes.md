# Dust and fumes

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

EAF steelmaking generates 10-20 kg dust per tonne of steel (containing Fe₂O₃, ZnO from galvanized scrap, Pb, Cr, Ni). Dust is collected in baghouses and either recycled (zinc recovery via Waelz kiln) or landfilled as hazardous waste. Hexavalent chromium (Cr⁶⁺) fume from stainless steel melting is a confirmed carcinogen — local exhaust ventilation and respiratory protection required during stainless steelmaking.
