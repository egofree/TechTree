# Garment change frequency

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Daily change for ISO 5 (Class 100) and cleaner environments — garments accumulate skin flakes and particles with each wearing. Twice-weekly for ISO 6 (Class 1000). Weekly for ISO 7 (Class 10,000). Garments are numbered and tracked by serial number through the laundry cycle to enforce change schedule.
