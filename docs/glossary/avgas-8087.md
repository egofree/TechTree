# Avgas 80/87

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

The standard low-lead aviation gasoline for low-compression engines. Octane rating 80 (lean mixture) / 87 (rich mixture). Leaded with tetraethyl lead (TEL) at 0.5 mL/L to boost octane and lubricate valves. Color: red dye. Suitable for engines with compression ratios up to 7:1. Phased out in many countries but still the simplest aviation fuel to refine from petroleum.
