# Mixing process

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

Combine lime putty and sand on a board or in a mortar tub. Beat vigorously with hoe or paddle mixer for 10-15 minutes — "fat" mortar should hold shape when cut with trowel and spread without tearing. Let stand 30 minutes before use (water fully penetrates lime particles, improving workability).
