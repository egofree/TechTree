# Starch

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Added at 0.5-2.5% on pulp at the wet end (cationic starch for retention) or applied as surface treatment at the size press. Dry strength additive: hydrogen bonding between starch hydroxyls and fiber hydroxyls increases tensile and burst strength 10-30%. Cationic starch (quaternary ammonium groups grafted onto starch backbone) also acts as a drainage and retention aid.
