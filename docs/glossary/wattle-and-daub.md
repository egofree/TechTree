# Wattle-and-daub

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Weave flexible saplings or split wood between vertical posts (wattle). Pack both sides with clay-soil-straw mixture (daub). Let dry 1-2 weeks. Produces solid, insulated walls. ~10 cm wall thickness. A 3 m by 4 m wattle-and-daub wall panel takes 2-3 days to build, including drying time. The clay-soil mix should be roughly 70% soil, 20% clay, 10% chopped straw by volume. The straw acts as reinforcement, reducing cracking as the wall dries.
