# Polymerization test

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

A simple field test for shellac freshness — dissolve 5 g of shellac flakes in 15 ml of ethanol. If the solution is clear and pourable after 4 hours with stirring, the shellac is usable. If the solution is cloudy, gel-like, or contains undissolved lumps, the shellac has polymerized beyond usefulness.
