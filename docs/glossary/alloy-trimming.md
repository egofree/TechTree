# Alloy trimming

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Add ferroalloys in the ladle to achieve final chemistry: FeMn (manganese, 0.3-1.5%), FeSi (silicon, 0.1-0.4%), FeCr (chromium for alloy grades), FeV, FeNb, FeTi as required. Aluminum wire for fine-tuning dissolved Al to 0.02-0.06%. The ladle furnace arc reheats steel to compensate for temperature losses during treatment (typical target: 20-40°C above liquidus for continuous casting, adjusted for time-in-ladle before casting).
