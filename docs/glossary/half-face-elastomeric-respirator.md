# Half-face elastomeric respirator

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Reusable rubber facepiece with replaceable cartridges. P100 particulate filters (99.97% efficiency) or chemical cartridges (organic vapor, acid gas, ammonia, depending on cartridge color coding). Protection factor: 10× (reduces exposure to 1/10th of ambient). For exposures up to 10× PEL.
