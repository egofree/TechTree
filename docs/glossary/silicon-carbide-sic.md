# Silicon carbide (SiC)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy, machine-tools

Rod or tubular elements operating to 1600°C. Non-metallic. Electrical resistance increases with age (silicon oxidation changes the composition), requiring periodic voltage adjustment to maintain power output. Very long service life under proper conditions (10,000+ hours at 1400°C). Silicon carbide has high thermal conductivity, reducing thermal gradients in the element.
