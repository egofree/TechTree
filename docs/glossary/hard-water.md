# Hard water

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

(high Ca²⁺/Mg²⁺): Calcium ions interfere with flotation reagent adsorption on mineral surfaces, reducing selectivity and recovery. Calcium also precipitates with xanthate collectors, wasting reagent. Treat hard water with lime softening (add Ca(OH)₂ to precipitate CaCO₃) or ion exchange before use in flotation circuits.
