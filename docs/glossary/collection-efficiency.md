# Collection efficiency

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

A well-maintained roof catchment system with gutters captures 80-90% of incident rainfall. Losses come from evaporation (3-5%), splash and overflow (5-10%), and first-flush diversion (1-2 mm per storm event). A 100 m² roof in a 600 mm/year rainfall zone with 85% collection efficiency yields approximately 51,000 liters annually.
