# Paramagnetic cell

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Oxygen is strongly paramagnetic (attracted to a magnetic field). A dumbbell suspended in a magnetic field deflects proportional to the O₂ concentration in the sample gas. Measures 0-25% O₂. Accurate, non-depleting, but mechanically delicate. Used for calibration and fixed installations.
