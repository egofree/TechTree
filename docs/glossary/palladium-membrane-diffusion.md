# Palladium membrane diffusion

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Hydrogen dissociates on a Pd or Pd-Ag alloy surface, diffuses through the metal lattice as atomic H, and recombines on the permeate side. Only hydrogen passes — impurities blocked. Purity: 99.99999% (7 nines). Flow: proportional to membrane area and √(pressure difference). Operating temperature: 300-400°C.
