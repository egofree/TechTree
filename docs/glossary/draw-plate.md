# Draw plate

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

Draw plate (the bootstrap tool) — hardened high-carbon steel (0.8–1.0% C) plate, 10–25 mm thick, with tapered holes graduating in size (e.g., 8.0 mm down to 1.0 mm in 0.5 mm steps). Each hole: ~30° included angle entrance, short straight bearing section (~1× diameter), back-relief. See: [Metal Forming](../machine-tools/forming.md)
