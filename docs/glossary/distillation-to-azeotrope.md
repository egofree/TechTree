# Distillation to azeotrope

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Beer column strips ethanol from fermented mash at atmospheric pressure. Overhead vapor: 40-50% ethanol (enriched from 8-12% in beer). Rectifying column further concentrates to 95.6% ethanol (the azeotropic composition with water at 78.2°C). Energy consumption: 2.5-4.0 kg steam per liter of 95% ethanol.
