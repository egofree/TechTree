# MIT model

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

MIT model (Massachusetts Institute of Technology, founded 1861) — Motto**: *Mens et Manus* (Mind and Hand) — theory and practice inseparable. Every student spends time in laboratory and workshop alongside classroom instruction. This was revolutionary: traditional universities were purely theoretical (classical education); traditional apprenticeships were purely practical (no theory).
