# Indigo vat

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Indigo vat: 30-40°C (hand-warm). The bacterial fermentation in a traditional vat is killed above 55°C. A chemical reduction vat (using sodium dithionite or thiourea dioxide) can operate at 50-60°C, but higher temperatures risk over-reduction and unstable color. See: [Dyeing](../textiles/dyeing.md)
