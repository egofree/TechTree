# 1400-1700°C

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

High-alumina (80-90% Al₂O₃), magnesite, or silica bricks. Applications: steelmaking furnaces (BOF, EAF at 1600-1700°C), glass tank furnaces (1550-1600°C), cement kiln burning zone (1450°C). Chemical attack from slag, glass melt, or cement clinker is severe. Careful matching of refractory chemistry to the process environment is essential.
