# Crucible design

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Clay-graphite crucibles (~60% fire clay + ~40% powdered graphite or charcoal dust) resist thermal shock and last through many heats. Form over a core, dry slowly, fire to harden. A 10 cm dia × 12 cm tall crucible holds ~2-3 kg molten copper. Always preheat crucibles before adding metal — a cold crucible in molten copper may shatter.
