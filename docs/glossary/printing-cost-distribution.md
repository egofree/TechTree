# Printing cost distribution

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

(typical job): Paper ~50% of total cost, labor (typesetting, press operation, binding) ~25%, ink ~5%, overhead (press depreciation, shop rent, power) ~20%. Ink is a surprisingly small fraction of total cost, but its properties (viscosity, tack, drying time) critically affect print quality and press productivity.
