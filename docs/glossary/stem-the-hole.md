# Stem the hole

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Fill the remaining hole above the powder with clay, damp sand, or crushed rock. Tamp firmly with a wooden tamping rod (NOT a metal rod — metal on rock can produce sparks). Stemming confines the blast, directing energy into breaking rock rather than venting out the hole. Stemming depth should be at least equal to the burden (distance to free face).
