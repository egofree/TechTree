# Cyanide (NaCN/KCN)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Lethal dose ~200-300 mg oral. Blocks cellular respiration. Symptoms: headache, dizziness, seizures, cardiac arrest in 15-30 min. ANTIDOTE: Hydroxocobalamin or sodium thiosulfate + sodium nitrite. MUST have antidote available before handling. NEVER allow acid contact with cyanide (generates lethal HCN gas at 100 ppm). Keep process pH above 10.
