# Byproduct coke oven

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Horizontal chamber (12-15 m long × 4-6 m tall × 0.4-0.5 m wide) made of refractory brick (silica or alumina). Load with crushed coal (3-5 cm pieces). Seal chamber. Heat externally by burning coke oven gas (from previous batch) in flues between chambers. Temperature reaches 1000-1100°C. Time: 14-24 hours per batch.
