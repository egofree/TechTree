# 1850-1870

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Early pneumatic drills appear (Cowan, Schumann, Sommeiller). Heavy, unreliable, but proved the concept of compressed-air-powered percussion. Compressed air generated by steam-driven compressors. Sommeiller used pneumatic drills to bore the Mont Cenis tunnel through the Alps (1857-1870).
