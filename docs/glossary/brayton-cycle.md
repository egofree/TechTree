# Brayton cycle

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Compressor pressure ratio 4:1 to 30:1. Early turbines at 4:1 to 8:1 achieved 20-28% efficiency. Modern units at 20:1 to 30:1 reach 35-40% simple-cycle. Turbine inlet temperature: 800-900°C (early), 1200-1400°C (modern industrial), 1500-1700°C (advanced aero). Each 50°C increase in TIT improves efficiency by roughly 1-2 percentage points.
