# Bucket design

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Each Pelton bucket is a double-hemispherical cup with a central splitter ridge that divides the incoming jet into two equal halves. The splitter ridge must be sharp (knife-edge) and aligned exactly with the jet centerline. The jet enters through a notch cut in the bucket rim so that the bucket can pass through the jet without interference from the preceding bucket. Bucket width is 3-4 times the jet diameter to fully contain the splashing water.
