# Prohibited items and practices

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

No cosmetics, perfumes, or aftershave (particles and volatile organics). No paper products — use cleanroom-compatible synthetic paper (polypropylene-based) for notes and documentation. No pencils (graphite particles) — use cleanroom-rated ballpoint pens with retained caps. No food, drink, or smoking within the gowning area.
