# Chlorosilane disposal

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Unreacted or off-spec chlorosilanes cannot be dumped — they react violently with water. Controlled hydrolysis in a dedicated treatment system: spray chlorosilane into a controlled excess of water in a sealed, vented vessel. Products: SiO₂ sludge + HCl solution. Neutralize HCl, filter and dispose SiO₂ sludge as non-hazardous waste.
