# Operating parameters

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals, photolithography

Pressure 10-100 mTorr. RF power 50-500 W. Gas flow 10-200 sccm. Wafer temperature: 20-80°C (cooled by helium backside cooling for uniform temperature). Lower pressure → more anisotropic (longer mean free path, more directional ions). Higher power → faster etch but less selective (more physical sputtering damages mask and select materials).
