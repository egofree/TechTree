# Beater (batten)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Hangs from the top of the loom frame on two metal or wooden hinged brackets (swords). Reed frame: 5 × 5 cm hardwood stock, 100 cm wide, holding the reed (comb of flat metal or cane dents spaced at the desired ends-per-inch). Swings forward to pack weft against the fell (the edge of the woven cloth).
