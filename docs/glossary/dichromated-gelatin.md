# Dichromated gelatin

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

(mid-19th century): - Mix gelatin (from animal collagen) with ammonium dichromate ((NH₄)₂Cr₂O₇, ~5-10%). Coat on substrate. UV exposure causes Cr(VI) → Cr(III) reduction, cross-linking gelatin (insoluble). Unexposed gelatin washes away in warm water. Resolution ~10-50 μm. Chromium compounds are toxic and carcinogenic — handle with care.
