# Alumina cement

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Alumina cement (calcium aluminate cement) — - Bauxite + limestone, fused at 1400-1600°C. Primarily monocalcium aluminate (CaO·Al₂O₃). Sets rapidly (2-6 hours), high early strength. Heat-resistant — service to 1600°C with alumina aggregate. Used for refractory castables and emergency repairs. See: [Cement & Concrete](../chemistry/cement.md)
