# Gear reduction

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals, energy

a large horizontal gear (crown gear, 1.5-2 m diameter, 40-60 wooden teeth) meshes with a smaller vertical gear (lantern gear, 20-30 cm diameter, 8-12 trunnions). The animal turns the crown gear at 2-4 RPM; the millstone turns at 5-15 RPM depending on gear ratio.
