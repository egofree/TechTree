# Fuel exhaustion

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

The most preventable cause of forced landings. Calculate fuel required for each leg plus 30-minute reserve. Visually verify fuel quantity before flight (dip the tank with a calibrated stick or sight gauge). Do not rely on memory of the last refueling. A 40 HP engine at cruise consumes 8-12 kg/hour. A 20 kg fuel tank gives 1.5-2.5 hours endurance.
