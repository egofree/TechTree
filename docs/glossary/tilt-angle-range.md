# Tilt angle range

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: computing

The input ball carriage travels radially across the disk from center to rim. At center (tilt angle 0°), output is zero. At the rim (maximum tilt, roughly 15° offset from center to edge), output equals full disk rotation rate. The ball position encodes a continuous variable from 0 to 1 (normalized).
