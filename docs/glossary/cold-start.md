# Cold start

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Cold start (from ambient temperature to full operation) — 1. **Inspect**: Check water gauge, pressure gauge, safety valves (lift by hand to verify free movement). Verify blowdown valve is closed. Check fusible plug intact. 2. **Fill boiler**: Pump feed water to working level (visible in sight glass, typically 2/3 full).
