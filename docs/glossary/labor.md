# Labor

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Tapping is labor-intensive and resists full mechanization — the precision cut must be made by a skilled worker who adjusts depth and angle to avoid damaging the cambium. A tapper processes 200-300 trees in a 3-4 hour morning shift. Labor costs represent 50-60% of total rubber production cost in most producing regions.
