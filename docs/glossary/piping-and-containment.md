# Piping and containment

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Electropolished 316L stainless steel tubing with VCR face-seal fittings throughout. Flash-back arrestors (sintered metal elements that quench flame propagation) installed on all connections between silane supply and process tools. Lines nitrogen-purged before introducing silane and after shutting off flow. Any air in the line causes immediate ignition at the silane/air interface.
