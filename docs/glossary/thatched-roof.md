# Thatched roof

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Bundle reed, straw, or long grass (~60 cm bundles). Tie to roof poles with cordage, overlapping from eave to ridge like shingles. 30-40 cm thatch depth sheds water for 20-30 years. Roof pitch minimum 45° for water run-off. Steeper pitch (50-60°) lasts longer because water clears faster. A 5 m by 8 m roof needs 300-500 bundles and 2-3 workers for a week. Harvest thatching reed in winter when dry and stiff.
