# Block construction

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Wooden shell (elm or ash, carved in two halves, bolted around the sheave). Sheave (pulley wheel) turned from lignum vitae (self-lubricating hardwood) or iron. Pin (iron axle) through shell and sheave. Hook or becket (attachment fitting) at the top. Size rated by rope diameter — sheave groove must match rope to prevent excessive bending and wear.
