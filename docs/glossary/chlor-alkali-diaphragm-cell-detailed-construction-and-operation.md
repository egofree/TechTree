# Chlor-alkali diaphragm cell — detailed construction and operation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: chemistry

Electrolysis cell using a permeable asbestos or polymer diaphragm to separate anode and cathode compartments while allowing ion flow. Brine feed produces chlorine at the anode and hydrogen plus caustic soda at the cathode; diaphragm prevents explosive Cl₂/H₂ mixing.
