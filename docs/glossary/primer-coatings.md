# Primer coatings

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Primers provide adhesion to the substrate and corrosion protection. Metal primers: zinc-rich (galvanic protection), epoxy (barrier protection), or wash primer (etching primer: polyvinyl butyral + phosphoric acid, which etches the metal surface for adhesion). Wood primers: oil-based or acrylic, designed to seal the porous wood surface, raise the grain for sanding, and provide a base for topcoat adhesion.
