# Expansive cement

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Forms ettringite (3CaO·Al₂O₃·3CaSO₄·32H₂O) or CaO hydration products that expand during early curing, compensating for the normal drying shrinkage of concrete. Type K expansive cement: an expansive clinker (containing free CaO, C₃A, and CaSO₄) is interground with OPC clinker and gypsum. Controlled expansion of 0.03-0.10% counteracts drying shrinkage of 0.03-0.08%, resulting in crack-free concrete with no net dimensional change.
