# Companion planting

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Plant aromatic herbs (garlic, marigold, wormwood) among crops to deter insects. Beans fix nitrogen for neighboring corn (Three Sisters: corn + beans + squash). - **Crop timing**: Plant before or after peak pest emergence. Early planting lets crops establish before insect populations build. - **Physical barriers**: Netting over fruit bushes (woven cordage or split reed mats), moats around grain stores, ash rings around seedlings to deter slugs.
