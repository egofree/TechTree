# Hindu-Arabic numerals

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

(India ~500 CE, transmitted via Islamic world ~800 CE, Europe ~1200 CE): Base-10 positional notation with zero. The single most important mathematical notation ever developed. Zero serves dual function: as a placeholder (distinguishing 1, 10, 100) and as a number in its own right (enabling negative numbers and algebra).
