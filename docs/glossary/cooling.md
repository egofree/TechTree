# Cooling

> **Type**: process | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics, chemistry, energy, metals, silicon

Compressed, cleaned air enters counter-current heat exchanger — cooled by outgoing cold nitrogen and oxygen product streams. Further cooled by expansion through throttle valve (Joule-Thomson effect: real gas cools when expanding at temperatures below its inversion temperature) or expansion engine (adiabatic expansion does work → larger temperature drop).
