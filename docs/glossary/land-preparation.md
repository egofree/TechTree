# Land preparation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Clear fields, break soil with digging sticks → hoes (stone blades on handles) → iron plows (Metallurgy). One hectare feeds ~4-6 people per year with hand tools. - **Planting**: Broadcast sowing (scatter seed by hand, rake in) or drill sowing (poke holes with dibber, drop seed). Seed rate: ~50-100 kg/hectare for wheat. - **Harvesting**: Sickle (stone or iron blade, curved handle) cuts grain stalks. Threshing (beat with flail to separate grain from chaff).
