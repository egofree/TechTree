# Grease making process

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: chemistry

Manufacture of lubricating grease by dispersing a thickener (soap, clay, or polyurea) into base oil with optional additives. The thickener forms a sponge-like matrix that holds the oil, producing a semi-solid lubricant that stays in place better than liquid oil. Process involves saponification, cooking, and milling.
