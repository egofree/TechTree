# Progressive roll forming

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

produces long, continuous profiles from flat strip stock. The strip passes through 3-20 paired roller stations arranged in line, each station making a small incremental bend of 10-20°. Line speed: 1-2 m/min for heavy gauge material, up to 30 m/min for light gauge. The process suits high-volume production of uniform cross-sections: C-channels, hat sections, corrugated roofing panel profiles.
