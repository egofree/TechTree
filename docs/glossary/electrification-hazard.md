# Electrification hazard

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Electrification hazard (later stage) — once electric traction is introduced, overhead catenary wires carry 1500-25,000 V. Any contact with or near these wires is immediately lethal. Clearance below the wire: minimum 4.5 m at maximum wire sag (hot weather, maximum span). Maintenance on or near the catenary requires the section to be de-energized and grounded. Never raise any object (pole, ladder, scaffold) within 2 m of a live catenary wire. See: [Railways](../transport/railways.md)
