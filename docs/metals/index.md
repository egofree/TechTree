# Metals

Capabilities in this domain:

- [Copper & Bronze Production](copper-bronze.md) — Copper smelting from malachite, azurite, and chalcopyrite in forced-draft charcoal furnaces at 1085-1200°C.
- [Iron & Steel Production](iron-steel.md) — Bloomery smelting of iron ore at 1200-1400°C producing wrought iron, carburization for steel, and crucible steel (Wootz method).
- [Blast Furnace & Cast Iron](blast-furnace.md) — Continuous iron smelting producing pig iron and cast iron via blast furnace and cupola furnace.
