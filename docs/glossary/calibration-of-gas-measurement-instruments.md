# Calibration of gas measurement instruments

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Mass flow controllers and rotameters are calibrated against a primary standard (a volumetric displacement calibrator or a gravimetric calibrator that measures the mass of gas delivered over a timed interval). Calibration is performed at the operating temperature and pressure, or correction factors are applied.
