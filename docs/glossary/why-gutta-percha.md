# Why gutta-percha

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

No other available material combined water resistance, electrical insulation, and manufacturability. Natural rubber was too permeable to water (water absorption ~1% vs. <0.2% for gutta-percha — the absorbed water creates conductive pathways that leak telegraph signals). Waxed cloth was too porous — seawater penetrated the weave over time.
