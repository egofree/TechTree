# Well sweep

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

a Class 1 lever with the fulcrum (a forked post) near one end. A long pole (3-5 m) pivots on the fork, with a bucket hanging from the short end and a counterweight (stone or heavy log) on the long end. Pull the long end down to lower the bucket into the well, and the counterweight helps raise the full bucket. Simple, reliable, and needs no rope or pulley. Used for shallow wells (5-10 m depth).
