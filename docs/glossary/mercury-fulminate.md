# Mercury fulminate

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Mercury fulminate (primary explosive for blasting caps) — Synthesis: Mercury dissolved in nitric acid, then add ethanol. Precipitate filtered, washed, dried. Extremely friction and shock sensitive. Handle in tiny quantities (<1 g at a time). Used as initiator charge in blasting caps — small detonation wave sets off less sensitive main charge.
