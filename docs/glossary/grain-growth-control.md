# Grain growth control

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

WC grain size determines hardness (Hall-Petch relationship). Nano-grain (<0.2 µm) cemented carbides require grain growth inhibitors: VC (0.3-1.0%), Cr₃C₂ (0.3-0.8%), or TaC (0.5-1.5%). These carbides segregate to WC grain boundaries, pinning them against migration during liquid-phase sintering. Sub-micron grades (0.2-0.5 µm) achieve 1900-2100 HV30 hardness; coarse grades (2-5 µm) trade hardness (1300-1500 HV30) for improved toughness (13-18 MPa√m).
