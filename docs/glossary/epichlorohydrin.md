# Epichlorohydrin

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Used in epoxy resin synthesis. OSHA PEL 2 ppm 8-hour TWA (skin notation — absorbed through skin). IARC Group 2A. Strong, irritating odor. Handle in closed systems with local exhaust. Skin contact causes delayed burns — wear chemical-resistant gloves and splash protection.
