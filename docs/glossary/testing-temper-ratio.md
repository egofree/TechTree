# Testing temper ratio

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Make three small test tiles (flat discs 5 cm across, 1 cm thick) with different temper amounts: 20%, 30%, and 40%. Dry and fire them alongside your regular pots. The tile that survives firing without cracking and rings when tapped gives you the right temper ratio for that clay source. Different clay beds need different temper levels, so test whenever you switch to a new clay source.
