# Producer gas composition

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

~28-32% CO, ~8-15% H₂, ~1-3% CH₄, ~55-60% N₂ (nitrogen from air dilutes the gas). Calorific value: ~4.5-6 MJ/m³. Lower than coal gas (~18 MJ/m³) but sufficient for furnace firing and internal combustion engines.
