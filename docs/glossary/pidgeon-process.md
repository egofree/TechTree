# Pidgeon process

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(silicothermic reduction, dominant in China — ~85% of world production): - **Calcination**: MgCO₃ → MgO + CO₂ at 1100-1200°C. - **Briquetting**: MgO + ferrosilicon (FeSi 75%) + CaO flux mixed and pressed into briquettes. - **Reduction**: MgO + FeSi → Mg(g) + iron silicates at 1150-1200°C under vacuum (1-10 Pa) in horizontal retorts (nickel-chromium steel, 250-300 mm diameter, 2.5-3 m long).
