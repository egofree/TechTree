# Yield learning curve

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

New semiconductor products start at 10-30% yield and improve to 80-95% over 12-24 months through systematic defect reduction. Each 1% yield improvement on a 300 mm wafer producing 500 die at $50/die represents $250,000 annual revenue increase. This economic incentive drives aggressive yield improvement programs: failure analysis of every failed die, root cause identification, and process or design modification.
