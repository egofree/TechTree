# Tortoiseshell

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

from the hawksbill sea turtle (*Eretmochelys imbricata*, now critically endangered and CITES Appendix I) was historically prized for its beautiful mottled brown and amber patterning. The material is thermoplastic at 120-150°C and can be pressed into sheets, molded, and welded similarly to horn.
