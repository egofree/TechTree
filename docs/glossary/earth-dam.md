# Earth dam

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

(simplest): Compacted clay core with earth and stone shoulders. Height 2-5 m, crest width 2-3 m, upstream slope 1:2 (horizontal:vertical), downstream slope 1:2.5. Puddle clay core (0.5-1 m thick) keyed into natural subsoil to prevent seepage. Overflow spillway on one side to pass flood water safely.
