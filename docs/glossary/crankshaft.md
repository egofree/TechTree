# Crankshaft

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy, transport

Forge from medium-carbon steel (0.4-0.5% C). Machine main bearing journals and crankpins to ±0.01 mm tolerance, surface finish <0.2 μm Ra. Induction harden journal surfaces (heat surface layer to 850°C with induction coil, quench → hard surface, tough core). Balance dynamically (add or remove metal from counterweights until no vibration at operating RPM).
