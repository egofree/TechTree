# Lift

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals, transport

L = ½ρv²SCL, where ρ = air density (1.225 kg/m³ at sea level), v = airspeed, S = wing area, CL = lift coefficient (0.5-1.5 depending on angle of attack and airfoil). At 80 km/h (22.2 m/s), 15 m² wing, CL = 0.8: L = ½ × 1.225 × 22.2² × 15 × 0.8 = ~3620 N (~370 kg force). Sufficient to lift ultralight aircraft.
