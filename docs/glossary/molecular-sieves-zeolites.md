# Molecular sieves (zeolites)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Crystalline aluminosilicates with uniform pore sizes (3Å, 4Å, 5Å, 10Å). Adsorb molecules smaller than the pore diameter while excluding larger molecules. 3Å sieves adsorb water but exclude all other molecules (ideal for drying gases). 4Å sieves adsorb water, CO₂, H₂S, and small hydrocarbons. Regenerable by heating to 250-350°C or by vacuum. Capacity: 15-25% by weight. Extremely effective: can dry gases to <1 ppm H₂O.
