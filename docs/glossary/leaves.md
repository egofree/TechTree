# Leaves

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

Harvest at peak flowering for maximum alkaloid and volatile oil content — the plant concentrates defense compounds in foliage during reproductive phase to protect flowers and seeds. For example, *Artemisia* species yield 2-3× more essential oil at early flowering than at vegetative stage. Harvest in the morning after dew has dried but before the heat of the day causes volatile oil evaporation.
