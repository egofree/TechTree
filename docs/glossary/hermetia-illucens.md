# Hermetia illucens

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: critical | **Domains**: animals

Black Soldier Fly (BSF) — the primary insect species for bioconversion of organic waste to animal feed protein. Adults do not bite, carry disease, or enter dwellings. Larvae consume almost any organic matter and convert it to body mass at remarkable efficiency: 1 tonne biowaste yields 150-200 kg fresh larvae in 14-18 days.
