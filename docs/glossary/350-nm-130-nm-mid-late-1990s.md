# 350 nm → 130 nm (mid-late 1990s)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: vlsi-scaling

ArF excimer laser (193 nm) introduced. Shallow trench isolation (STI) replaces LOCOS. Gate oxide thins to ~2 nm — quantum mechanical tunneling leakage begins. Copper replaces aluminum interconnects (see below). 200 mm → 300 mm wafer transition begins. Yield challenge: across-wafer uniformity on 300 mm wafers requires fully automated wafer handling.
