# Grain refinement

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Add Al-Ti-B master alloy (typically Al-5%Ti-1%B in rod form) to the melt. TiB₂ particles nucleate fine equiaxed grains during solidification. Grain size: 100-300 μm with refinement vs. 1-5 mm without. Fine grain improves mechanical properties, reduces hot tearing, and improves feeding during solidification.
