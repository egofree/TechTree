# Cathode purity

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

99.99%+ Cu. Impurity limits for Grade A copper (LME standard): Ag <15 ppm, As <3 ppm, Sb <2 ppm, Bi <1 ppm, Se <1 ppm, Te <1 ppm, Fe <5 ppm, Pb <5 ppm, Ni <5 ppm, Sn <3 ppm, S <15 ppm, O <20 ppm (for cathode, before melting). Higher purity (99.999%, 5N) achievable with careful anode selection and electrolyte purification, required for electronic applications.
