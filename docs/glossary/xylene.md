# Xylene

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

(C₈H₁₀, mixture of ortho-, meta-, para- isomers, bp ~140°C): - Higher-boiling aromatic solvent. Used where slower evaporation is desired (paints, coatings, cleaning). - Applications: histology staining solvent, paint and coating formulations, cleaning agent for silicon wafers in semiconductor processing. - Toxicity: moderate — irritant and nervous system depressant at high concentrations. Less hazardous than benzene.
