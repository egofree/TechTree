# Brood lac management

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

The key to successful cultivation is timing the inoculation (placement of brood lac bundles on new host branches) to coincide with the emergence of larvae from the brood. Larvae emerge 2-3 weeks after the eggs were laid inside the female's resinous cell. The tiny crawler larvae (0.3-0.5 mm) disperse from the brood bundle onto the fresh branch, settle, insert their proboscis, and begin secreting resin.
