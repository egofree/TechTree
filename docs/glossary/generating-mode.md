# Generating mode

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Generating mode (discharging) — - Water is released from the upper reservoir through a penstock to a turbine-generator unit. The turbine extracts the gravitational potential energy, driving the generator to produce electricity. Spent water returns to the lower reservoir. - Generation occurs during peak demand hours (day) when electricity is most valuable. See: [Pumped Hydroelectric Storage](../energy/pumped-hydro.md)
