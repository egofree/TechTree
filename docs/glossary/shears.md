# Shears

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Steel cutting shears with long handles (40-50 cm) and short blades (8-12 cm). Blade edges are ground straight, not serrated. Used to trim excess glass, cut the piece from the blowpipe, and shape hot glass. Spring-loaded models reduce hand fatigue. Shears must be kept sharp — dull blades crush rather than cut, leaving ragged edges.
