# Inkjet inks

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Low viscosity (2-20 mPa·s) — must flow through 10-50 µm nozzles at >10 kHz drop frequency. Dye-based (soluble dye in water/glycol) for vivid color but poor fade resistance. Pigment-based (sub-micron pigment particles dispersed in water) for archival quality. Industrial inkjet: UV-curable inks (acrylate oligomers + photoinitiators) cured by UV LED instantly on the substrate — enables printing on non-porous surfaces (plastic, metal, glass).
