# Carbon ink

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Carbon ink (printing ink base) — soot (lampblack from burning oil or resin) + linseed oil binder + rosin (improves tack and drying). Grind soot with oil on stone slab with muller (flat glass or stone disk) until uniform smooth paste. Consistency: thick enough to adhere to type face without running, thin enough for even transfer.
