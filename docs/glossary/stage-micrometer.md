# stage micrometer

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement, optics

is a microscope slide with a precision-ruled scale, typically 1 mm divided into 100 divisions (0.01 mm per division). Place on the stage, focus, and align with the **eyepiece reticle** (a glass disc with engraved scale in the eyepiece focal plane). The reticle scale is calibrated against the stage micrometer at each magnification. After calibration, any specimen feature can be measured by counting reticle divisions.
