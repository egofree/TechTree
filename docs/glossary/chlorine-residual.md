# Chlorine residual

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

DPD (N,N-diethyl-p-phenylenediamine) test kit. Tablet or liquid reagent turns water pink in proportion to free chlorine concentration. Compare color to chart. Target: 0.2-0.5 mg/L free chlorine at the point of use. Absence of residual means either insufficient dosing or contamination entering the system downstream of the dosing point.
