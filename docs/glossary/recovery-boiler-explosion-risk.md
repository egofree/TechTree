# Recovery boiler explosion risk

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Smelt-water explosion — water contacting molten smelt (Na₂CO₃/Na₂S at 800-1000°C) causes violent steam explosion. Multiple safety systems: dissolving tank with agitator (smelt flows through spout into water), emergency shutdown procedures, blast walls. Historically the most catastrophic event in pulp mills.
