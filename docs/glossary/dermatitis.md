# Dermatitis

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Shellac resin and ethanol solvent both cause skin drying and irritation with repeated contact. Nitrile or rubber gloves recommended for prolonged handling. Some individuals develop allergic contact dermatitis to specific shellac components — aleuritic acid (the major hydroxy fatty acid component, ~35% of shellac composition) has been identified as a contact sensitizer in rare cases.
