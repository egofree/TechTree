# Hot mix asphalt (HMA)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Aggregate (crushed stone 0-20 mm graded) mixed with bitumen binder (5-6% by weight) at 150-180°C in a batch plant. Laid with a paving machine at 135-165°C. Compact with steel-wheel roller (initial pass at 135°C, finish at 90°C minimum).
