# Closure

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Simple lacerations — bring edges together with adhesive strips (butterfly bandages from adhesive tape) or sutures (sterilized catgut or silk thread + curved needle). Catgut: made from sheep intestine, stretched, twisted, dried. Absorbs over 7-14 days. Silk: non-absorbable, remove after 7-14 days.
