# Pouring

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics, machine-tools, metals, silicon

Liquid silicon from the taphole flows into the mold via a refractory-lined launder (channel). Pour temperature: ~1550-1650°C. Pour rate controlled to avoid splashing and turbulence (turbulent flow traps gas bubbles in the solidifying silicon). Fill the mold steadily without interruption.
