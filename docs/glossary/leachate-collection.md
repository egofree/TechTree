# Leachate collection

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Perforated HDPE pipes (100-200 mm diameter) embedded in gravel drainage layer (0.3 m thick) above the liner. Leachate flows by gravity to sumps, where it is pumped out for treatment. Leachate composition: high BOD (>10,000 mg/L), ammonia (>500 mg/L), heavy metals, dissolved organic compounds. Treat by: recirculation back through the waste (biological stabilization), on-site treatment (aeration, chemical precipitation), or transport to sewage treatment plant.
