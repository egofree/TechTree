# Classification by alumina content

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Low-duty (25-30% Al₂O₃, pyrometric cone equivalent PCE 15-20, service to 1200-1300°C) for boiler linings and moderate-temperature furnaces. Medium-duty (30-35% Al₂O₃, PCE 20-27, service to 1350-1450°C) for steel reheating furnaces and kiln linings. High-duty (35-45% Al₂O₃, PCE 27-33, service to 1450-1500°C) for high-temperature zones in kilns and furnaces. Super-duty (>45% Al₂O₃) begins to overlap with high-alumina brick classification.
