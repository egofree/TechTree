# Electrolyte composition

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

CuSO₄·5H₂O (150-200 g/L Cu²⁺, equivalent to 38-50 g/L Cu) + H₂SO₄ (150-200 g/L) in aqueous solution. Temperature 50-55°C (reduces viscosity and resistance, improves Cu²⁺ diffusion). The sulfuric acid increases solution conductivity and prevents CuSO₄ precipitation. Electrolyte density ~1.2 g/mL.
