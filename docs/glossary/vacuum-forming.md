# Vacuum forming

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

(simplest method): Clamp heated sheet over mold. Apply vacuum through small holes drilled in mold surface (0.5-1 mm holes, closely spaced at 10-20 mm centers). Atmospheric pressure (0.1 MPa) pushes softened sheet onto mold. Simple, low-cost. Mold can be wood, plaster, cast aluminum — no steel tooling required. Products: trays, containers, signs, blister packs, boat hulls (large molds — a 3 m boat hull can be vacuum-formed from 6 mm HDPE sheet over a wooden male mold), aircraft fairings.
