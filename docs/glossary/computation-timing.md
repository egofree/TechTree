# Computation timing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: computing

Addition: 0.3 seconds (three 0.1-second machine cycles). Multiplication: 6 seconds. Division: 15.3 seconds. Logarithm: 71.4 seconds. Sine: ~60 seconds. Compared to a human with a mechanical calculator, Mark I was 5-10× faster on addition and 3-5× faster on multiplication.
