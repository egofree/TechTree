# Gas composition

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, energy

Charcoal gasified with limited air produces producer gas with the following approximate composition by volume: CO 20-25%, H₂ 10-15%, CH₄ 2-4%, CO₂ 8-12%, N₂ 50-55%. Calorific value: 4-6 MJ/m³, roughly one-third the energy density of natural gas (35 MJ/m³) and far below gasoline vapor. This low energy density is the fundamental limitation: engine power output drops to 40-60% of gasoline rating when running on producer gas.
