# ESD damage to devices

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Static discharges as low as 50 V destroy MOS gate oxides. Wear grounded wrist straps (1 MΩ current-limiting resistor) at all times when handling wafers or packaged devices. Maintain humidity at 40-50 % RH; use ionizers on non-groundable surfaces. Test wrist-strap continuity daily.
