# Discharge zone

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Discharge zone (bottom) — dRI is continuously discharged. It can be hot-charged directly to an EAF (hot DRI at ~600-700°C, saving ~100 kWh/t of EAF electricity) or cooled and stored for later use. See: [Blast Furnace and Cast Iron](../metals/blast-furnace.md)
