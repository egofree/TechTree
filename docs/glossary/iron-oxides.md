# Iron oxides

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Fe₂O₃ (red), Fe₃O₄ (black), FeOOH (yellow ochre). Natural or synthetic. The oldest pigments — used since cave paintings. Low cost, excellent lightfastness, non-toxic. Synthetic versions: precipitate FeSO₄ + NaOH, then oxidize at controlled temperature and pH to control particle size and color.
