# Vulcanization

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Sulfur cure (1-2 phr sulfur, 0.5-1.5 phr accelerator) via the ENB diene sites. Peroxide cure (dicumyl peroxide, 2-4 phr) also effective — produces carbon-carbon cross-links that are more thermally stable than sulfur cross-links.
