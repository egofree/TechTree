# Fuel quality

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Cetane number ≥40 (preferably 45-55) for ignition quality. Viscosity: 2.0-4.5 cSt at 40°C. Sulfur: historically up to 2%, now limited to 10-50 ppm. Lubricity additives compensate for lubricating properties lost with sulfur removal.
