# Electrical telephone

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Electrical telephone (requires electrical infrastructure) — magneto telephones (hand-cranked ring generator) connected by cable. The standard method in 20th-century mines. Explosion-proof housings required in gassy mines. Cables vulnerable to damage from blasting and rock falls — run through protected conduits. See: [Mining Extraction](../mining/extraction.md)
