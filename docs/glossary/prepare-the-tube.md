# Prepare the tube

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement

Clean glass tube with dilute nitric acid (dissolves metal contamination), rinse repeatedly with distilled water, dry in oven at 120°C. Any contamination or residue on the inner wall creates nucleation sites for air bubbles, causing permanent reading errors. Verify the sealed end is fully closed — pressurize with a rubber bulb and immerse in water; any bubbles indicate a pinhole leak.
