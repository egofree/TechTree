# Hot phosphoric acid

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

H₃PO₄ at 155-180°C in a reflux system (condenser returns evaporated acid). Etch rate: 5-10 nm/min for Si₃N₄. Selectivity to SiO₂: 10:1 (phosphoric attacks nitride much faster than oxide). Selectivity to silicon: >50:1. The high temperature requires a reflux condenser to prevent acid evaporation and maintain constant concentration. Silicon nitride is used as a hard mask and as a selective etch stop because it resists most silicon and oxide etchants.
