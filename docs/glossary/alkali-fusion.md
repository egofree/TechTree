# Alkali fusion

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

Mix zircon sand with NaOH, heat to 600-700°C → sodium zirconate forms 2. **Water leach**: Dissolve sodium zirconate in water, filter off insoluble silica 3. **Precipitation**: Add acid (HCl or H₂SO₄) to precipitate zirconium hydroxide 4. **Calcination**: Heat to 800-1000°C → pure ZrO₂ powder
