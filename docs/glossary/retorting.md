# Retorting

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Heat the amalgam in a sealed iron retort to 400-500°C. Mercury vaporizes (boiling point 357°C) and condenses in a water-cooled condenser for reuse. Gold remains in the retort as a porous sponge (sponge gold), which is then melted into a doré button. Mercury recovery rate: 95-98% with a good condenser.
