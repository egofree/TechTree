# Fermentation vat

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

(traditional): Fill a tall narrow vessel with warm water (40-50°C). Add woad or indigo powder (50-100 g/L), wheat bran (nutrient for bacteria), and wood ash lye (pH 9-10). Maintain 35-45°C for 3-7 days until the liquor turns yellow-green and a coppery scum forms. Stir gently, wait for sediment to settle.
