# Laser safety

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: computing, measurement

CD/DVD/Blu-ray drives contain Class 1 laser systems (safe under normal operation). Never operate a drive with the enclosure removed. The 780 nm (CD), 650 nm (DVD), and 405 nm (Blu-ray) lasers can cause retinal damage with direct exposure. Service manuals require laser safety glasses matched to the specific wavelength.
