# Foraging rate

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Foraging rate: 80-120 insects per chicken per hour of active foraging. Target prey includes grasshoppers, beetles, caterpillars, slugs, fly larvae, and ants. Chickens also consume weed seeds, reducing the seed bank in the soil. See: [Biological Pest Management](../animals/pest-management.md)
