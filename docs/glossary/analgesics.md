# Analgesics

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

(see medicine.md for clinical use): - Willow bark: salicin extraction by water decoction (30 g bark per 500 mL). Later acetylated to aspirin at 85°C with acetic anhydride. - Opium: morphine extraction — dissolve opium in hot water, precipitate with NaOH, re-dissolve in HCl, recrystallize morphine hydrochloride. Highly addictive — controlled use only.
