# Digging sticks

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Fire-harden tip of hardwood stick (heat until brown, not black) for soil penetration. A fire-hardened digging stick penetrates packed soil that would blunt a raw wood tip. Length 1-1.5 m, diameter 2-3 cm. Attach a foot-step (crosswise peg or stone weight lashed near the tip) to push with the foot for deeper digging.
