# Fluidized bed reactor polysilicon deposition

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Silane is the preferred feedstock for FBR polysilicon production. SiH₄ injected into a fluidized bed of silicon seed particles at 600-700°C decomposes on contact: SiH₄ → Si + 2H₂. Seed particles grow from 0.1 mm to 1-2 mm and are continuously withdrawn as granular polysilicon. Energy consumption: 15-30 kWh/kg, compared to 100-200 kWh/kg for Siemens rod reactors. The granular product flows freely and loads easily into CZ crucibles, unlike the fragile rod segments from Siemens process.
