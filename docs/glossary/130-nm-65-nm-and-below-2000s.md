# 130 nm → 65 nm and below (2000s+)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: vlsi-scaling

Immersion lithography (water between lens and wafer) boosts numerical aperture above 1.0. Gate dielectric hits physical limit — SiO₂ at ~1.2 nm has intolerable leakage (see Gate Dielectric Scaling below). Strained silicon engineering introduced. Multiple patterning (double, quad) extends 193 nm lithography far beyond its natural resolution. Yield challenge: variability — random dopant fluctuations, line-edge roughness, systematic layout-dependent effects.
