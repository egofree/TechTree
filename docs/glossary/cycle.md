# Cycle

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, energy

Pressurize vessel with compressed air (~6-10 bar) → adsorbent captures target gas, desired gas passes through as product → depressurize vessel → adsorbed gas desorbs and is vented → repressurize. Two vessels operate in alternating cycles for continuous output. Cycle time 30-120 seconds.
