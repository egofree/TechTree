# Lime cycle

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

The kraft mill operates a parallel calcium cycle. CaCO₃ (lime mud from causticizing) is fed to a lime kiln (2-4 m diameter × 40-80 m long rotary kiln, firing natural gas or fuel oil). Kiln temperature: 900-1100°C. CaCO₃ → CaO + CO₂. CaO is slaked with water to Ca(OH)₂ (slaked lime), which is then used to causticize green liquor.
