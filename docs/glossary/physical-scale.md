# Physical scale

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: computing

Physical scale refers to 765,000 parts, 51 feet (15.5 m) long, 8 feet (2.4 m) tall, weighed approximately 10,000 lb (4,500 kg). 530 miles of wire connected 3,500 relays. The machine occupied most of a large room. See: [Electromechanical Computing](../computing/electromechanical.md)
