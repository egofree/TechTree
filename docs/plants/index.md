# Plants & Botanical Resources

Capabilities in this domain:

- [Edible Plants](edible-plants.md) — Foraging and cultivation of plant species for human consumption including vegetables, fruits, seeds, roots, and herbs.
- [Medicinal Plants](medicinal-plants.md) — Identification, preparation, and application of plant species with therapeutic properties for treating illness, injury, and disease.
- [Fiber Plants](fiber-plants.md) — Cultivation and harvesting of plant species that yield fibers for cordage, textiles, and weaving.
- [Structural Plants](structural-plants.md) — Harvesting of plant species used for construction, building materials, and structural applications.
- [Dye Plants](dye-plants.md) — Cultivation and processing of plant species that yield natural colorants for dyeing textiles, leather, and other materials.
