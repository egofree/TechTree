# Coating systems for steel structures

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: metals

Multi-layer protective paint systems for structural steel: primer (zinc-rich or inhibitive), intermediate coat (epoxy barrier), and topcoat (polyurethane for UV resistance). Total dry film thickness 200-400 μm; surface preparation to Sa 2.5 (near-white blast) is critical for adhesion.
