# Jaw crusher

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Jaw crusher (Machine Tools) — Two jaw plates (cast iron or manganese steel), one fixed, one oscillating. Ore fed in top, crushed between jaws. Gap adjustable for output size (10-50 mm). Powered by water wheel or steam engine. 5-50 tonnes/day. See: [Ore Processing](../mining/processing.md)
