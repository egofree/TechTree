# Mediterranean (hot dry summer, mild wet winter)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

Wheat, barley, olive, grape, chickpea. Winter-sown cereals exploit rainy season. Olive (*Olea europaea*) provides long-lived fat source — trees produce 20-50 kg olives/year at 15-40 years of age, pressing 15-20% oil by weight - **Subtropical/tropical wet**: Rice (paddy), cassava, sweet potato, taro, banana.
