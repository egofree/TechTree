# Copper (Dumet) to soda-lime

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Dumet wire is a copper-clad nickel-iron alloy (42% Ni, 58% Fe core, Cu cladding). The composite CTE (~7 × 10⁻⁶/°C) matches soda-lime glass (~9 × 10⁻⁶/°C) closely enough for reliable sealing. The copper cladding provides a plastically deformable layer that absorbs residual stress from the small CTE mismatch. Used for incandescent lamp stems and electrical feedthroughs.
