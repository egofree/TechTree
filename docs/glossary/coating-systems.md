# Coating systems

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

for cemented carbide cutting tools: - **TiN** (titanium nitride): Gold-colored PVD coating, 2-5 µm thick. Hardness ~2100 HV. Reduces friction coefficient to 0.4-0.5 (vs. 0.6-0.8 for uncoated WC-Co). Operating temperature limit: ~600°C (TiN oxidizes above this). - **TiAlN** (titanium aluminum nitride): Purple/grey PVD coating, 2-4 µm. Hardness ~3200 HV.
