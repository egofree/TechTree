# Powered air-purifying respirator (PAPR)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

battery-driven fan (4-8 hours per charge) pulls air through HE filters and delivers clean air under positive pressure to a hood or full facepiece. No breathing resistance. Filter class HE (high efficiency, 99.97% at 0.3 μm). The positive pressure means that even a poor face seal does not allow contaminated air inward.
