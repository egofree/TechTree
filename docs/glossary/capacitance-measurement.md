# Capacitance measurement

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement

A capacitance bridge (similar to Wheatstone bridge but with AC excitation) compares the unknown capacitor against a standard. The Schering bridge measures capacitance and dissipation factor (tan δ) simultaneously, important for assessing capacitor quality. A capacitor with high dissipation factor wastes energy as heat and indicates poor dielectric material or manufacturing defects.
