# Retting

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants, textiles

Retting (separating fiber from stem) — Submerge flax stalks in water (pond, stream, or dew-retting on grass) for 5-14 days. Bacteria decompose pectin binding fibers to woody core (shives). Test: when fibers separate easily from stem, retting is complete. Over-retting weakens fibers irreversibly — check daily after day 5.
