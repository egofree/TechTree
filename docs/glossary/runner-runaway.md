# Runner runaway

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

If the generator disconnects from the grid (load rejection) and the wicket gates fail to close, the turbine accelerates to runaway speed — typically 1.8-2.5× rated speed. Centrifugal forces scale with the square of speed — a 2× overspeed quadruples stress. Structural failure is possible. Prevention: governor-closed wicket gates with independent overspeed trip, jet deflectors (Pelton), and emergency gate upstream that can drop closed.
