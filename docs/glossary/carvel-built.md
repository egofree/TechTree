# Carvel-built

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

(edge-to-edge planks): - Erect the frames first on the keel, then fasten planks edge-to-edge against the frames. Plank edges meet flush, producing a smooth hull surface with less drag than clinker. - Plank thickness 3-6 cm (oak or pine). Fasten with iron nails or wooden trenails driven through plank and frame. Gaps between planks caulked with oakum (frayed hemp rope fibers hammered into seams with a caulking iron) and sealed with pitch or tar. - Carvel construction scales larger than clinker....
