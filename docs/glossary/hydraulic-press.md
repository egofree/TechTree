# Hydraulic press

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

Oil at 100-300 bar drives one or more pistons. Tonnage range: 20-10,000 tons. Full rated tonnage is available at any point in the stroke, not just at bottom dead center. Ram speed is adjustable from 0.5-50 mm/s. Slower cycle times than mechanical presses but superior for deep drawing and forming operations that require controlled force over a long distance.
