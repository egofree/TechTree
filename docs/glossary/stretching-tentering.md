# Stretching (tentering)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Stretch damp cloth on a tenter frame (wooden frame with hooks on adjustable bars spaced every 2-5 cm along both edges). Cloth dries under tension to the desired width and straight grain. Tenter hooks give us the phrase "on tenterhooks." Dry in shade — sun yellows wool and fades dyed cloth.
