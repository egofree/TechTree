# Height-to-diameter ratio

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Fractional distillation columns typically have H/D ratios of 10-30:1. This ratio ensures adequate separation stages while maintaining reasonable vapor velocity. Too tall and narrow (H/D > 30) leads to poor liquid distribution and channeling in packed columns. Too short and wide (H/D < 10) requires large diameter for vapor handling but provides few theoretical plates.
