# Sizing calculation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Gasifier thermal output (kW) determines the physical dimensions. A 100 kW (thermal) downdraft gasifier processes ~30-40 kg/hour of dry wood (energy content 16-18 MJ/kg). Gasifier hearth diameter: ~300 mm for 100 kW, scaling roughly with the square root of throughput. Overall height 1.5-2.5 m. Fuel hopper volume: sufficient for 1-4 hours of operation (20-160 kg wood).
