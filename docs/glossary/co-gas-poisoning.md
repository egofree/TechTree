# CO gas poisoning

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Carbon monoxide is colorless, odorless, and lethal at 0.1% concentration in air for 1 hour. Off-gas system leaks are the primary risk. Fixed CO detectors with audible alarms mandatory in furnace building. Personnel must evacuate at >50 ppm. Never enter furnace hood area without supplied-air respirator during operation.
