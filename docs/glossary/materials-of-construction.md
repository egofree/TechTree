# Materials of construction

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Concentrated H₂SO₄ (>85%): carbon steel (forms protective FeSO₄ film, corrosion rate <0.5 mm/year). Cast iron: suitable for >65% at ambient temperature. 316L stainless steel: good for all concentrations up to 60°C (above 60°C, corrosion accelerates). PTFE, polypropylene, and PVDF: excellent chemical resistance for all concentrations at moderate temperatures. Glass-lined steel: for high-temperature service and high-purity applications.
