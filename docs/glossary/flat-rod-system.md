# Flat-rod system

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

(Stangenkunst — transmitting power over distance): Wooden or iron rods connected in a push-pull arrangement transmit reciprocating motion from water wheel to distant mine pumps or machinery. Rods supported on rocking posts every 10-20 m. Can transmit 5-10 HP over 1-2 km. Used extensively in Central European mining from the 16th century.
