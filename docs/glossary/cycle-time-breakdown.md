# Cycle time breakdown

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Injection (1-5 seconds) → packing/holding (2-10 seconds, maintains pressure as part cools and shrinks) → cooling (10-90 seconds, typically 50-70% of total cycle — part must cool below deflection temperature before ejection) → mold open/eject (2-5 seconds). Total cycle: 15-120 seconds. Faster cycles mean higher production rate — a 20-second cycle produces 180 parts/hour per cavity.
