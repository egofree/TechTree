# Secondary operations

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Sizing (re-pressing at 400-600 MPa for dimensional precision ±0.025 mm), machining (drilling, tapping, turning), steam treatment (400-550°C in superheated steam → Fe₃O₄ surface layer seals pores, increases hardness and corrosion resistance), oil impregnation (fill open pores with oil for self-lubricating bearings — 10-30% oil by volume).
