# Seal the tube

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement

Heat the open end of the capillary in flame until it collapses and seals closed. Leave a small expansion chamber above the top scale mark — without it, thermal expansion of fill fluid at the top of the range can burst the tube. Ensure the seal is pure glass with no trapped mercury (mercury trapped in the seal vaporizes during annealing and causes drift).
