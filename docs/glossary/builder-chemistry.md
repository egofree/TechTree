# Builder chemistry

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

(critical detergent component): - Detergents cannot function effectively without builders — chemicals that sequester calcium and magnesium ions, preventing them from interfering with the surfactant. Builder choice determines detergent performance in hard water. - **Sodium tripolyphosphate (STPP)**: Most effective builder. Sequesters Ca²⁺ and Mg²⁺, disperses soil particles, provides alkalinity.
