# Step 2 — Secondary reformer

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Residual CH₄ (5-10% from primary) is reformed with air (which also introduces the N₂ needed for ammonia synthesis). Air is injected and partially combusted: CH₄ + O₂ → CO₂ + H₂O (provides heat), then remaining CH₄ + H₂O → CO + H₂ over Ni catalyst. Exit temperature: 950-1000°C. Gas composition: ~56% H₂, ~22% N₂, ~12% CO, ~8% CO₂ + H₂O.
