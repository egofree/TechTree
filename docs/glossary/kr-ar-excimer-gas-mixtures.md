# Kr / Ar excimer gas mixtures

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: vlsi-scaling

The noble gases (Kr, Ar) with fluorine are asphyxiation risks in confined spaces — they are odorless, colorless, and displace oxygen. Use O₂ monitors in laser rooms; alarm at 19.5 % O₂. Fluorine component adds the reactivity hazards noted above.
