# Microscopes

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health, optics

Microscopes (critical for semiconductor inspection) — Compound microscope**: Objective lens (4x-100x) + eyepiece (10x). Total magnification 40x-1000x. Resolution limited by diffraction: ~λ/(2·NA) where NA = numerical aperture.
