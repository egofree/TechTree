# Zinc fume fever

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(metal fume fever): Inhalation of zinc oxide fume (from welding/brazing galvanized steel) causes influenza-like symptoms 4-8 hours post-exposure: chills, fever, muscle ache, metallic taste. Self-limiting (resolves in 24-48 hours) but repeated episodes cause lung damage. Prevention: local exhaust ventilation, respiratory protection when welding galvanized steel.
