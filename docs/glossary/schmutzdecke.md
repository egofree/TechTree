# Schmutzdecke

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

The biologically active layer that forms on top of the sand bed. Composed of algae, bacteria, protozoa, and trapped organic matter. This layer performs the actual purification — it is NOT a clogging problem to be eliminated but the working heart of the filter. Takes 1-4 weeks to mature after new filter startup. Removes 95-99% of bacteria, 90-99% of turbidity, and significant reduction in viruses and parasites.
