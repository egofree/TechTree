# Thickness

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: optics

λ/4 optical thickness = λ/(4n) physical thickness. For center of visible spectrum (λ = 550 nm): thickness = 550/(4 × 1.38) = ~100 nm. At this thickness, the coating provides optimal anti-reflection at 550 nm (green) and reduced reflection across the visible band.
