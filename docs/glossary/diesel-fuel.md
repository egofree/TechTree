# Diesel fuel

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Petroleum fraction boiling at 180-360°C. Cetane number 40-55 (measures ignition quality — higher = shorter ignition delay). Energy density ~45 MJ/kg (~38 MJ/L). Cloud point and pour point critical for cold weather operation — paraffin wax crystallizes at low temperatures, clogging fuel filters.
