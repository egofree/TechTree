# Biocompatibility

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Inert in the body — does not trigger immune response, inflammatory reaction, or tissue irritation. The highly crystalline, chemically inert nature of trans-polyisoprene resists both hydrolysis and enzymatic degradation. Seals the root canal to prevent bacterial reinfection — the most common cause of root canal failure.
