# Flax/linseed oil

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Flax/linseed oil: 30-35% oil content. Dual purpose, serving as both food oil and industrial material. As a drying oil, it polymerizes on exposure to air, making it useful as a paint binder, wood finish, and waterproofing treatment for canvas and leather. Food-grade flax oil should be pressed fresh and used within weeks (it turns rancid).
