# Sub-boiling distillation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Heat acid below its boiling point in a PTFE or quartz still. Only the purest vapor fraction condenses on a cooled surface. Slow (days per batch) but achieves ultra-trace purity (<0.1 ppb for most metals). Used for ACS reagent-grade and semiconductor-grade acids.
