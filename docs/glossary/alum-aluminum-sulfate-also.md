# Alum (aluminum sulfate, Al₂(SO₄)₃)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

The traditional papermaking chemical, used for over 150 years. Dissolves in water to form an acidic solution (pH 4.0-5.5). Functions: cationic charge source (attracts anionic fibers and fines), assists rosin sizing, scavenges anionic trash (dissolved and colloidal substances that interfere with wet-end chemistry).
