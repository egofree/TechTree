# Gas doping during FZ

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: silicon

Introduction of gaseous dopants (typically phosphine PH₃ for n-type or diborane B₂H₆ for p-type) into the float-zone molten zone during crystal growth, enabling precise doping control without crucible contamination. Gas doping allows radial and axial resistivity uniformity adjustment.
