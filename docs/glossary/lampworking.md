# Lampworking

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Lampworking (bench-scale glassblowing with torch) — Torch**: Gas-air (moderate temperature, ~1200°C) for soft glass. Gas-oxygen (~2000°C) for borosilicate. Propane-oxygen for quartz. - **Basic operations**: Rotate tubing in flame to heat evenly. Pull to draw thin walls or capillary. Push to thicken. Blow (by mouth through tube) to expand.
