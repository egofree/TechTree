# 4×/5× reticles (projection steppers)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Pattern drawn at 4× or 5× final size on the reticle. Stepper optics reduce to 1× on wafer. Allows higher mask resolution and easier defect management. Written by electron-beam (e-beam) lithography: focused 10-30 keV electron beam raster-scans the resist on the chrome. Spot size 10-50 nm. Write time: 1-8 hours per reticle depending on pattern density.
