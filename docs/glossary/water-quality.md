# Water quality

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Deionized water required (<1 µS/cm conductivity for PEM, <5 µS/cm for alkaline). Dissolved minerals cause scaling on electrodes and diaphragm blockage. Water consumption: ~9 L per kg H₂ (stoichiometric) + ~2 L/kg for purification system blowdown.
