# End-point control

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Sublance measurement (temperature + carbon by liquidus method) at 80-90% of expected blow time. Target: 1600-1650°C and 0.03-0.08% C. If temperature is high and carbon is low, stop the blow. If adjustments needed, continue blowing or add coolants. Modern BOFs achieve target within ±5°C and ±0.01% C on >90% of heats.
