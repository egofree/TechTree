# Bubble inspection

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Illuminate the crucible wall with a bright light. Bubbles appear as bright spots. For semiconductor-grade crucibles, bubbles larger than 50 μm are rejectable in the inner (silicon-contact) wall. Outer wall bubbles up to 200 μm are tolerated. Bubbles near the inner surface can detach during CZ pulling, contaminating the silicon melt.
