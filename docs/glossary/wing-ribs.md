# Wing ribs

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Thin plywood (2-3 mm) or built-up wood (capstrips + gussets). Airfoil shape cut into rib. Spars pass through notches in ribs. Ribs spaced 200-400 mm along span. Maintain airfoil shape between spars. Attach fabric covering to rib flanges.
