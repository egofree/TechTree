# Calorific value

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

3-4 MJ/Nm³ (compared to ~38 MJ/Nm³ for natural gas). This is low-grade fuel — the high nitrogen and CO₂ content dilute the combustible CO and H₂. Despite the low heating value, the enormous volume produced (2,000-4,000 Nm³ per tonne of pig iron) makes BFG a significant energy resource.
