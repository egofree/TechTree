# Oxygen transfer

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Aerobic fermentations (citric acid, antibiotics, single-cell protein) require continuous oxygen supply. The critical dissolved oxygen concentration is typically 10-30% of air saturation; below this, oxygen becomes rate-limiting.
