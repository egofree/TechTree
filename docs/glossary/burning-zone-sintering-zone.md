# Burning zone / sintering zone

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

(1400-1500°C): CaO reacts with SiO₂, Al₂O₃, Fe₂O₃ to form clinker minerals (C₃S, C₂S, C₃A, C₄AF). Material becomes partially liquid (20-30% melt) — nodules form by agglomeration. Residence time in burning zone: 10-20 minutes. Temperature controlled by fuel rate and raw meal feed rate.
