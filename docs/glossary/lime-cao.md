# Lime (CaO)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, glass

10-12% — stabilizer, prevents water solubility of the glass - **Alumina (Al₂O₃)**: 1-3% — improves chemical durability and hardness - **Magnesia (MgO)**: 0.1-1% — prevents devitrification (crystal formation during cooling)
