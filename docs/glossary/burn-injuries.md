# Burn injuries

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

All friction-fire methods generate embers at 300-500°C and open flames shortly after. Hand drill and bow drill methods place hands and face close to the ember point. Flint-and-steel sparks reach ~800°C. Keep tinder bundles on a non-combustible surface (bare earth, flat stone). Have water or sand ready to extinguish accidental fires.
