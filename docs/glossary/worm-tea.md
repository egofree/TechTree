# Worm tea

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: agriculture

Liquid extract of vermicompost used as foliar spray or soil drench. Aerated version: steep vermicompost with molasses and airstone for 24-48 hours. Provides nutrients and beneficial microorganisms. Use within 4 hours of brewing. Non-aerated version steeped 7-14 days.
