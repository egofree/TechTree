# Butter churning

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Fill a clay or wooden churn 1/3 to 1/2 with cream (skimmed from milk left standing 12-24 hours). Agitate with a wooden plunger for 20-40 minutes until butter granules form and separate from buttermilk. Yield: 3-5 kg butter per 100 liters milk. Wash butter in cold water to remove residual buttermilk (extends shelf life). Buttermilk byproduct is drunk fresh or used in baking.
