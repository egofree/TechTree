# Bearing PV limits

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

The product of bearing pressure P (MPa) and surface velocity V (m/s) determines the heat generation rate in plain bearings. Each bearing material has a maximum PV rating: bronze (phosphor bronze) 1.75 MPa·m/s, Babbitt metal 1.05 MPa·m/s, PTFE (unfilled) 0.35 MPa·m/s, acetal (Delrin) 0.15 MPa·m/s, nylon 0.10 MPa·m/s.
