# Kerosene

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, energy

(150-250°C): Lamp fuel, heating, jet fuel precursor (~15-25%) - **Diesel/gas oil** (250-350°C): Engine fuel, heating oil (~15-25%) - **Lubricating oil** (350-500°C): Bearing oil, machine oil, later hydraulic fluid (~10-20%) - **Residual fuel oil / asphalt** (>500°C): Boiler fuel, road paving, waterproofing (~15-30%) - **Yield**: ~40-60% usable products from simple distillation.
