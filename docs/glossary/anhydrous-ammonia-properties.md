# Anhydrous ammonia properties

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: chemistry

Physical and chemical characteristics of pure NH₃: boiling point −33°C, density 0.68 kg/L as liquid, autoignition 651°C, explosive limits 15-28% in air. Stored as a liquid under pressure (~10 atm at 25°C) or refrigerated (−33°C).
