# Pumping capacity

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

A 3 m diameter Halladay windmill at 5 m/s wind speed pumps roughly 500 L/hour from a 10 m lift. A 6 m diameter unit at 6 m/s pumps roughly 3,000-5,000 L/hour from a 20 m lift. The gearbox provides a 3.5:1 ratio, converting the slow rotor rotation (15-40 RPM) to a faster crank speed for the pump. Pump stroke: 100-200 mm. Pump cylinder diameter: 50-150 mm depending on required flow and lift.
