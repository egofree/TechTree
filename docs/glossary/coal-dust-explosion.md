# Coal dust explosion

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

In coal mines and coal handling facilities (coaling stages, tender loading), suspended coal dust explodes when ignited by a spark or flame. Keep coal dust concentrations below 50 g/m³ by ventilation and wet suppression. No smoking or open flames in coal handling areas. The same hazard applies to grain elevators (grain dust is explosively flammable above 40-60 g/m³).
