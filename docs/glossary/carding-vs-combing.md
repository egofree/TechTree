# Carding vs combing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Two distinct preparation methods yielding different yarn types. **Carding**: Tease scoured wool between two carding paddles (leather pads covered with bent wire teeth). Disentangles fibers, removes remaining debris, aligns roughly for spinning. Produces rolag (rolled cylinder of fiber) → woolen yarn (fluffy, warm, fuzzy surface, shorter fibers). **Combing**: Draw long-staple wool through metal comb rows (wool combs).
