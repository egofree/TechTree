# Monitoring retting progress

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Starting at day 5, pull a few stalks from the bundle and try to separate fibers from the woody core by hand. Under-retted: fibers cling to shives, difficult to separate. Correctly retted: fibers separate cleanly with gentle pulling. Over-retted: fibers are weak, break easily, feel slimy. Check daily — the window between under- and over-retting can be as short as 1-2 days in warm water.
