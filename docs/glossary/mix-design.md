# Mix design

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

(by volume, typical structural concrete): - **Cement**: 1 part - **Fine aggregate** (sand, 0-5 mm): 2 parts - **Coarse aggregate** (gravel/crushed stone, 5-25 mm): 4 parts - **Water**: water-cement ratio 0.4-0.7 by mass (lower = stronger but less workable; 0.45-0.55 for structural work)
