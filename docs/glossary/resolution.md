# Resolution

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement, photolithography

With a sodium lamp (λ = 589.3 nm), one fringe corresponds to 294.7 nm of mirror travel. Sub-fringe interpolation (measuring fractional fringe position with photocells) reaches λ/100, approximately 5 nm. With a stabilized laser, λ/1000 is achievable.
