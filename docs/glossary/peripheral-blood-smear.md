# Peripheral blood smear

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Drop of blood on glass slide, spread into thin film by a second slide held at 30° angle. Air dry. Stain with Wright's or Giemsa stain. Examine under oil immersion (1000×). Assess red cell morphology (size, shape, color), white cell differential (neutrophils, lymphocytes, monocytes, eosinophils, basophils), and platelet estimate.
