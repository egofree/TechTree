# Antiseptics

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

(see medicine.md for clinical application): - Ethanol: 60-70% concentration optimal (denatures proteins, disrupts cell membranes). 95% ethanol less effective — insufficient water penetration into cells. - Iodine: 2-7% I₂ in ethanol with KI (potassium iodide keeps iodine in solution). Broad-spectrum antiseptic. - Hydrogen peroxide: 3% solution for wound cleaning. Produced by electrolysis of sulfuric acid followed by vacuum distillation.
