# Altimeter

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

(barometric): - An aneroid barometer calibrated in altitude. A sealed metal capsule (thin-walled brass or beryllium copper, partially evacuated) expands as atmospheric pressure decreases with altitude. A mechanical linkage converts capsule expansion to needle rotation on a dial marked 0-3000 m (or higher).
