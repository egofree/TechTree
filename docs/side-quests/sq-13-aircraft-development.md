# SQ 13: Aircraft Development

**Starts**: Phase 2  
**Priority**: Medium — propulsion and aviation dramatically expand logistics, reconnaissance, and morale, but are not on the critical path to semiconductors.

## Problem

Without propulsion technology, civilization is limited to ground and water transport. Aircraft enable rapid reconnaissance, cargo delivery over difficult terrain, and serve as a powerful morale boost for the entire project. The Wright brothers flew in 1903 with bicycle-shop tools and a custom engine. With modern ultralight plans in hand, the challenge is manufacturing capability, not invention.

## Technologies & Systems

### Internal Combustion Engines
- Small single or twin-cylinder piston engines (20-65 HP)
- Precision cylinder boring, piston rings, crankshaft, valves, carburetor
- Magneto ignition for reliable spark without battery
- Power-to-weight ratio is the critical metric — steam is too heavy for flight
- Lubrication: castor oil or mineral oil ([SQ 10](sq-10-lubricants-oils.md))
- Fuel: gasoline from petroleum distillation or ethanol ([SQ 12](sq-12-petrochemicals.md))

### Airframe & Structure
- Steel tube or wood spar construction with wire bracing
- Control surfaces: ailerons, elevator, rudder via cable-and-pulley systems
- Brazing or welding for steel tube joints
- Wire bracing provides structural rigidity — same technology as bicycle frames

### Propeller
- Carved hardwood propeller, balanced and pitched for efficient thrust
- Requires lathe and dividing head from Phase 3 machine shop
- Blade angle (pitch) must vary along the length for optimal performance

### Fabric Covering & Finishing
- Cotton or linen fabric ([SQ 9](sq-09-textiles-fiber.md)) stretched over airframe
- Treated with nitrocellulose dope for weatherproofing and rigidity
- Dope chemistry from Phase 5 and [SQ 12](sq-12-petrochemicals.md)

### Fuel System
- Gravity-feed fuel tank, fuel lines, simple carburetor (venturi principle)
- Gasoline from [SQ 12](sq-12-petrochemicals.md) is ideal
- Ethanol from fermentation works with lower energy density

## Integration Points

| Phase/SQ | Contribution |
|----------|-------------|
| Phase 2 | Steel tubing and wire for airframe structure |
| Phase 3 | Machine tools for precision engine parts, propeller carving |
| Phase 4 | Internal combustion engine development, spark ignition |
| Phase 5 | Nitrocellulose dope for fabric, aluminum for later airframes |
| SQ 9 | Fabric for aircraft covering (cotton, linen) |
| SQ 10 | Castor oil and mineral oil for engine lubrication |
| SQ 12 | Gasoline fuel, acetone for dope, solvents for finishes |

## Key Deliverables

- Flyable ultralight airframe (tube-and-fabric or wood-and-fabric)
- Reliable small internal combustion engine (20-65 HP)
- Carved and balanced wooden propeller
- Doped fabric covering system
- Fuel supply chain (gasoline or ethanol)
- Ground test infrastructure (engine test stand, runway)
