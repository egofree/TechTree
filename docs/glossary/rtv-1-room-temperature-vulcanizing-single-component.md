# RTV-1 (room temperature vulcanizing, single-component)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Acetoxy-functional silane crosslinker cures on exposure to atmospheric moisture (water vapor diffuses into the silicone from the surface inward). Skin forms in 10-30 min, full cure 24-48 hours (cure rate depends on humidity — 50% RH typical, cure time doubles at 25% RH). The reaction releases acetic acid (vinegar smell — the characteristic odor of curing silicone caulk). Familiar as "bathtub caulk" and general-purpose silicone sealant.
