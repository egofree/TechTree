# Four-point probe

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

(sheet resistance): Four collinear tungsten probes (1 mm spacing) contact the wafer surface. Outer two probes pass constant current I (1 μA-100 mA), inner two measure voltage V. Sheet resistance Rs = (π/ln 2)·(V/I) ≈ 4.532·(V/I) Ω/sq. Measures doped layers (diffused or implanted), metal films, poly-Si. Corrects for wafer diameter with geometric correction factors. Accuracy ±1%. For metal film thickness: t = ρ/Rs where ρ is bulk resistivity.
