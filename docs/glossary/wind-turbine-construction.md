# Wind turbine construction

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Tower (steel lattice or tubular, 10-50 m height — higher = more consistent, faster wind). Rotor (2-3 blades, wood + fabric or later fiberglass, 5-20 m diameter). Generator (DC or AC, 1-100+ kW). Yaw mechanism (vane or tail keeps rotor facing wind). Speed regulation (blade pitch control or mechanical brake to prevent overspeed in high winds).
