# Steel rule

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement

Hardened and tempered spring steel, photo-etched or engine-divided graduations. Length 150-1000 mm. Graduated in mm and 0.5 mm. Flatness ≤0.02 mm over 300 mm. Made by surface grinding against lapped surface plate (from the Machine Tools stage iterative metrology bootstrap).
