# Properties of reclaimed rubber

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Tensile strength ~60-80% of virgin compound. Reduced cross-link density after re-vulcanization. Lower hysteresis (less heat buildup). Lower cost — reclaim sells for 30-50% of virgin rubber price. Used as an extender in: tire inner liners, low-cost molded goods, playground surfaces (crumb rubber bound with polyurethane binder), road asphalt modifier (rubberized asphalt — improves fatigue resistance and reduces road noise).
