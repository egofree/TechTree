# Midair collision

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

See and avoid other aircraft. Scan the sky in 10° sectors, pausing at each sector for 1-2 seconds to detect traffic. Blind spots below the nose and behind the tail. Fly standard traffic patterns (left-hand circuits at 300-400 m altitude, downwind leg parallel to runway) at designated airfields to maintain predictable separation.
