# Iron tire

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

flat bar iron, 3-5 cm wide, 5-8 mm thick, bent to match the wheel circumference. Shrink-fitted by heating the tire until it expands, placing it on the wheel, and cooling rapidly with water. The tire contracts and clamps the entire wheel assembly tightly. This is the critical step that holds the wheel together under load. Without the iron tire shrink fit, spokes work loose within weeks.
