# Tilapia

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: critical | **Domains**: agriculture

Oreochromis spp. — the standard aquaponics fish. Fast growth (500 g in 6-8 months), tolerate crowding (20-40 kg/m³), omnivorous. Require warm water (>20°C for growth, >15°C to survive). Mouthbrooding females produce 100-500 fry per spawn. Accept BSF larvae, duckweed, and agricultural byproducts as feed.
