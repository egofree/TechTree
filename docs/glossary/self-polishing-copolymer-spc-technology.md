# Self-polishing copolymer (SPC) technology

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

The dominant antifouling mechanism for commercial ship hulls. The binder is a copolymer of tributyltin methacrylate (now banned) or copper acrylate with acrylic backbone. In seawater (pH 8.1), the surface layer of the binder hydrolyzes at a controlled rate (5-10 μm/month), releasing biocidal copper ions and continuously exposing fresh biocide-containing paint.
