# Hackling (combing)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Draw fibers through graduated iron or thorn combs (hackles). Coarse hackle removes remaining shives. Fine hackle separates long line fibers (30-90 cm) from short tow (5-15 cm). Line fibers become high-quality linen; tow becomes coarse cloth or rope.
