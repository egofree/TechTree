# Slag composition

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals, silicon

Slag composition (typical) — 30-45% CaO, 30-40% SiO₂, 5-15% Al₂O₃, 1-10% MgO, with minor amounts of FeO, MnO, and sulfur (as CaS). The CaO/SiO₂ ratio (basicity) is typically 1.0-1.3.
