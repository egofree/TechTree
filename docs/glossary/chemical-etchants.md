# Chemical etchants

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: optics

Lens and prism preparation may use hydrofluoric acid (HF) for glass etching — HF penetrates skin, attacks bone, causes delayed deep-tissue necrosis. Calcium gluconate gel must be available as first aid. Cerium oxide and rouge (Fe₂O₃) polishing slurries are low hazard but avoid inhalation of dried powders.
