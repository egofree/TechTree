Vacuum, Optics & Advanced Glass

Controlled environments and inspection capability. Vacuum pumps and chambers for deposition and evaporation, advanced glass production (borosilicate, fused silica, tubing), optical inspection tools (microscopes, comparators, spectroscopes), and precision temperature/pressure measurement.

Capabilities

  • Advanced Glass Production

    Borosilicate glass (Pyrex-type, thermal shock resistant), fused silica/quartz glass (1700°C+, critical for CZ crucibles), glass tubing production, glassworking techniques (lampworking, sealing), and quartz crucible fabrication for semiconductor use.

  • Optics & Inspection

    Lens grinding and polishing (rough grind → fine grind → polish with cerium oxide), compound microscopes (40x-1000x), optical comparators, spectroscopes, thermocouples, RTDs, pressure gauges, and electrical measurement (galvanometers, Wheatstone bridges). Essential for crystal defect inspection and lithography alignment.

  • Vacuum Technology

    Piston pumps (rough vacuum 10-50 Torr), rotary vane pumps (workhorse, 10⁻²-10⁻³ Torr), diffusion pumps (high vacuum 10⁻⁶-10⁻⁸ Torr), vacuum chambers (steel or glass), vacuum measurement (Bourdon, Pirani, ion gauges), and leak detection (bubble test, helium leak detector).
