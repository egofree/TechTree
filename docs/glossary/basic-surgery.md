# Basic surgery

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Basic surgery (with anesthesia) — - **Setup**: Clean room. Boil all instruments 20+ minutes (sterilization). Surgeon washes hands and forearms with soap and water, then soaks in 70% ethanol or 2% phenol solution. Sterile drapes over patient. Good lighting (focused oil lamp or, later, electric light). - **Anesthesia**: Ether inhalation via mask (wire frame with cloth pad, drip ether onto pad). Monitor breathing — maintain steady depth.
