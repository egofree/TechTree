# Smokehouse drying

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Sheets are hung on bamboo poles in a smokehouse — a simple structure with a smoldering wood fire on the floor. Temperature: 60-70°C. Duration: 2-4 weeks. The smoke deposits creosote and phenolic compounds that act as natural fungicides, preventing mold growth. Properly dried RSS contains <0.5% moisture. The smokehouse must maintain consistent temperature and smoke density — too hot causes surface oxidation, too cool allows mold.
