# Supply chain trust

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: vlsi-scaling

Hardware Trojans — malicious modifications inserted during design or fabrication — are a concern for critical applications. Counterfeit ICs (remarked parts, recycled parts sold as new) cause reliability failures. Trusted fabrication facilities and supply chain tracking mitigate these risks. In a bootstrapping context, the fabricator is the designer — supply chain trust is inherent.
