# Alkali extraction (E stage)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

NaOH at 1-2% on pulp, 50-70°C, 60-90 minutes. Dissolves the chlorinated/oxidized lignin fragments from the C stage into solution. The brown-colored extract is washed away. Modern extraction stages often reinforce with oxygen (Eo stage, 0.5-1.0 MPa O₂) or hydrogen peroxide (Ep stage, 0.5-1.5% H₂O₂) for additional delignification without chlorine.
