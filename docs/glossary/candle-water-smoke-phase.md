# Candle / water-smoke phase

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

Candle / water-smoke phase (ambient → 150°C) — ramp ~60°C/hour. Hold 1-2 hours. Drives off residual mechanical water. Rapid heating causes steam explosions. Ware may audibly "pop" if rushed. See: [Kiln Firing](../ceramics/kiln-firing.md)
