# Galvanized steel priming

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

New galvanizing has a smooth, non-porous zinc surface that most paints will not adhere to. Weathering (3-6 months outdoor exposure) creates a natural zinc carbonate patina that improves adhesion. For immediate painting: wash primer (acid-based etching primer, vinyl butyral + H₃PO₄) or acrylic etch primer to promote adhesion.
