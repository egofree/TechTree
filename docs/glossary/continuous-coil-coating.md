# Continuous coil coating

> **Type**: process | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Metal coils (steel or aluminum, 0.2-2.0 mm thick, 600-1800 mm wide, coil weight 5-25 tonnes) are uncoiled, cleaned, pretreated, coated, cured, and recoiled in a continuous process at line speeds of 20-200 m/min. This is the most efficient method for coating flat metal sheet used in building panels, appliances, and automotive components.
