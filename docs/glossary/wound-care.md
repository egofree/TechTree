# Wound care

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Cool burn with running water (15-20°C) for 20 minutes (effective up to 3 hours post-burn). Do NOT apply ice (causes frostbite on top of the burn). Apply non-adherent dressing (petrolatum gauze). Do NOT burst blisters (intact blister is a sterile biological dressing). Escharotomy (longitudinal incisions through burned skin) for circumferential burns of limbs or chest that restrict circulation or breathing.
