# Scotch tension

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

A brake band (thin cord or leather) wraps around the bobbin flange and is tensioned by a spring or adjustable screw. The brake slows the bobbin relative to the flyer, causing yarn to wind on. Adjusting brake tension controls take-up speed. Simple, effective, easy to build. Preferred for beginners and most general spinning.
