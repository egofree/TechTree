# Stove arrangement

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

A blast furnace has 3-4 stoves. With 3 stoves, typically two are on-gas (heating) while one is on-blast (supplying hot air), then they rotate. With 4 stoves, the sequencing can overlap more smoothly, providing more consistent blast temperature. Valving is critical — large butterfly or gate valves (1-2 m diameter) switch each stove between gas and blast duties.
