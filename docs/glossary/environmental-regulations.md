# Environmental regulations

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Even in bootstrap conditions, solvent contamination of soil and groundwater creates long-term liabilities. Chlorinated solvents (CCl₄, TCE, perc) persist for decades in groundwater. Heavy metals from spent catalysts never degrade. Design solvent systems with containment, recovery, and recycling from the start — remediation is 10-100× more expensive than prevention.
