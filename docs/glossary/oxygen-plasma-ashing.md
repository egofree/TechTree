# Oxygen plasma ashing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

RF plasma (13.56 MHz, 100-500 W, O₂ flow 50-200 sccm, 0.5-2 Torr, 150-300°C). Oxygen radicals oxidize resist to CO₂ + H₂O (volatile products). Removes ~1-5 μm/min depending on conditions. Dry process — no liquid waste. Typically used as first step to remove bulk resist, followed by wet clean for residue.
