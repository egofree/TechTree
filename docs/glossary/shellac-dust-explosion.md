# Shellac dust explosion

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Dry shellac flakes ground or crushed into fine powder present a dust explosion hazard. Shellac is an organic material with sufficient calorific value (~25 MJ/kg) to sustain combustion when dispersed as a fine dust in air. Maintain good housekeeping in flake processing areas. Ground equipment to prevent static discharge.
