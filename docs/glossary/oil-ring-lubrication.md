# Oil-ring lubrication

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Brass or steel ring (20-40 mm diameter) rides on shaft, dips into oil reservoir below bearing. Shaft rotation carries ring → ring drags oil up to shaft top → oil flows along shaft into bearing. Continuous, automatic, self-contained. For horizontal shafts at moderate speed (100-3000 RPM).
