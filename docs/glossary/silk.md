# Silk

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health, textiles

(*Bombyx mori*): - **Cultivation**: Domesticated silkworms fed exclusively on white mulberry leaves. Larvae spin cocoons over 3-4 days. Each cocoon contains a single continuous filament 600-900 m long. Requires controlled temperature (23-25°C) and humidity. - **Cocoon processing**: Kill pupae by steaming or baking cocoons at 60-80°C (prevents moth from emerging and breaking filament).
