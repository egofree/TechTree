# Nitric acid plant scale

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Typical capacity 500-3,000 tonnes/day (as 100% HNO₃). Integrated with an ammonia plant (Haber-Bosch). Energy balance: ammonia oxidation is highly exothermic (NH₃ + 5/4 O₂ → NO + 3/2 H₂O, ΔH = -226 kJ/mol). The hot process gases generate steam in waste heat boilers (recovering 60-70% of the reaction heat). A 1000 t/d nitric acid plant produces 25-40 MW of exportable power from steam turbines.
