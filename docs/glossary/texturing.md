# Texturing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Texturing (optional, improves light trapping) — Anisotropic etch in KOH/IPA solution (2-5% KOH, 5-10% IPA, 70-80°C, 20-30 min). Pyramidal texture on <100> surface reduces reflection from ~35% to ~10%. See: [Basic Semiconductor Devices](../silicon/basic-devices.md)
