# Plastic dust explosions

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Thermoplastic powders (PVC, PE, nylon, PS) form explosive dust clouds when fine particles are suspended in air during grinding, pelletizing, or transfer operations. Dust explosion requires: suspended dust above MEC (minimum explosive concentration), confinement, and an ignition source. Maintain ventilation to keep dust concentrations below MEC.
