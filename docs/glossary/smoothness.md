# Smoothness

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Rough paper produces uneven ink transfer — some areas receive heavy ink, others none. Calendering (pressing between polished rollers) smooths the surface for letterpress. For intaglio, the paper must be soft enough to be pressed into engraved grooves — dampening before printing (soaking in water for 2-4 hours, then blotting) makes paper fibers pliable.
