# Cell shell

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Rectangular steel box (10-15 mm plate), typically 8-12 m long × 3-4 m wide × 1-1.5 m deep. Externally reinforced with steel cradle. Shell sits on electrically insulated supports. Thermal insulation (firebrick, calcium silicate board) between shell and carbon lining to reduce heat loss.
