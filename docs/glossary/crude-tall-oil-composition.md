# Crude tall oil composition

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

30-50% rosin acids (abietic acid, pimaric acid), 30-50% fatty acids (oleic acid, linoleic acid), 5-20% neutral materials (sterols, hydrocarbons, lignin). Composition varies with wood species: softwood pulping produces more rosin; hardwood produces more fatty acids. Global crude tall oil production: ~2 million tonnes/year, entirely from kraft softwood pulping.
