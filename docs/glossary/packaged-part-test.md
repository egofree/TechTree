# Packaged part test

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

After packaging, full functional test at speed, AC timing, thermal testing (hot/cold). (3) **Burn-in**: Stress testing at elevated temperature (125-150°C) and voltage (1.2-1.5× nominal) for 24-168 hours to weed out infant mortality failures. Devices that pass burn-in have much lower field failure rate.
