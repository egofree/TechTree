# Separation and washing

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

After nitration (30-60 minutes), the mixture is allowed to settle. Nitroglycerin (density 1.59 g/cm³) separates as the lower layer beneath the spent acid. The NG is drawn off and washed: first with cold water (removes bulk acid), then with sodium carbonate solution (neutralizes residual acid), then with water again until the wash water tests neutral.
