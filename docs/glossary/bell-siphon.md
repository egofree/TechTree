# Bell siphon

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: critical | **Domains**: agriculture

Auto-siphon mechanism for flood-and-drain cycling in media-based aquaponic grow beds. No moving parts or electricity required. Standpipe + bell + media guard. Fills grow bed to standpipe height, then siphons rapidly (2-5 min drain). Repeats 3-4 cycles per hour.
