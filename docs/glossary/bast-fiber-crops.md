# Bast fiber crops

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

Bast fiber crops (flax, hemp, jute) — sow densely (flax at 40-60 kg/ha) to produce tall, unbranched stalks with long fibers. Excessive branching shortens fiber length. Harvest before seed maturation for best fiber quality (stalks yellow at bottom third for flax; pollen shedding for hemp). See: [Fiber Plants](../plants/fiber-plants.md)
