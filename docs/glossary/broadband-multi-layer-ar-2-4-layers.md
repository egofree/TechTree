# Broadband multi-layer AR (2-4 layers)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: optics

Anti-reflection coatings using 2-4 dielectric thin-film layers of alternating high/low refractive index materials to minimize reflection over a broad wavelength range (e.g., 400-700 nm). Achieves <0.5% reflectance across the band versus single-layer MgF₂ (~1.5%).
