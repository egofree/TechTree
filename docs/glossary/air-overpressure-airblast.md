# Air overpressure (airblast)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

The shock wave transmitted through air from the blast. Measured in decibels (dB) or pressure (kPa). Typical values at 200 m: 110-130 dB for confined blasts, 130-150 dB for unconfined. Threshold for glass breakage: ~150 dB. Complaint threshold: 115-120 dB (audible as a low-frequency thump).
