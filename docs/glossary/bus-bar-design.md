# Bus bar design

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Massive copper or aluminum bus bars carry current between rectifier and cells. At 200 kA, even 0.001 Ω resistance dissipates 40 kW as heat. Bus bars must be short, thick, and well-cooled. Typical cross-section: 200-1000 mm² per 1000 A.
