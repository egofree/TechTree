# Mass-produced mechanical calculators

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: computing

(~1880-1970): Enabled by precision machine tools, interchangeable parts, and specialized factory production. The Arithmometer (1820, Thomas de Colmar) was the first commercially successful calculator; the Comptometer (1887, Felt) and later Friden and Marchant machines dominated business computing for decades.
