# Harvest timing

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

Active compound concentration varies with plant maturity and season. Flowering herbs (e.g., *Artemisia* species) generally peak in volatile oil content just before full bloom. Root crops (e.g., *Agrimonia pilosa*) concentrate medicinal compounds in autumn as above-ground growth dies back. Harvest at the wrong time and potency may be 2-5x lower than peak.
