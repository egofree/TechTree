# Volume measurement

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Graduated cylinders (10-1000 mL) for liquids. Pipettes (1-25 mL, graduated or volumetric) for precise liquid transfer. Volumetric flasks (25-1000 mL) for preparing solutions of exact concentration. All glassware should be Class B tolerance or better. Calibration: mark volumetric flasks at the meniscus line.
