# Bulk gas vs specialty gas

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Bulk gases (N₂, O₂, Ar, H₂, He) are produced on-site by air separation or electrolysis and distributed through a central manifold to all process tools. Distribution at 0.5-1.0 MPa through 1/2 to 1 inch OD stainless steel header pipes. Specialty gases (SiH₄, PH₃, CF₄, SF₆, Cl₂) are supplied in cylinders (size 44L, containing 5-20 kg of gas at 2-10 MPa) located in dedicated gas cabinets, one cylinder per gas per tool.
