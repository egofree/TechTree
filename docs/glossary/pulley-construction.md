# Pulley construction

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

A wooden block with a groove around the circumference, rotating on a wooden or metal axle pin. The rope rides in the groove. A well-made wooden pulley with a smooth, polished groove lasts for years under moderate loads. For heavy loads, bind the pulley block with iron bands to prevent splitting. Multiple sheaves (pulleys) can share a single axle block, giving the block and tackle its name.
