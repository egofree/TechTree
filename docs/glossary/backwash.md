# Backwash

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

When head loss exceeds ~2 m (typically every 24-48 hours), reverse flow through the bed at 30-50 m/h for 5-10 minutes. Optional air scour before backwash improves cleaning. Filter is offline during backwash. Wasted backwash water: 1-5% of production volume.
