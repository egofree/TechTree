# Annealing for optical glass

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Far more critical than for ordinary glass. Hold at annealing point for days to weeks (not hours). Cool at 0.1-1°C/hour through the strain point. A 100 mm lens blank may require 2-4 weeks of annealing. The goal is to reduce residual stress below the level that would cause measurable optical distortion (stress birefringence). Refractive index tolerance: ±0.001 for standard optical glass, ±0.0001 for precision grades.
