# Ferritic stainless steels

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Ferritic stainless steels (400 series, magnetic) — - **Structure**: Body-centered cubic (BCC, α-iron), little or no nickel. Chromium 10.5-30%. Lower cost than austenitic grades (no nickel). Good stress corrosion cracking resistance (unlike austenitic grades, which are susceptible in chloride + tensile stress environments). - **Type 430** (17% Cr): General-purpose ferritic grade.
