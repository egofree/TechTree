# Cathode compartment

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Perforated steel mesh or screen (2-3 mm wire, 3-5 mm openings) acts as both cathode and diaphragm support. Hydrogen gas evolves at the cathode surface: 2H₂O + 2e⁻ → H₂↑ + 2OH⁻. The NaOH solution (10-12% from diaphragm cell) drains from the cathode compartment by gravity.
