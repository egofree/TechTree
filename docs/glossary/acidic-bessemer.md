# Acidic Bessemer

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Silica (SiO₂) refractory lining. Cannot remove phosphorus — the P₂O₅ is acidic and would attack the acidic lining. Requires low-phosphorus pig iron (<0.05% P), limiting it to certain ores. Used in Britain with hematite pig iron.
