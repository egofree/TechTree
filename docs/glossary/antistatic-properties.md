# Antistatic properties

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Floor panels coated with conductive vinyl or epoxy (surface resistance 10⁶-10⁹ Ω/sq). Grounded through the pedestal base to the building earth. This prevents static charge accumulation from personnel walking, which could discharge onto wafers or damage MOS devices. Conductive flooring is tested with a megohmmeter at installation and annually thereafter.
