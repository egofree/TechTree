# Slubbing (uneven drafting)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

The yarn varies between thick and thin sections. Cause: inconsistent draft length — the spinner pulls uneven amounts of fiber from the fiber supply. Fix: pre-draft the fiber into uniform strips before spinning. Practice drafting with the wheel disconnected, pulling a consistent 5-8 cm length of fiber per draft.
