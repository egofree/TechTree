# Carbon microphone

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

(Edison/Berlinner improvement): - A capsule containing carbon granules (0.1-0.5 mm crushed carbon) between two metal plates. One plate is the diaphragm. Sound pressure compresses the granules, changing their contact resistance. A DC bias current (from a battery, 3-6 V) flows through the granules. The resistance variation modulates the current, producing a much stronger signal than the Bell electromagnetic transmitter (100× the output). - Requires a battery at each telephone set.
