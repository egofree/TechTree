# Cryogenic trap handling

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Cold traps cooled with liquid nitrogen (LN₂, -196°C) present cold burn and condensation hazards. Skin contact with LN₂-cooled surfaces causes immediate frostbite (tissue destruction identical to burns). Wear insulated cryogenic gloves (not just leather work gloves — cold penetrates thin gloves rapidly) and face shield when handling LN₂ or manipulating cold traps.
