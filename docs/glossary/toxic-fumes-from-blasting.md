# Toxic fumes from blasting

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Black powder produces large volumes of smoke containing CO, CO₂, NOₓ, SO₂, and particulate carbon. After blasting, wait for dust and fumes to clear before re-entering the mine. Sulfur dioxide (SO₂) is immediately irritating at low concentrations — if you smell it or feel throat irritation, evacuate.
