# Blasting caps

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Required to initiate high explosives (dynamite, guncotton). Caps contain primary explosive (mercury fulminate, lead azide, or DDNP) detonated by fuse or electric current. Never handle caps near detonating cord or main charge during loading. Caps are MORE sensitive than main charge — treat with extreme caution.
