# Orientation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Determine crystal orientation using X-ray Laue diffraction. Expose the seed to an X-ray beam; the diffraction pattern (spots on film) reveals the crystal orientation relative to the seed surface. For <100> orientation, the surface is cut perpendicular to the [100] direction. For <111>, perpendicular to the [111] direction.
