# Batch retort

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Iron or steel cylinder (1-2 m diameter × 2-4 m long), loaded with hardwood billets. Seal. Heat externally (burn wood waste or previous batch gas). Internal wood pyrolyzes: releases volatile gases (which can be burned to heat the retort — self-sustaining after initial heating), leaves charcoal. Temperature 400-600°C. Time: 12-24 hours. Cool before opening (charcoal ignites spontaneously when hot and exposed to air). Yield: 25-35% charcoal by weight.
