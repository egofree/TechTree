# Construction ramps

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

For building a wall or raised structure, build a compacted earth ramp on one side. Lengthen the ramp as the wall grows taller to keep the slope manageable. A 10 m long ramp rising 2 m (MA = 5:1) lets two people roll a 200 kg block into position. Dismantle the ramp when construction finishes. Temples, pyramids, and castle walls were all built with this method.
