# Injection blow molding

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

First, injection mold a preform (test-tube-shaped thick-walled tube with finished thread neck — the neck finish is molded to final precision in this step). Then transfer preform to blow mold, heat to soften (for PET: heat to 100-110°C, above Tg of 73°C but below cold crystallization temperature of 140°C), inflate with air (0.3-1.5 MPa) and stretch axially with a stretch rod.
