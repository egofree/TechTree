# Pit tanning

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

The traditional large-scale method. Dig pits 1.5-2 m deep, 2-3 m square, lined with stone or timber. Layer hides with ground bark in each pit. Fill with water. Hides spend 6-12 months in the pit for sole leather (thick, hard), 2-4 months for harness leather (medium flexibility). Bark consumption runs 3-5 kg bark per kg of raw hide.
