# Circulation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Loan transaction: record borrower name, item title, class mark, loan date, due date in the loan register (bound ledger or card system). Stamp due date on the due-date slip in the book pocket. On return: strike through the loan record, re-shelve. Overdue tracking: review loan register weekly for items past due date. Send reminder notices. After 90 days overdue: declare item lost, begin replacement process.
