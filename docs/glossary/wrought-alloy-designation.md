# Wrought alloy designation

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(4-digit system): - **1xxx**: ≥99% Al. Excellent corrosion resistance, conductivity. Not heat-treatable. Used for electrical conductors, foil. - **2xxx** (Al-Cu, e.g., 2024): Precipitation-hardened (Al₂Cu). High strength (~470 MPa for 2024-T3) but poor corrosion resistance — usually clad with pure aluminum (alclad).
