# Crank-and-connecting-rod

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

converts rotary motion to reciprocating motion (or vice versa). A crank arm on the rotating shaft connects via a wooden or iron rod to a piston, saw blade, or bellows. The crank radius determines the stroke length. Used in animal-powered threshing machines, sawmills, and forge bellows.
