# Step 3 (Leaching)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Crush and leach black ash with water. Na₂CO₃ dissolves (soluble). CaS and unreacted solids remain as residue. Crystallize Na₂CO₃·10H₂O (washing soda) by cooling, or calcine to anhydrous Na₂CO₃ (soda ash) by heating to 150°C.
