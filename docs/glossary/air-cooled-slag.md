# Air-cooled slag

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Molten slag is directed into shallow pits and allowed to cool and solidify slowly in air. The resulting rock-like mass is crushed and screened to produce aggregate (20-75 mm) for road base, railroad ballast, and concrete aggregate. Air-cooled slag aggregate is denser (~2.5 t/m³) and more angular than natural gravel, providing excellent interlock in road construction. It is free of organic matter and has good frost resistance.
