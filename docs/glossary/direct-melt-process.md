# Direct melt process

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

(standard industrial method): - Raw batch materials (silica sand, limestone, alumina, boric acid) are melted in a refractory furnace at 1400-1600°C. The molten glass is refined (bubbles rise and escape over 6-12 hours) and conditioned (temperature stabilized and homogenized) as it flows through channels to the fiber-forming station. - The melt enters a platinum alloy bushing (the fiber-forming plate).
