# Chemical structure

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Trans-1,4-polyisoprene (C₅H₈)ₙ, molecular weight 30,000-100,000 g/mol. The trans configuration allows tight chain packing into a crystalline lattice at room temperature (crystallinity 50-65%). This crystallinity gives gutta-percha its hardness and rigidity, unlike the amorphous cis-1,4-polyisoprene of natural rubber.
