# Woven basket traps

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Willow or reed woven into a cylindrical trap 40-60 cm long with a funnel entrance 5-10 cm wide. Bait with bread, meat scraps, or fermented grain. Fish swim in through the funnel but cannot find the exit. Check and empty daily. Effective in streams, ponds, and tidal areas. Can be set and left unattended while other work proceeds. - **Weirs**: Stone or wooden fences built across streams to concentrate fish into pools or channels where they can be netted, speared, or trapped.
