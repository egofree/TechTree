# Cold-work tool steels

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: metals

Tool steels (O, A, D series) designed for operations at or near room temperature—stamping, blanking, cold extrusion—requiring high hardness (58-64 HRC), wear resistance, and dimensional stability. Oil-hardening (O) and air-hardening (A) grades minimize distortion during heat treatment.
