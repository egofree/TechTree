# Power calculation reminder

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

The fundamental formula P = η × ρ × g × Q × H applies to all turbine types. For quick estimates: P(kW) ≈ 7 × Q × H for small installations (η ≈ 0.7), or P(kW) ≈ 8.5 × Q × H for well-designed installations (η ≈ 0.85). Where Q is flow in m³/s and H is head in meters.
