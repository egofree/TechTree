# Staple length

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

The single most important quality metric. Short staple (<25 mm): upland varieties, 85% of world crop. Medium (25-30 mm): higher quality upland. Long staple (30-35 mm): Egyptian, Pima. Extra-long staple (>35 mm): Sea Island, premium Egyptian. Longer staple → stronger, finer yarn.
