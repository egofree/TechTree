# Thermal conductivity

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics, chemistry

Measured by hot wire method (ASTM C1113) or guarded hot plate. Critical for estimating heat loss through furnace linings and calculating the required insulation thickness. A typical furnace lining: 230 mm working brick + 115 mm insulating brick + steel shell. Heat loss: 1-5 kW/m² depending on temperature and lining design. Adding 50 mm of ceramic fiber blanket reduces heat loss by 30-50%.
