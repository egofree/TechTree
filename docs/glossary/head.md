# Head

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

50-1000 m. Higher head is better: energy stored per unit volume scales linearly with head, so a 500 m head site stores 5× the energy of a 100 m site per cubic meter of water. Sites below 50 m require impractically large reservoir volumes. Above 700 m, single-stage pump-turbines reach their limits and multi-stage machines are needed.
