# Gas hazards in enclosed spaces

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Sewers, septic tanks, and enclosed latrines can accumulate methane (explosive) and hydrogen sulfide (toxic — lethal at 100 ppm, paralyzes olfactory nerve so you stop smelling it at dangerous concentrations). Never enter enclosed sewage spaces without ventilation and gas testing. Multiple fatalities common when rescuers enter without protection.
