# Boiler

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy, transport

Fire-tube boiler (fire tubes carry hot gas through water space). Pressure 0.7-1.5 MPa. Diameter 1-1.5 m, length 3-5 m. 50-200 fire tubes, 5 cm diameter each. Coal-fired firebox at one end, smokebox and chimney at other. Steam dome on top for dry steam collection.
