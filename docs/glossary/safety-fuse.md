# Safety fuse

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Black powder core (2-4 g/m) enclosed in textile wrapping with asphalt or plastic coating. Burn rate: 100-150 seconds per meter (with ±10% manufacturing tolerance). Cut to length for the desired delay: 1 m fuse provides 100-150 seconds of delay. Ignited with a fuse lighter (match-head type) or hot wire.
