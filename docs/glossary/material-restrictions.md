# Material restrictions

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

No oil, grease, or hydrocarbon lubricants on any oxygen-contact component. Use PTFE-based lubricants only where lubrication is required (valve seats). Gaskets: PTFE or virgin PTFE-coated materials only. Never use Viton (fluorocarbon elastomer is acceptable for O₂ at moderate pressures but not at >200 bar).
