# Liquid oxygen (LOX)

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Stored at -183°C (1 atm), density 1.14 g/mL (1.14 kg/L, about 1000× denser than gaseous O₂ at STP). One liter of LOX expands to 860 L of gaseous O₂ at 15°C and 1 atm. Vacuum-insulated storage tanks: inner vessel stainless steel, outer vessel carbon steel, vacuum space with perlite or multi-layer insulation (MLI — alternating layers of aluminum foil and glass fiber paper).
