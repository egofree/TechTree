# Bauxite

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics, chemistry

Bauxite (gibbsite, Al(OH) — ₃): Source of high-alumina refractories (Al₂O₃ 60-90%). Calcined at 1500-1600°C to produce dense, stable corundum (α-Al₂O₃). Service temperature: 1600-1800°C. Used in steelmaking furnace roofs and sidewalls. See: [Advanced Ceramics & Refractories](../ceramics/advanced-ceramics.md)
