# Mild alkaline degumming

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Na₂CO₃ (sodium carbonate) at 5-10 g/L in water at 80-90°C for 1-2 hours. The alkaline solution hydrolyzes sericin's peptide bonds while leaving fibroin largely intact (fibroin's higher crystallinity and β-sheet structure resist alkaline attack at these temperatures). Weight loss of 20-30% confirms complete degumming.
