# Welded tube

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Flat strip or plate formed into a cylindrical shape on a roll-forming line and welded longitudinally (ERW — electric resistance welding, for 10-600 mm diameter) or spirally (SAW — submerged arc welding, for 300-2500 mm diameter). Lower cost than seamless. Used for line pipe, structural piling, mechanical tubing, and plumbing. ERW weld quality has improved dramatically — modern ERW pipe is suitable for many sour service and high-pressure applications that formerly required seamless.
