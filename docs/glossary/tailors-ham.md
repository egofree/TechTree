# Tailor's ham

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Tailor's ham (pressing curved seams) — A firmly stuffed fabric cushion (sawdust or sand filling, approximately 30 × 20 × 10 cm) shaped like a ham. Curved seams (darts, princess seams, sleeve caps) are pressed over the ham to preserve their 3D shape. Pressing flat would flatten the curve and distort the fit. See: [Sewing & Tailoring](../textiles/sewing-tailoring.md)
