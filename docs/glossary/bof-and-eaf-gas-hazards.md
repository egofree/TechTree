# BOF and EAF gas hazards

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

BOF off-gas is 70-85% CO (toxic, flammable, explosive range 12.5-74% in air). EAF off-gas contains CO and metallic fumes. Gas collection and cleaning systems (wet scrubbers, baghouses) are mandatory. CO monitors in all operator areas. Evacuation alarm if CO exceeds 50 ppm. Confined space entry near furnace off-gas systems requires SCBA.
