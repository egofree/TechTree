# Single-crystal (SX) blades

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

A helical grain selector at the base of the mold allows only one grain to propagate into the blade cavity. Zero grain boundaries. Further 2-3× improvement in creep life over DS. Used in modern jet engine and industrial gas turbine first-stage blades operating at >1,000°C (with internal air cooling channels and thermal barrier coatings).
