# Y-TZP

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

Y-TZP (yttria-tetragonal zirconia polycrystal) — Fine-grained, fully tetragonal. The toughest technical ceramic. Used for cutting tools, dental crowns, knife blades. See: [Advanced Ceramics & Refractories](../ceramics/advanced-ceramics.md)
