# Medium-scale (kettle process)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

1-5 tonnes/batch. Equipment: iron or steel kettle (2-5 m³), steam heating, mechanical stirrer, salt supply, pressing frames. Capital cost: $5,000-20,000. Batch cycle: 1-2 days saponification + graining, plus pressing and drying. Glycerol recovery adds distillation equipment ($10,000-50,000). Suitable for regional production.
