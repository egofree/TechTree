# Unity formula

> **Type**: process | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

(Seger formula): The standard method for expressing glaze composition. Recalculates raw material percentages into molar ratios of three component groups: fluxes (RO/R₂O), alumina (Al₂O₃), and silica (SiO₂). The flux sum is normalized to 1.0. This allows direct comparison of glazes regardless of which raw materials supply the oxides.
