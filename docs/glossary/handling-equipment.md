# Handling equipment

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Bucket ladder reclaimer for stockpile retrieval (500-5000 t/hour). Conveyor belts (0.5-2.0 m wide, 2-5 m/s belt speed) for movement between receiving, storage, and consumption points. Chutes and transfer towers must be designed with minimum 50° angles to prevent coal bridging and plugging.
