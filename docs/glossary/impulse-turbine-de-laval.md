# Impulse turbine (De Laval)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Steam expands through nozzles, high-velocity jet impinges on bucket-shaped blades. All pressure drop occurs in nozzles; blades rotate from impulse force only. Simple, single-stage, very high speed (10,000-30,000 RPM). Requires large reduction gearing for practical output speeds.
