# Host tree preferences

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

The quality and yield of lac varies significantly by host species: - *Schleichera oleosa* (kusum): Produces the highest quality lac — large grained, light colored, low impurity content. Preferred for premium shellac production. Yields 1.5-3 kg sticklac per tree per harvest. - *Butea monosperma* (palas/dhak): Most widely used host in India. Fast-growing, tolerant of poor soils.
