# Cart construction

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations, transport

(Metallurgy): - **Wheels**: Wooden spoke wheels (hub, 8-12 spokes, felloes/rim). Hub: turned on lathe from elm or oak (interlocking grain resists splitting). Spokes: ash (strong, flexible). Rim: bent oak or ash felloes, joined by iron strakes (iron bands shrunk on — heat band, place on wheel, cool → tight fit). Wheel diameter 1-1.5 m. - **Axles**: Hardwood (oak) or iron.
