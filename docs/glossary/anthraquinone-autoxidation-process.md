# Anthraquinone autoxidation process

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Anthraquinone autoxidation process (industrial standard) — 1. Dissolve 2-ethylanthraquinone in a mixed organic solvent (typically C₉-C₁₀ aromatics + trioctyl phosphate). The "working solution." 2. Hydrogenate the working solution over Pd or Ni catalyst at 40-60°C, 1-3 bar H₂. Anthraquinone reduces to anthrahydroquinone. 3. Filter out the catalyst.
