# Caking property

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

The coal must soften, swell, and re-solidify into a coherent porous mass when heated. Coals that do not cake (anthracite, some sub-bituminous coals) produce powder, not coke. Free swelling index (FSI) of 4-9 is typical for coking coals.
