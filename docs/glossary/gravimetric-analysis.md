# Gravimetric analysis

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Precipitate the analyte as an insoluble compound, filter, dry, and weigh on an analytical balance (±0.1 mg). Example: sulfate determination by BaCl₂ addition → BaSO₄ precipitate, filtered on ashless filter paper, ignited at 800°C, weighed. Precision ±0.1% for major components. Slow but accurate. Also used for moisture determination (dry sample at 105°C to constant weight) and ash content (ignite at 550°C to destroy organics).
