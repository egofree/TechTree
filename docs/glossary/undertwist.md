# Undertwist

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

The yarn drifts apart under tension. Fibers pull out of the yarn body. Cause: insufficient twist for the fiber staple length. Short-staple fibers (cotton, 20-30 mm) need more twist per inch than long-staple fibers (linen, 60-90 mm) to hold together. Fix: increase wheel ratio or slow the draft.
