# Enamel (magnet wire)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Polyurethane, polyester, or polyimide coatings applied by repeated dipping and curing cycles. Film builds of 0.02-0.10 mm. Polyurethane solderable at 380°C (no strip needed). Polyimide (class 220, 220°C continuous rating) for motors and transformers.
