# Argon asphyxiation

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Continuous argon flow (10-30 L/min) into an enclosed chamber. Any leak into the workroom displaces oxygen — argon is heavier than air and pools at floor level. O₂ monitors with alarms at floor level and breathing height. Exhaust vented outdoors. If a puller chamber must be opened after argon fill, purge with air first or use supplied-air respirator.
