# Glass lining for chemical vessels

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Thick glass coating (1-3 mm) applied to the interior of steel tanks and reactors. Provides resistance to all acids except HF and hot concentrated phosphoric acid. The glass composition is typically a borosilicate modified for low thermal expansion to match the steel substrate. Thermal shock resistance is the main limitation: maximum temperature change rate ~120°C per hour.
