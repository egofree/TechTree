# Acid etching

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass, silicon

Apply wax resist to areas to be preserved, then immerse in hydrofluoric acid (HF). HF dissolves glass on unprotected surfaces, creating a matte, frosted texture. Dangerous — HF causes severe chemical burns and systemic fluoride poisoning. Use only with full protective equipment and calcium gluconate gel available as first aid.
