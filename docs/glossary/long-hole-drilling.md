# Long-hole drilling

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

(mechanized underground): - Hydraulic or pneumatic long-hole drill on a boom mount. Diameter: 50-100 mm. Depth: 10-30 m. Used for bench mining, sub-level caving, and large stope blasting. - Larger diameter allows bulk emulsion or ANFO charging. A single long-hole round may contain 500-5000 kg of explosive, breaking 1000-10,000 tonnes of rock.
