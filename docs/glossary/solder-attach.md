# Solder attach

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Eutectic solder (Au-Si at 363°C, or soft solder Sn-Pb at 183°C) bonds the die to the package header or lead frame. Good thermal and electrical conductivity. The die back side is metallized (Ti/Ni/Ag or Ti/Pd/Au) for solder wetting.
