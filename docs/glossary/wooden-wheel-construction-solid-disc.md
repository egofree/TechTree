# Wooden wheel construction (solid disc)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Cut 3-4 planks to form a disc 60-100 cm in diameter and 4-6 cm thick. Join planks with wooden dowels or strap the rim with a heated iron band that shrinks tight as it cools. Bore a central hole for the axle, 5-8 cm diameter. Solid disc wheels handle heavy loads at low speed on rough terrain. Spoked wheels require a lathe-turned hub and steam-bent felloes, which are an advanced step. Fit the iron tire band while hot; as it cools, it shrinks and compresses the wheel tight.
