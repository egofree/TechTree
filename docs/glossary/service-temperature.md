# Service temperature

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

1200-1500°C - **Thermal conductivity**: 1-2 W/(m·K) - **Porosity**: 15-25% - **Applications**: Furnace linings, kiln interiors, forge hearths, chimney flues - **Limitation**: Softens above 1400°C. Poor slag resistance. Inadequate for steel furnaces and silicon processing.
