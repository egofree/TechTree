# Procedure format

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

— every technical process documented in a standardized structure: 1. **Title and identification**: Process name, document number, revision date, author 2. **Purpose**: One-sentence statement of what the procedure produces 3. **Scope**: Applicable materials, size ranges, and limitations 4. **Safety requirements**: Required PPE listed explicitly (safety glasses, leather gloves, hearing protection, respirator as appropriate), specific hazards identified (molten metal at 700°C, acid splash risk, ...
