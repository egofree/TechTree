# Woven wire

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Galvanized steel mesh (no stone-age substitute; use post-and-rail or hedges until wire is available). Mesh size 8-10 cm for sheep and goats, height 1.0-1.2 m. Add a single strand of barbed wire at 0.8 m height to deter cattle from leaning over.
