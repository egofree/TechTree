# Blowing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Gather molten glass on blowpipe (iron tube, 1-1.5 m). Roll on marver (flat iron plate) to shape gather. Blow to inflate, reheat as needed. Shape with wet wooden paddles, jacks (tongs), and shears. Speed is essential — glass stiffens noticeably in 30-60 seconds at working temperature.
