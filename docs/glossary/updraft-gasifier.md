# Updraft gasifier

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Air enters below the grate and flows upward through the fuel bed. Gas exits at the top. Temperature gradient: ambient at top (drying zone), 200-500°C in the middle (pyrolysis), 800-1200°C at the bottom (combustion/reduction). High thermal efficiency (80-90% of fuel energy in the gas) because outgoing gas preheats incoming fuel.
