# HETP

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

HETP (Height Equivalent to a Theoretical Plate) — For packed columns, the height of packing needed for one theoretical plate. Raschig rings: 0.4-0.6 m. Pall rings: 0.3-0.4 m. Structured packing: 0.1-0.3 m. Total packing height = HETP × required theoretical plates. Add 20% safety margin for maldistribution effects. See: [Distillation](../chemistry/distillation.md)
