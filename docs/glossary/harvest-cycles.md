# Harvest cycles

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Two harvests per year in most regions of India: - **Katki crop** (June-July): Harvested during the monsoon. Brood lac from this harvest is used for the next inoculation. - **Baisakhi crop** (October-November): Harvested in the post-monsoon period. Generally larger yield than Katki.
