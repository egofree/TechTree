# Steel extrusion

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(less common, specialized): - Steel requires 1100-1250°C and 50-150 MPa — far more force than aluminum. - Glass lubrication (A SEA process): glass powder placed on billet surface melts during preheating to form a viscous lubricating film. The glass also acts as a thermal barrier between hot billet and cooler tooling. - Used for stainless steel tubing and special profiles where rolling cannot achieve the shape.
