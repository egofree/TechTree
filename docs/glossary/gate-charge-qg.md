# Gate charge (Qg)

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Total charge that must be supplied to the gate to turn the device fully on. Determines switching speed: ton ≈ Qg/Ig where Ig is the gate driver current. Typical values: 1-100 nC depending on device size. Gate charge is a key parameter for switching loss calculation: Pswitch = Qg × Vgs × f.
