# Written examinations

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Written examinations (for theoretical knowledge) — Problem-solving format**: Present an engineering scenario requiring calculation and decision. Example: "A cylindrical pressure vessel 500 mm diameter, 10 mm wall thickness, made of boiler plate steel (tensile strength 400 MPa, yield 250 MPa), is to operate at 2 MPa internal pressure. Calculate the hoop stress. Is the vessel safe with a safety factor of 4? If not, what wall thickness is required?" This tests both calculation skill and engineering judgment. - **...
