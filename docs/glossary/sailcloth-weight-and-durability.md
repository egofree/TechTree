# Sailcloth weight and durability

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Standard hemp or flax canvas weighs 0.4-0.6 kg/m² and lasts 2-5 years before UV degradation and fatigue require replacement. Cotton canvas is lighter but degrades faster. Sailcloth must be kept dry when the mill is idle to prevent rot. Tarred sailcloth (painted with pine tar or linseed oil) lasts longer but is heavier and stiffer.
