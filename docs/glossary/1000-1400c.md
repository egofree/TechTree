# 1000-1400°C

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Medium to high-duty fireclay, high-alumina (60-70% Al₂O₃) bricks. Applications: steel reheating furnaces (soaking zone 1250-1300°C), ceramic kilns, non-ferrous melting furnaces (aluminum at 700-800°C, copper at 1100-1200°C). Slag resistance becomes important. For aluminum furnaces, high-alumina or non-wetting castables (containing BaSO₄ additive) resist aluminum penetration.
