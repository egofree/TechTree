# High carbon steel (0.60-1.00% C)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: metals

Steel containing 0.60-1.00% carbon, characterized by high hardness and wear resistance after heat treatment but lower ductility and weldability than lower-carbon grades. Used for springs, cutting tools, chisels, and high-strength wire. Requires careful heat treatment to balance hardness with toughness.
