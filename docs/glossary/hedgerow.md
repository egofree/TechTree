# Hedgerow

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Hawthorn, blackthorn, or osage orange planted in a double row 30-40 cm apart. Saplings spaced 20-30 cm within each row. Takes 5-8 years to form an impenetrable barrier, then requires annual trimming to maintain density. Provides windbreak, wildlife habitat, and occasional fruit or nuts.
