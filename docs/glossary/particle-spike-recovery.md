# Particle spike recovery

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

After a transient particle event (e.g., a wafer dropping), the cleanroom particle count returns to baseline in approximately 3-5 air change cycles. For a Class 100 (ISO 5) cleanroom with 40 air changes per hour, recovery takes 5-8 minutes. The time is calculated as t_recovery ≈ -ln(C_target/C_initial) × (V/Q), where V is room volume and Q is airflow rate.
