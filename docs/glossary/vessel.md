# Vessel

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

A Bessemer converter is a pear-shaped refractory-lined steel vessel, 4-6 m tall, mounted on trunnions so it can be tilted for charging and pouring. The bottom contains 100-200 tuyeres (6-10 mm diameter holes) through which air is forced at 1.5-2.5 bar. Typical capacity: 15-30 tonnes per heat.
