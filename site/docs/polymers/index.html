Polymers & Composites

Natural rubber vulcanization, synthetic rubbers (nitrile/NBR, neoprene, silicone), thermosetting polymers (Bakelite, epoxy, phenolic/FR-4 laminates, polyester, polyurethane), thermoplastic polymers (polyethylene, PVC, nylon, polystyrene, PTFE), polymer processing equipment (extruders, injection molding), and composite materials (fiberglass, carbon fiber). Provides seals, gaskets, insulation, packaging, and structural materials.

Capabilities

  • Composite Materials

    Fiberglass production (E-glass fiber from silica/boron batch, 5-25 μm filaments), hand layup, vacuum bagging, resin transfer molding, and filament winding. Carbon fiber (PAN precursor, aspiration). Polymer processing equipment: extruders, injection molding machines, compression presses, and calenders.

  • Rubber Production

    Natural and synthetic rubber production, vulcanization, and rubber product manufacturing.

  • Thermoplastic Polymers

    Polyethylene (LDPE/HDPE — films, containers, wire insulation), PVC (rigid pipe, flexible tubing, requires heat stabilizers), nylon (bearings, gears, engineering components), polystyrene (labware, insulation foam), and PTFE/Teflon (chemically inert, ultra-low friction, -200°C to +260°C, for seals and non-stick surfaces).

  • Thermosetting Polymers

    Phenol-formaldehyde (Bakelite — electrical insulators, photoresist binders), epoxy resins (structural adhesive, die attach, IC encapsulation), phenolic/epoxy FR-4 laminate (PCB substrate), unsaturated polyester resin (fiberglass matrix), and polyurethane (foams, elastomers).
