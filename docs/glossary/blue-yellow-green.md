# Blue + yellow → green

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Dye the fiber with weld yellow first (100-150% WOF, alum mordant, 85°C, 60 min). Rinse, dry. Then overdye in an indigo vat (1-3 dips depending on desired green depth). The indigo deposits blue over the yellow base, producing green. More indigo dips → darker, cooler green. Fewer dips → brighter chartreuse.
