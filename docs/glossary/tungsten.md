# Tungsten

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

WO₃ + 3H₂ → W + 3H₂O at 700-1000°C in a multi-zone furnace (4-6 zones, progressively higher temperature). Hydrogen flow: 5-20 L/min per kg WO₃. Reduction time: 2-8 hours depending on desired particle size. Particle size: 0.5-20 µm (controlled by oxide feedstock, temperature, and H₂ dew point). Fine tungsten powder (<5 µm) for cemented carbides; coarse powder (10-20 µm) for heavy alloys and sintered contacts.
