# Tallow

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Render beef suet (kidney fat) by cutting into small pieces, heating slowly in water at 70-80°C for 4-6 hours. Strain through cloth. Yield: 70-80% of raw suet weight. Tallow produces hard, long-lasting soap with stable lather. For large-scale rendering, use steam-jacketed tanks (500-5000 L) with mechanical agitation. Skim rendered fat from water surface, filter through coarse screen then fine cloth.
