# Non-ferrous metallurgy

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Copper reverberatory furnace: magnesite-chrome. Aluminum melting furnace: high-alumina (resistant to Al₂O₃ formation at metal-refractory interface — molten aluminum reduces SiO₂ from fireclay). Zinc distillation: silicon carbide (withstands zinc vapor at 900-950°C — SiC resists zinc attack better than alumina-silicates).
