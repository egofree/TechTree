# Roller application

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Wet film thickness 100-150 μm. Nap length determines texture transfer: short nap (6-10 mm) for smooth surfaces, medium (12-18 mm) for lightly textured, long (20-25 mm) for rough or exterior surfaces. Roller sleeves are woven or knitted polyester/nylon. Transfer efficiency 40-60%.
