# Septic tank systems

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Septic tank systems (individual households or small clusters) — Design**: Rectangular or cylindrical watertight tank (concrete, fiberglass, or brick with cement mortar). Minimum 2-day hydraulic retention time. For a household of 5: tank volume 2.5-3.5 m³ (sizing rule: 1 m³ base + 0.2 m³ per person). Inlet and outlet T-pipes (or baffles) prevent short-circuiting and retain floating scum.
