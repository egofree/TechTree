# Wool-cotton blend

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Wool-cotton blend (50/50) — Combines wool's warmth and moisture-wicking with cotton's softness and washability. Card the fibers together on a drum carder. The different fiber lengths (wool 5-15 cm, cotton 2-3 cm) require careful carding to avoid the shorter cotton clumping in the yarn. See: [Spinning](../textiles/spinning.md)
