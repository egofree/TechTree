# SQ 5: Public Health, Sanitation &amp; Medicine

**Starts**: Phase 1+  
**Priority**: High — skilled workers are the most valuable and irreplaceable resource.

## Problem

Industrial work is dangerous. Chemical processing (Phase 5+) involves lethal substances (HF acid, toxic gases, heavy metals). Mining, metalworking, and construction carry injury risks. Epidemics can decimate specialist populations. Losing experienced practitioners sets the project back years.

## Technologies &amp; Systems

### Sanitation Infrastructure
- **Clean water supply**: Wells, aqueducts, filtration, later chlorination
- **Sewage systems**: Waste removal and treatment
- **Waste disposal**: Organized collection and safe disposal of industrial waste

### Hygiene &amp; Prevention
- Handwashing and surgical hygiene (Semmelweis principle)
- Quarantine protocols for infectious diseases
- Food safety (preservation, storage, preparation)
- Ventilation in workshops and mines

### Medicine
- **Herbal pharmaceuticals**: Traditional plant-based remedies as baseline
- **Chemical pharmaceuticals** (Phase 5): Aspirin (salicylic acid), antiseptics (phenol/carbolic acid), anesthetics (ether, chloroform)
- **Antibiotics** (much later): Requires advanced microbiology and organic chemistry
- **Surgical capability**: Wound treatment, fracture setting, basic surgery with anesthesia

### Industrial Safety
- **Personal protective equipment (PPE)**: Eye protection, gloves, respirators, aprons
- **Ventilation and fume extraction**: For chemical work, smelting, grinding
- **Emergency protocols**: First aid training, chemical spill response, fire suppression
- **HF acid specific**: Calcium gluconate gel stations, specialized training, exposure protocols
- **Gas detection**: For CO, H₂S, Cl₂, and other toxic gases

### Hospitals &amp; Care
- Infirmaries at major work sites (mines, foundries, chemical plants)
- Central hospital with surgical capability
- Trained medical personnel on-site for hazardous operations

## Integration Points

| Phase | Contribution |
|-------|-------------|
| Phase 1 | Basic hygiene, clean water, herbal medicine |
| Phase 2 | Mining injuries, burn treatment, metal fume fever |
| Phase 3 | Machine shop safety (entanglement, eye protection) |
| Phase 4 | Boiler safety, electrical injury treatment |
| Phase 5 | HF acid protocols, toxic gas safety, chemical burn treatment |
| Phase 7–8 | Cleanroom health protocols, toxic dopant gas safety |

## Key Deliverables

- Clean water and sewage systems
- Basic hospital/infirmary capability
- PPE standards and supply for all hazardous work
- HF acid emergency protocol and supplies
- Trained medical personnel at industrial sites
