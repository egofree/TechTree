# Nitrogen fixation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Legumes host Rhizobium bacteria in root nodules that fix atmospheric nitrogen into soil-available forms. A legume crop adds ~40-80 kg nitrogen/hectare. Eliminates need for synthetic fertilizer in a 3-field cycle. - **Benefit**: Maintains soil fertility indefinitely. Without rotation, continuous grain cropping depletes soil in 3-5 years, yields drop to uneconomic levels.
