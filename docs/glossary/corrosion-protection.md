# Corrosion protection

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Steel in alkaline concrete (pH 12-13) forms passive oxide film — naturally protected. Carbonation (CO₂ penetration) and chloride ions (de-icing salt, seawater) destroy passivation → rust → expansion → concrete spalls. Solutions: adequate cover, dense low-permeability concrete (low water-cement ratio), epoxy-coated rebar for aggressive environments.
