# Voltage relationships

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Line-to-line voltage (V_LL) = √3 × line-to-neutral voltage (V_LN). For a 400V three-phase system: V_LN = 400/√3 = 231V. Equipment nameplates specify both: "400V/231V" means 400V line-to-line, 231V line-to-neutral.
