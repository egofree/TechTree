# Low-temperature shift (LTS)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Low-temperature shift (LTS): 200-250°C, Cu/ZnO/Al₂O₃ catalyst. Converts most remaining CO. Exit: ~0.2-0.3% CO. Copper catalyst is more active at lower temperature but sulfur-sensitive — requires clean feed from HTS. See: [Hydrogen & Silane Production](../chemistry/hydrogen-silane.md)
