# Morphine

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

Most potent natural analgesic. Indispensable for severe pain (surgery, trauma, terminal illness). Effective dose: 5-15 mg orally; duration 4-6 hours - **Codeine**: Milder analgesic and antitussive (cough suppressant). 15-60 mg orally - **Papaverine**: Smooth muscle relaxant (vascular spasm, GI cramping) - **Raw opium** (whole latex): Used as analgesic, antidiarrheal (paregoric)
