# Valve springs

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Steel wire coil springs (spring steel: 0.6-0.7% C, heat-treated). Keep valves closed and following cam profile at maximum RPM. Spring force 100-300 N at installed height. Prevent valve float (spring cannot follow cam at excessive RPM → valves don't close → loss of compression → engine damage).
