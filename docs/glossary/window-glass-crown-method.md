# Window glass (crown method)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Blow a large sphere (~30 cm diameter), transfer to punty, reheat, and spin the blowpipe rapidly. Centrifugal force flattens the sphere into a disc 1-1.5 m diameter, 2-4 mm thick. The thick center (bullion or bullseye) is discarded or used for less demanding applications. The thinner outer portions are cut into panes. Quality is uneven — the glass varies in thickness and has concentric distortion rings. Largely replaced by cylinder and float methods.
