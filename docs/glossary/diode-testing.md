# Diode testing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Measure forward voltage at a specified current (e.g., Vf at 1 mA, 10 mA, 100 mA). Normal silicon diode: Vf ≈ 0.6-0.7V at 1 mA. Higher Vf indicates poor contact or high series resistance. Measure reverse leakage at rated voltage (e.g., leakage at 50V). Good rectifier diode: leakage <1 μA at rated voltage.
