# Combining uncertainties

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement

For independent sources, the combined standard uncertainty is the root-sum-square of individual contributions: u_combined = √(u₁² + u₂² + ... + uₙ²). Expand to a 95% confidence interval by multiplying by a coverage factor k = 2: U = 2 × u_combined.
