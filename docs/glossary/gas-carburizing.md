# Gas carburizing

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(modern industrial standard): - Endothermic gas carrier (N₂ + CO + H₂, from air-methane reaction) enriched with natural gas (CH₄) or propane provides carbon. Furnace temperature: 900-950°C. Carbon potential of the atmosphere is measured and controlled by oxygen probe or dew point, targeting 0.7-0.9% C at the surface. - Faster than pack carburizing (better gas-solid contact).
