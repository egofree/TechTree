# Wire saw

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

(standard for 300 mm wafers, gaining adoption for smaller diameters): - A single continuous steel wire (0.12-0.18 mm diameter) is wound on grooved wire guides (pulley-like drums, 300-500 mm diameter) in a web of 200-500 parallel strands, spaced at the desired wafer thickness plus kerf (approximately 1.0-1.1 mm pitch for 300 mm wafers). - The ingot is mounted on a holder and pressed down against the entire wire web simultaneously.
