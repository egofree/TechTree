# Specific energy for grinding

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Specific energy for grinding: 10-30 kWh per tonne of ore for ball milling to 80% passing 75 μm. Harder ores (high Bond work index, >20 kWh/t) consume more energy than softer ores (<10 kWh/t). The Bond work index is determined by a standard locked-cycle grindability test: grind a known mass of ore in a standard ball mill, measure the product size distribution, and calculate the energy required per tonne. See: [Ore Processing](../mining/processing.md)
