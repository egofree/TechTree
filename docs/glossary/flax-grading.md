# Flax grading

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Line fibers sorted by length, color, and fineness. #1 line: longest (60+ cm), pale blonde, fine and strong. #2 line: 40-60 cm, light brown. Tow: short (5-15 cm), darker, coarser — sold for rope, twine, and coarse cloth.
