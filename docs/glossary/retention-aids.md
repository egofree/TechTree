# Retention aids

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Cationic polyacrylamide (CPAM) and bentonite clay — flocculate fine particles (fillers, fines) onto fiber surfaces, preventing them from washing through the paper machine wire into the white water. Retention >80% essential for economic filler use and clean water circuits. Microparticle retention system (CPAM + bentonite) forms small, dense flocs that drain well and give good formation (uniform fiber distribution).
