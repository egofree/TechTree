# Thermal design power (TDP)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: vlsi-scaling

Modern GPUs dissipate 150-450W in a die area of 200-800 mm² — power density of 50-200 W/cm², approaching the flux of a rocket nozzle. Thermal solutions: integrated heat spreader (copper or nickel-plated copper) with thermal interface material (TIM), soldered or glued to die. Liquid cooling (cold plates, microchannels) necessary for >300W. Thermal throttling reduces clock frequency when junction temperature exceeds limit (typically 90-105°C).
