# Boat shuttle

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Boat-shaped wooden body (25-35 cm) with hollow center holding a removable bobbin (small cylinder wound with weft yarn). Yarn feeds through a hole in shuttle side. Flies through shed on a treadle loom — much faster than stick shuttle. Requires bobbins wound evenly (use bobbin winder — small hand-cranked spindle). Shuttle weight: 50-150 g depending on size.
