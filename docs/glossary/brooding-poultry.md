# Brooding (poultry)

> **Type**: process | **Tier**: critical | **Domains**: animals

Providing supplemental heat to poultry chicks for the first 3-6 weeks of life. Start at 32-35°C, decrease by 2-3°C per week until ambient temperature reached. Requires draft-free enclosure with solid walls (brooder guard), clean bedding, and accessible feed/water.
