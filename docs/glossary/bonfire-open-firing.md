# Bonfire / open firing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

(simplest, earliest — temperatures 600-900°C): 1. **Air dry** pots for 3-7 days until bone dry. Any moisture causes steam explosions. 2. **Pre-heat**: Place pots around edge of small kindling fire for 30-60 minutes. Warm slowly to drive off residual moisture. 3. **Build fuel bed**: Layer dry wood, straw, or dung in a circle ~1 m diameter, 20-30 cm tall.
