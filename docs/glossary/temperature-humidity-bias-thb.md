# Temperature-humidity-bias (THB)

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

85°C/85%RH with applied bias, 1000 hours (the "85/85" test). Detects: corrosion, ionic contamination, moisture ingress. (4) **Electrostatic discharge (ESD)**: Human body model (HBM, 2000V), charged device model (CDM, 500V). (5) **Pressure cooker (PCT)**: 121°C, 100%RH, 2 atm, 96-168 hours — accelerated version of THB. (6) **Mechanical**: vibration (5-2000 Hz), shock (1500g, 0.5 ms), constant acceleration (30,000g).
