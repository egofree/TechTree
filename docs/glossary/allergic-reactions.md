# Allergic reactions

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants, polymers

Some individuals develop contact dermatitis from gutta-percha — reported primarily among dental workers handling gutta-percha points. The reaction is typically a delayed hypersensitivity (Type IV) response to residual proteins or processing chemicals. Dental patients with gutta-percha root canal fillings rarely develop reactions due to the material's isolation from the immune system within the root canal.
