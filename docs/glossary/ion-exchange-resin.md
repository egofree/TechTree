# ion exchange resin

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Pre-functionalized polymer beads (typically from water softeners) that selectively exchange cations or anions. In SEM Tech, these beads are pulverized to <200μm and dispersed in PVC/CPVC binder to form homogeneous ion exchange membranes. Types include strong/weak acid cation and strong/weak base anion resins.
