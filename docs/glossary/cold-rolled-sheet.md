# Cold-rolled sheet

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Hot-rolled strip pickled (acid-cleaned to remove scale) and cold-rolled to 0.15-3 mm thickness at room temperature. Higher dimensional accuracy and better surface finish than hot-rolled. Subsequently annealed (650-750°C to restore ductility lost during cold work) and temper-rolled (0.5-2% cold reduction for flatness and surface finish).
