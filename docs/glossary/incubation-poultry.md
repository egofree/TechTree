# Incubation (poultry)

> **Type**: process | **Tier**: critical | **Domains**: animals

Maintaining eggs at 37-38°C with 50-65% humidity for species-specific periods to hatch chicks. Chicken eggs: 21 days, duck: 28 days, goose: 28-30 days, turkey: 28 days, quail: 17 days, guinea fowl: 26-28 days, pigeon: 17 days. Turn eggs 3-5 times daily for first 18-25 days (species-dependent).
