# Schottky diode

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Metal-semiconductor junction (not a p-n junction). Lower forward voltage drop (0.2-0.4V, depending on the metal). Faster switching (no minority carrier storage — majority carrier device). Higher reverse leakage current than silicon p-n diodes. Used in high-frequency rectifiers, power supply OR-ing, RF detectors.
