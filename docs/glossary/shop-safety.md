# Shop safety

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Eye protection mandatory for all machining, grinding, chipping. No loose clothing or jewelry near rotating machinery. Lockout/tagout before maintenance (physically disconnect power, attach tag). Fire extinguisher training (sand bucket for metal fires, water for wood/paper, CO₂ or dry chemical for electrical and oil fires). First aid for cuts, burns, eye injuries.
