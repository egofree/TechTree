# pH and permanence

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Acidic paper (groundwood pulp, alum-rosin sized) degrades within 50-100 years (see [Libraries & Information Systems](libraries.md) for preservation). For archival printed works, use alkaline paper (kraft pulp, calcium carbonate filler, neutral or alkaline sizing). The cost premium for archival paper is modest (perhaps 20-50% above acidic paper) and pays for itself many times over in reduced preservation costs and survived knowledge.
