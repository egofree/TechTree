# Belt conveyor

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

The standard material transport for crushed ore and concentrate. Rubber belt (500-1500 mm wide) on idler rollers, driven by an electric motor at the head pulley. Capacity: 100-5,000 tonnes/hour depending on belt width, speed (1-5 m/s), and material density. Angle of inclination: up to 18° for most ores (steeper angles cause material rollback). Cover the belt to control dust in dry climates.
