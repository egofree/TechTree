# Bootstrapping note

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Gas turbines are late-stage technology. They require precision investment casting of heat-resistant superalloys, precision machining of compressor and turbine blades to tight tolerances, advanced metallurgy (directionally solidified and single-crystal casting), and sophisticated bearing and sealing technology. Not achievable until well into the industrial era — after precision machine tools, advanced foundry practice, and alloy development are established.
