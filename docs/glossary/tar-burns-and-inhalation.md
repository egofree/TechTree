# Tar burns and inhalation

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Hot pine tar (60-80°C) causes thermal burns on skin contact. Tar vapors are irritating to eyes and respiratory tract. Apply tar in a well-ventilated area. Wear heavy gloves, eye protection, and long sleeves. Hot tar is difficult to remove from skin — use vegetable oil or petroleum jelly to loosen before washing.
