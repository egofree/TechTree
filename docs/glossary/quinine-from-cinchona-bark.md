# Quinine from Cinchona bark

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Cinchona bark contains 2-7% total alkaloids (quinine, quinidine, cinchonine, cinchonidine). Extract with hot water (decoction, 10 g bark per 100 mL water, boil 20 minutes). Filter hot. Add lime (CaO) or NaOH to alkalize the solution (pH >9). Quinine precipitates as free base. Collect precipitate by filtration.
