# ALD precursors

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: vlsi-scaling

Trimethylaluminum (TMA) is pyrophoric — ignites spontaneously on contact with air. Use in closed, nitrogen-purged delivery systems with leak detection. Tetrakis(dimethylamido)hafnium (TDMAHf) is moisture-sensitive and decomposes to toxic amine vapors. Handle in glove boxes or ventilated enclosures. In case of TMA fire, use Class D (dry powder) extinguisher — never water.
