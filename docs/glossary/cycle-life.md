# Cycle life

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

200-500 cycles for automotive starting batteries (thin plates, optimized for high current bursts not deep cycling). 500-1500 cycles for deep-cycle traction batteries (thick plates, 3-5 mm active material, designed for 80% depth of discharge). Cycle life degrades sharply below 20% state of charge, where irreversible lead sulfate crystallization (sulfation) accelerates.
