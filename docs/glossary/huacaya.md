# Huacaya

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Alpaca breed (90% of alpacas) with fluffy, crimped fleece growing perpendicular to the skin. Crimp gives natural elasticity and loft, ideal for knitting and felting. Fiber diameter 20-30 microns. 22 recognized natural colors. Hardier and easier to manage than Suri.
