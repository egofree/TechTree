# Trapping

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Snap traps baited with peanut butter, bacon, or dried fruit, placed along walls where rodents travel (perpendicular to the wall, trigger end toward the wall). Set multiple traps (10-20) in areas with active signs. Check daily. Dispose of dead rodents by burial or incineration (do not handle with bare hands).
