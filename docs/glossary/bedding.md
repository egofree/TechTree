# Bedding

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Straw (wheat, oat, barley) at 2-5 kg/day per large animal. Wood shavings or sawdust work where timber is plentiful. Peat moss absorbs 8-10 times its weight in liquid. Replace soiled bedding daily in tie stalls; deep-litter systems add fresh material weekly and muck out every 4-6 weeks. Composted manure and bedding, stacked for 6-12 months, produces stable fertilizer safe for direct field application.
