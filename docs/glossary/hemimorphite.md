# hemimorphite

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

hemimorphite (Zn₄Si₂O₇(OH)₂·H₂O) — Oxide/carbonate ores, historically important ("calamine"), now largely depleted. Easier to reduce than sulfides but rare in economic concentrations. See: [Non-Ferrous Metal Production](../metals/non-ferrous.md)
