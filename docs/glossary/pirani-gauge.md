# Pirani gauge

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Heated wire element (tungsten or platinum) in the vacuum system. Gas molecules conduct heat away from the wire. At higher pressure, more molecules collide with the wire, cooling it (lower resistance). At lower pressure, fewer collisions, wire stays hotter (higher resistance). A Wheatstone bridge circuit measures resistance change and converts to pressure reading. Range: 1-10⁻³ mbar.
