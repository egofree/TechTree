# Chemical processing to pure ZrO₂

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: ceramics

Refinement of zirconium compounds to pure zirconium dioxide (ZrO₂) through calcination of zirconium hydroxide or oxychloride at 500-800°C, producing a white powder used for advanced ceramics, thermal barrier coatings, and oxygen sensor electrolytes.
