# Ecole Polytechnique

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

(Paris, founded 1794): - Created to address France's shortage of trained engineers and scientists after the Revolution. Standardized, rigorous, meritocratic admission (competitive examination — no aristocratic preference). Curriculum: mathematics (advanced calculus, differential equations, descriptive geometry), physics, chemistry, mechanics, drawing. Duration: 3 years.
