# Epoxy sensitization

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Repeated skin contact with uncured epoxy resin or amine hardeners causes allergic contact dermatitis that becomes permanent once sensitized — even trace future exposure triggers severe rash. Once sensitized, a worker may be unable to continue any epoxy-related work.
