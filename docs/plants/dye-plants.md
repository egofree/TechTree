# Dye Plants

> **Node ID**: plants.dye-plants

### Overview

Cultivation and processing of plant species that yield natural colorants for dyeing textiles, leather, and other materials. Source of indigo, madder, weld, and other traditional dyes.

### Key Species

No specific species cataloged yet. Common dye plants include indigo (Indigofera tinctoria, blue), madder (Rubia tinctorum, red), weld (Reseda luteola, yellow), and woad (Isatis tinctoria, blue).
