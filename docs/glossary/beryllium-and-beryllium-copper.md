# Beryllium and beryllium copper

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: metals

Alloy system incorporating 0.5-2.5% Be in copper, producing age-hardenable alloys with high strength (UTS up to 1400 MPa), excellent electrical conductivity, and non-sparking properties. Beryllium itself is a lightweight structural metal (density 1.85 g/cm³) with high stiffness.
