# People

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

People (the dominant contamination source) — At rest: a fully gowned cleanroom operator emits ~100,000 particles ≥0.3 μm per minute (skin flakes, hair fragments, lint from garments, breath droplets). At moderate activity (walking, reaching): ~1,000,000 particles per minute. This is why gowning, behavior protocols, and laminar airflow are all essential simultaneously. - **Skin flakes**: Humans shed ~10⁹ skin cells per day.
