# OFHC copper

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(Oxygen-Free High Conductivity, C10100/C10200): Produced by melting and casting copper under a reducing (CO/N₂) atmosphere or in a vacuum induction furnace, eliminating dissolved oxygen (which forms Cu₂O inclusions in tough-pitch copper). OFHC copper achieves 101% IACS (58.5 MS/m) minimum. Superior ductility, weldability, and hydrogen embrittlement resistance compared to tough-pitch.
