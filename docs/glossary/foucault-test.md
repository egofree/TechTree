# Foucault test

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: optics

Foucault test (knife-edge test) — place point light source at radius of curvature. Move razor blade across returning cone of light. Shadows reveal zones: sphere shows uniform gray; paraboloid shows characteristic "doughnut" shadow pattern. Measure zonal radii with Couder mask to quantify deviation from paraboloid. See: [Optical Inspection](../optics/inspection.md)
