# Inverter

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Convert DC battery voltage to AC (sine wave, 50 or 60 Hz, 220-240V). Motor-generator set (DC motor drives AC generator — simple, rugged, inefficient) or solid-state inverter (Photolithography — transistors switch DC at high frequency → synthesize sine wave, 85-95% efficient).
