# Journeyman

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

(after completing apprenticeship): Leaves the master's workshop to work for wages at other workshops — often required to travel (*Wanderjahre*, journeyman years) to learn different methods and approaches from multiple masters. This travel phase is critical: it prevents local skill stagnation and spreads innovations across regions. Journeyman produces a *journeyman piece* — a demonstration artifact proving competence. The journeyman can work independently but cannot yet take apprentices.
