# Lead glaze toxicity

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

Lead oxide (PbO) is a cumulative neurotoxin affecting the nervous system, kidneys, and reproductive system. Inhalation of lead dust during glaze mixing and lead fumes during firing are both hazardous. **Never use lead glazes on food-contact surfaces.** Use respiratory protection and gloves when handling lead compounds. Wash hands thoroughly after any contact.
