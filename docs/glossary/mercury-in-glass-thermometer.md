# Mercury-in-glass thermometer

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement

Capillary tube (0.1-0.3 mm bore) with bulb, filled with mercury. Mercury expands linearly with temperature (over range -38°C to +357°C). Mark scale by immersing in reference point baths. Divide scale by precision dividing engine. Accuracy: ±0.1°C for well-made thermometers. Read to 0.01°C with magnifier and etched scale.
