# Consumption rate

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

5-15 kg per tonne of steel (EAF) vs. 2-8 kg for graphite electrodes. For SAF silicon production: 60-100 kg per tonne of silicon vs. 30-60 kg for graphite. The higher consumption rate is driven by oxidation (amorphous carbon oxidizes faster than graphite above ~500°C) and mechanical erosion (lower thermal shock resistance).
