# Bottle production

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Blow the bubble into a wet wooden mold (two-part hinged mold, soaked in water). The mold shapes the exterior while the interior follows the bubble shape. Neck is constricted with jacks before molding. Mold seam lines are visible on finished bottles. Production rate with a skilled team (gaffer + assistant): 30-60 bottles per hour.
