# Recycled fiber processing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Recovered paper is pulped in a hydrapulper (helical rotor at 4-6% consistency, 20-30 minutes), deinked (surfactant + air flotation removes ink particles 10-150 μm), cleaned (heavy and light contaminant removal), and bleached (H₂O₂ or FAS reductive bleaching). Yield: 75-85% of input recovered paper becomes reusable fiber.
