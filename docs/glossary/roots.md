# Roots

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

Dig in autumn after aerial parts die back, when the plant has translocated storage compounds (starches, alkaloids, glycosides) downward for winter reserves. Spring-harvested roots may contain only 30-50% of autumn peak potency. Wash roots immediately, remove fibrous side roots, and slice longitudinally for even drying.
