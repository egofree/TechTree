# Ethanol flammability

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Shellac is dissolved in ethanol, which is highly flammable (flash point ~13°C, auto-ignition ~365°C, lower explosive limit 3.3% in air). All shellac mixing, application, and drying must occur in well-ventilated areas with no open flames, sparks, or hot surfaces. Store ethanol in approved containers. Ground metal containers to prevent static discharge. Fire extinguisher (Class B — CO₂ or dry chemical) must be immediately available in shellac working areas.
