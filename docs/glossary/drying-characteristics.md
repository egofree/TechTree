# Drying characteristics

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Shellac dries by solvent evaporation (physical drying), not chemical reaction. Touch dry: 15-30 minutes for a 2 lb cut at 20°C, 50% RH. Hard dry: 1-2 hours. Full hardness (maximum film formation): 24 hours. Shellac can be recoated within 2-4 hours without lifting the previous coat (the solvent softens the underlying layer, promoting intercoat adhesion).
