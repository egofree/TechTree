# Cure system

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

MEKP (methyl ethyl ketone peroxide) catalyst at 1-2% by resin weight. Cobalt naphthenate or cobalt octoate accelerator at 0.2-0.5%. The cobalt ion decomposes the peroxide into free radicals, which initiate the chain-growth copolymerization of styrene with the unsaturated polyester chains. Gel time: 15-30 minutes at 25°C (adjustable by varying catalyst and accelerator concentration — more cobalt = faster gel).
