# Light fastness (1-8 Blue Wool scale)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Expose half the dyed sample to direct sunlight for 100-200 hours (or use a UV lamp for accelerated testing). Cover the other half. Compare the exposed and unexposed halves against the Blue Wool reference cards (8 strips of wool dyed with standard dyes of known lightfastness, each one twice as fast as the previous).
