# Steam-powered platen press

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Steam-powered platen press (~1850) — 2,000-4,000 sheets/hour with steam-driven mechanism replacing human arm power. One operator feeds sheets by hand, another stacks output. The steam engine drives the platen through a flywheel and toggle linkage, providing consistent impression force on every stroke. Paper feeding speed becomes the bottleneck, not press mechanism speed.
