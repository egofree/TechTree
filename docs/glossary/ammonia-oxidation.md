# Ammonia oxidation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

NH₃ + 5/4 O₂ → NO + 3/2 H₂O over a Pt/Rh (90/10 or 95/5) gauze catalyst at 850-900°C, 0.1-1.0 MPa. The gauze consists of multiple layers of fine woven wire mesh (0.06 mm wire diameter, 1024 mesh/cm²). Contact time: 0.1-1.0 milliseconds (extremely short). Conversion efficiency: 95-98%. Side reaction: 4NH₃ + 3O₂ → 2N₂ + 6H₂O (wastes ammonia, minimized by proper temperature and contact time).
