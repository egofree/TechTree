# Root attachment (fir-tree design)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

The blade root locks into the rotor disc and carries the full centrifugal load. The fir-tree (dovetail) design uses interlocking serrated teeth, typically 3-6 pairs of contact faces, each machined to ±0.01 mm tolerance. The blade slides axially into the disc groove and is locked by a small pin or peened tab.
