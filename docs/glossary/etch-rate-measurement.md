# Etch rate measurement

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Film thickness measured before and after etching using spectroscopic ellipsometry or reflectometry. Typical etch rates: polysilicon in CF₄/O₂ plasma: 100-300 nm/min; SiO₂ in CHF₃/CF₄ plasma: 50-150 nm/min; photoresist in O₂ plasma: 200-500 nm/min; aluminum in Cl₂/BCl₃ plasma: 300-800 nm/min. Etch uniformity across a 300 mm wafer: ±3-5% (center-to-edge variation controlled by gas distribution, electrode design, and temperature uniformity).
