# Temperature measurement

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Mercury or alcohol thermometer, 0-200°C range, 1°C graduations. Used for monitoring: melting points (purity verification), reaction temperatures during synthesis, storage temperatures, autoclave cycles. Melting point apparatus: Thiele tube (oil-filled tube with side arm, heated with Bunsen burner) holds capillary tube containing the sample. Observe the temperature range over which the sample melts (sharp melting over 1-2°C = pure; broad range = impure).
