# Specific energy consumption

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Modern cryogenic ASU — 0.35-0.55 kWh per Nm³ of O₂ produced (as gas). For liquid products: 0.8-1.2 kWh/Nm³ O₂ (additional energy for liquefaction). The compressor is the dominant energy consumer (>90% of total plant power).
