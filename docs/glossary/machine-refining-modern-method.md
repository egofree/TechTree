# Machine refining (modern method)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Industrial operations use heated hydraulic presses with canvas or metal mesh filter plates. Seedlac is heated to 80-90°C and pressed through the filter at 5-10 MPa. The filtered melt is extruded as a continuous sheet, cooled on a steel roller, and broken into flakes. Throughput: 100-500 kg/hour. Machine-refined shellac has more consistent quality than hand-refined material.
