# Frame assembly

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Frame assembly (for a 1.2-1.5 m weaving width) — - Side posts: 10 × 10 cm × 140 cm, mortised for cross-members. Stand on flat feet to prevent tipping. - Cross-members: 5 × 10 cm, bolted through posts with 12 mm iron bolts or secured with wedged through-tenons for a no-metal-required version. - Cloth beam and warp beam: 8-9 cm diameter × 130-150 cm rollers with iron ratchet and pawl mechanisms.
