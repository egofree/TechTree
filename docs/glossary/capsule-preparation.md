# Capsule preparation

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Capsule preparation (requires gelatin) — - **Hard gelatin capsules**: Two-piece cylindrical shells (cap and body) made from gelatin, water, and plasticizer. Fill body with powdered drug mixture by hand or semi-automatic machine. Cap slips over body to seal. Available in sizes 000 (largest, ~1.4 mL) to 5 (smallest, ~0.13 mL).
