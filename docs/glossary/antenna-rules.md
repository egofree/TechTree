# Antenna rules

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: vlsi-scaling

During plasma etching, charge accumulates on long metal wires connected to transistor gates. If the accumulated voltage exceeds gate oxide breakdown (~5-10 V for thin oxides), the transistor is destroyed. Antenna rules limit the ratio of metal area connected to a gate to the gate area. Typical limit: 100-400×. Violations are fixed by adding antenna diodes or breaking the wire with a jumper to an upper metal layer.
