# Abrasive blast cleaning

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

The single most important factor in coating performance. ISO 8501-1 defines four blast grades: Sa 1 (light blast, removes loose mill scale and paint), Sa 2 (thorough blast, removes most mill scale and rust), Sa 2.5 (near-white metal, removes all visible mill scale, rust, and coating — the standard for high-performance systems), Sa 3 (white metal, complete removal to bare, uniform metal).
