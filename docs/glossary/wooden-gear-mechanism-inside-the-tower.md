# Wooden gear mechanism inside the tower

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

The brake wheel (large crown gear, 1.5-3 m diameter) is mounted on the horizontal windshaft. It meshes with the wallower (a lantern pinion) on the vertical upright shaft. Below, the great spur wheel (another large crown gear on the upright shaft) drives the stone nuts (smaller gear wheels directly above each pair of millstones).
