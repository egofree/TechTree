# Rectifier diode fabrication flow

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

(simplest discrete device): 1. **Starting wafer**: n-type <111> silicon, 5-20 Ω·cm, 300-500 μm thick. 2. **Boron diffusion**: Spin boron dopant source (BBr₃ in N₂ carrier) onto wafer surface. Load in diffusion furnace at 1000-1100°C for 30-120 minutes. Drive-in at 1100°C for 30-60 minutes to deepen junction.
