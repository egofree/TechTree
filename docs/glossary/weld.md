# Weld

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

(*Reseda luteola*): Biennial or annual. Sow in spring, thin to 20 cm spacing. Harvest entire above-ground plant at flowering (peak luteolin concentration). Very easy to grow — tolerates poor soil. Yield: ~2-3 kg dried plant per 10 m². The most light-fast natural yellow dye.
