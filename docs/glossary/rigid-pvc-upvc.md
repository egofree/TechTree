> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Lubricant (1-5 phr, calcium stearate or paraffin wax — reduces friction in extruder and prevents sticking to mold surfaces). Impact modifier (0-10 phr, MBS or CPE — prevents brittle fracture).
