# Oil purification

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Oil purification (for extending vacuum oil life) — Filtration**: Pass oil through 1-5 μm filter to remove particulates. - **Degassing**: Heat oil under vacuum to remove dissolved gases and volatile contaminants. - **Adsorption**: Pass through activated alumina or molecular sieve to remove acidic and polar contaminants. See: [Lubricants, Oils & Fluid Mechanics](../chemistry/lubricants.md)
