# Sound interpretation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Resonant (low-pitched, hollow) over normal air-filled lung. Dull (high-pitched, flat) over consolidated lung (pneumonia), fluid (pleural effusion), or solid organs (liver, heart). Tympanitic (drum-like) over air-filled stomach or bowel. Stony dull over large pleural effusion.
