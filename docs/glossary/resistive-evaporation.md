# Resistive evaporation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: optics

A tungsten or molybdenum boat (0.1-0.5 mm thick sheet, folded into a "V" or dimpled shape) carries the source material. Current of 10-50 A at 3-10 V heats the boat by Joule heating. Temperature control is by variac or SCR power supply. Deposition rate depends on source temperature and source-to-substrate distance: typical rates 0.1-5 nm/s for common coating materials.
