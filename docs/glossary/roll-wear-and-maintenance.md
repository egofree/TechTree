# Roll wear and maintenance

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Work rolls in the finishing mill wear rapidly — a set of work rolls lasts 2,000-5,000 tons of steel before requiring regrinding. Rolls are removed, ground to restore concentricity and surface finish, and reinstalled at progressively wider gauge positions. Total roll life: 30,000-100,000 tons before the roll diameter is too small for further use. Backup rolls (forged steel, 1250-1600 mm diameter) last 10-20× longer than work rolls.
