# Paper-based tape

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: computing

Paper strip (6-12 mm wide) coated with iron oxide powder in a cellulose binder. Coating process: mix Fe₃O₄ powder (~0.5 μm particle size) with binder, coat onto paper, dry, calender for smooth surface. Coercivity: 200-400 Oe. The simplest magnetic recording medium.
