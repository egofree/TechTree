# Soda ash waste

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Spent or off-spec Na₂CO₃ can be redirected to glass batch as a cullet replacement, where it serves as the flux to lower silica melting temperature. Soda ash waste is also usable in water softening (precipitates Ca²⁺ and Mg²⁺ as carbonates). This circular routing avoids disposal entirely.
