# Metallization — front contacts

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Screen-print silver paste in grid pattern (finger width 50-100 μm, bus bar 1-2 mm). Dry at 150-200°C, fire at 700-800°C (fast ramp, belt furnace, ~30 sec at peak). Ag paste etches through SiNₓ to contact n+ emitter.
