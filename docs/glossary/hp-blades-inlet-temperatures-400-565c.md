# HP blades (inlet temperatures 400-565°C)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

HP blades (inlet temperatures 400-565°C): 12% chromium martensitic stainless steel (AISI 422 or similar). The chromium provides oxidation resistance and hardenability. These blades face the highest temperatures and pressures in the turbine. Creep resistance is the critical property: the blade must not slowly elongate under centrifugal stress at operating temperature. For temperatures above 565°C, austenitic stainless steels or nickel-based alloys are required. See: [Steam Turbines](../energy/steam-turbines.md)
