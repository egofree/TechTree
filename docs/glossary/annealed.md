# β-annealed

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

β-annealed (lenticular α in prior-β grains) — slow cooling from above the β transus (~995°C) produces a Widmanstätten (basketweave) α+β structure. Higher fracture toughness and fatigue crack growth resistance, but lower ductility. See: [Specialty Alloys](../metals/alloys.md)
