# Alkyl ketene dimer (AKD)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Reactive size — reacts with cellulose hydroxyl groups forming covalent β-ketoester bonds. Added to pulp slurry before paper machine. Cures over 1-7 days after papermaking. **Alkenyl succinic anhydride (ASA)**: Faster curing than AKD (reacts on the paper machine). Both supplied as aqueous emulsions (cationic starch stabilizer). Dosage: 0.05-0.3% on dry fiber weight.
