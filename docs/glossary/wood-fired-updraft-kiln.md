# Wood-fired updraft kiln

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

Wood-fired updraft kiln (0.5 m³ chamber) — 20-50 kg hardwood per bisque firing, 40-80 kg for stoneware glaze. Softwoods burn faster and produce less heat per kg — use hardwood (oak, ash, maple) for sustained high temperatures. See: [Kiln Firing](../ceramics/kiln-firing.md)
