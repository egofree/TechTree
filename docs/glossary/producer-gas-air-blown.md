# Producer gas (air-blown)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

CO 20-25%, H₂ 10-15%, CH₄ 2-3%, CO₂ 8-12%, N₂ 50-55%, water vapor 5-15%, tar 0.01-50 g/m³ depending on gasifier type. Calorific value 4-6 MJ/Nm³ (dry). This is a low-energy gas because nitrogen from the air dilutes the combustible components. Stoichiometric air-gas ratio for combustion: 1.0-1.5:1 by volume (much less air needed than for natural gas at 10:1).
