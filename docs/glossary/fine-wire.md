# Fine wire

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools, metals

Fine wire (< 1 mm) — use draw bench with capstan (rotating drum). Speed 30–100 m/min for copper. Dies are tungsten carbide inserts. Copper wire down to 0.1 mm achievable — the wire for [electromagnetic coils](../energy/electricity.md).
