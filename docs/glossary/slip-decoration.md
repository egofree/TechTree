# Slip decoration

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics, foundations

Apply a thin layer of differently colored liquid clay (slip) to the pot surface before drying. Red or white slip on a darker body clay produces contrast. Apply with fingers, a rag, or by dipping. Slip can also be trailed on in lines using a spout made from a hollow reed or bone tube, creating freehand painted designs.
