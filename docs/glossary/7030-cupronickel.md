# 70/30 cupronickel

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(C71500: 69% Cu, 30% Ni, 0.7% Fe, 0.5% Mn): Higher strength (~380 MPa annealed, ~520 MPa cold-worked) and superior corrosion resistance in high-velocity seawater (up to 3.5 m/s). Used for naval ship hulls, submarine pipework, offshore platform systems. More expensive (higher Ni content) but lasts longer in severe service. Added chromium (C71900, 30% Ni + 2.8% Cr) for weldability and localized corrosion resistance.
