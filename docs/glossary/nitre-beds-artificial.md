# Nitre beds (artificial)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Layer organic matter (manure, vegetable waste, urine) with limestone rubble and ashes in a heap. Keep moist but not waterlogged. Nitrifying bacteria convert nitrogen compounds to nitrate over 12-24 months. Leach the heap with water, evaporate the leachate to crystallize KNO₃. Add wood ash (potash, K₂CO₃) to convert calcium nitrate to potassium nitrate (Ca(NO₃)₂ + K₂CO₃ → CaCO₃↓ + 2KNO₃). Filter, evaporate, recrystallize to purity.
