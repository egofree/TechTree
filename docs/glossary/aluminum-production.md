# Aluminum production

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

(Hall-Héroult process — enormous power consumer): - **Feedstock**: Purified alumina (Al₂O₃) from bauxite via Bayer process: dissolve bauxite in hot NaOH (150-250°C, 5-30 bar), filter impurities (red mud), precipitate Al(OH)₃, calcine at 1100-1200°C → Al₂O₃. - **Electrolysis**: Dissolve Al₂O₃ in molten cryolite (Na₃AlF₆, melting point 1012°C) at 950-1000°C.
