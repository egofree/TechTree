# Inbreeding avoidance

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

A minimum effective breeding population of 50 animals per species maintains genetic diversity at levels that avoid inbreeding depression (reduced fertility, slower growth, higher mortality). In practice this means at least 15-20 breeding females and 3-5 unrelated males. Rotate or exchange breeding males with neighboring settlements every 3-4 generations.
