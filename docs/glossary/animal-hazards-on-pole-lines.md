# Animal hazards on pole lines

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Pole line construction crews work outdoors in all weather. Snake bites, insect stings, and large animal encounters are real risks in rural and wilderness line construction. Carry a first aid kit with antivenin protocols, tourniquets, and epinephrine for anaphylactic reactions to stings. Line crews typically work in pairs for this reason.
