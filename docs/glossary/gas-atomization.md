# Gas atomization

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Gas atomization (premium spherical powders) — - Molten metal stream (induction or arc melted under inert atmosphere) is disintegrated by high-pressure argon or nitrogen gas at 2-7 MPa through a converging-diverging nozzle. The gas kinetic energy overcomes the melt's surface tension, forming droplets that spheroidize during free fall. - Particle size: 10-150 µm median (d₅₀), controlled by gas pressure, melt flow rate, and nozzle geometry.
