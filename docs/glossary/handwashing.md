# Handwashing

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Soap + running water. Scrub all surfaces for 20+ seconds. Dry with clean cloth or air dry. Required before eating, after latrine use, before wound care, before food preparation. Soap making: animal fat + lye (NaOH from leached wood ash — see Chemistry) → soap + glycerol. Saponification at 60-80°C for 1-2 hours. Pour into molds. Cure 4-6 weeks.
