# Ribbons

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Thin silicon sheets (100-300 μm thick, 50-100 mm wide, meters long) produced directly without wafering. Eliminates the kerf loss of wire saw slicing (~40-50% of silicon in conventional wafering is lost as kerf). Ribbon silicon is lower quality (more grain boundaries and defects) but suitable for low-cost solar cells.
