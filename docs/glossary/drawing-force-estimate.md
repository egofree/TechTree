# Drawing force estimate

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

F = π × D × t × σ_y × (D/d − 0.7), where D = blank diameter, d = punch diameter, t = sheet thickness, σ_y = yield strength. Example: a 100 mm blank drawn to a 60 mm cup from 1 mm mild steel (σ_y = 250 MPa) requires F = π × 100 × 1 × 250 × (100/60 − 0.7) ≈ 78.5 kN (~8 tonnes).
