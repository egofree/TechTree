# Connecting rod

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy, transport

Forge from steel. I-beam cross-section (stiff, light). Big end (crankpin end): split, with bearing cap bolted on. Bearing: replaceable babbitt-lined bearing shells (babbitt = Sn + Cu + Sb alloy — soft, embeds particles, conformable) or bronze bushings. Small end (piston pin end): bronze bushing. Piston pin: hardened steel, 15-20 mm diameter, floating (rotates in both piston and rod).
