# Printing

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Place sheet of paper (slightly damp for flexibility) on the inked block. Rub the back of the paper with a baren (flat, smooth disk — traditionally bamboo-sheath wrapped over a coiled fiber core) or a wooden spoon. Pressure transfers ink from block to paper. Peel off printed sheet. Hang to dry.
