# Blending

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Power stations often blend high-ash and low-ash coals to meet boiler specifications. A typical blend ratio: 60% medium-ash (20% ash) + 40% low-ash (8% ash) → blended ash ~15.2%. Sulfur can also be managed by blending — mix high-sulfur coal with low-sulfur coal to stay below emission thresholds.
