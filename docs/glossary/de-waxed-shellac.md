# De-waxed shellac

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Processed to remove natural wax (by filtration through fine cloth at ~50°C in ethanol solution — the wax is insoluble and filtered out). De-waxed shellac dries harder, adheres better to other finishes, and is preferred as a sealer under polyurethane or varnish. All grades (orange, blonde, bleached) are available in de-waxed form.
