# eyepiece reticle

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement

eyepiece reticle (a glass disc with engraved scale in the eyepiece focal plane). The reticle scale is calibrated against the stage micrometer at each magnification. After calibration, any specimen feature can be measured by counting reticle divisions. See: [Optical Measurement Instruments](../measurement/optical-instruments.md)
