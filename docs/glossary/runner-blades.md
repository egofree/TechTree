# Runner blades

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

4-8 propeller blades mounted on a central hub, each rotating on its own trunnion. An internal linkage (pushrods or hydraulic piston inside the hub) moves all blades in unison. Blade angle: 15-40° relative to the rotation plane. The mechanism must be watertight: oil-filled hubs with rotary shaft seals keep river water out.
