# Reconstitution

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Soak glue granules or broken sheets in cold water for 2-4 hours to swell. Heat the swollen glue in a glue pot (double boiler) at 50-60°C until fully liquid. Never heat above 70°C. Working concentration: 15-25% glue by weight in water, adjusted based on the application.
