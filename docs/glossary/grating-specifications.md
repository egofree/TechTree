# Grating specifications

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement

300-1200 lines/mm for typical instruments. Resolving power R = mN, where m is the diffraction order (typically 1 or 2) and N is the number of grooves illuminated. A 50 mm wide grating at 600 lines/mm has N = 30,000 grooves, giving R = 30,000 in first order, resolving lines 0.017 nm apart at 500 nm. This far exceeds prism performance.
