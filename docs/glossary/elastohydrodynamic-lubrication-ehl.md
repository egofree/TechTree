# Elastohydrodynamic lubrication (EHL)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: chemistry

A lubrication regime occurring in rolling contacts (ball bearings, gears) where the lubricant film pressure is high enough to elastically deform the metal surfaces, creating a thin but load-supporting fluid film. EHL is critical for preventing metal-to-metal contact in high-load, high-speed machinery.
