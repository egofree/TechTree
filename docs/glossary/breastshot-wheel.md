# Breastshot wheel

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Breastshot wheel (intermediate — requires moderate head) — - **Principle**: Water enters buckets or paddles at roughly the axle height (breast level) and exits at the bottom. Combines impulse (water hitting buckets) with partial gravity (water weight in buckets as they descend). Requires a headrace delivering water at or near the axle height. - **Efficiency**: ~35-50%.
