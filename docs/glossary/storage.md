# Storage

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals, chemistry, energy, foundations, gas-handling, health, mining, plants, polymers, silicon, textiles

Store flakes in a cool (15-20°C), dry, dark place in sealed containers (tin cans or glass jars with tight lids — do not use plastic bags, which are permeable to moisture). Shelf life: ~6-12 months for bleached shellac, ~2-3 years for orange (unbleached) shellac. Minimize storage temperature — heat accelerates esterification dramatically.
