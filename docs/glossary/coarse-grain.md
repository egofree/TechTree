# Coarse grain

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Coarse grain (2-4 mm) — slower burn, more lifting power — used for mining and quarrying - **Fine grain** (<1 mm): faster burn, more shattering — used for firearms and blasting - **FFFg** (extra fine): fast-burning sporting powder for small arms. See: [Explosives and Propellants](../chemistry/explosives.md)
