# Dissolved oxygen

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: critical | **Domains**: agriculture

Amount of oxygen dissolved in water, critical for fish and aerobic bacteria in aquaponic systems. Must remain above 5 mg/L. Warm water holds less DO (7.5 mg/L at 30°C vs 11.3 mg/L at 10°C). Maintained by continuous aeration with air pump and airstones.
