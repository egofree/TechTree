# Hoof care

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Cattle, sheep, goats, horses, and donkeys all need regular hoof trimming. Cattle on soft pasture may need trimming every 6-12 months; horses on dry ground self-wear hooves, but stabled horses need trimming every 6-8 weeks. Overgrown hooves cause lameness, which reduces feed intake, draft power, and breeding success.
