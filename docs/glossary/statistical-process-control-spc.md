# Statistical process control (SPC)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, photolithography

X̄-R (mean and range) charts track process parameters over time. Sample 3-5 units per batch, measure critical quality attribute, plot average (X̄) and range (R). Upper and lower control limits set at ±3σ from the process mean. A point outside control limits signals a special cause of variation requiring investigation. Western Electric rules detect trends: 7 consecutive points on one side of the center line, or 7 consecutive trending points, also signal loss of statistical control.
