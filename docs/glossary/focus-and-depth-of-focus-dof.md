# Focus and depth of focus (DoF)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

DoF ≈ ±k₂·λ/NA² where k₂ ≈ 0.5-1.0. For i-line (365 nm) at NA 0.45: DoF ≈ ±1.8 μm. Gate-level lithography requires tight focus control — any wafer topography or tilt degrades critical dimension uniformity. Auto-focus systems (air gauge or optical) compensate at each exposure field.
