# Sulfuric acid

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(older process): 5-15% H₂SO₄ at 50-80°C. Slower than HCl, requires heating. Lower fuming (H₂SO₄ has lower vapor pressure). Leaves iron sulfate scale on parts if not thoroughly rinsed. Largely replaced by HCl in continuous strip lines but still used for batch pickling.
