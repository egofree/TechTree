# Chemical handling

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Acetone, IPA, and MEK solvents are highly flammable (flash points well below room temperature). Use only in ventilated solvent benches with spark-free equipment. Store in flammable-materials cabinets; keep fire extinguishers (CO₂ or dry chemical) within 10 m of solvent stations.
