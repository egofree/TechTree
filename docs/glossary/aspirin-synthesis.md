# Aspirin synthesis

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

(Chemistry): Salicylic acid (from willow or Kolbe-Schmitt synthesis: phenol + CO₂ + NaOH at 125°C, 1 MPa → sodium salicylate → acidify) + acetic anhydride (from acetic acid + acetic acid → acetic anhydride) at 85°C for 15 minutes → acetylsalicylic acid + acetic acid. Crystallize from water. Filter, dry. Tablet with starch binder.
