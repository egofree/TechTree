# Heat balance

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Of the coal's calorific value, roughly 80-90% is released as sensible heat in the furnace. Losses include: dry flue gas (10-20% — heated nitrogen and CO₂ leaving the stack), moisture in fuel and air (2-5%), unburned carbon in ash (1-5%), radiation from boiler surfaces (1-3%).
