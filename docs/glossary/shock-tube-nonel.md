# Shock tube (Nonel)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Hollow plastic tube (3 mm OD, 1.5 mm ID) with 0.5-1.0 mg/m of HMX/aluminum powder coated on the inner wall. Initiated by a starter cap or mechanical clicker, the shock propagates at 2,000 ± 50 m/s through the tube (visible as a flash traveling through the translucent plastic). The shock tube output initiates a delay element and detonator at the borehole. Advantages over electric caps: immune to radio frequency, static electricity, and stray currents.
