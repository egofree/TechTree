# Animal fat rendering

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Animal fat rendering is related to [Animals → Animal Materials](../animals/animal-materials.md) for tallow (beef/sheep fat) and lard (pig fat) extraction, clarification, and storage. Rendered fats are the primary cooking fat in many cultures and also serve as fuel for lamps, raw material for soap making, and leather dressing. See: [Agriculture & Food Production](../foundations/food-agriculture.md)
