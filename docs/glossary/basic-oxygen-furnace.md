# Basic oxygen furnace

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

~40-50 kg CaO per tonne of steel. Lime reacts with silica, phosphorus, and sulfur impurities to form a basic slag (calcium silicates, phosphates) that floats on molten steel and is skimmed off. Without lime flux, these impurities remain in the steel and degrade mechanical properties.
