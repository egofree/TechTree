# Always have calcium gluconate gel (2.5 %) immediately available at every HF station.

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Apply to any skin contact immediately, then seek emergency medical care. Wear heavy-duty acid gloves (Neoprene or thick nitrile), face shield, acid apron, and work only in a fume hood. HF burns can be fatal at body surface area exposures as small as 2-5 %.
