# NaOH concentration

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

The dilute caustic solution (10-12% NaOH from the causticizer) is concentrated by evaporation. Multiple-effect vacuum evaporators (typically triple-effect) use steam to boil off water at progressively lower pressures and temperatures (first effect at 100-120°C, third effect at 50-70°C). Energy consumption: 2-3 tonnes steam per tonne of NaOH (as 50% solution).
