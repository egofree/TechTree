# Austenitic stainless steels

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Austenitic stainless steels (200 and 300 series) — - **Structure**: Face-centered cubic (FCC, γ-iron), stabilized by nickel. Non-magnetic, non-hardenable by heat treatment, strengthened only by cold work. - **Type 304** (18Cr-8Ni, the workhorse): ~18% Cr, ~8% Ni, ≤0.08% C. Excellent corrosion resistance in atmospheric and mildly corrosive environments. Used for food processing, chemical vessels, architectural applications.
