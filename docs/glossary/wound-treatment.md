# Wound treatment

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals, health

Osmotic effect draws moisture from bacteria (dehydrating them). Glucose oxidase enzyme produces low levels of hydrogen peroxide (antiseptic). Low pH (3.2-4.5) inhibits microbial growth. Apply medical-grade honey directly to wounds — Manuka-type honey (high methylglyoxal content) is particularly effective.
