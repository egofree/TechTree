# Pasteurellosis (rabbits)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Respiratory disease in rabbits caused by Pasteurella multocida ('snuffles'). Symptoms: white/yellow nasal discharge, sneezing, wet forepaws. Highly contagious. Prevention through good ventilation and avoiding overcrowding. Nearly impossible to eradicate once established — cull affected animals.
