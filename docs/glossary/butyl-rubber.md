# Butyl rubber

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Copolymer of isobutylene (97-99%) with a small amount of isoprene (1-3% to provide unsaturated cross-linking sites). Polymerized at -80 to -100°C in methyl chloride solvent with AlCl₃ catalyst (carbocationic polymerization — the extreme cold is required to prevent chain transfer and achieve high molecular weight, 300,000-500,000 g/mol).
