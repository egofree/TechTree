# Membrane separation

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Hollow fiber polymer membranes (polyimide or polysulfone) — O₂ permeates faster than N₂ due to smaller molecular size and higher solubility. Compressed air (7-12 bar) flows through fiber bores; O₂-rich permeate exits the shell side. No moving parts, modular, compact. N₂ purity: 95-99% (higher purity = lower recovery = higher energy). Typical: 500 Nm³/h N₂ at 97% purity, energy 0.4-1.0 kWh/Nm³. Used for tire inflation, fuel tank inerting, small-scale chemical processing.
