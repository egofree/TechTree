# Tungsten hexafluoride (WF₆)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Source gas for tungsten CVD, used for contact and via filling in semiconductor interconnect. WF₆ is a liquid at room temperature (bp 17°C), stored in stainless steel cylinders. Reduced by silane (SiH₄ + 2WF₆ → 2W + SiF₄ + 2HF + 2F₂, nucleation step) then by hydrogen (WF₆ + 3H₂ → W + 6HF, bulk fill). Deposition temperature 300-400°C. WF₆ is highly corrosive: all wetted parts must be Monel or Inconel. HF byproduct scrubbed in NaOH wet scrubber.
