# Toxicity and addiction

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

Morphine causes respiratory depression at 2-3x therapeutic dose; lethal dose ~120-250 mg (naive adult). **Highly addictive** — regular use produces physical dependence within 2-3 weeks. Withdrawal symptoms: agitation, sweating, nausea, muscle pain, insomnia (peaks at 48-72 hours, resolves over 7-10 days). Strict dosage protocols and limited treatment courses are essential. Reserve for severe pain; do not use chronically except in terminal care.
