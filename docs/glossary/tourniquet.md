# Tourniquet

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Apply only when direct pressure fails (typically for arterial hemorrhage of a limb). Place 5-10 cm above the wound, never over a joint. Tighten until bleeding stops. Note the application time prominently on the patient. Do not remove in the field. Tourniquet ischemia time: safe for 2 hours with minimal risk; increasing muscle damage after 4-6 hours.
