# Narrow therapeutic window

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

Therapeutic dose is 60-80% of the toxic dose. Signs of toxicity: nausea, vomiting, visual disturbances (yellow-green halos, xanthopsia), bradycardia (heart rate below 60 bpm). Lethal dose: ~5 mg digitoxin for an adult (approximately 2-3 g of dried leaf). **This plant demands precise dosing — train practitioners carefully before use.**
