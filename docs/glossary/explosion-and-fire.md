# Explosion and fire

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Black powder is a low explosive (deflagrates rather than detonates), but it is extremely flammable and can transition to detonation under confinement. Friction, impact, spark, static electricity, and heat can all cause ignition. Ground all equipment. Use non-sparking (copper, brass, wood) tools exclusively when handling powder. NEVER grind mixed powder. Keep powder storage separate from manufacturing area — earth embankments between buildings.
