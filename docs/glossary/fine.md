# Fine

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

(<22.5 μm fiber diameter): Merino and similar breeds. Softest, warmest, most crimp (6-12 waves per cm). Used for fine clothing, knitting yarns. Premium price — Merino fleece typically 18-24 μm. Spinning count: 64s-80s (yields very fine yarn).
