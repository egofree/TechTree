# Track worker safety

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Lookouts with flags or horns warn of approaching trains. Track workers must be able to clear the track within the sighting distance minus the stopping distance of the train. At 60 km/h, a 200-tonne freight train needs 300-500 m to stop. On single-track lines, use track warrants (authority to occupy a section of track, issued by dispatcher via telegraph).
