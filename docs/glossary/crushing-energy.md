# Crushing energy

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Crushing energy: 0.5-2 kWh per tonne for primary and secondary crushing combined (jaw crusher → cone crusher to <20 mm). Much less than grinding because breaking large rocks is more energy-efficient than grinding fine particles (fracture mechanics favor breaking along existing flaws, which are abundant in coarse material). See: [Ore Processing](../mining/processing.md)
