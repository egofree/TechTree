# Francis pump-turbine

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

(most common): - A Francis turbine runner operates efficiently in reverse as a centrifugal pump. The same spiral casing, runner, and draft tube serve both modes. Rotation direction reverses between pumping and generating. - **Head range**: 50-700 m. Most pumped-hydro installations operate in the 100-500 m head range. - **Efficiency**: 85-92% as turbine, 80-90% as pump. Round-trip efficiency: 70-85% (product of pump and turbine efficiencies, less hydraulic losses and motor/generator losses). -...
