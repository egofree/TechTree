# Rotary vane vacuum pump

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Mechanical pump with eccentrically mounted rotor and spring-loaded vanes. Achieves 0.01-1 mmHg (ultimate vacuum). Two-stage pumps reach 0.001 mmHg. Pumping speed 1-50 m³/hour. Oil-sealed (hydrocarbon vacuum oil lubricates vanes and seals). Suitable for laboratory and small industrial vacuum distillation. Oil must be changed regularly (contamination by solvent vapors degrades performance). Not suitable for corrosive vapors without cold traps.
