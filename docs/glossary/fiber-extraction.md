# Fiber extraction

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Harvest plant stems (hemp, flax, jute, nettle, dogbane). Ret in water for 1-2 weeks to decompose pectin binding fibers. Beat stems against stone or wood to separate bast fibers from woody core (shives). Comb through coarse teeth to separate and align. The resulting long, aligned fibers are called tow or sliver.
