# Burden distribution control

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

The way raw materials are charged to the top of the furnace critically affects gas flow, reduction efficiency, and smooth operation. Modern furnaces use either: - **Bell-less top (Paul Wurth type)**: A rotating chute in the furnace throat distributes materials in programmed patterns (rings, spirals, sectors).
