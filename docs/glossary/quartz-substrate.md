# Quartz substrate

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Synthetic fused silica, 152×152×6.35 mm (6"×6"×¼"), polished to λ/4 flatness (λ = 633 nm, so ~160 nm peak-to-valley surface deviation). Surface roughness <0.5 nm Ra. The substrate must transmit >90% at the exposure wavelength (i-line 365 nm). Soda-lime glass is NOT suitable because it absorbs below ~350 nm.
