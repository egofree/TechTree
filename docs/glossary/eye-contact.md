# Eye contact

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Flush at eyewash station for 15-30 minutes, holding eyelids open. Acid burns cause immediate pain; alkali burns (NaOH, KOH, ammonia) may feel less painful initially but cause more severe damage because alkalis saponify cell membranes and penetrate deeper. All chemical eye exposures require ophthalmological evaluation after first aid.
