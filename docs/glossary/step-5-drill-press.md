# Step 5: Drill press

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

(tolerance ~0.1 mm for hole location) - **Spindle**: Vertical, driven by belt. Morse taper (MT2) for drill chucks. - **Table**: Adjustable height. Angle plate for angled holes. - **Speed range**: 200-3000 RPM via step pulleys. - **Critical for**: Every subsequent machine and mechanism needs holes.
