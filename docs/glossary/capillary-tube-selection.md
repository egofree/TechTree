# Capillary tube selection

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement

Draw tube from borosilicate glass (see [Glass — Advanced](../glass/advanced.md) for tube production methods). Select a length with uniform bore — test by drawing a short mercury thread through and verifying it moves freely without sticking. Bore must be consistent to within 5% or scale readings will be non-linear. Reject tubes with visible constrictions or diameter variations.
