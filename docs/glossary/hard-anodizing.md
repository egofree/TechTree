# Hard anodizing

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Hard anodizing (Type III) — - Electrolyte: 150-250 g/L H₂SO₄, -1 to +5°C (refrigerated bath), 25-75 V DC. Current density: 20-40 mA/cm². Time: 30-120 minutes. Low temperature suppresses acid dissolution of the oxide, allowing thicker buildup. - Coating: 25-100 µm of dense Al₂O₃. Hardness: 400-600 HV (some sources report up to 700 HV on select alloys).
