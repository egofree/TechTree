# Heating value

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

~16-18 MJ/m³ (richer than producer gas at 5-6 MJ/m³, comparable to natural gas at 35-40 MJ/m³). Sufficient to heat the coke ovens themselves (self-sustaining process) and supply surplus for steelworks heating, boiler fuel, and town gas.
