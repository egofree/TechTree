# Agglomeration phase

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Agglomeration phase (first 6-8 hours) — fine seed particles agglomerate into larger clusters. **Growth phase** (20-40 hours): Al(OH)₃ crystallizes on agglomerate surfaces. Total precipitation time: 30-60 hours. See: [Aluminum Production](../metals/aluminum.md)
