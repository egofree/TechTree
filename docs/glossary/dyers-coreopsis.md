# Dyer's coreopsis

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Dyer's coreopsis (*Coreopsis tinctoria*) — annual, easy from seed. 60-90 cm tall, covered with red-orange-yellow flowers. Harvest flowers as they open. Rich in flavonoid dyes (luteolin, apigenin, coreopsin). Self-seeds prolifically. See: [Dyeing](../textiles/dyeing.md)
