# Rod detail

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: computing

Each of the ten rods (digits 0-9) is divided into 9 cells. Each cell shows the product of the rod's digit × the row number (1-9), with tens digit above a diagonal slash and units digit below. Rod 7, row 8: cell reads "5/6" (56, with 5 above and 6 below the diagonal).
