# 1× masks (contact/proximity printing)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Pattern generated at 1:1 scale. Photo-repeater: a precision X-Y stage positions a small aperture over the mask blank; a single pattern feature is exposed, then the stage steps to the next position. Repeats the same pattern across the entire mask. Alignment marks exposed at the same time. Resolution limited to ~2-3 μm due to 1:1 imaging.
