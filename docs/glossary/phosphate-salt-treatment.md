# Phosphate salt treatment

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Phosphate salt treatment (more durable) — Diammonium phosphate (DAP, 10-15% solution) applied by padding (immersion and squeeze rollers). The phosphate promotes char formation rather than flaming. Char acts as an insulating barrier that slows heat transfer to the underlying fabric. - DAP treatment is durable through 10-15 washes. It does not affect fabric hand (feel) significantly.
