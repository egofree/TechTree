# Breath sounds

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Vesicular (soft, rustling) normal over peripheral lung fields. Bronchial (harsh, tubular) over consolidated lung (pneumonia) — abnormal when heard in the periphery. Wheezing (high-pitched musical sounds) in asthma, bronchitis. Crackles/rales (discrete popping sounds) in pneumonia, pulmonary edema, fibrosis. Absent breath sounds in pleural effusion or pneumothorax.
