# Surface course (wearing surface)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Surface course (wearing surface) refers to 50 mm compacted depth of 20 mm nominal stone. Fine aggregate (stone dust, 0-5 mm) spread on top and broomed into surface voids. Water-bound: hose surface, roll until water runs clear (fines wash into voids and lock the surface). Alternatively, tar-bound: spray hot coal tar (60-80°C) at 1.5-2.0 liters/m², spread stone chips, roll. See: [Road & Bridge Construction](../transport/roads.md)
