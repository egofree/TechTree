# Worm castings

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: critical | **Domains**: agriculture

Vermicompost produced by earthworms — dark, crumbly, nutrient-rich soil amendment. Contains 1.5-2.5% N, 1.0-1.5% P, 1.0-1.5% K in plant-available form, plus humic acids and plant growth hormones not found in thermal compost. Superior to thermophilic compost in microbial diversity.
