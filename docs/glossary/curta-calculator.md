> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: computing

Crank handle on top performs addition. Multiplication by repeated cranking with carriage shift. Subtraction via complement method with reversed crank direction (crank folds out for subtraction). - **Capacity**: 11-digit input, 15-digit result counter, 8-digit revolution counter.
