# Toxic dopant gases (AsH₃, PH₃, BF₃)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: vlsi-scaling

Arsine and phosphine are immediately dangerous to life or health (IDLH) at concentrations of only a few ppm — they cause hemolysis (AsH₃) or respiratory failure (PH₃) with rapid onset. Store cylinders in ventilated, gas-cabinet enclosures with continuous toxic-gas monitoring (electrochemical sensors, alarm at 0.5× PEL). Automatic cylinder shutoff valves on alarm.
