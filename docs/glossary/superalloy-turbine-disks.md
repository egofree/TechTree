# Superalloy turbine disks

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

HIP investment-cast Ni-base superalloy (IN718, René 88DT) disks at 1160-1220°C, 150 MPa for 4 hours. Eliminates casting microporosity (reduces pore volume from 0.5-2% to <0.01%), improving fatigue life by 3-10×. Cost savings: HIP allows conventional casting to replace directionally solidified or single-crystal casting for components that don't need the grain structure control.
