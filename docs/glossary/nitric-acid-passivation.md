# Nitric acid passivation

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(traditional, ASTM A967): - Immersion in 20-45% HNO₃ at 20-50°C for 15-60 minutes depending on grade. Nitric acid is an oxidizing acid — it dissolves free iron and iron oxides while passivating the chromium-rich surface. Rinse thoroughly in clean water. - Type-specific treatments: Austenitic grades (304, 316) respond well to 20-25% HNO₃ at 20-40°C.
