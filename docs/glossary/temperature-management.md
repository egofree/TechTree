# Temperature management

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Billet enters at 1050-1150°C. Deformation heating compensates for radiation losses. Finishing temperature: 850-1000°C. Controlled cooling on the conveyor after the last stand: Stelmor line — rod lays in overlapping loops on a moving conveyor while fans blow air at 0.5-3.0 m/s. Cooling rate controls microstructure: slow cooling for coarse pearlite (high ductility for drawing), faster cooling for fine pearlite or bainite (higher strength).
