# ClO₂ generation

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Sodium chlorate (NaClO₃) reduced by methanol in sulfuric acid medium: 6NaClO₃ + CH₃OH + 6H₂SO₄ → 6ClO₂ + 6NaHSO₄ + CO₂ + 5H₂O. ClO₂ absorbed in cold water (7-10 g/L) and stored/transported as chilled solution. On-site generation mandatory — ClO₂ cannot be stored or transported as gas (explosive at >10 kPa partial pressure). Alternative generation: electrolytic (NaCl → Cl₂ → ClO₂), or hydrogen peroxide reduction of NaClO₃.
