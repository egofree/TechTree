# Pressure regulator use

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Always use the correct regulator for the gas type (CGA connection matching). Never force a regulator onto a mismatched fitting. Open cylinder valve slowly — sudden pressurization can damage the regulator diaphragm or cause adiabatic compression heating (fire hazard with oxygen). Stand to the side of the regulator when opening the cylinder valve, not in front of the gauge face (glasses can blow out). Never use oil or grease on oxygen fittings — spontaneous ignition.
