# Laser slicing

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

(advanced, later stage): - **Principle**: High-power laser (typically Nd:YAG, 1064 nm) creates a stress crack along a defined plane in the ingot. Subsequent mechanical stress separates the wafer along the crack. No abrasive contact, so surface damage is minimal. - **Status**: Still in development for mainstream silicon wafering.
