# Crucible life

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

100-300 hours of cumulative hot time before the thinned wall cracks under thermal stress or the weight of a fresh charge. Some operations track crucible hours and retire them on a schedule. A crucible failure during a pull is catastrophic: molten silicon spills onto the susceptor and heater, destroying the hot zone and requiring a full rebuild (days of downtime).
