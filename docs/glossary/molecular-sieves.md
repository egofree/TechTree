# Molecular sieves

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Synthetic zeolite beads (3Å, 4Å, 5Å, 13X pore sizes). Adsorb molecules smaller than pore diameter. 3Å removes water. 4Å removes H₂O, CO₂, H₂S. 13X removes larger molecules. Regenerate by heating to 200-300°C under vacuum or dry gas purge. Use in pairs — one adsorbing, one regenerating (twin-tower system).
