# Steam ejectors

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Multi-stage steam ejector systems achieve 0.1-10 mmHg for large industrial vacuum columns. First-stage ejector uses 0.7-1.0 MPa steam, pulls vacuum to ~100 mmHg. Second stage pulls to ~10 mmHg. Third and fourth stages pull to 1-5 mmHg. Inter-stage condensers (water-cooled) remove condensed steam and vapors, reducing the load on subsequent stages.
