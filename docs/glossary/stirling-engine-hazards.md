# Stirling engine hazards

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Pressurized working gas (up to 20 MPa in high-performance engines with helium/hydrogen). Vessel failure releases high-pressure gas explosively. Pressure relief valves mandatory on all sealed Stirling systems. Hydrogen working fluid is flammable and permeates many seal materials — detect and eliminate leaks before pressurizing.
