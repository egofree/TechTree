# Falling film evaporator

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Modern evaporators use falling film design. Black liquor is distributed over the top of vertical tube bundles (or plate packs) and flows downward as a thin film while steam condenses on the opposite side of the tubes. Heat transfer coefficient: 800-2000 W/m²·K for dilute liquor, decreasing to 300-800 W/m²·K for concentrated liquor (viscosity increases dramatically above 50% solids). Heat transfer area: 5,000-15,000 m² total for a large mill.
