# Vacuum bag improvements

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Applying vacuum (80-95 kPa) over the hand layup compresses the laminate, squeezing out excess resin and increasing fiber content from 25-35% to 40-55% by weight. The resulting laminate has 30-50% higher flexural strength and 20-30% lower weight than a hand layup of the same thickness. The vacuum also eliminates voids (trapped air) that concentrate stress and initiate cracks.
