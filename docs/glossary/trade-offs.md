# Trade-offs

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: vlsi-scaling

Extremely slow for thick films (100 nm = ~1000 cycles = ~1 hour). Unmatched for ultra-thin (1-20 nm), conformal coatings on high-aspect-ratio structures. Used where CVD cannot reach: inside deep trenches, around nanowire gates, as gate spacers.
