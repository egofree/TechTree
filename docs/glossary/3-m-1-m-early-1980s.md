# 3 μm → 1 μm (early 1980s)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: vlsi-scaling

Step-and-repeat reduction projection (5:1) replaces 1:1 projection. A single small, high-quality reticle is stepped across the wafer — far cheaper than full-wafer masks and allows much better resolution. Requires precision wafer stage with interferometric position feedback (~0.1 μm accuracy). Dry (RIE) etching replaces wet etching for anisotropic profiles — critical at 1 μm where undercut becomes unacceptable. I-line (365 nm) sources introduced.
