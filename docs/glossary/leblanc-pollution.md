# Leblanc pollution

> **Type**: process | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

The Leblanc process was notorious. CaS waste (4 tonnes per tonne soda ash) released H₂S gas. HCl emissions killed vegetation around plants. The Alkali Act of 1863 (UK) forced producers to absorb HCl in water (creating a market for hydrochloric acid). This was the first modern environmental legislation.
