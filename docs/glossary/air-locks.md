# Air locks

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

two doors in series, 5-10 m apart. Only one door is open at a time (person enters the lock, closes the first door, then opens the second). Air locks reduce leakage to near zero and are required at high-pressure barriers (between intake and return near the fan). The lock chamber is sized to accommodate a mine crew (2-3 m wide, 3-5 m long).
