# Engine design

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Engine design (single-cylinder or twin-cylinder, air-cooled) — - **Configuration**: Horizontally opposed twin (boxer) — compact, good balance, air cooling. Displacement 500-1200 cc. Power target 20-65 HP at 2000-3500 RPM. Weight target <30 kg (power-to-weight >0.5 HP/kg). - **Bore and stroke**: Bore 80-100 mm, stroke 70-90 mm. Undersquare (stroke < bore) favors higher RPM. Compression ratio 6:1 to 8:1 (limited by fuel octane — see fuel section). See: [Aviation](../transport/aviation.md)
