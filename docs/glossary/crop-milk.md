# Crop milk

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: critical | **Domains**: animals

Protein-rich secretion from the crop lining of both male and female pigeons, fed to squab for the first 14-18 days. Composition: 60% protein, 32-36% fat by dry weight — richer than mammal milk. Enables squab to double hatch weight in 2 days. Unique among domestic poultry.
