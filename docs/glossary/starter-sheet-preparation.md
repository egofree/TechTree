# Starter sheet preparation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Thin copper sheets (~1 mm thick) serve as cathode starter sheets. Produced by stripping the deposited copper from titanium or stainless steel blank cathodes after a short (12-24 hour) plating cycle, or by rolling strip stock to gauge. Starter sheet dimensions match anode dimensions. Mechanically straightened and masked at the top edge to prevent edge growth bridging to adjacent anodes.
