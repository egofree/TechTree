# Borosilicate glass

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Borosilicate glass (Pyrex-type — thermal shock resistant) — - **Composition**: 70-80% SiO₂, 7-13% B₂O₃ (boron trioxide from borax or boric acid), 4-8% Na₂O, 2-7% Al₂O₃. Thermal expansion coefficient ~3×10⁻⁶/°C (vs. soda-lime glass ~9×10⁻⁶/°C — 3x more resistant to thermal shock). - **Boron sources**: Borax (Na₂B₄O₇·10H₂O) from dry lake deposits (Turkey, California, Tibet).
