# Baking soda (NaHCO₃) via Solvay intermediates

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: chemistry

Production of sodium bicarbonate by carbonating ammoniated brine intermediate from the Solvay process, precipitating NaHCO₃ at 35-40°C. Also producible by passing CO₂ through a solution of sodium carbonate (trona ash).
