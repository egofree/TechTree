# Mercury lamp UV emission

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement

Mercury spectral lamps emit intense ultraviolet radiation (253.7 nm and 365 nm lines). Direct exposure causes photokeratitis (welder's flash) and skin erythema. Enclose lamps in housing with UV-absorbing glass window. Never view the lamp directly without eye protection. The 253.7 nm line causes ozone formation in air, requiring ventilation in enclosed spaces.
