# Filler

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Fumed silica (10-40 phr, 7-40 nm SiO₂ particles produced by flame hydrolysis of SiCl₄ — the same process used to produce high-purity silica for optical fiber preforms) is essential for mechanical reinforcement — unfilled PDMS has tensile strength <0.5 MPa (essentially a weak, sticky gum); filled reaches 6-10 MPa.
