# Chlorine (Cl₂)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: chemistry

Chlorine (Cl₂), a toxic greenish-yellow gas (boiling point −34°C) used as a bleach, disinfectant, and chemical reagent. Produced industrially by electrolysis of brine; essential for water treatment, PVC production, and as an etching gas in semiconductor manufacturing.
