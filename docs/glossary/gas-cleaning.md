# Gas cleaning

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Raw top gas contains 10-40 g/Nm³ of dust (fine ore particles, coke fines, soot). Before the gas can be burned, it must be cleaned: 1. **Dust catcher** (primary): Large cyclonic vessel where the gas velocity drops and coarse dust settles out by gravity and centrifugal force. Removes ~60-80% of the dust.
