# Infiltration load

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Warm air entering through door openings. Each air change introduces warm, humid air that must be cooled and dehumidified. Estimate: 1-2 air changes per hour for rooms with moderate door traffic. The enthalpy difference between outside and inside air determines the load. At 30°C ambient and -18°C room temperature, each kilogram of infiltrated air carries roughly 60-80 kJ of heat that the refrigeration system must remove.
