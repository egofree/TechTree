# Energy accounting for the silicon production chain

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: silicon

Quantitative analysis of cumulative energy consumption across all silicon production steps from quartz mining through metallurgical-grade silicon, purification, crystal growth, and wafering. Total energy from mine to finished wafer is approximately 1000–2000 kWh/kg, making silicon one of the most energy-intensive manufactured materials.
