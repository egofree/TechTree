# Clay/shale extraction

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Clay provides silica (SiO₂), alumina (Al₂O₃), and iron oxide (Fe₂O₃). Dug from nearby clay pits, crushed to <50 mm. Alternative raw materials: bauxite (alumina source), iron ore (Fe₂O₃), sand (silica) — added to adjust chemistry.
