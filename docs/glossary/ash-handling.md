# Ash handling

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

All coal combustion produces ash (5-20% of fuel weight). Bottom ash falls through the grate into an ash pit — remove regularly to prevent airflow blockage. Fly ash (fine particles entrained in flue gas) must be captured with cyclone separators (60-80% capture) or electrostatic precipitators (99%+ capture, requires electricity). Fly ash is usable as pozzolanic additive in cement, replacing 15-30% of Portland cement.
