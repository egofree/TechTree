# Infusion (tea)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

Pour boiling water over dried or fresh plant material (typically leaves, flowers). Steep 10-15 minutes. Strain through cloth. Extracts water-soluble compounds: tannins, glycosides, some alkaloids. Use within 24 hours — water extracts spoil rapidly. Ratio: 1-2 g dried herb per 150 mL water.
