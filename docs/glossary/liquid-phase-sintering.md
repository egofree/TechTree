# Liquid-phase sintering

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

The critical step. Heat to 1350-1450°C (above the W-C-Co ternary eutectic at ~1280°C). Cobalt melts and flows by capillary action through the WC particle network, wetting WC particles (contact angle <5°).
