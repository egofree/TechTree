# Powder blend

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Water-atomized iron powder (ASC 100.29, Hoeganäs or equivalent) + graphite powder (0.3-1.0% C) + die lubricant (0.5-0.8% zinc stearate or amide wax). Graphite provides carbon for steel matrix formation during sintering; lubricant reduces ejection force and tool wear during compaction.
