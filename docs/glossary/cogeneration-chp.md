# Cogeneration (CHP)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Combined heat and power. Gas engine-generator produces electricity (25-30% electrical efficiency). Engine jacket cooling water and exhaust heat recovered for space heating, drying, or process heat. Overall CHP efficiency: 70-85%. Small-scale biomass CHP (50-500 kWe) is economically attractive in locations with reliable biomass supply and high electricity prices. District heating systems in Scandinavian communities commonly use biomass gasification CHP.
