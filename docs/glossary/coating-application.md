# Coating application

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Reverse roll coater applies paint to the top side (precision metering: wet film thickness controlled to ±2 μm). Bottom side coated simultaneously by a second roll coater. Typical coil coat film: primer 5-8 μm dry + topcoat 15-25 μm dry on the exterior side, backer 5-10 μm on the reverse.
