# Double jack drilling

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

(two people, one sledge): - One person holds and rotates the drill steel. A second swings a 4-6 kg sledge hammer. Advance rate roughly doubles to 4-6 m/day in hard rock. - The holder faces away from the striker and times rotation to the striker's rhythm. Trust and communication are critical. The holder signals readiness by tapping the drill against the rock face between strikes.
