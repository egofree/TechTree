# Lead glaze

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

Lead glaze (⚠️ TOXIC) — Composition**: 50-80% lead oxide (PbO, from galena — lead sulfide mineral, roasted to oxide) mixed with silica sand and clay. Melts at 700-900°C — very low temperature, easy to fire. - **Produces**: Brilliant, glossy, transparent glaze. Bright colors with added copper (green) or iron (amber/brown).
