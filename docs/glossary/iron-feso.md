# Iron (FeSO₄)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Darkens and "saddens" colors at 2-5% owf. Iron shifts yellow dyes to olive-green, shifts blue dyes to gray-blue, and combined with tannin produces gray-black. Iron forms colored complexes with dye molecules (iron-gall black from tannin + iron is the classic ink formula). Warning: iron catalyzes oxidative degradation of protein fibers.
