# Spacing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics, chemistry

Leave 1-2 cm between pieces for hot gas circulation. Pieces placed too close together shield each other from heat, producing underfired spots. Stack bowls and cups using bisque wads (small balls of fired clay, 5-8 mm) at 3 points to separate nesting pieces. Wads leave small scars on the glaze — place them where the mark will not show.
