# Density rules

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: vlsi-scaling

Minimum and maximum metal density per unit area (typically checked in 100 μm × 100 μm windows). CMP removes material non-uniformly — large empty areas dish (polish too deep), large dense areas mound (polish too little). Density must be 20-80% (or similar range). Dummy fill patterns (non-functional metal shapes) are added automatically to meet density rules.
