# Equipment

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, machine-tools, metals, polymers, silicon

Pressure vessel: forged steel or wire-wound vessel, 300-1500 mm bore diameter, rated to 200 MPa. Heating elements and insulation fit inside the pressure vessel. Argon gas supply: gas compressor or pump to 200 MPa. Capital cost: $2-10 million depending on vessel size.
