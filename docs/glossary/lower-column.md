# Lower column

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

(at 5-6 atm): Air enters at the bottom. At this pressure, N₂ boils at ~-177°C and O₂ boils at a higher temperature. Nitrogen vapor rises to the top and condenses against the colder upper-column liquid. Liquid nitrogen (LN₂) accumulates at the top; oxygen-enriched liquid (~35-40% O₂, called "rich liquid") collects at the bottom.
