# Environmental disposal

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Used oil and grease must not be dumped on ground or in waterways. Collect in sealed containers. Re-refine mineral oils (vacuum distillation removes contaminants, additives are replenished). Animal and vegetable oils can be composted in small quantities. Used cutting fluid emulsions require treatment (break emulsion with acid or flocculant, separate oil phase, treat water phase before discharge).
