# Double-weave

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Two separate cloth layers woven simultaneously on the same loom, connected at one or both edges to form a tube, or at intervals to create pockets. Uses twice as many shafts as a single layer. Produces: tube fabric (bags, sleeves, belts), double-width cloth (wider than the loom's nominal weaving width), and double-thickness cloth for heavy applications. 8+ shafts for basic double-weave.
