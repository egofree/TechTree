# Bell jar

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Quartz (fused silica) or stainless steel cylindrical vessel, 500-1000 mm diameter, 1-2 m tall. Quartz is preferred for purity (no metal contamination) but is fragile and expensive. The jar must withstand atmospheric pressure on the outside when evacuated, then contain inert gas at slightly above atmospheric pressure during deposition.
