# Drive band

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Replace the drive band (cord or leather belt connecting wheel to spindle/flyer) when it stretches, slips, or frays. A cotton or linen cord lasts 100-200 hours; a leather band lasts 500+ hours. Adjust tension by moving the wheel slightly relative to the spindle assembly, or by using a tensioning device on the band itself.
