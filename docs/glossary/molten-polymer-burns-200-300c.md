# Molten polymer burns (200-300°C)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Extruded, injected, or compressed thermoplastics at processing temperatures stick to skin on contact and cannot be wiped off — wiping spreads the burn over a larger area. If molten polymer contacts skin, cool immediately under running water for at least 15 minutes. Do not attempt to peel or wipe off.
