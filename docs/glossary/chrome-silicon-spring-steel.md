# Chrome-silicon spring steel

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(0.55% C, 0.7% Cr, 1.5% Si, e.g., AISI 9254): Silicon raises the elastic limit and improves temper resistance. Oil-quenched from 860-900°C, tempered at 400-450°C. Used for high-stress valve springs, clutch springs. Silicon also increases resistance to stress relaxation at elevated temperatures.
