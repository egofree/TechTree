# Acetic acid production

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Acetic acid production (CH₃COOH) — - **Vinegar method**: Expose ethanol solution to air with Acetobacter bacteria (present on fruit surfaces). Aerobic fermentation at 25-30°C for days-weeks. Produces 5-12% acetic acid. Slow but simple. - **Chemical oxidation**: Pass ethanol vapor over heated copper catalyst (copper gauze at 300-400°C) with air.
