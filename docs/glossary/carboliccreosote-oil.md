# Carbolic/creosote oil

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

(170-230°C): ~10% of tar. Contains phenol (C₆H₅OH), cresols (methylphenols). Phenol extracted with NaOH solution → sodium phenolate → re-precipitate with CO₂ or H₂SO₄. Phenol critical for Bakelite resin, pharmaceuticals, disinfectants.
