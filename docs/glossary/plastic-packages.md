# Plastic packages

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Over 95% of ICs use plastic (epoxy molding compound) packages. DIP (dual in-line package): 8-40 leads, 2.54 mm lead pitch, through-hole mounting. SOP (small outline package): 8-28 leads, 1.27 mm pitch, surface mount. QFP (quad flat package): 44-240 leads on all four sides, 0.5-0.8 mm pitch. QFN (quad flat no-lead): compact, leads on all four sides but flush with package body, 0.4-0.65 mm pitch. BGA (ball grid array): solder balls on the package bottom, 0.5-1.27 mm pitch, 100-2000+ connections...
