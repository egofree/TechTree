# Passive climate control

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Thick masonry walls (≥400 mm stone or brick) provide thermal mass that damps daily temperature fluctuations. Earth-sheltered or bermed construction leverages stable ground temperature (~10-15°C year-round at 2 m depth). Ventilation: stack effect (low intake vents, high exhaust vents) provides air circulation without powered fans.
