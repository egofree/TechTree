# Slaked lime (Ca(OH)₂)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

CaO + H₂O → Ca(OH)₂. Highly exothermic (65 kJ/mol). Add quicklime to water in a slaking tank — never water to quicklime. The resulting lime putty or milk of lime (slurry) is used directly in Solvay ammonia recovery, mortar making, water treatment, and causticization.
