# Acetone and isopropanol

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: optics

Both solvents are flammable (acetone flash point -20°C, isopropanol 12°C). Use in well-ventilated areas away from ignition sources. Acetone defats skin and causes cracking with repeated contact. Isopropanol is less aggressive but still drying. Wear nitrile gloves for prolonged solvent use. Store in flammable-liquid cabinets with grounding to prevent static ignition.
