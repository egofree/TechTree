# Signet rings

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Signet rings (later development) — Seal engraved on a metal ring — always available, cannot be misplaced. Used with wax seals on letters and documents. The impression in wax authenticates the document's origin and proves it has not been opened (broken wax = tampered document). See: [Writing & Record-Keeping](../knowledge/writing.md)
