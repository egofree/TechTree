# Natural ventilation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Natural ventilation (single shaft) — Single shaft with partition (brattice — wooden or canvas wall dividing shaft into upcast and downcast). Warm air (heated by mine lights and geothermal) rises in one side, cool air descends in other. Limited to ~100 m depth. Seasonal — reverses in winter. See: [Mining Extraction](../mining/extraction.md)
