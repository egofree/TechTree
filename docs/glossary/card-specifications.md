# Card specifications

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: computing

187.3 × 83.3 mm, 0.18 mm card stock. 12 rows × 80 columns = 960 punch positions per card. Rows numbered 12, 11, 0, 1-9 (top to bottom). Numeric encoding: one punch per column in rows 0-9. Alphabetic: zone punch (row 12, 11, or 0) plus one digit punch. Special characters: two or three punches per column. Maximum information density: ~960 bits per card, though typical data encoding uses fewer.
