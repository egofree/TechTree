# Smelting

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Flash smelting (Outokumpu process) or electric furnace smelting. Partial oxidation of sulfides provides heat (exothermic) and concentrates nickel in matte (Ni-Cu-Fe sulfides). Converter blowing removes iron and sulfur, producing Bessemer matte (~75% Ni+Cu). Slow cooling and flotation separate Cu₂S from Ni₃S₂.
