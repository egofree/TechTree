# Thrust

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

T = (η × P) / v, where η = propeller efficiency (0.6-0.8 for well-designed prop), P = engine power (W), v = forward speed (m/s). At 40 HP (30 kW) and 25 m/s (90 km/h): T = 0.7 × 30000 / 25 = 840 N (~86 kgf thrust). This accelerates aircraft to cruise speed and maintains flight.
