# Common defects

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Orange peel (poor flow/leveling, caused by insufficient solvent or too-rapid drying). Runs and sags (excessive wet film thickness or too-slow drying). Pinholing (air entrapment during application, or solvent boil from too-rapid topcoat over primer). Cratering/fisheyes (surface contamination with silicone or oil causing the wet paint to de-wet). Dry spray (overspray landing as dry particles, caused by spray gun too far from surface or excessive solvent evaporation in hot weather).
