# Cylinder method (broad glass)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Blow a large cylinder (2-3 m long, 15-25 cm diameter). Score lengthwise with a diamond or steel wheel, reheat, and flatten on a hot iron table. Produces larger, more uniform panes than the crown method. Common in the 18th and 19th centuries before float glass.
