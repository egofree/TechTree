# Alpha ionizers

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Polonium-210 (Po-210) radioactive sources emit alpha particles that ionize air molecules, creating equal numbers of positive and negative ions. The ions neutralize static charges on wafer surfaces and equipment. No power required. Limited lifetime (Po-210 half-life = 138 days; replace every 12-18 months). Regulatory issues with radioactive materials in some jurisdictions.
