# Titanium dioxide (TiO₂)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

The most important white pigment — extreme opacity (refractive index 2.7, vs 1.5 for most binders) from light scattering. Rutile form preferred (higher RI than anatase). Global production: 7 million tonnes/year. Used in virtually every white and light-colored paint. Produced by chloride process (TiCl₄ oxidation at 1000°C) or sulfate process (ilmenite digestion with H₂SO₄).
