# Quicklime (CaO)

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics, chemistry

Store in dry, airtight containers. Quicklime is hygroscopic — absorbs moisture and CO₂ from air, gradually reverting to calcium carbonate (air-slaking). Even 5% air-slaking noticeably reduces quality. Sealed barrels or covered bins in a dry building.
