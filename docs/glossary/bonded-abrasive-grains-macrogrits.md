# Bonded abrasive grains (macrogrits)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

FEPA F-series, F8 through F220. These designate the coarse particles used in grinding wheels and sharpening stones. The grit number corresponds to the mesh size of the sieve through which the particles pass: F60 ≈ 250 μm median particle diameter, F80 ≈ 180 μm, F120 ≈ 125 μm, F150 ≈ 100 μm, F180 ≈ 75 μm, F220 ≈ 63 μm.
