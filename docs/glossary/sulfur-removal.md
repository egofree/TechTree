# Sulfur removal

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Natural gas feedstock contains H₂S and organic sulfur compounds (mercaptans, thiophenes) which permanently poison the Ni reforming catalyst. Desulfurization: pass gas over ZnO absorbent at 350-400°C (H₂S + ZnO → ZnS + H₂O) after hydrogenation of organic sulfur over Co-Mo catalyst.
