# Dam basics

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

(creating the head and pond): - **Earth dam** (simplest): Compacted clay core with earth and stone shoulders. Height 2-5 m, crest width 2-3 m, upstream slope 1:2 (horizontal:vertical), downstream slope 1:2.5. Puddle clay core (0.5-1 m thick) keyed into natural subsoil to prevent seepage. Overflow spillway on one side to pass flood water safely. - **Timber crib dam**: Log cribs (rectangular timber frames) filled with stone, stacked and pinned.
