# Bakelite (1907)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Leo Baekeland's phenol-formaldehyde thermoset — the first fully synthetic plastic (no natural polymer component). Key innovation: the "pressure plus heat" cure in a closed mold controlled the volatile byproducts (water and formaldehyde vapor) and produced dense, void-free moldings with excellent surface finish.
