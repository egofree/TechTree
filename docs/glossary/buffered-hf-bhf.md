# Buffered HF (BHF)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

NH₄F:HF 7:1 mixture. Etch rate 70-100 nm/min for thermal SiO₂. The ammonium fluoride buffers the HF concentration, maintaining a stable etch rate over time. Selectivity to photoresist ~5:1. Selectivity to silicon >100:1 (HF does not attack crystalline silicon). Temperature: room temperature (20-25°C). Used for: contact hole opens, gate oxide removal, general oxide patterning.
