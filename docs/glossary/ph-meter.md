# pH meter

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Glass electrode with Ag/AgCl reference. Calibration with two buffer solutions (pH 4.00 and 7.00 at 25°C, or pH 7.00 and 10.00 for alkaline measurements) before each use. Slope must be 95-105%. Electrode stored in 3M KCl solution when not in use. Electrode life: 6-24 months depending on usage and sample matrix (strongly acidic or alkaline samples shorten electrode life).
