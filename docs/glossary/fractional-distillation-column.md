# Fractional distillation column

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Fractional distillation column (continuous) — - **Construction**: Vertical column 5-20 m tall, 0.3-1.5 m diameter. Iron or steel construction. Internal trays (bubble-cap or sieve trays) every 0.3-0.6 m — each tray is a theoretical separation stage. 15-40 trays typical. Reboiler at bottom (steam-heated or oil-fired). Condenser at top (water-cooled). - **Operation**: Preheated crude oil fed into column at midpoint (~tray 10-15 of 30). Hot vapors rise through trays. Each tray holds a liquid layer.
