# Double-base

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Double-base (nitrocellulose + nitroglycerin) — nitroglycerin plasticizes nitrocellulose, increasing energy content. Burn rate controlled by grain geometry (perforations, flake thickness). Used for artillery and rifle ammunition. See: [Explosives and Propellants](../chemistry/explosives.md)
