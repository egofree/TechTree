# Varieties by climate

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

Upland cotton (*G. hirsutum*) — temperate/subtropical, 90% of world production; Pima/Egyptian (*G. barbadense*) — hot arid, extra-long staple (33-38 mm); Tree cotton (*G. arboreum*) — tropical, drought-tolerant, short staple.
