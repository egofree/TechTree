# Hot work precautions

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Before welding, cutting, or brazing on or near a vessel or piping system that contained flammable gas, purge the system with nitrogen until combustible gas concentration is below 1% LEL AND oxygen is below 1%. Both conditions must be met. Use a combustible gas detector to verify. Continuous monitoring during hot work is required, with a fire watch posted for 30 minutes after work completion.
