# Ceramic saddles

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Ceramic saddles (Intalox, Berl) — saddle-shaped elements. Better liquid distribution than rings because shape prevents nesting and channeling. HETP: 0.3-0.5 m. Used where ceramic resistance to corrosion is needed (acid distillation). See: [Distillation](../chemistry/distillation.md)
