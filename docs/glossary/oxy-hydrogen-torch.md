# Oxy-hydrogen torch

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

(~2800°C): - Hydrogen and oxygen burned at torch tip. Very hot, very clean (only combustion product is water vapor). Transparent blue flame. - Required for fused silica / quartz glass (softening point ~1600°C). Also used for glass-to-glass seals on high-silica glasses. - Hydrogen explosion risk — leak-check all connections before each use. Flashback arrestors required on both gas lines.
