# Alkyd enamel

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

(traditional gloss paint): Alkyd resin (70% solids in mineral spirits) 65%, TiO₂ 25%, driers (Co/Mn/Zr naphthenates) 1%, mineral spirits 9%. Dry film thickness: 30-50 µm. Drying: touch dry 4-6 hours, hard dry 12-18 hours.
