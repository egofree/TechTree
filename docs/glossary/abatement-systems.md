# Abatement systems

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Two options for silane exhaust treatment. Burn box: oxidizing furnace at 800-1000°C converts SiH₄ to SiO₂ fume and H₂O, which are then filtered. Used for continuous vent streams. Wet scrubber: 5-20% NaOH solution in a packed-column scrubber absorbs silane and any chlorosilane by-products, converting them to sodium silicate and sodium chloride. Used for batch venting and emergency relief. Most installations use both in series.
