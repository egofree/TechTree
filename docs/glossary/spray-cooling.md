# Spray cooling

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Secondary cooling zone below the mold. Water sprays (and air-mist sprays for uniform cooling) extract heat to complete solidification. Cooling intensity: 0.5-2.5 L water per kg steel. Spray pattern must be uniform — uneven cooling causes thermal stress, transverse cracking (particularly at the straightening point where the strand is bent from curved to straight), and surface defects.
