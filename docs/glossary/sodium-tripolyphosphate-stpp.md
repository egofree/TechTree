# Sodium tripolyphosphate (STPP)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Most effective builder. Sequesters Ca²⁺ and Mg²⁺, disperses soil particles, provides alkalinity. Produced from phosphoric acid + NaOH. Environmental concern: phosphate discharged to waterways causes algal blooms and eutrophication. Phosphates banned in laundry detergents in many jurisdictions.
