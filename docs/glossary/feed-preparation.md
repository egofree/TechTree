# Feed preparation

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Saturated NaCl brine (25-28% NaCl, 310-340 g/L) must be ultra-purified before entering the membrane cell. Calcium and magnesium ions (even at ppm levels) irreversibly damage the Nafion membrane by forming insoluble hydroxides within the membrane structure.
