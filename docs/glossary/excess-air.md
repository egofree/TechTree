# Excess air

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Practical combustion requires 20-60% excess air above stoichiometric to ensure complete burn. Hand-fired grates: 40-60% excess air. Pulverized coal: 15-25% excess air. Too much excess air wastes heat (heating nitrogen that goes up the stack). Too little produces CO and unburned carbon in ash.
