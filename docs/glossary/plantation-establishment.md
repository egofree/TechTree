# Plantation establishment

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Trees are propagated from budded stumps (grafting high-yielding clones onto seedling rootstock). Spacing: 500-600 trees per hectare (4-5 m between trees, 7-8 m between rows). Trees begin producing latex at 5-7 years and remain productive for 20-30 years. Annual rainfall requirement: 1800-2500 mm. Altitude: below 200 m for optimal yield.
