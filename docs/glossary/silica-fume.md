# Silica fume

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Silica fume: 200-400 kg per tonne of silicon. When collected, it is a valuable pozzolanic additive for high-strength concrete (reacts with calcium hydroxide in cement, improving strength and durability). Particle size: 0.1-0.5 μm (100× finer than cement). Market value offsets collection costs. See: [Metallurgical-Grade Silicon Production](../silicon/mg-si-production.md)
