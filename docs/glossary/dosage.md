# Dosage

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health, plants

Dosage: 500-600 mg quinine sulfate every 8 hours for 7 days (malaria treatment). Therapeutic dose produces "cinchonism": tinnitus, headache, nausea — these reversible effects indicate adequate blood levels. Reduce dose if visual or auditory symptoms become severe.
