# Temperature/pressure depend on mineralogy

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Gibbsite (trihydrate) digests at 140-150°C, atmospheric or low pressure (0.3-0.5 MPa). Boehmite (monohydrate) requires 200-250°C at 2-4 MPa. Diaspore requires the harshest conditions: 250-280°C at 4-6 MPa. Most industrial bauxites contain a mix.
