# Mold powder (flux)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Fed continuously onto the meniscus (liquid steel surface inside mold). Functions: (a) thermal insulation preventing premature solidification at meniscus, (b) lubrication between shell and mold — melts at steel temperature to form a 0.1-0.3 mm liquid film, (c) inclusion absorption — captures alumina and oxide inclusions floating to the surface.
