# Harvesting (lancing)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

When petals fall, the green pod enlarges. Score the unripe pod surface with a multi-bladed lancing knife (3-4 shallow parallel cuts, 1-2 mm deep, not penetrating the pod wall). Latex exudes white, oxidizes to brown overnight. Collect the dried brown latex the following morning with a blunt scraper. Each pod yields 20-50 mg dried opium. A skilled worker processes ~5,000 pods/day. One hectare yields 8-15 kg raw opium.
