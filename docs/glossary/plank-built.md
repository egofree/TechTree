# Plank-built

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Keel timber (hardwood, 10-20 cm square section) laid first. Attach curved frames (ribs) of naturally bent timber at 40-60 cm spacing. Fasten planking (2-5 cm thick pine or oak) to frames with wooden trenails or iron nails. Caulk seams with oakum (frayed rope fibers) driven into gaps, seal with pitch or tar. Size ranges 5-50 m depending on purpose and available timber.
