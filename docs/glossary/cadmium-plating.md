# Cadmium plating

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Excellent corrosion resistance in marine environments, low contact resistance, galvanic compatibility with aluminum. Historically dominant in aerospace fasteners. Being phased out globally — cadmium is highly toxic (carcinogen, bioaccumulates). Replacements: zinc-nickel alloy (12-15% Ni, 500+ hours salt spray), ion vapor deposition aluminum.
