# Trichlorosilane (SiHCl₃) hazards

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Corrosive liquid (bp 31.8°C — evaporates readily). Fumes are HCl on contact with atmospheric moisture. Skin and respiratory burns. Store in sealed, vented containers. Full chemical suit and self-contained breathing apparatus (SCBA) for spill response.
