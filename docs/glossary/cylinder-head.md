# Cylinder head

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy, transport

Aluminum alloy casting (Al + 8-12% Si — aluminum-silicon alloy, good fluidity for casting, good thermal conductivity for cooling). Cast in sand mold. Machine combustion chamber, valve seats, spark plug hole, and bolt holes. Large cooling fins cast integral with head. Bolt to cylinder barrel via studs (steel studs threaded into barrel, nuts on top of head — allows thermal expansion).
