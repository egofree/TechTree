# Yield and cost

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

1.9-2.0 tonnes TiCl₄ + 1.0-1.1 tonnes Mg → 1.0 tonne Ti sponge. MgCl₂ byproduct is electrolyzed back to Mg and Cl₂ for recycle. Batch process: 2-5 tonnes per retort, 5-10 days cycle. Cost: $6-12/kg sponge (10-20× the cost of steel per kg).
