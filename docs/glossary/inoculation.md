# Inoculation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Collect "brood lac" (sticklac containing developing larvae from the previous season — identified by the presence of female insects with eggs visible through the resin). Cut brood lac into 5-10 cm sections. Tie brood lac bundles to fresh branches of host trees at the start of the growing season using cotton twine (the twine must be biodegradable so it doesn't girdle the growing branch).
