# Destruction schedule

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Not every document is worth keeping forever. Establish retention schedules: routine correspondence — destroy after 5 years; routine financial records — destroy after 10 years; engineering drawings — retain permanently; research data — retain permanently. Uncontrolled accumulation of records is as much a problem as loss — it buries valuable information in a mass of trivia, making retrieval impossible.
