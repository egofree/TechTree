# Stoneware bisque

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

(Cone 08-06, 950-1000°C): - Total firing time: 8-10 hours. Fuel: 25-50 kg hardwood. - Ramp: 50°C/hr to 200°C, 80°C/hr to 600°C, 120°C/hr to peak. Soak 30 min. - Bisque to a lower temperature than the final glaze fire. This leaves the body porous for glaze absorption while still developing enough strength to survive handling and glaze application without chipping.
