# Two-stage pump system

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

(standard for high vacuum): - **Roughing pump** (rotary vane or diaphragm): Pulls system from atmospheric pressure (~10⁵ Pa) down to ~0.1-1 Pa. This is the "rough vacuum" phase. The roughing pump handles the bulk of the gas load — most of the air molecules are removed in this stage. - **High-vacuum pump** (turbomolecular or diffusion pump): Takes over at the **crossover pressure** (~1 Pa, or ~10⁻² Torr). Below this pressure, molecular flow dominates and the high-vacuum pump becomes effective....
