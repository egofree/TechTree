# Stage 2: Early Haber-Bosch

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Sufficient to replace coke-oven ammonia for Solvay supply and begin synthetic fertilizer production. - Key bottleneck: high-pressure compressor reliability.
