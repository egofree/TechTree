# Airspeed indicator

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

(pitot-static): - A pitot tube (open-ended tube facing forward into the airstream) measures total pressure (static + dynamic). A static port (flush opening on the fuselage side) measures ambient static pressure. The instrument subtracts static from total pressure to obtain dynamic pressure: q = ½ρv².
