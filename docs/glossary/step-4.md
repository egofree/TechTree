# Step 4

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

Test fire the glaze on small test tiles (2 × 5 cm bisque tiles dipped in glaze). Evaluate at 3 different temperatures (below, at, and above target) to determine the melting range. Adjust the recipe based on results: too runny → increase alumina; too matte → decrease alumina or increase flux; crawling → thin the glaze or clean the bisque surface.
