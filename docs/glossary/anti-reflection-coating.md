# Anti-reflection coating

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: optics, silicon

(single-layer MgF₂): - Magnesium fluoride (MgF₂) deposited on lens surface. Thickness: λ/4 (quarter-wavelength, ~100 nm for center of visible spectrum). Refractive index n ≈ 1.38, between air (1.0) and glass (~1.5). - Reduces surface reflection from ~4% to ~1.3% per surface. On a 4-element lens (8 surfaces): transmission improves from ~72% to ~90%. - Deposition: thermal evaporation in vacuum chamber at 10⁻⁴ Pa (10⁻⁶ Torr). Heat MgF₂ in tungsten boat until it sublimes.
