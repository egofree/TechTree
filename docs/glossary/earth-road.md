# Earth road

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Earth road (improved dirt) — - Crown the surface 2-4% (center 10-15 cm higher than edges on a 3-5 m road). Grade the subgrade to this profile using a scraper blade behind animals or a tractor. - Side ditches: V-shaped or trapezoidal, 30-50 cm deep, 50-80 cm top width, 1-2% longitudinal slope for positive drainage.
