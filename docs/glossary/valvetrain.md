# Valvetrain

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy, transport

Intake and exhaust valves operated by camshaft via pushrods (OHV) or directly (OHC). Valve springs close valves. Valve timing: intake opens 5-30° before TDC, closes 40-70° after BDC; exhaust opens 40-70° before BDC, closes 5-30° after TDC. Duration ~240-300° of crankshaft rotation.
