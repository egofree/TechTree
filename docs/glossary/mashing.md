# Mashing

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Mix grist with hot water (65-68°C) in insulated vessel. Hold 1-2 hours. Amylase converts starch to maltose (C₁₂H₂₂O₁₁) and glucose (C₆H₁₂O₆). Test with iodine — blue = starch still present, no blue = conversion complete. Strain liquid (wort) from grain.
