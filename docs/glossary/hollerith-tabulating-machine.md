# Hollerith tabulating machine

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: computing

Hollerith tabulating machine (1890 census) — Card format**: Original 1890 card: 24 columns × 12 rows. Punched holes represent data values. Later standardized to 80 columns × 12 rows (the "IBM card," 187 × 83 mm, 0.18 mm card stock). - **Sensing**: Spring-loaded pins press against card. Where a hole exists, pin passes through, completes electrical circuit through mercury pool beneath.
