# Urine glucose

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Add 8 drops Benedict's solution to 5 mL urine. Heat in boiling water bath for 5 minutes. Blue (negative) → green (trace) → yellow (+) → orange (++) → red-orange (+++) → brick red (++++). Quantitative estimate: trace ~0.25%, + ~0.5%, ++ ~1.0%, +++ ~2.0%, ++++ >2.0% glucose.
