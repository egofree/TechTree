# Hydrochloric acid production

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Global ~20 million tonnes/year (as 100% HCl). Most HCl is produced as a byproduct of organic chemical manufacturing (chlorination reactions). Direct synthesis (H₂ + Cl₂) accounts for a smaller share but produces higher purity acid. A synthesis burner producing 100 tonnes/day HCl gas requires 2.7 tonnes/day H₂ and 97.3 tonnes/day Cl₂, consuming about 500 kW for compression and absorption equipment.
