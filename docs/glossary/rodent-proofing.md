# Rodent-proofing

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Smooth stone or clay cone caps on support posts (rats cannot climb inverted cones). Tight-fitting lid. Thatched roof overhangs walls by 30+ cm to shed rain. - **Capacity**: 2×3 m granary stores ~1-2 tonnes grain (1-2 years food for a family). Build multiple for larger communities.
