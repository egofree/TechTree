# Plastic-based tape

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: computing

Cellulose acetate or PVC backing with gamma-Fe₂O₃ coating. Superior to paper in strength and uniformity. Tape width: 6.35 mm (¼ inch) for audio, 12.7 mm (½ inch) for instrumentation. The basis of the recording industry from ~1950 onward.
