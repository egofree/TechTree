# Voltage budget

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

For a 100 km line of 4 mm iron wire (resistance ~40 Ω/km), total loop resistance is 4000 Ω (100 km out + 100 km ground return, though ground return is typically 10-20 Ω and negligible). Add sounder resistance (5-20 Ω). Battery voltage needed: V = I × R = 0.030 A × 4020 Ω = 120 V. Stack 110 Daniell cells in series (1.1 V each). At 50 km, only 60 V needed (55 cells).
