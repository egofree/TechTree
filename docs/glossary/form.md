# Form

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Conical points (standardized sizes, ISO 15-140, where the number represents tip diameter in hundredths of a millimeter) matching root canal instrument sizes. Points are hard at room temperature for insertion, can be softened with heat (60-70°C) for compaction. Standard taper 2% (0.02 mm/mm) or greater tapers (4%, 6%) for modern instrumentation techniques.
