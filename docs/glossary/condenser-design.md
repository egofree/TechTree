# Condenser design

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Shell-and-tube condenser is standard. Cooling water flows on the shell side (lower pressure drop, easier to clean), vapor condenses inside the tubes. Tube material: 316L stainless steel or copper-nickel alloy for corrosion resistance. Water inlet temperature 20-30°C, outlet 35-45°C (limited to prevent scale deposition).
