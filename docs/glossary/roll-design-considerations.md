# Roll design considerations

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

Each station pair consists of a top roll and bottom roll with matching profile contours. The strip enters flat at station 1 and exits fully formed at the last station. Roll material is hardened tool steel (D2 or equivalent) for wear resistance on long production runs. Roller alignment is critical: lateral misalignment exceeding 0.1 mm per station accumulates through the line and produces a twisted profile at exit.
