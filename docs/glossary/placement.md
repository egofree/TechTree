# Placement

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals, chemistry

Concrete must be placed before initial set (typically 45-120 minutes after mixing, depending on temperature and admixtures). In hot weather (>30°C), set time shortens and workability decreases. In cold weather (<5°C), set time extends and early strength gain is slow. Pumping: concrete pumped through 100-150 mm diameter steel or rubber pipeline at 5-10 MPa pressure.
