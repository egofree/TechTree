# Tie-dye

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Tie-dye (bound resist) — Bind sections of pre-wetted cloth tightly with waxed linen thread or sinew. Wrap 5-10 times at each binding point, pulling very tight to compress the fabric through its full thickness. The compressed areas resist dye penetration. Immersion dyeing for 30+ minutes in a warm dye bath gives the best penetration.
