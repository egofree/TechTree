# Blowpipe

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Iron tube 1.2-1.5 m long, 15-20 mm diameter, with a rounded or flared tip. Wall thickness 1-2 mm. The gather end is thickened slightly to hold molten glass. Weight: 0.5-1.5 kg depending on size. Iron must be kept clean and free of scale — rust particles contaminate the glass. Season new blowpipes by heating to red heat and dipping in dry sand several times to build a thin oxide layer that reduces glass adhesion.
