# Washing and kneading

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

The crude product contains residual plant matter, pigments, and water-soluble impurities. Knead repeatedly in warm water (50-60°C) to remove contaminants. Each washing cycle removes more contamination. The material darkens during processing (from pale yellow to dark brown/black) due to oxidation and residual tannins from the leaf tissue. Minimum 5-6 washing cycles for acceptable purity.
