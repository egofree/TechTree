# Pressure cooking (autoclaving)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Pressure cooking (autoclaving) refers to 121°C at 15 psi for 15 minutes. Kills all microorganisms including spores. Wrap instruments in cloth or place in sterilization pouches before autoclaving. The gold standard for sterilization. Verify effectiveness with indicator tape (changes color at sterilization temperature) or biological indicators (spore strips cultured after autoclaving — no growth = successful sterilization).
