# Typical specifications

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: optics

60/40 for general visual optics (eyepieces, finderscopes). 40/20 for camera lenses and binoculars. 20/10 for imaging optics and precision instruments. 10/5 for laser optics and photolithography lenses (any defect scatters laser energy or creates imaging artifacts).
