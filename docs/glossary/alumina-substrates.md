# Alumina substrates

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

Alumina substrates: 96% Al₂O₃, 0.5-1.0 mm thick, cofired with tungsten metallization. Hybrid circuit substrates, resistor networks. Excellent thermal conductivity for heat dissipation. See: [Advanced Ceramics & Refractories](../ceramics/advanced-ceramics.md)
