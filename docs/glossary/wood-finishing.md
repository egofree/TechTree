# Wood finishing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations, polymers

Scrape surfaces smooth with a steel or stone scraper held at 80 degrees to the wood. Sand with progressively finer abrasives: coarse sandstone, then fine sandstone, then dried horsetail stems (natural emery). Rub with linseed oil or beeswax to seal the surface and slow moisture absorption. A well-finished wood surface resists rot and checking.
