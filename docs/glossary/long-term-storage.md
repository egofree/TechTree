# Long-term storage

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

25°C ± 2°C / 60% RH ± 5% RH. Test intervals: 0, 3, 6, 9, 12, 18, 24, 36 months. Establishes shelf life (expiry dating). The statistical analysis of degradation data determines the period during which the product remains within specification at the 95% confidence level.
