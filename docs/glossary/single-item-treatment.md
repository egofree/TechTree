# Single-item treatment

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Spray or brush with alkaline buffer solution — magnesium bicarbonate or calcium bicarbonate in water or ethanol. The alkaline reserve neutralizes existing acid and provides ongoing protection. Wei T'o process: non-aqueous deacidification using magnesium methoxide in methanol and fluorocarbon solvent — penetrates paper without wetting or distorting it.
