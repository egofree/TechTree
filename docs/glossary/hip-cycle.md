# HIP cycle

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Load into a water-cooled pressure vessel with internal graphite or Kanthal (FeCrAl) heating elements. Pressurize with argon to 100-200 MPa (1000-2000 bar) while heating to 1000-1250°C (temperature depends on alloy). Typical cycle: 2-4 hours at temperature + pressure, then cool under pressure. Total cycle time: 8-16 hours including heat-up and cool-down.
