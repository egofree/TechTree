# ArF excimer laser

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: vlsi-scaling

ArF excimer laser (193 nm) — features down to ~0.13 μm (dry), ~0.07 μm (immersion). Immersion: water layer between lens and wafer increases numerical aperture (NA >1.0). See: [Advanced Lithography](../vlsi-scaling/advanced-lithography.md)
