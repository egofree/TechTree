# Trickling filter

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Circular bed of stone or plastic media (1-3 m deep). Wastewater distributed by rotating arms over the bed surface. Biofilm grows on media surface, consuming organic matter as wastewater trickles through. Oxygen supplied by natural ventilation through the bed. BOD removal 65-85%. Fly and odor nuisance in warm weather.
