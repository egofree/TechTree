# Refractory ceramic fiber (RCF) safety

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

RCF fibers are respirable (diameter 2-4 μm, length >10 μm) and classified as IARC Group 2B (possibly carcinogenic). During installation and removal, airborne fiber concentrations can exceed 1 fiber/cm³. Control measures: wet methods (spray binder to suppress dust), respiratory protection (P100 respirator minimum), protective clothing (disposable coveralls), and enclosed handling systems.
