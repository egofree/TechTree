# Carbon monoxide (CO)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Incomplete combustion of any carbon fuel produces CO — odorless, colorless, lethal at 400 ppm sustained concentration. Symptoms: headache (mild), nausea and dizziness (moderate), unconsciousness and death (severe). Never burn fuels in enclosed spaces without ventilation. CO detectors where fuel-burning equipment operates.
