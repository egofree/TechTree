# Lifetime tracking

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Mercury arc lamps degrade with use. Output intensity drops ~10% per 100 hours of operation. Replace at manufacturer-rated lifetime (typically 800-1200 hours) or when intensity drops below the process minimum (established during qualification). A lamp that fails catastrophically (envelope rupture) releases mercury into the exposure tool housing, requiring extensive cleanup.
