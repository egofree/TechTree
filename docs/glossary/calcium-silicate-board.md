# Calcium silicate board

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Hydrated calcium silicate (CaSiO₃·H₂O) boards, density 200-1000 kg/m³, provide rigid insulation for temperatures up to 1000-1100°C. Thermal conductivity 0.06-0.15 W/m·K at 200°C. Used for furnace backup insulation, refractory backup behind brick linings, and as permanent formwork for castable refractories. Non-fibrous (crystalline structure), avoiding the respiratory hazards of fibrous insulation. Can be machined to precise shapes with woodworking tools. Water resistant but not waterproof.
