# Dye penetrant testing (PT)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools, metals

Detects surface-breaking cracks, porosity, and seams that are too fine for unaided visual inspection. Procedure: (1) Clean the weld surface thoroughly (solvent wipe). (2) Apply red penetrant dye (fluorescent or visible red dye with low surface tension) to the surface. Allow 10-30 minutes dwell time for the dye to seep into surface defects by capillary action.
