# Drifter drill

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Drifter drill (mounted on a boom or carriage) — - Larger piston (60-100 mm bore), heavier frame, higher feed force. Cannot be hand-held. Mounted on a drill jumbo (underground) or crawl carriage (surface). - Air consumption: 8-15 m³/min. Drills holes 38-64 mm diameter, 3-5 m deep. Feed force: 500-3000 N, adjustable via pneumatic chain or screw feed. - Drill jumbo: a mobile platform with 1-3 hydraulic or pneumatic booms, each carrying a drifter drill.
