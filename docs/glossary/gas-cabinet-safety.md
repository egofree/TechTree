# Gas cabinet safety

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Automatic shutoff valve (normally closed pneumatic valve — closes on power loss, gas detection alarm, or fire alarm). Exhaust ventilation: 100+ ft³/min flow through cabinet. Continuous gas monitoring: toxic gas sensors at cabinet exhaust, room ambient, and exhaust stack (three-tier monitoring). Flashback arrestors on hydrogen lines. Excess flow valves (shut off if flow exceeds setpoint — indicates line break). Emergency abort button (closes all cylinder valves, purges lines with N₂).
