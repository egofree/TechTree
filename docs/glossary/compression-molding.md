# Compression molding

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Place uncured rubber preform in heated mold cavity. Close mold (hydraulic press, 10-50 tons). Pressure: 5-20 MPa. Temperature: 140-160°C. Time: 15-60 minutes depending on thickness. Simple, low-cost tooling. Flash (excess material squeezed out at mold parting line) must be trimmed. Used for: seals, gaskets, simple tire curing, belt splices.
