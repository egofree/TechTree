# Stone wall

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Dry-laid or mortared fieldstone, 1.0-1.2 m high, 0.5-0.6 m thick at base tapering to 0.3-0.4 m at top. Permanent, requires no maintenance, but labor-intensive to build. A single skilled waller builds 3-5 m of wall per day.
