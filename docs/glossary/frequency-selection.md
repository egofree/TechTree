# Frequency selection

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Lower frequency penetrates deeper into the charge. Mains frequency (50-60 Hz) suits large furnaces (1-30 tonnes) for iron and steel melting. Medium frequency (150-1000 Hz) suits smaller furnaces (0.1-5 tonnes) and provides better stirring. High frequency (1-10 kHz) for surface hardening and brazing of small parts.
