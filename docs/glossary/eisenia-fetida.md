# Eisenia fetida

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: critical | **Domains**: agriculture

Red wiggler — the standard vermicomposting worm worldwide. Surface-dwelling epigeic species, 6-13 cm, found in leaf litter and manure. Consumes 25-35% of body weight per day. Doubles population every 2-3 months under ideal conditions. Hermaphroditic but requires partner for mating.
