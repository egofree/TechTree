# Electron Beam Cold Hearth Remelting (EBCHR)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Alternative for premium quality. Electron beam melts feedstock in a cold copper hearth; high-density inclusions (W, Ta) settle in the hearth lip before the clean melt overflows into the ingot mold. Better inclusion removal than VAR. Used for turbine disk material.
