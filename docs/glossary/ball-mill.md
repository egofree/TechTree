# Ball mill

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Rotating cylinder (1-3 m diameter, 2-5 m length) loaded with steel or cast iron balls (25-100 mm diameter). Ore fed at one end, discharged at the other. Rotation speed: 60-80% of critical speed (speed at which centrifugal force holds balls against shell — calculated as 42.3/√D RPM where D = diameter in meters).
