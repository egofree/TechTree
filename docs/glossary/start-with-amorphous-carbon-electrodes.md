# Start with amorphous carbon electrodes

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Baked carbon electrodes (baked only to ~1200°C, no graphitization step) are functional in EAFs and SAFs. They have higher electrical resistivity, higher consumption rate, and lower thermal shock resistance — but they work. Expect 3-5× higher electrode consumption per tonne of product compared to graphitized electrodes.
