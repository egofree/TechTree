# Polyalphaolefin (PAO)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Hydrocarbon synthetic — made by oligomerizing α-olefins (primarily decene, C₁₀) with BF₃ catalyst, then hydrogenating. Uniform molecular weight distribution (no light or heavy fractions) gives: better low-temperature fluidity (pour point -50 to -65°C vs -15°C for mineral oil), higher viscosity index (130-150 vs 95-105), better oxidation stability (2-3× longer oil life). Used in: jet engine oils, Arctic machinery, long-drain automotive oils.
