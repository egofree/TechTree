# Shaft sinking

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Dig vertical shaft, typically 2-3 m × 1.5-2 m (enough for one-way traffic + bucket winding). Line walls with timber (oak or pine, 15-25 cm diameter) or stone/brick for permanent shafts. Shaft compartments: one for winding (ore/people), one for ventilation, one for pumping.
