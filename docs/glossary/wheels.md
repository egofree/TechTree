# Wheels

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals, transport

solid disc or solid-web construction, 60-80 cm diameter, 6-10 cm thick. Made from two or three planks bolted or doweled together, then cut round. A hole bored through the center receives the axle stub. Axle is a seasoned hardwood pole, 10-12 cm diameter, fixed to the cart bed. Wheels rotate on the axle (dead axle).
