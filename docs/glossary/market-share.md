# Market share

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Powder coating is the fastest-growing coating technology, now representing ~15% of the global industrial coatings market. Key advantage: zero VOC, no hazardous solvent emissions. Limitation: requires substrate that can withstand 150-200°C curing temperature (rules out wood, plastic, and heat-sensitive substrates unless UV-curable powder is used, which cures at 100-120°C with UV radiation).
