# High-vacuum pump

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

(turbomolecular or diffusion pump): Takes over at the **crossover pressure** (~1 Pa, or ~10⁻² Torr). Below this pressure, molecular flow dominates and the high-vacuum pump becomes effective. Crossover too early → gas load overloads the high-vacuum pump. Crossover too late → wastes time and risks oil backstreaming from roughing pump.
