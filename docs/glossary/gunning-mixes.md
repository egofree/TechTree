# Gunning mixes

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Dry or pre-mixed refractory material pneumatically sprayed onto hot furnace surfaces to repair worn areas without cooling the furnace. Applied at 20-40 m/s impact velocity. Adhesion by impact bonding. Used for in-situ repair of BOF linings, cement kilns, and incinerators — extends campaign life without costly shutdowns.
