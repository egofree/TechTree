# Recertification

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Skills degrade without practice. Implement recertification testing every 2 years for safety-critical skills (welding, electrical work, chemical handling). The recertification exam is a shortened version of the original practical test — if the candidate cannot pass, mandatory refresher training is required before they resume independent work. This prevents skill attrition in practitioners who shift to supervisory roles and no longer practice daily.
