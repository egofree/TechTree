# Dust suppression

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Respirable coal mine dust (≤10 μm, reaches deep lung) is controlled by water sprays at the cutting face (10-30 L/min), ventilation (minimum 0.5 m/s air velocity at the face), and respiratory protection. Quartz content above 5% in the dust reduces the PEL proportionally (silica is more toxic than coal dust alone).
