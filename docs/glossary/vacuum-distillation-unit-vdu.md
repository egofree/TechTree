# Vacuum distillation unit (VDU)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Atmospheric residue (bp >350°C) would thermally crack (decompose) if distilled further at atmospheric pressure. The VDU operates at 10-20 mmHg (absolute), reducing boiling points by 150-200°C. This allows distillation of high-boiling materials at 350-400°C without thermal degradation. Products: vacuum gas oil (VGO, 350-540°C bp range, FCC or hydrocracker feed), lubricating oil base stocks (light, medium, heavy neutral grades), and vacuum residue (bp >540°C, used for bitumen/asphalt production...
