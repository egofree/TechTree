# Peroxide hazard

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Ether slowly reacts with atmospheric oxygen to form organic peroxides (explosive upon concentration or mechanical shock). Test for peroxides with potassium iodide-starch paper before distilling old ether. Add iron wire or BHT inhibitor to storage containers. Discard ether older than 6 months if uninhibited.
