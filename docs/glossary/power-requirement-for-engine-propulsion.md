# Power requirement for engine propulsion

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

P = Δ^(2/3) × V³ / K, where P is shaft power in kW, Δ is displacement in tonnes, V is speed in knots, and K is a hull form factor (150-170 for fine-lined vessels, 170-200 for full-bodied cargo hulls). A 420-tonne vessel at 8 knots with K = 180: P = 420^(2/3) × 8³ / 180 = 57.2 × 512 / 180 = 163 kW (218 HP).
