# Drop spindle

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Drop spindle (Foundations stage, simplest) — - **Construction**: Round whorl (50-100 g, 3-5 cm diameter) on straight wooden shaft (20-30 cm). Whorl materials: fired clay (most common — shape on wheel, dry, fire to hardness), stone (sandstone or limestone, drilled center hole), or turned wood (hardwood, weighted with clay or lead if needed).
