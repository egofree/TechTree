# Pedagogical content knowledge

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Knowing *how to teach* a subject is distinct from knowing the subject. Effective technical instruction requires: breaking complex procedures into teachable steps, anticipating common misunderstandings, designing exercises that reinforce concepts, providing feedback that corrects errors without discouraging the learner, and assessing understanding (not just performance — a student who produces a correct result by a flawed method has not truly learned).
