# Mercury toxicity

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health, measurement

Mercury vapor from spills, broken thermometers, and barometer filling causes cumulative neurological damage (tremor, memory loss, kidney damage). IDLH: 10 mg/m³. Vapor pressure at 20°C is 0.0012 mm Hg — enough to exceed safe limits in unventilated rooms after a spill. Fill barometers in fume hoods or under local exhaust ventilation.
