# Graduated cylinder

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Blow a uniform cylinder (20-50 mm diameter, 200-300 mm tall) with a flat base and poured lip. Anneal carefully (uniform wall thickness is critical for accurate volume measurement). Etch graduation marks at regular intervals using hydrofluoric acid through a wax mask. Calibrate by weighing the water contained at each mark (1 mL water = 1.000 g at 4°C). Accuracy: ±1% with careful calibration.
