# Blend for balanced bar

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Blend for balanced bar: 40% tallow (hardness, lather stability) + 30% coconut oil (quick lather, cleaning power) + 30% olive oil (conditioning, mildness). This approximates the classic "three-oil" formulation. See: [Soap & Detergent Production](../chemistry/soap.md)
