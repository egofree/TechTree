# Water recycling

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Process water from the tailings pond is pumped back to the plant for reuse. Recycling reduces fresh water consumption by 70-90%. A typical copper flotation circuit uses 2-3 m³ water per tonne of ore. Without recycling, a 50,000 tonne/day operation would consume 100,000-150,000 m³/day of fresh water — impractical in most mining locations.
