# Chapman-Jouguet (C-J) theory

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

The detonation wave is a self-sustaining shockwave coupled to a chemical reaction zone. The C-J condition states that the detonation products exit the reaction zone at the local speed of sound relative to the shock front. The C-J detonation velocity can be estimated: VOD = √(2Q(γ²-1)/(γ+1)) where Q is the heat of explosion per unit mass and γ is the ratio of specific heats of the detonation products.
