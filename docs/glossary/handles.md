# Handles

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Split green wood, shape with stone tools, dry slowly to prevent checking. Ash and hickory make the best tool handles because they absorb shock without splitting. A handle for a felling axe is 70-90 cm long, 3-4 cm thick at the grip, and flared at the end to prevent the hand from sliding off. A shorter handle (35-50 cm) suits a hatchet or adze.
