# Operating costs

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, transport

Raw materials 30-50%, energy (steam, electricity, compressed air) 15-25%, labor 10-20%, maintenance and depreciation 15-25%. For ethanol from corn: feedstock (corn) accounts for 50-65% of production cost. For citric acid: sugar (glucose or molasses) is 25-35% of cost. Energy for sterilization (steam at 121°C) and aeration (compressed air at 0.5-1.5 vvm for aerobic processes) are major utility costs.
