# Transfer molding

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, polymers

(most common for plastic packages): Place die + wire-bonded lead frame in mold cavity. Inject molding compound (epoxy resin + silica filler 70-80% by weight + catalyst + mold release agent) at 175°C, 6-10 MPa pressure. Cure 2-5 minutes. Mold compound protects die from moisture, mechanical damage, contamination. Silica filler reduces thermal expansion coefficient (match silicon → less stress).
