# Air emissions

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Kraft mills emit reduced sulfur compounds (TRS — total reduced sulfur: H₂S, CH₃SH, CH₃SCH₃, CH₃SSCH₃) from the recovery boiler, lime kiln, and digestor relief. TRS compounds have extremely low odor thresholds (detectable at 1-10 ppb, causing the characteristic "rotten egg" smell near kraft mills). Collection and thermal oxidation in the recovery boiler or dedicated incinerator reduces TRS emissions to <5 ppm.
