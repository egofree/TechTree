# Tall oil recovery

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Black liquor from softwood pulping contains 3-6% soap (sodium salts of resin acids and fatty acids, derived from wood extractives). Soap skims from liquor surface during evaporation (soap separates as pH drops and concentration increases). Acidified with H₂SO₄ → crude tall oil (CTO).
