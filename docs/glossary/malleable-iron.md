# Malleable iron

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Heat white iron castings at ~900°C for 40-80 hours (annealing). Cementite decomposes → graphite forms as discrete nodules (temper carbon) rather than flakes. The rounded nodules don't act as stress concentrators the way graphite flakes do → improved ductility and toughness vs. gray iron. Can handle moderate impact and bending. Used for pipe fittings, brackets, agricultural implements, hardware.
