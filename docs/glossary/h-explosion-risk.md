# H₂ explosion risk

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Hydrogen has an extremely wide flammable range (4-75% in air) and very low ignition energy (0.017 mJ — a static spark is sufficient). All H₂ piping must be purged with N₂ before opening. Explosion-proof electrical equipment in H₂ areas. Hydrogen detectors with automatic shutdown and ventilation.
