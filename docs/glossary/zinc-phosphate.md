# Zinc phosphate

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(most common for steel): - Bath: ZnO dissolved in H₃PO₄, with accelerators (sodium nitrite, nitrate, or chlorate). Total acid 20-30 points, free acid 2-4 points (titration measurement). Temperature: 40-70°C depending on formulation. Immersion time: 3-10 minutes. - Reaction: Steel dissolves in phosphoric acid → local pH rises at surface → zinc phosphate precipitates as hopeite (Zn₃(PO₄)₂·4H₂O) crystals on the surface.
