# Bagasse pulp

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Sugarcane bagasse (the fibrous residue after juice extraction) is 30-40% of cane mass. Composition: cellulose 40-45%, hemicellulose 25-30%, lignin 18-22%. Pith (soft interior) must be removed by depithing (mechanical separation) before pulping as it consumes chemicals without contributing fiber. Bagasse pulp yield: 45-50% by kraft process.
