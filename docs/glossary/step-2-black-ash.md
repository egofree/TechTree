# Step 2 (Black ash)

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Na₂SO₄ + 2C (coke/charcoal) + CaCO₃ (limestone) → Na₂CO₃ + CaS + 2CO₂. Rotary furnace or reverberatory furnace at 900-1000°C. Mix and roast for 1-2 hours. The coal reduces Na₂SO₄ to Na₂S, which reacts with CaCO₃.
