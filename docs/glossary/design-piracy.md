# Design piracy

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: vlsi-scaling

An IC mask set or HDL source represents years of engineering effort and millions in investment. Reverse engineering a chip (delayer by layer, image each layer, reconstruct schematics) is technically possible but expensive and time-consuming. Legal protections (mask work rights under treaties, patent protection on novel circuits) provide recourse against copying.
