# Drainfield (soil absorption system)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Network of perforated pipes (100 mm diameter) in gravel-filled trenches (0.3-0.6 m wide, 0.6-1.0 m deep). Effluent percolates into surrounding soil, which provides final treatment through filtration, adsorption, and biological oxidation. Soil percolation rate must be tested before construction (dig 0.3 m hole, fill with water, measure drop in level over time). Unsuitable for clay soils (percolation rate <15 mm/hour) or areas with high water table (minimum 1.2 m to seasonal high water table).
