# Fuel injection

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

High-pressure fuel pump (100-2000 bar for modern common-rail, 100-350 bar for older systems) forces fuel through precision injector nozzles. Nozzle orifice diameter 0.1-0.3 mm. Injection timing: begins 5-20° before TDC, continues 20-40° of crank rotation. Injection duration and timing control power output and emissions.
