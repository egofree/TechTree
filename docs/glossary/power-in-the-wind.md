# Power in the wind

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

The fundamental relationship is P = 0.5 × ρ × A × v³ × Cp. Air density ρ = 1.225 kg/m³ at sea level and 15°C (decreasing with altitude and temperature). The cubic dependence on wind speed means small increases in wind speed produce large increases in power. A site with 8 m/s average wind delivers (8/5)³ = 4.1× the power of a 5 m/s site.
