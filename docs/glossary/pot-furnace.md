# Pot furnace

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

(batch, small-scale): One or more fireclay crucibles (pots) placed in a furnace chamber, heated by wood, charcoal, or gas fire. Each pot holds 10-100 kg of molten glass. The pot is both container and reaction vessel. Replace pots when they erode or crack (typical lifespan: 3-6 months of daily use). Temperature control is manual — adjust fuel and draft.
