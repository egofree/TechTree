# Bagging and storage

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Fill woven polypropylene bags (500-1000 kg each, called "bulk bags" or "FIBCs") with dried concentrate. Stack on pallets under cover. Concentrate is dense (copper concentrate ~2.0-2.5 t/m³, iron ore pellets ~2.0 t/m³). A standard shipping container holds 20-25 tonnes of bagged concentrate.
