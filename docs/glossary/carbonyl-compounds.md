# Carbonyl compounds

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: metals

Metal carbonyls (e.g., Fe(CO)₅, Ni(CO)₄) used in powder metallurgy to produce ultrafine, high-purity metal powders via thermal decomposition. Nickel carbonyl process (Mond process) decomposes Ni(CO)₄ at 200°C to deposit pure nickel; extremely toxic gases require sealed systems.
