# Type 316

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(17Cr-12Ni-2.5Mo): Addition of 2-3% molybdenum significantly improves pitting and crevice corrosion resistance in chloride environments. The go-to grade for marine, chemical processing, and pharmaceutical equipment. Molybdenum stabilizes the passive film against localized breakdown by Cl⁻ ions.
