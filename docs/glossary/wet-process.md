# Wet process

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

(fertilizer-grade, 85% of production): - Phosphate rock (Ca₃(PO₄)₂, fluorapatite Ca₅(PO₄)₃F) reacted with concentrated sulfuric acid (93-98%) in a stirred tank at 70-80°C: Ca₃(PO₄)₂ + 3H₂SO₄ → 2H₃PO₄ + 3CaSO₄. - Filter calcium sulfate (phosphogypsum) — massive byproduct (~5 tonnes per tonne P₂O₅ produced, often stacked in waste piles).
