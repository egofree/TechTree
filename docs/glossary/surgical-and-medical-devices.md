# Surgical and medical devices

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Before modern synthetic polymers, gutta-percha was used for surgical splints, drainage tubes, and catheters. Its rigidity at body temperature, chemical inertness, and ability to be sterilized by boiling made it suitable for these applications. Lister used gutta-percha drainage tubes in early antiseptic surgery (1860s-1870s).
