# Minimum size

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Minimum size: 110 cm at the shoulder (roughly 11 hands) for effective defense against coyotes. Smaller donkeys lack the size and kick force to deter determined predators. Standard and mammoth donkeys (120-140 cm) provide better protection against wolves and mountain lions. See: [Biological Pest Management](../animals/pest-management.md)
