# Phosphorus pentoxide (P₂O₅)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Chemical desiccant that reacts with water to form phosphoric acid. Ultimate drying capacity: can reduce water vapor to <0.00001 mg/L (the most effective common desiccant). Not regenerable. Corrosive and hazardous (reacts violently with water in bulk). Used as a final polishing desiccant after silica gel or molecular sieve.
