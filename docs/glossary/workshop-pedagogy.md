# Workshop pedagogy

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

— the core of technical training: - **Demonstration-imitation-practice cycle**: (1) Master demonstrates the task at normal speed. (2) Master demonstrates again at slow speed, narrating each step and the reason for it. (3) Apprentice performs the task under direct supervision, with the master correcting errors in real time.
