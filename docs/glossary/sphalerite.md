# Sphalerite

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Sphalerite (ZnS, zinc blende) — The dominant ore (~95% of primary production). Often found with galena (PbS) in hydrothermal veins. Color varies from yellow-brown to black depending on iron content — high-iron varieties are called "marmatite." Typical ore grade: 3-12% Zn.
