# Gas leak response

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

If a gas leak is detected (odor, alarm, or visible damage to piping), the first priority is to stop the flow. Close the nearest upstream valve. For cylinder leaks where the valve cannot be closed (valve packing failure, damaged valve seat), evacuate the area and summon the fire department or hazmat team. Do not attempt to cap or plug a leaking cylinder. For toxic gas leaks, evacuate the area immediately and do not re-enter until the gas detector reads zero and the space has been verified safe...
