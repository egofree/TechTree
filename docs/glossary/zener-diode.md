# Zener diode

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Designed to operate in reverse breakdown. Breakdown voltage specified from 2.7V to 200V in standard values. Tolerance ±5% (tighter available). At breakdown, voltage remains nearly constant over a wide current range (dynamic impedance 1-100 Ω). Used for voltage regulation and reference. Requires series resistor to limit current: R = (Vin - Vz)/Iz.
