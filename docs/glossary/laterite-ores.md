# Laterite ores

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Laterite ores (~70% of production) — Nickeliferous limonite (Fe-Ni oxides, 1-1.8% Ni) and saprolite (Mg-Ni silicates, 1.5-2.5% Ni). Formed by tropical weathering of ultramafic rocks. Major deposits: Indonesia, Philippines, New Caledonia, Cuba. No valuable byproducts. Higher mining cost, lower grade, but much larger total resources than sulfides.
