# Mask 1 — Active area

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Grow 500 nm SiO₂ (wet oxidation, 1000°C, ~2 hours). Spin photoresist. Expose through Mask 1 (active areas = where transistors will be). Develop. Etch SiO₂ in buffered HF (BHF: NH₄F:HF 7:1, ~700 nm/min). Strip resist. Result: oxide islands defining transistor regions.
