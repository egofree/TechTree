# Needles with eyes

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Split bone or antler, grind slender with abrasive stone, drill eye with stone drill bit or punch through with an awl. Essential for tailored clothing. Bone needles are surprisingly strong for their size; a well-made needle sews through heavy leather without breaking. Keep needles in a dry bone or bark tube to protect the fine tip.
