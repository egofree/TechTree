# Asbestos exposure

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Steam locomotives used asbestos lagging (insulation) on boiler pipes, firebox crowns, and cylinder jackets. Asbestos fibers released during maintenance cause mesothelioma and asbestosis (latent period 20-40 years). Wet asbestos lagging before removal. Wear respiratory protection. Substitute with mineral wool or calcium silicate insulation where available.
