# Inductance measurement

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement

The Maxwell-Wien bridge measures inductance in terms of a known capacitance and resistance. The Hay bridge is better for high-Q inductors. A simple resonance method (connect the inductor in parallel with a known capacitor, find the resonant frequency with a signal generator and oscilloscope: L = 1/(4π²f²C)) gives inductance to ±2% with minimal equipment.
