# Hand-operated rolling mill

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

(the bootstrap starting point): - Two hardened steel or cast iron rolls, 75–150 mm diameter, 150–300 mm face length. Mounted in a cast iron or fabricated steel frame with adjustable gap (screw-down mechanism). - Drive: hand crank or foot treadle through gear reduction (3:1 to 6:1). Two operators — one cranks, one feeds stock. - Capacity: hot rolling of copper, bronze, and soft iron bar up to ~10 mm thick.
