# Copper powder

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Electrolyze CuSO₄ + H₂SO₄ solution at 2000-4000 A/m², 25-35°C. Dendritic particles with high surface area. Apparent density: 1.5-3.5 g/cm³ (vs. 8.96 g/cm³ for solid copper). Used in self-lubricating bearings (oil-impregnated porous Cu) and friction materials.
