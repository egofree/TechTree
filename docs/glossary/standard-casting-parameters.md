# Standard casting parameters

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Cast iron molds preheated to 200-300°C prevent thermal cracking of the ingot surface. Typical ingot size for chemical-grade production: 5-50 kg. Cooling time before demolding: 2-4 hours for standard ingots. Larger ingots (>100 kg, used for aluminum alloying or direct sales) require controlled cooling at 50-100°C/hour through the 1414°C solidification point to prevent internal stress cracking from silicon's unusually high volume contraction (~10%) on solidification.
