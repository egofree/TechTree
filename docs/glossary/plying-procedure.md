# Plying procedure

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Wind singles onto separate bobbins. Mount bobbins on lazy kate (rack holding bobbins with tension). Feed singles through plying hook onto spinning spindle or wheel bobbin. Spin in opposite direction to singles twist. Aim for balanced yarn — hang a length; if it hangs straight without twisting on itself, it is balanced.
