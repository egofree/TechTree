# Wafer chuck temperature control

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

The electrostatic chuck (ESC) holds the wafer by Coulombic attraction (dielectric layer on the chuck surface, 500-1500 V DC applied between chuck electrode and wafer). Helium backside cooling: He gas (2-10 Torr) flows between the wafer backside and chuck surface, providing thermal contact for heat removal.
