# Welding technique

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

Open acetylene valve, ignite (sooty orange flame). Gradually open oxygen until neutral flame is achieved. Hold torch at ~45° angle to work. Move steadily along joint at 2-5 mm/second. Dip filler rod into leading edge of the molten puddle — rod melts and adds metal to the joint. Joint types: butt (square or beveled for plate >3 mm), lap, fillet, and corner.
