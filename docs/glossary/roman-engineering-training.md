# Roman engineering training

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

(Republic and Empire, ~300 BCE - 400 CE): - Roman engineers (*architecti* and *fabri*) were trained through a combination of military service and apprenticeship. The Roman army's engineering corps built roads, bridges, aqueducts, fortifications, and siege works — every legion included specialist engineers.
