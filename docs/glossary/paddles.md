# Paddles

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Flat boards of fruitwood (cherry, apple, pear) or graphite, 10-20 cm square, 1-2 cm thick, on a wooden handle 30-40 cm long. Wooden paddles are soaked in water before use — the steam layer prevents the paddle from sticking to hot glass and cools the surface slightly for controlled shaping. Graphite paddles are preferred for borosilicate work (no charring, smoother surface). Used to flatten bottoms, shape sides, and shield specific areas from reheating.
