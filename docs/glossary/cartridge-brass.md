# Cartridge brass

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(70% Cu / 30% Zn): Melting range 915-955°C. Tensile strength ~320 MPa (annealed), up to 520 MPa (work-hardened). Excellent deep-drawing capability — the standard alloy for ammunition casings, radiator cores, lamp fixtures. Up to 70% cold reduction before annealing required.
