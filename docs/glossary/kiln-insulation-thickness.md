# Kiln insulation thickness

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

Wall thickness determines steady-state heat loss. For soft insulating firebrick (thermal conductivity ~0.25 W/(m·K)), the following minimum thicknesses maintain acceptable heat loss at target temperature: 230 mm (9") for 1260°C service, 345 mm (13.5") for 1300°C service, 460 mm (18") for 1400°C service.
