# NOx control

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Formed from nitrogen in combustion air at temperatures above 1300°C. Control methods: staged combustion (primary zone fuel-rich, secondary zone air-rich — reduces peak temperature), flue gas recirculation (dilutes combustion air with inert gas, lowering peak temperature by 100-200°C), and low-NOx burners (designed to delay fuel-air mixing).
