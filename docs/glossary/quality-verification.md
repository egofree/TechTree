# Quality verification

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Measure electrical resistivity (target: <10 μΩ·m), bulk density (>1.60 g/cm³), and flexural strength (>8 MPa). X-ray diffraction confirms the degree of graphitization by measuring the d002 interlayer spacing (graphite: 3.354 Å; amorphous carbon: >3.44 Å).
