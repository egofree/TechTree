# Backfill

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Fill mined-out stopes with waste material to provide wall support and allow extraction of adjacent ore: - **Hydraulic fill**: Mill tailings mixed with water (70-80% solids) pumped into the stope. Water drains out through a filter wall, leaving a solid fill mass. Add Portland cement (3-5% by weight) to create a strong, self-supporting fill (cemented paste fill). - **Rock fill**: Waste rock dropped into the stope from the level above.
