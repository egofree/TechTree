# ED glass

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

ED glass (extra-low dispersion) — borosilicate with rare earth oxides (lanthanum, La₂O₃), RI 1.65-1.80, Abbe number 45-55. Further reduces chromatic aberration in apochromatic lens designs. Requires rare earth element extraction from monazite or bastnäsite ores. See: [Advanced Glass Production](../glass/advanced.md)
