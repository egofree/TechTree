# Jacks

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Steel tweezers with two curved blades, 5-10 cm long, on spring-loaded handles 25-35 cm overall. Blade edges are rounded, not sharp. Used to neck in (constrict) the gather, shape the rim, and score the piece for removal from the blowpipe. The jack blade radius determines the narrowest constriction possible. Keep blades clean and lightly oiled to prevent rust.
