# Chemical oxidation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Chemical oxidation (faster, higher yield) — - Pass ethanol vapor over heated copper catalyst (copper gauze at 300-400°C) with air. - Ethanol oxidizes to acetaldehyde (CH₃CHO), then to acetic acid. Faster, higher concentration achievable. - Applications: food preservation (vinegar), cellulose acetate (photographic film, synthetic fibers), acetic anhydride (aspirin synthesis, cellulose acetate production), metal etching, solvent.
