# Tents

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

(shelter): - Canvas (cotton duck, 300-400 g/m²) is the standard tent fabric. Heavy but waterproof when wet (cotton fibers swell to close the pores), breathable (reduces condensation inside), and repairable with needle and thread in the field. - Seams: double-stitched (two parallel rows, 6-8 mm apart) with heavy linen thread.
