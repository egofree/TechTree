# Photoresist disposal

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Spent resist, developer, and rinse water containing dissolved organics are hazardous waste. Collect in labeled, compatible containers. Do not pour down any drain. PGMEA and ethyl lactate waste can be reclaimed by fractional distillation; contaminated waste must be incinerated by licensed hazardous-waste contractor.
