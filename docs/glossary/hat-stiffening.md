# Hat stiffening

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Shellac solution brushed into the brims and crowns of felt hats (top hats, bowler hats) provides permanent stiffness and shape retention — a major application in the 19th century hatting industry. The phrase "mad as a hatter" relates to mercury exposure in felt processing, not shellac — but shellac workers did face ethanol exposure hazards.
