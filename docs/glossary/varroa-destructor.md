# Varroa destructor

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

mite: Primary threat to managed bees worldwide. External parasite that attaches to bees and feeds on fat body tissue. Weakens adults, transmits viruses (deformed wing virus). Monitor with sugar roll or alcohol wash — sample 300 bees, count mites. Treatment threshold: 2-3 mites per 100 bees. Treatments: oxalic acid drip (2-3% solution, applied in broodless period), formic acid pad, thymol crystals. Without treatment, most colonies collapse within 2-3 years.
