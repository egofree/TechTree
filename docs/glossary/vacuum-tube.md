# Vacuum tube

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: computing

Vacuum tube (thermionic valve) — Heated cathode emits electrons → electrons flow toward positively-charged anode (plate) through vacuum. Control grid between cathode and anode modulates electron flow — small voltage change on grid produces large change in plate current. This is amplification, the basis for all active electronic switching. See: [Electronic Computing](../computing/electronic.md)
