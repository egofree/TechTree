# Accelerated storage

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

40°C ± 2°C / 75% RH ± 5% RH for 6 months. Predicts degradation pathways and provides early data for shelf-life estimation using the Arrhenius relationship. If the product passes accelerated testing (remains within specs), it is likely stable under long-term conditions. Failure at accelerated conditions does not necessarily mean failure at long-term, but triggers additional investigation.
