# Campaign life

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Modern blast furnace campaigns (the period between major relinings) last 10-20 years. A campaign ends when the refractory lining in the hearth or bosh has worn to the point where it must be replaced to avoid a breakoff (catastrophic shell failure due to molten metal contact). Campaign life depends on cooling system effectiveness, lining quality (carbon blocks, ceramic cups), and operating practice (avoiding thermal cycling).
