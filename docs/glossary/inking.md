# Inking

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Apply a thin, even layer of ink to the raised surface using a pad (tampon of rice straw wrapped in cloth, or later, a roller). The ink must be thin enough not to fill carved-away areas but thick enough to adhere to raised surfaces. Water-based ink (sumi — pine soot + animal glue + water) for East Asian woodblock; oil-based ink for European practice.
