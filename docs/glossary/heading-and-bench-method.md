# Heading and bench method

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Advance a small heading (1.5 × 2 m pilot tunnel) first, then widen by removing bench below. Heading provides advance knowledge of ground conditions. Timber support sets (posts + cap + lagging boards) installed immediately after excavation. Spacing 0.5-1.5 m depending on rock quality. In solid rock, support may be unnecessary.
