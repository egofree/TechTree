# Capital costs

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Carbon steel distillation column: $50,000-200,000 per meter of height and $100,000-500,000 per meter of diameter (2020 basis, depending on internals, pressure rating, and alloy). A 20 m tall × 1 m diameter sieve tray column with 30 trays, reboiler, condenser, reflux drum, pumps, and instruments: $500,000-2,000,000 installed. Stainless steel doubles the cost.
