# Fulling

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Beat cloth in warm water (40-50°C) with fuller's earth (clay) or stale urine (ammonia source). Wool fibers felt and interlock → cloth shrinks 15-25%, becomes denser and warmer. Fuller's earth (aluminum silicate clay) absorbs oils and acts as a mild detergent. For detailed finishing processes, see [Finishing](finishing.md).
