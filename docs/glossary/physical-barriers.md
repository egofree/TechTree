# Physical barriers

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals, foundations

fencing (woven wire, 1.2-1.5 m height, with a bottom apron buried 15-20 cm to prevent digging) excludes ground predators. Netting (bird net, 2-5 cm mesh) protects fruit from avian pests. Row covers (spun-bonded polypropylene or fine mesh) exclude flying insects from seedlings.
