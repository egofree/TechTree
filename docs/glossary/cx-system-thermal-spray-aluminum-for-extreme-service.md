# CX system (thermal spray aluminum for extreme service)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Abrasive blast to Sa 3. Thermal spray aluminum (200-300 μm) using arc wire spray or flame spray. Sealed with silicone or epoxy sealer. Provides both barrier and cathodic protection. Used on offshore platforms, splash zones, and chimney stacks. Service life 30-50 years with proper maintenance.
