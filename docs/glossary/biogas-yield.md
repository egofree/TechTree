# Biogas yield

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, health

0.3-0.5 m³ biogas per kg volatile solids (VS) added, depending on feedstock degradability. Cattle manure: 0.20-0.30 m³/kg VS. Food waste: 0.40-0.60 m³/kg VS. Energy crops (maize silage): 0.50-0.65 m³/kg VS. Hydraulic retention time (HRT): 15-30 days mesophilic, 10-15 days thermophilic. Organic loading rate (OLR): 1-4 kg VS/m³/day. Loading above 4 kg VS/m³/day risks acidification (volatile fatty acid accumulation exceeds methanogenic consumption rate).
