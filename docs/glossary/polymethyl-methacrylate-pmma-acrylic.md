# Polymethyl methacrylate (PMMA, acrylic)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Methyl methacrylate (MMA) monomer bulk-polymerized or suspension-polymerized at 60-80°C with peroxide initiator (benzoyl peroxide 0.1-0.5%). The bulk process (casting between glass plates) produces optically clear sheet — "cast acrylic" with the best optical quality of any thermoplastic (light transmission 92%, refractive index 1.49).
