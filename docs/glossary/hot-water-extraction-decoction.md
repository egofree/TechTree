# Hot water extraction (decoction)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

Chop or crush fresh or dried plant material, simmer in water at 60-90°C for 30-60 minutes. Strain through cloth. This is the simplest and most widely applicable method, effective for most dye plants including madder, weld, walnut hulls, and onion skins. The dye bath can be reused until exhausted — typically 2-3 dips per batch of material.
