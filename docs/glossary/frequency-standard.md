# Frequency standard

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: measurement

A highly stable oscillator used as a reference for time and frequency measurements: quartz crystal oscillators (10⁻⁶ stability), rubidium standards (10⁻¹¹), and cesium atomic clocks (10⁻¹²). Along with length, temperature, voltage, and resistance standards, frequency standards form the metrology foundation.
