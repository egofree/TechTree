# Fire assay for gold

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

The most reliable gold determination method, accurate to 0.01 g/t. Fuse 15-50 g of powdered sample with lead oxide (litharge), soda ash, borax, and silica at 1000-1100°C in a crucible. Lead collects precious metals into a metallic button. Cupel the lead button at 950°C — lead oxidizes and is absorbed by the cupel (bone ash dish), leaving a precious metal bead. Weigh the bead.
