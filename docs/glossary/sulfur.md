# Sulfur

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy, mining

Sulfur refers to 10% by weight. Secondary fuel and ignition promoter. Lowers ignition temperature (sulfur ignites at ~232°C, well below charcoal's ~340°C). Makes the powder easier to ignite and burn more consistently. Also contributes to the characteristic smoke.
