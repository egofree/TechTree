# TMAH (tetramethylammonium hydroxide)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

5-25% in water at 70-90°C. Etch rate ~0.6 μm/min at 25%, 80°C. CMOS-compatible (TMAH is an organic base — no alkali metal contamination). Selectivity to SiO₂: ~20:1 (lower than KOH's ~50:1). Smoother sidewalls than KOH but slower etch rate.
