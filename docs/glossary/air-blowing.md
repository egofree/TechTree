# Air blowing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Blow compressed air through a submerged lance into the molten silicon. Oxygen preferentially reacts with Al and Ca, forming Al₂O₃ and CaO that float to the surface as slag skimmed off. Removes 50-70% of aluminum and 60-80% of calcium. Also removes some iron as iron silicide slag.
