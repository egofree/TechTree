# Fuel selection and temperature

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: foundations

Choice of fuel based on required fire temperature: dried grass/twigs (flash fire, ~300°C), charcoal (sustained heat, ~1100°C), coal (high temperature, ~1500°C), and coke (intense heat, ~2000°C). Higher temperatures require denser, carbon-rich fuels and forced air supply.
