# Moisture contamination

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Water vapor is the most pervasive contaminant in gas systems. Even 1 ppm H₂O in silane causes SiO₂ formation in CVD films. Moisture sources: residual water adsorbed on internal surfaces of tubing and fittings (desorbed when gas flows), permeation through elastomer seals, and leaks (air ingress brings moisture).
