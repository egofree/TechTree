# Beryllium copper (Be-Cu)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: metals

Precipitation-hardening copper alloy containing 1.8-2.0% beryllium, achieving UTS of 1200-1400 MPa after aging at 315°C. Used for non-sparking tools (mining, explosive environments), electrical connectors, and precision instrument components. Beryllium dust is a serious inhalation hazard.
