# Annual energy

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

If the flow is available 80% of the year (operating 7,000 hours), annual energy production is roughly 2,085 × 7,000 = 14,595 MWh ≈ 14.6 GWh. At a capacity factor of roughly 80%, this is a productive mid-scale installation suitable for powering a small town or industrial facility.
