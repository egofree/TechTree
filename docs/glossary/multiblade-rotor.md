# Multiblade rotor

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Multiblade rotor: 15-30 flat or slightly curved vanes on a lattice frame, 3-8 m diameter. High blade count gives high starting torque for starting a loaded pump. Low tip-speed ratio (~1.0) optimized for torque, not speed. Starts reliably in 3 m/s wind. See: [Wind Power](../energy/wind.md)
