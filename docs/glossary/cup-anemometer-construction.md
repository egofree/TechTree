# Cup anemometer construction

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Three or four hemispherical cups (3-5 cm radius) mounted on arms from a central vertical shaft. Shaft rotation speed proportional to wind speed. Calibrate by carrying in known wind or timing rotation in steady breeze. ~1 rotation/second ≈ 1 m/s (depends on cup size and arm length).
