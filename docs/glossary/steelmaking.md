# Steelmaking

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

BOF converters use MgO-C bricks (magnesite-carbon — carbon improves thermal shock resistance and reduces slag wetting). Campaign life: 10,000-25,000 heats (line is periodically patched with gunning mix between relining). Electric arc furnaces: MgO-C sidewalls, water-cooled roof panels. Ladles: alumina-spinel or MgO-C working lining. Continuous casting tundish: alumina or zirconia metering nozzles. Blast furnace: carbon hearth, SiC or alumina bosh, fireclay stack.
