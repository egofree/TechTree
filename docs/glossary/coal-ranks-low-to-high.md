# Coal ranks (low to high)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: foundations

Classification of coal by carbon content and energy density, from low to high: peat (~50% C, low energy), lignite/brown coal (~60-70%), sub-bituminous (~70-76%), bituminous (~76-86%), and anthracite (~86-97%). Higher rank coals have higher heat value, lower moisture, and harder consistency.
