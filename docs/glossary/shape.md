# Shape

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Swing the blowpipe to elongate the bubble (gravity stretches it). Roll the pipe steadily on the arms of the blowing bench. Use jacks to constrict the neck. Use paddles and blocks to shape the body. Reheat in the glory hole (the working opening in the furnace) as needed — glass stiffens noticeably every 30-60 seconds at working temperature.
