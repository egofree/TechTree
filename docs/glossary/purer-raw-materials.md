# Purer raw materials

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

(mined bauxite, zircon sand, quartzite rather than common clay) - **Higher firing temperatures** (1600-2200°C, requiring improved kilns and fuel) - **Precision forming** (dry pressing, isostatic pressing, slip casting with controlled rheology) - **Controlled atmospheres** (reducing, inert, or vacuum sintering)
