# Corona ionizers

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

High-voltage needles (±5-10 kV) generate corona discharge, producing ions. AC ionizers alternate polarity every few milliseconds, providing balanced ion output. DC ionizers use separate positive and negative emitter points. Balance adjustment: ±50V offset from zero (measured with a charged plate monitor). Require periodic cleaning (needle contamination reduces ion output).
