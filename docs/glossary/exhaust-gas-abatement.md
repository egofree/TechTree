# Exhaust gas abatement

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Exhaust gas abatement (critical safety system) — - All dopant gas exhaust lines connect to dedicated abatement system. Burn exhaust gases at 800-1000°C (thermal oxidation). Resulting oxides captured: As₂O₃, P₂O₅, B₂O₃ — solid particulates trapped in HEPA filters, water-soluble compounds in wet scrubbers (NaOH solution). Scrubber water tested for heavy metals before discharge. Spent HEPA filters disposed as hazardous waste. See: [Dopant & Etch Gases](../chemistry/dopant-etch-gases.md)
