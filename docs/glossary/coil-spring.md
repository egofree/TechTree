# Coil spring

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Wind high-carbon steel wire (drawn through successively smaller dies) around mandrel of desired diameter. Heat to austenitizing temperature (~820°C for 0.8% C steel). Quench in oil. Temper at 300-400°C. Compression springs: closed and ground ends. Extension springs: hooks formed at ends.
