# Binding periodicals

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

After a volume year is complete, bind all issues together as a single book. Remove original paper covers, collate issues in order, sew through the fold, bind in cloth or buckram covers with the journal title, volume number, and year stamped on the spine. Bound periodicals are more durable, easier to shelve, and easier to handle than loose issues.
