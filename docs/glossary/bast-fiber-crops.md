# Bast fiber crops

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

(flax, hemp, jute): Sow densely (flax at 40-60 kg/ha) to produce tall, unbranched stalks with long fibers. Excessive branching shortens fiber length. Harvest before seed maturation for best fiber quality (stalks yellow at bottom third for flax; pollen shedding for hemp).
