# Furnace temperatures (800-1200 °C)

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Severe burn hazard. Use heat-resistant gloves (Kevlar, rated to 1000 °C) and face shield when loading/unloading wafer boats. Quartz furnace tubes are fragile when hot — avoid thermal shock (push/pull boats slowly, <5 cm/min). Allow furnaces to cool below 200 °C before maintenance.
