# Example 2, undershot wheel

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

A river with no head but 1.5 m/s flow velocity, channel width 1.5 m, paddle depth 0.3 m. Effective flow area: 0.45 m². Flow intercepted: 0.45 × 1.5 = 0.675 m³/s. Equivalent head from velocity: H = v²/(2g) = 1.5²/19.62 = 0.115 m. At 25% efficiency: P = 0.25 × 1000 × 9.81 × 0.675 × 0.115 = 190 W ≈ 0.25 HP. Marginal but usable for small tasks.
