# Seamless tube

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Pierced from a heated round billet on a rotary piercing mill (Mannesmann process). The billet is rotated and pushed over a pointed mandrel — tensile stresses at the center cause a cavity, and the mandrel shapes the bore. Further rolled on a plug mill or mandrel mill to final wall thickness. Diameter range: 20-700 mm.
