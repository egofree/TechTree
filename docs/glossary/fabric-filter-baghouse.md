# Fabric filter (baghouse)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Fine particulate filtration through woven or felted fabric bags (polypropylene, PTFE, or fiberglass depending on temperature). Filtration efficiency: >99% for particles >1 µm. Must operate above tar dew point (~150-200°C) to prevent blinding. Pulse-jet cleaning: compressed air pulses inflate bags in reverse, dislodging dust cake.
