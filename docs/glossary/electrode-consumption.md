# Electrode consumption

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals, silicon

Electrode consumption: 80-120 kg per tonne of silicon produced. Electrodes burn away at the tip (oxidation and sublimation of carbon in the reaction zone at >1800°C). New electrode sections are added at the top (Søderberg paste or threaded prebaked joints).
