# Brush and roller

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Simplest — manual application. Brush: natural (china bristle for solvent-borne) or synthetic (nylon/polyester for water-borne). Roller: woven or knitted fabric sleeve on a rotating core. Nap length 6-25 mm (shorter for smooth surfaces, longer for textured). Transfer efficiency: 40-60% (fraction of paint reaching the substrate — the rest stays on brush/roller or is lost to overspray).
