# Letterpress and offset inks

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

High-viscosity paste inks (10-50 Pa·s). Formulation: pigment 15-20%, resin binder (rosin-modified phenolic or alkyd) 20-30%, mineral oil or vegetable oil solvent 40-50%, additives 5-10% (waxes for rub resistance, driers, gelling agents). Offset printing: ink transferred from plate to rubber blanket to paper — requires precise ink-water balance (lithographic principle: ink adheres to image areas, water to non-image areas).
