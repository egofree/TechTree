# Spark optical emission spectroscopy (Spark OES)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Electric spark vaporizes a small area of the solid silicon sample. Emitted light is dispersed by a grating spectrometer, and the intensity of characteristic wavelengths quantifies each element. Measures: Si, Fe, Al, Ca, Ti, Mn, Cr, Ni, Cu, V, and others. Accuracy: ±0.01% for trace elements. Faster than XRF for trace metals. Requires calibration with certified reference standards.
