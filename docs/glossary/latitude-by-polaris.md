# Latitude by Polaris

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

In northern hemisphere, Polaris altitude above horizon equals observer's latitude. Measure with astrolabe (accuracy ±1°, or 60 nautical miles) or quadrant (accuracy ±0.5°). At the equator Polaris sits on the horizon (0°). At London (51.5°N) Polaris is 51.5° above horizon.
