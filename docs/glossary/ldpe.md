# LDPE

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

LDPE (low-density) — High-pressure free radical process. 1000-3000 atm, 100-300°C, peroxide initiator (e.g., benzoyl peroxide). Tubular or autoclave reactor. Branched chain structure gives flexible, transparent material. See: [Thermoplastic Polymers](../polymers/thermoplastics.md)
