# Spraying

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

Spray glaze with an airbrush or spray gun at 15-25 psi. Build the coating in 3-4 light passes rather than one heavy pass (runs and drips are unrecoverable after firing). Requires a spray booth with exhaust ventilation. Best for large pieces, multi-color effects, and precise layering.
