# Vacuum Swing Adsorption (VSA) oxygen

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Lithium-exchanged zeolite (LiLSX) selectively adsorbs N₂ from air at near-ambient pressure (1.2-1.5 bar). Desorption by applying vacuum (50-100 mbar). Simpler than PSA (no compressor — blower only), lower energy. Purity: 90-93% O₂. Flow: 5-150 tonnes/day. Suitable for steelmaking, glass furnaces, wastewater treatment.
