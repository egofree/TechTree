# ESD (electrostatic discharge) control

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

MOS devices are destroyed by static discharges as low as 50-100 V. Human body model (HBM) discharge: 100 pF through 1.5 kΩ — a person walking on a synthetic floor can accumulate 5-20 kV.
