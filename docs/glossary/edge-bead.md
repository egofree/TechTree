# Edge bead

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Centrifugal force drives excess resist to the wafer edge, forming a thickened ring (edge bead) 2-5 mm wide. Edge bead removal (EBR): spray solvent (PGMEA or ethyl lactate) at the wafer edge while spinning at 500-1500 RPM. Removes the bead, preventing subsequent contact printing problems (edge bead holds the mask away from the wafer center).
