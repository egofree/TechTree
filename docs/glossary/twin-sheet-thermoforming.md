# Twin-sheet thermoforming

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Heat two sheets simultaneously, form each against a separate mold half, then clamp the two mold halves together while the sheets are still hot — the two halves weld together at the contact surfaces to form a hollow, double-wall part. Used for: pallets, structural panels, hollow luggage, double-wall containers.
