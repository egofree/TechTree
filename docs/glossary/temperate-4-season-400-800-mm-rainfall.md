# Temperate (4-season, 400-800 mm rainfall)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

Wheat, barley, potato, rye, oats. Wheat and potato together provide complete nutrition (wheat: protein and calories; potato: vitamin C, potassium, calories). Rye (*Secale cereale*) is the fallback — tolerates poor acid soils and late planting that would fail for wheat. Yield: 1.5-2.5 tonnes/ha rye on marginal land - **Mediterranean (hot dry summer, mild wet winter)**: Wheat, barley, olive, grape, chickpea. Winter-sown cereals exploit rainy season.
