# Electrolyte circulation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

CuSO₄ + H₂SO₄ solution continuously pumped through tanks (20-30 L/min per tank) to maintain uniform temperature (50-65°C — warmed by steam heat exchangers) and composition, and to prevent stratification. Additives (glue, thiourea, chloride ions at 20-40 mg/L) promote smooth, dense cathode deposits and suppress dendritic growth that causes short circuits.
