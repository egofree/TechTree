# Kanthal APM

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

(Fe-22Cr-5.8Al, with additions): Operates to 1400°C in air. Forms a protective Al₂O₃ scale instead of Cr₂O₃, which is more stable at high temperature. Higher resistivity (1.45 Ω·mm²/m) than Nichrome, meaning shorter elements for the same power. Becomes brittle after prolonged high-temperature exposure due to aluminum precipitation, making element replacement the norm rather than repair.
