# Nickel carbonyl

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(Mond process): One of the most toxic industrial gases. TLV-TWA: 0.001 ppm (1 ppb). Immediately fatal at 30 ppm. Colorless, odorless. Continuous air monitoring mandatory. Full-face SCBA for any potential exposure. Emergency evacuation if detectors alarm.
