# Cold pressing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Crush oilseeds (flax, rapeseed, castor, olive, sunflower) in screw press or wedge press at room temperature. Oil expressed, collected. Cake (pressed seed) may be re-pressed warm for additional yield. Castor oil: press castor beans. Very high viscosity (100x olive oil) — excellent for high-speed, high-temperature applications.
