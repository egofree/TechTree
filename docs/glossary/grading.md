# Grading

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Cut high spots, fill low spots. Target cross-section: crowned surface (10-15 cm higher at center than edges) for drainage, 3-5 m wide for two-way cart traffic. Ditches on both sides, 30-50 cm deep. Compact roadbed by rolling (heavy stone or iron roller, 1-3 tonnes, drawn by animals or winch).
