# TiAlN

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

TiAlN (titanium aluminum nitride) — Purple/grey PVD coating, 2-4 µm. Hardness ~3200 HV. Al₂O₃ forms in-situ at high temperature, providing oxidation protection to ~900°C. The dominant coating for steel machining. See: [Powder Metallurgy & Advanced Materials](../metals/powder-metallurgy.md)
