# Macadam road

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Three layers of progressively finer broken stone (base: 10 cm stone, middle: 5 cm, surface: 2-3 cm). Each layer compacted with heavy roller. Interlocking angular stones create stable surface. Water-bound macadam: water + stone dust fills voids → sets hard. Tar-bound macadam: hot coal tar penetrates surface → waterproof, more durable.
