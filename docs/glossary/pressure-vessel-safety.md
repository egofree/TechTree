# Pressure vessel safety

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Install pressure relief valve at 1.1× MAWP (maximum allowable working pressure). Rupture disc as secondary relief. Vacuum columns need wall thickness ≥3-5 mm steel for <1 m diameter to resist collapse. Hydrostatic test at 1.5× design pressure before commissioning. Column differential pressure monitoring — sudden increase signals flooding, reduce boilup immediately.
