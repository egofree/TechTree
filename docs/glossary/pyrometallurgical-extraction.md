# Pyrometallurgical extraction

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Pyrometallurgical extraction (Imperial Smelting Process) — Roast**: ZnS + 1.5O₂ → ZnO + SO₂ at 900-1000°C in fluidized-bed roasters. SO₂ is captured for sulfuric acid production (see Mineral Acids). The "dead-roasted" calcine is >98% ZnO. - **Reduction**: ZnO + CO → Zn(g) + CO₂ at 1000-1100°C. The Imperial Smelting Furnace (ISF) is a blast furnace charged with sintered ZnO calcine and preheated coke. Air blast at 1000°C.
