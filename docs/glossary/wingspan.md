# Wingspan

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

8-12 m. **Wing area**: 12-20 m². **Wing loading**: 20-40 kg/m² (lower = lower stall speed, gentler handling). **Empty weight**: 150-250 kg. **Gross weight**: 250-400 kg. **Stall speed**: 35-50 km/h. **Cruise speed**: 70-120 km/h. **Range**: 100-300 km.
