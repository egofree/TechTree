# Crepe rubber

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Coagulated latex passed through creping rollers (deeply grooved, producing a creped texture) and air-dried (not smoked). Pale crepe (highest grade, from carefully selected latex) and estate brown crepe (from cup lump and scrap). Air-dried crepe has cleaner taste and odor than smoked sheet — preferred for food-contact and medical applications.
