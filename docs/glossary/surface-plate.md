# Surface plate

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

(first reference flat): Three plates, scraped alternately against each other (Whitworth's three-plate method). Start with a known-flat granite surface plate if available, or generate flats from scratch: 1. Scrape plate A against plate B until they show even bearing (red marking compound shows contact points). 2. Scrape plate C against A. 3. Scrape B against C. 4. Repeat.
