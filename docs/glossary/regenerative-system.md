# Regenerative system

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Four checker chambers (two for air, two for fuel gas) filled with refractory brick checkerwork. Hot exhaust gases pass through one pair, heating the bricks to 1200-1300°C. Every 15-20 minutes, reversing valves switch flow direction — incoming cold air and fuel gas now pass through the hot checkers, absorbing stored heat. This cycle cuts fuel consumption by 60-70% compared to non-regenerative furnaces.
