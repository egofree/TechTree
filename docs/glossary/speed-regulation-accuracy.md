# Speed regulation accuracy

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

For electrical generation, the turbine must maintain speed within ±0.1% to produce AC power at the correct frequency (50.00 ± 0.05 Hz or 60.00 ± 0.06 Hz). This requires a governor with high gain and fast response. Electro-hydraulic governors achieve this with electronic PID (proportional-integral-derivative) controllers driving hydraulic servo valves. The integral term eliminates steady-state speed error; the derivative term dampens oscillations.
