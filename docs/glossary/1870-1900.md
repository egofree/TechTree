# 1870-1900

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Pneumatic drill refinement. Ingersoll Rock Drill (1871), Leyner drill (1897, first with hollow drill steel for water flush). Rates climbed to 30-80 m/day with mechanized drills. Hand steel persisted in narrow veins and small mines where jackhammers could not fit.
