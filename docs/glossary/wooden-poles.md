# Wooden poles

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Treated timber poles (preservative: creosote or pentachlorophenol) in lengths of 8-15 m, set 1.5-2.5 m into the ground. Span between poles: 40-100 m depending on conductor weight and terrain. Pole class is determined by the horizontal force at the top from conductor tension and wind loading.
