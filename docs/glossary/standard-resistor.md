# Standard resistor

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement

Manganin wire (84% Cu, 12% Mn, 4% Ni — near-zero temperature coefficient of resistance, ~20 ppm/°C) wound bifilar (folded back on itself to cancel inductance) on brass bobbin. Sealed in oil-filled container (oil provides thermal mass and insulation from humidity). Values: 0.001 Ω to 100,000 Ω. Stability: ±10 ppm/year.
