# Cleanroom garments

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, health

Cleanroom garments (Photolithography) — woven synthetic coveralls, hoods, booties (from synthetic polymer fibers — see Polymers). Lint-free. Washed in ultra-pure water. Worn over street clothes. Changed every entry to cleanroom. See: [Semiconductor Packaging & Testing](../chemistry/packaging-testing.md) | [Medicine & Surgery](../health/medicine.md)
