# LP last-row blades

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Precipitation-hardened stainless steel or titanium alloy (Ti-6Al-4V). Titanium preferred for the longest blades (>900 mm) due to its high strength-to-weight ratio — reduces centrifugal stress on the rotor. Titanium also resists water-droplet erosion better than steel.
