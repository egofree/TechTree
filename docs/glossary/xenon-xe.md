# Xenon (Xe)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

0.09 ppm in air. Boiling point -108°C (highest of the noble gases). Co-extracts with krypton. Additional distillation separates Kr from Xe. Uses: ion propulsion (spacecraft), anesthesia, high-intensity lamps. Extremely valuable — ~$10-50/L of pure Xe gas.
