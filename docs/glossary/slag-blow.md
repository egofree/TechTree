# Slag blow

> **Type**: process | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Air oxidizes FeS in the matte to FeO + SO₂. Silica flux added to form fayalite slag (2FeO·SiO₂). Slag is poured off by tilting the converter. Duration: 2-6 hours depending on iron content. This stage removes most iron and sulfur.
