# Current density and voltage

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Current density and voltage: 200-300 A/m² (cathode current density). Cell voltage only 0.2-0.3 V (very low because the thermodynamic potentials of anode dissolution and cathode deposition are nearly equal). Energy consumption: 250-350 kWh per tonne of refined copper — among the lowest of all electrolytic processes. Daily cathode production: ~0.8 kg Cu per m² of cathode area at 250 A/m². See: [Electrolysis](../chemistry/electrolysis.md)
