# Slurry pipeline

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Transport finely ground ore as a slurry (30-50% solids by weight) through steel or HDPE pipes. Pumps: centrifugal slurry pumps with hardened impellers. Velocity: 1.5-3.0 m/s (too slow → particles settle and block the pipe; too fast → excessive wear). Used for long-distance transport (1-100+ km) between mine and concentrator or between concentrator and smelter. The Bougainville copper slurry pipeline transported concentrate 27 km.
