# Glass composition for fiber

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: glass

Chemical formulations optimized for glass fiber production: E-glass (alumino-borosilicate, 54% SiO₂, general purpose), S-glass (magnesia-alumina-silicate, 65% SiO₂, high strength), and A-glass (soda-lime, window glass composition). Composition determines fiber strength, temperature resistance, and chemical durability.
