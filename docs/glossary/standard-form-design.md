# Standard form design

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Every common document type should have a pre-printed (or pre-drawn) form with labeled fields — spaces for date, originator, recipient, subject, quantities, signatures/witnesses. Forms reduce ambiguity, ensure completeness, and speed up recording. Critical form types: work orders, material requisitions, inspection reports, inventory tallies, property records, contracts.
