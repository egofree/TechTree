# Temperature capability

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Ferritic steels to ~500°C, austenitic stainless to ~750°C, Ni-superalloys to ~1,000°C (cooled components in gas turbines to ~1,100°C). Creep rate accelerates exponentially with temperature — a factor of 10× per 20-30°C increase in many alloys.
