# Glory hole radiation

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

The reheating furnace (glory hole) emits intense infrared and visible radiation. Prolonged exposure causes cataracts. Wear didymium glass lenses (which filter sodium flare, the bright yellow sodium emission from hot soda-lime glass) for soda-lime work. Use shaded lenses (#3-#5) for borosilicate, and #5-#7 for quartz work.
