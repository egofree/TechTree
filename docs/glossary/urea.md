# Urea

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

(CO(NH₂)₂, 46% N — highest nitrogen content of any solid fertilizer): - **Step 1**: 2NH₃ + CO₂ → NH₂COONH₄ (ammonium carbamate) at 140-200°C, 14-20 MPa. - **Step 2**: NH₂COONH₄ → CO(NH₂)₂ + H₂O (endothermic dehydration). Conversion: 50-70% per pass in conventional plants; 70-80% in advanced designs with higher temperature/pressure. - **Unreacted recycle**: The urea solution contains unreacted carbamate and NH₃/CO₂.
