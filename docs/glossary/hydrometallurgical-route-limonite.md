# Hydrometallurgical route (limonite)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

High-pressure acid leach (HPAL). Ore slurry leached with sulfuric acid at 240-270°C, 4-5 MPa in titanium-lined autoclaves. Dissolves Ni and Co; iron precipitates as hematite. Solvent extraction or precipitation recovers Ni. Complex, capital-intensive, but growing.
