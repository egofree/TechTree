# Water scrubber

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, gas-handling

Gas bubbled through water tank (or sprayed with water in packed column). Dissolves water-soluble tars and ammonia. Cools gas to near-ambient temperature. Removes fine particulates. Water must be circulated and periodically replaced (tar-laden water is a disposal issue — contains phenols and creosotes).
