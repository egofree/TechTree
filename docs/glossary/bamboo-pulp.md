# Bamboo pulp

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Bamboo (Bambusa species) contains 40-50% cellulose, making it a viable pulp feedstock. Used extensively in India and China where bamboo is abundant. Kraft pulping of bamboo: similar conditions to hardwood, yield 42-48%. Fiber length 1.5-3.0 mm (comparable to softwood), giving excellent strength properties.
