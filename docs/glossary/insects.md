# Insects

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals, knowledge

(silverfish, booklice, beetles): Maintain temperature below 20°C where possible. Cedar lining in storage cabinets repels some insects. Periodic inspection: look for frass (insect droppings), exit holes in wood, feeding damage on paper edges. Freezing treatment: bag item, freeze at -20°C for 72 hours, thaw slowly — kills all life stages without chemicals.
