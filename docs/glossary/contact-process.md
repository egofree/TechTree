# Contact Process

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Contact Process (later, higher purity — 96-98% H₂SO₄) — - **Principle**: Catalytic oxidation of SO₂ to SO₃, then absorption in H₂SO₄. - **Steps**: 1. **SO₂ generation**: Burn elemental sulfur in dry air. S + O₂ → SO₂. Gas must be clean (dust, arsenic poison the catalyst). Electrostatic precipitator or water scrubber for cleaning. 2. **Catalytic conversion**: Pass SO₂ + air over vanadium pentoxide (V₂O₅) catalyst at 400-450°C. 2SO₂ + O₂ ⇌ 2SO₃.
