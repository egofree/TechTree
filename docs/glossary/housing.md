# Housing

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals, knowledge

Heavy wooden frame (elm or oak timbers, mortise-and-tenon joints, bolted with iron rods for rigidity). Approximately 1.8 m tall, 1.2 m wide, 0.6 m deep. Weight: 200-300 kg. Later iron-frame presses (19th century) are more rigid, allowing higher pressures and more consistent impression.
