# Medicinal Plants

> **Node ID**: plants.medicinal-plants
> **Domain**: [Plants & Botanical Resources](./)
> **Timeline**: Years 0-10
> **Outputs**: medicinal_herbs, therapeutic_compounds, herbal_remedies

### Overview

Identification, preparation, and application of plant species with therapeutic properties for treating illness, injury, and disease. Foundation of all pharmacology. Basic herbal remedies available from Year 0 reduce mortality and improve labor productivity.
