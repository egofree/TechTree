# Drum geometry

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: computing

A brass cylinder approximately 40 mm diameter × 50 mm long, with 9 longitudinal ribs (teeth) of increasing axial extent. The first rib has a tooth length of 1 unit, the second 2 units, up to the ninth rib at 9 units. A 10th position (no teeth) represents zero.
