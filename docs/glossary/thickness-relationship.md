# Thickness relationship

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

t ∝ 1/√ω, where t is film thickness and ω is angular velocity. Doubling spin speed reduces thickness by ~30% (1/√2). For a given resist viscosity η and spin speed ω: t ≈ k × (η/ω)^(1/2), where k is a constant depending on resist solids content and evaporation rate.
