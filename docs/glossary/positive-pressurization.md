# Positive pressurization

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

The cleanroom is maintained at 10-20 Pa (0.04-0.08 inches of water gauge) above the surrounding corridors. This ensures that when doors open, clean air flows outward, preventing infiltration of contaminated air from less-clean areas. Pressure is maintained by supplying slightly more air than is exhausted. Pressure differential is monitored continuously with a magnahelic gauge; an alarm sounds if pressurization is lost.
