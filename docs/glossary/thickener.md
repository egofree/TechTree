# Thickener

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

A large circular tank (10-30 m diameter) with a slowly rotating rake mechanism. Slurry (25-35% solids) is fed into the center. Solids settle to the bottom, where the rake pushes them to a central discharge. Clear water overflows the perimeter weir. Thickened underflow: 50-70% solids. Thickener area required: 0.5-5 m² per tonne of dry solids per day depending on particle size. The settled solids are easier and cheaper to filter than dilute slurry.
