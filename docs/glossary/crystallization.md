# Crystallization

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Over time at room temperature, gutta-percha becomes more crystalline and therefore harder and more brittle. This is a gradual, irreversible process. Freshly processed material is more thermoplastic and easier to mold than aged material. The degree of crystallinity increases from ~55% (freshly processed) to ~65-70% (after 5-10 years storage).
