# Delivery and storage

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Refractory bricks are shipped on pallets (500-1000 kg each) wrapped in plastic film for moisture protection. Storage life: unlimited for fired bricks if kept dry. Castable and gunning mixes (containing calcium aluminate cement) have a shelf life of 6-12 months when stored in original packaging under cover (cement hydrates absorb moisture from the air, pre-hydrating and losing strength).
