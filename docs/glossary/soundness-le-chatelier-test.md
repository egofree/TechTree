# Soundness (Le Chatelier test)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Detects excessive free lime (CaO) or magnesia (MgO) in cement that would cause delayed expansion and cracking in hardened concrete. Cement paste placed in a Le Chatelier mold, immersed in water at 20°C for 24 hours, then boiled for 3 hours. Expansion must not exceed 10 mm. Free CaO hydrates slowly (CaO + H₂O → Ca(OH)₂, volume increase ~100%) after the concrete has hardened, causing disruptive expansion.
