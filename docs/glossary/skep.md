# Skep

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

(woven basket hive): Inverted basket 30-40 cm diameter at base, 25-35 cm height. Woven from straw or willow withies reinforced with hazel sticks for rigidity. Coat exterior with cow dung mixed with clay for weatherproofing — dries to a hard, water-resistant shell. No internal structure; bees build comb freely attached to the interior walls.
