# Bronze

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

(copper-tin alloy, typically 88-92% Cu, 8-12% Sn): Melts at ~950°C. Easier to cast than iron — lower temperature, more fluid, less shrinkage. Ideal for bearings, bushings, gears, decorative hardware, and marine fittings. Very low shrinkage (~1%) reduces casting defects.
