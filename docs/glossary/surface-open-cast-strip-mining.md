# Surface / open-cast (strip mining)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Remove overburden (soil and rock above the coal seam) with dragline or shovel, then dig coal. Used for near-surface seams (<60 m depth). Overburden-to-coal ratio must be favorable (typically <10:1). Fastest, cheapest, safest method. Reclaim land after mining by replacing overburden and topsoil. Suitable for lignite and sub-bituminous deposits. The method of choice where geology permits — most of the world's lignite is mined this way.
