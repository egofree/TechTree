# Plasma/RIE gases

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

SF₆ (GWP 23,900× CO₂), CF₄ (GWP 6,630× CO₂), and NF₃ (GWP 17,200× CO₂) are potent greenhouse gases. Install point-of-use abatement (burn boxes or plasma destruct units, >99 % destruction efficiency) on all exhaust lines. NF₃ and CF₄ also produce toxic byproducts (HF, COF₂) in plasma — downstream scrubbing required.
