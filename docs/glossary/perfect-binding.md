# Perfect binding

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Stack single sheets (not folded signatures) and roughen the spine edge with a sanding belt to expose fiber. Apply a layer of EVA (ethylene-vinyl acetate) hot-melt adhesive at 170-190°C to the roughened spine. The adhesive penetrates the roughened fibers and bonds them. Wrap a cover around the glued spine and clamp until set (30-60 seconds). Used for paperback books, thick magazines, and telephone directories.
