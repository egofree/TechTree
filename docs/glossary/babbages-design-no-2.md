# Babbage's Design No. 2

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: computing

(1847-1849, never built in his lifetime): 7th-order polynomial evaluator with 31-digit numbers. Estimated 25,000 parts, total weight approximately 15 tons. The Science Museum London built it from Babbage's plans in 1991: it works perfectly, vindicating his design after 150 years.
