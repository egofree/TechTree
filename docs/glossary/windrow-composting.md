# Windrow composting

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Organic waste (food scraps, yard waste, agricultural residue, treated sewage sludge) arranged in long piles (windrows) 1.5-2.5 m high, 3-5 m wide. Turn weekly with front-end loader or compost turner to aerate. Temperature rises to 55-70°C from microbial activity (thermophilic phase), killing pathogens and weed seeds.
