# Turbidity

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Fill clear glass container, hold against white background. Cloudy water indicates suspended particles (clay, silt, organic matter). Turbidity >5 NTU is visible; >1 NTU may shield bacteria from chlorination. Turbidity tube (graduated plastic tube with black cross pattern on the bottom): fill until cross disappears, read turbidity from calibration marks.
