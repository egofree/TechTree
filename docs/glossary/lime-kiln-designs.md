# Lime kiln designs

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

(1) Flare kiln (batch): stone arch with fuel underneath, limestone stacked above. Simple, labor-intensive. (2) Shaft kiln (continuous): feed at top, discharge at bottom, fuel injected at middle. Most common for moderate scale. (3) Rotary kiln: inclined rotating tube 30-60 m long. Fuel and limestone enter same end, product exits the other. Best quality and throughput.
