# Cell construction

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, energy

Two nickel-plated steel or pure nickel electrode plates separated by 2-5 mm gap. Diaphragm of asbestos or polymer fabric (polypropylene, polysulfone) prevents gas mixing. Electrolyte: 25-30% KOH in deionized water (specific gravity ~1.23-1.28). Cell body: carbon steel (KOH is less corrosive than NaOH to steel at operating temperature). Gasket: EPDM rubber.
