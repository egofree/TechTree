# Pultrusion

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Pultrusion (continuous profile production) — Fiberglass rovings are pulled through a resin bath (polyester or epoxy), then through a heated die (chromium-plated steel, 150-200°C, 0.5-1.0 m long) that shapes the composite into a constant cross-section (rod, tube, angle, channel, I-beam). The die cures the resin continuously as the profile passes through at 0.5-3 m/min. - Pull speed: 0.5-3 m/min.
