# Stellite

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(Co-30Cr-5W-1C, various grades): Cobalt matrix with massive chromium and tungsten carbide volume. Outstanding wear resistance, corrosion resistance, and hot-hardness. Used for hard-facing (weld overlay on worn parts), valve seats, cutting tools for high-speed machining of difficult materials, surgical prosthetics. Hardness: HRC 40-55 depending on grade.
