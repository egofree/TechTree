# Crown glass

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

(low dispersion): Soda-lime or borosilicate base, RI 1.50-1.54, Abbe number 55-65. Used for the convex (positive) elements in achromatic lens doublets. The low dispersion means it bends different wavelengths of light nearly equally, reducing chromatic aberration.
