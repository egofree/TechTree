# Total aerobic microbial count

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

<10⁵ CFU/g (colony-forming units per gram) for oral dosage forms. Plate diluted sample on nutrient agar, incubate at 35°C for 48-72 hours, count colonies. Exceeding this limit indicates poor hygiene during production or degraded raw materials.
