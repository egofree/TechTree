# Copper (Cu₂O/CuO)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Red (metallic copper colloid in reducing flame), turquoise blue (Cu²⁺ in oxidizing flame), or green depending on oxidation state and concentration. 0.5-3% addition. Red copper glass ("gold ruby" when gold is also present) requires careful control of the reducing atmosphere.
