# BSF defatting

> **Type**: process | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Pressing dried BSF larvae in a screw press or hydraulic press to extract fat (BSF oil). Yield: 15-25% of dry weight as oil. Remaining defatted meal has 55-60% protein — comparable to premium fish meal. BSF oil usable as animal feed, biodiesel, or soap.
