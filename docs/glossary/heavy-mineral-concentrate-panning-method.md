# Heavy mineral concentrate (panning) method

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Pan 10-15 kg of stream sediment to a 10-30 g heavy mineral concentrate. Examine under binocular microscope at 10-30× magnification. Identify gold, cassiterite, scheelite, chromite, and other heavy minerals. Count grains per kilogram of original sediment. Gold counts >5 grains/kg concentrate indicate economic potential. Scheelite (CaWO₄, tungsten ore) fluoresces blue-white under shortwave UV — scan the concentrate with a UV lamp.
