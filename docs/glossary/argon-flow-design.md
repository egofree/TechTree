# Argon flow design

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Gas enters above the crystal, flows down past the crystal and melt, exits at the bottom. Flow rate 20-60 L/min. The flow path must avoid dead zones where SiO or CO accumulates and re-deposits on the crystal surface. Baffles direct flow along the crucible wall to sweep SiO downward and away.
