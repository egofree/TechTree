# Calcium gluconate gel (2.5%) must be on-site and accessible before any HF work begins.

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Apply to exposed area immediately and seek emergency medical treatment — do not wait for pain. PPE: neoprene or thick nitrile gloves (double-gloving recommended), face shield, chemical splash apron, closed-toe shoes. Work in fume hood with local exhaust. Never store or handle HF in glass containers — use PTFE or polyethylene only.
