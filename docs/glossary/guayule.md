# Guayule

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Guayule (*Parthenium argentatum*) — shrub native to the southwestern US and Mexico. Harvest whole shrubs, crush, and extract rubber by flotation in water. Lower yield per acre than Hevea but grows in arid regions. See: [Rubber Production](../polymers/rubber.md) | [Natural Rubber & Vulcanization](../polymers/natural.md)
