# Eop

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

— NaOH extraction with oxygen and hydrogen peroxide reinforcement at 70-80°C. Dissolves oxidized lignin fragments. O₂ and H₂O₂ provide additional delignification without chlorine. (3) **D₁** — second ClO₂ stage, higher temperature (70-75°C). Further bleaching. (4) **EP** — alkaline extraction with H₂O₂. (5) **D₂** — final ClO₂ stage, targets >88% ISO brightness.
