# Rag paper production

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Rag paper production (pre-industrial detail) — Rag collection and sorting**: Linen (flax) and cotton rags sorted by quality. Remove buttons, seams, and non-fiber contaminants. Cut into 5-10 cm squares. - **Stamping**: Rags soaked in water for 3-7 days (fermentation breaks down inter-fiber bonds). Stamp with water-powered trip hammer (stamper) — wooden shafts armed with iron nails beat the rag mass in a stone trough.
