# O-series

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(Oil-hardening, e.g., O1): 0.9% C, 1.0% Mn, 0.5% Cr, 0.5% W. Moderate alloy content, deep hardening in oil quench. Low distortion during quenching — important for precision tooling. Used for gauges, dies, cutting tools. Hardness: HRC 57-62.
