# Low-k dielectrics

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: vlsi-scaling

Replace SiO₂ (k ≈ 4.0) to reduce interconnect capacitance: - Organosilicate glass (OSG/SiCOH): k ≈ 2.5-3.0, deposited by PECVD. - Porous OSG: k ≈ 2.0-2.5, introduced pores reduce dielectric constant but weaken mechanical strength. - Air gap (ultimate low-k): k ≈ 1.0, selective removal of sacrificial material leaves air gaps between wires. Research/early production.
