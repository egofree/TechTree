# Nest box construction

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

a wooden box with interior dimensions of roughly 40 × 40 × 60 cm (width × depth × height). Entrance hole: 15 cm diameter, positioned near the top of the front panel. No perch (perches attract larger raptors and predators). Mount at 4-6 m height on a barn wall, silo, or utility pole. Line the floor with 2-3 cm of wood shavings or dry straw.
