# Sine bar accuracy

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement

±3 arcseconds when used with grade 0 gauge blocks on a surface plate of grade A or better flatness. The sine bar provides angle measurement traceable to the length standard (gauge blocks) — the angle is computed from sin(θ) = h/L, where h is the gauge block stack height and L is the sine bar roller center distance (typically 100 mm or 200 mm).
