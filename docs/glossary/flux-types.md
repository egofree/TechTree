# Flux types

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: machine-tools

Soldering and welding fluxes classified by activity and residue: rosin fluxes (mild, for electronics), organic acid fluxes (moderate, general purpose), inorganic acid fluxes (aggressive, for difficult metals), and brazing fluxes (high-temperature borax-based). Flux removes surface oxides to enable metallurgical bonding.
