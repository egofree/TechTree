# Cermets

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

TiC or Ti(C,N) based composites with Ni-Mo binder. Combustion synthesis produces ultra-fine TiC grain (0.5-2 µm) in a single step. Hardness 1400-1800 HV, toughness 8-12 MPa√m. Used for high-speed finishing of steel — lower coefficient of friction than WC-Co, reducing built-up edge on the cutting tool.
