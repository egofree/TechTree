# Half-face respirator

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

with P100 filter cartridges: tight seal required on face. Fit testing mandatory (quantitative fit test: the wearer performs exercises in a test aerosol, and the respirator must demonstrate a fit factor > 100). Inhalation resistance increases as filter loads with dust. Cartridges replaced when breathing becomes noticeably difficult or after 40-80 hours of use.
