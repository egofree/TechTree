# Petrochemical

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

FCC unit: high-alumina castable with steel fiber reinforcement (withstands erosion by catalyst particles at 700°C). Hydrogen reformer: high-alumina castable (resistant to hydrogen attack at 900°C — hydrogen reduces SiO₂ from fireclay, causing volume changes). Ethylene cracking furnace: high-alumina castable (1200°C, exposure to carburizing atmosphere).
