# Aluminum foil

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

0.02-0.05 mm thickness for blister packs (push-through packaging for tablets and capsules). Aluminum provides essentially zero permeability to moisture, oxygen, and light when pinhole-free. Foil below 0.025 mm may have pinholes. Cold-formed blister packs use 0.045-0.060 mm aluminum foil formed into cavities by a die — provides superior barrier compared to thermoformed PVC blisters. Aluminum-aluminum laminates (foil + adhesive + PET film) provide tamper evidence and moisture protection.
