# Rope drive

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals, energy

(transmitting rotation over distance): Endless hemp or wire rope running on grooved pulleys (V-groove for grip). Can transmit 5-50 HP over 100-500 m. Rope speed 10-20 m/s. Sag and stretch require tensioning device. Superseded flat rods for new installations once good rope was available.
