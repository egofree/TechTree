# Form the bulb

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement

Heat one end of the capillary tube in gas-oxygen flame until soft. Blow gently through the open end to expand a bulb (5-10 mm diameter). The bulb must be symmetrical and thin-walled (~0.5 mm) for fast thermal response. Wall thickness uniformity matters — thin spots risk breakage during filling and use.
