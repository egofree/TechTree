# Liquid production vs gaseous

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

An ASU configured for maximum gaseous product output produces minimal liquid (just enough to maintain column inventory). To produce liquid products (LOX, LIN, LAR), additional refrigeration is needed. A liquefier (dedicated nitrogen compression and expansion cycle) adds 30-50% to the plant's power consumption but produces 100-500 tonnes/day of liquid products for distribution by tanker truck.
