# Improvements through 1820s-1840s

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Roberts' self-acting loom added automatic shuttle changing and warp let-off. Houldsworth's loom improved the shedding mechanism. By 1850, a single power-loom weaver tended 2-4 looms simultaneously. Production: 20-40 m²/hour per loom — 10-20× handloom rate.
