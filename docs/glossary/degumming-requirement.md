# Degumming requirement

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

Raw ramie contains 20-30% gum (pectin and hemicellulose). Must be degummed before spinning. Chemical degumming: boil in 1-2% NaOH solution for 1-2 hours. Biological degumming: ferment in water 24-48 hours - Fiber length: 10-20 cm individual cells; white, silky luster. Used for fishing nets (resists rot in water), fine cloth
