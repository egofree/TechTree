# Compression ignition detail

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

The diesel engine compresses air alone to 14:1 to 24:1 ratio, raising the charge temperature to 700-900°C at top dead center. Fuel is then injected directly into this hot compressed air. The auto-ignition temperature of diesel fuel is roughly 210°C, so the compressed air is well above the threshold.
