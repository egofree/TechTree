# Final closure

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

When landfill reaches design capacity, install final cover: low-permeability clay layer (0.5 m), geomembrane, drainage layer, topsoil (0.3 m), and vegetation (grass). Cap slopes >5% to promote runoff. Monitor groundwater quality (test wells upgradient and downgradient) for 30+ years after closure.
