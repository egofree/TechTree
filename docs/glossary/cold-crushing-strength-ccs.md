# Cold crushing strength (CCS)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Load a 50 mm cube or cylinder of refractory material in compression until failure (ASTM C133). Record maximum load / cross-sectional area. CCS correlates with abrasion resistance and structural load-bearing capacity. Typical values: fireclay 15-50 MPa, high-alumina 50-100 MPa, magnesite 40-80 MPa.
