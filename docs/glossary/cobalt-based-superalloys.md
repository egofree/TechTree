# Cobalt-based superalloys

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: metals

High-temperature alloys using cobalt as the base metal (rather than nickel), with additions of Cr, W, Mo, and Ta for strengthening. Maintain strength above 1000°C; used in gas turbine vanes, combustion chambers, and furnace components. Less common than nickel superalloys but superior in hot corrosion resistance.
