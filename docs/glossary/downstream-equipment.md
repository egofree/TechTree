# Downstream equipment

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Cooling bath (water tank, often with sizing sleeves for pipes — the pipe passes through a precisely-sized cooled metal ring that sets the OD while water contacts the outer surface), puller (pair of rollers or caterpillar belt at controlled speed — maintains tension and dimensions; pull speed must be precisely matched to extruder output for consistent dimensions), cutter (rotating saw for rigid profiles, hot wire or knife for flexible).
