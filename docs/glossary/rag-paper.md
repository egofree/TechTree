# Rag paper

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

1 kg of sorted linen or cotton rags yields 10-15 sheets of writing-quality paper (approximately 500-750 g of usable fiber after stamping, with 30-40% loss as waste). Rag supply is the perennial bottleneck — a busy print shop consuming 500 sheets/day requires 30-50 kg of rags per week, which must be collected, sorted, and retted before processing.
