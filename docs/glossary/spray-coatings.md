# Spray coatings

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, polymers

Polyurea for fast-cure protective linings (reaction time <10 seconds — the amine-isocyanate reaction is so fast that the coating gels before it can sag on vertical surfaces). Applied by plural-component spray equipment (two heated hoses delivering isocyanate and amine components to a mixing gun at >2000 psi impingement pressure).
