# NaOH and KOH handling

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Concentrated lye solutions (30-50%) cause severe chemical burns. Dissolve lye pellets in water slowly — the dissolution is strongly exothermic (solution temperature can exceed 90°C). Always add lye to water, never the reverse (prevents violent spattering). Wear chemical splash goggles, nitrile or neoprene gloves, and long sleeves. If skin contact occurs, flush with copious cool water for 15+ minutes.
