# Filter frame construction

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Rigid frame of aluminum or galvanized steel (wood frames warp). Gasket seal (neoprene or closed-cell foam) between filter and ceiling grid — any bypass leak defeats the entire filter. Gel sealant (non-drying silicone or urethane) for critical installations. Filter life: 3-7 years depending on particulate loading; pressure drop monitoring indicates replacement time.
