# Adhesion test (tape test)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: optics

Press adhesive tape (3M 610 or equivalent) firmly onto the coated surface. Pull off rapidly at 180° angle. No coating should transfer to the tape. Perform after a 24-hour post-deposition aging period (some coatings, particularly MgF₂, continue to stress-relieve over hours).
