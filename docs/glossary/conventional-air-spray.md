# Conventional air spray

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Compressed air at 40-60 psi (3-4 bar) atomizes paint at the nozzle. Produces the finest atomization for smooth finishes. Transfer efficiency only 25-40% (high overspray). Requires spray booth with exhaust and filter. HVLP (high volume, low pressure) guns reduce overspray and increase transfer efficiency to 50-65%.
