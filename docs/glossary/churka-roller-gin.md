# Churka (roller gin)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

— ancient Indian design, gentle on fiber: - Two wooden rollers (5-8 cm diameter, 20-30 cm long) mounted side by side in a wooden frame, separated by a gap slightly narrower than a cotton seed (~3-4 mm). One roller is covered with leather or rough cloth; the other is smooth hardwood. A crank turns both rollers in opposite directions (one clockwise, one counterclockwise). - Feed cotton bolls into the gap.
