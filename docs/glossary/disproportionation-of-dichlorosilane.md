# Disproportionation of dichlorosilane

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

2SiH₂Cl₂ → SiH₄ + SiCl₄ (similar to trichlorosilane route but starting from SiH₂Cl₂, bp 8.3°C). SiH₂Cl₂ itself is produced from SiHCl₃ redistribution. This is actually part of the standard route when optimized.
