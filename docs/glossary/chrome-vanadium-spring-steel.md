# Chrome-vanadium spring steel

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Chrome-vanadium spring steel (0.5% C, 0.8% Cr, 0.15% V, e.g., AISI 6150) — oil-quenched and tempered to HRC 42-48. Vanadium refines grain size, improving fatigue life. Used for automotive leaf and coil springs, torsion bars. See: [Specialty Alloys](../metals/alloys.md)
