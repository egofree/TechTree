# Semiconductor fab cooling

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Cleanrooms require precise temperature control (22 ± 0.5°C) and humidity control (40-50% RH). Chilled water (5-7°C) feeds air handling units. Process tools (implanters, CVD reactors) may require glycol-chilled water at -10 to +10°C.
