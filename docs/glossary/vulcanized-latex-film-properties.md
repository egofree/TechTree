# Vulcanized latex film properties

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Tensile strength 20-30 MPa, elongation at break 700-900% (higher than dry-mixed vulcanized rubber due to the unique film formation process — latex particles fuse into a continuous film with optimal polymer chain orientation). Thin latex films have very low hysteresis (minimal heat build-up on repeated flexing).
