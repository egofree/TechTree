# Penstock materials

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Steel pipe (welded or riveted) is standard for medium and high heads. Wood-stave pipe (wooden staves bound with steel bands) is a viable alternative for heads up to 30-50 m where timber is abundant. Concrete pipe suits low-head, large-diameter applications. PVC and GRP (glass-reinforced plastic) are modern alternatives for small installations.
