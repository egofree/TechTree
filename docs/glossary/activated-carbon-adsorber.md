# Activated carbon adsorber

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Bed of granular activated carbon (GAC) through which gas flows. Carbon adsorbs organic vapors, oil mist, and odors by physical adsorption on its enormous surface area (~1,000 m²/g). Used for: removing trace organic contaminants from gas streams, protecting compressor intakes, purging solvent vapors from exhaust. Saturates over time (replace or regenerate by steam desorption at 120-150°C).
