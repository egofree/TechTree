# Molten silicon spill

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

10-30 kg of silicon at 1420°C. Worst case: quartz crucible ruptures (wall thins from dissolution, thermal shock cracks) and molten silicon contacts water-cooled chamber shell → steam explosion. Crucible inspection before each run. Automatic thermal runaway shutdown. Secondary containment tray under crucible platform.
