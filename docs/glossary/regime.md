# Regime

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Industrial plants monitor lubricant condition to schedule oil changes based on actual condition, not fixed intervals. Key tests: (1) **Viscosity**: Change >10% indicates oxidation, contamination, or wrong oil grade. (2) **Acid number**: Increase indicates oxidation — acidic byproducts corrode bearings.
