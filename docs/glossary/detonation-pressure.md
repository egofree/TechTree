# Detonation pressure

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

P_CJ = ρ₀ × VOD² / (γ + 1), where ρ₀ is the initial explosive density. For TNT at 1.60 g/cm³: P = 1600 × 6900² / (2.24) = 34 GPa. This extreme pressure (340,000 atm) is what gives detonation its shattering (brisant) effect. Higher density explosives with higher VOD produce higher detonation pressures: RDX at 1.80 g/cm³ produces P_CJ ≈ 39 GPa.
