# Frother

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

(MIBC or pine oil, 20-50 g/tonne): Stabilizes air bubbles into a persistent froth. Without frother, bubbles coalesce and pop too quickly. (3) **Modifier** (lime, cyanide, zinc sulfate): Adjusts pH and suppresses unwanted minerals. Lime raises pH to 9-11 (depresses pyrite). Cyanide depresses sphalerite (zinc sulfide) in copper-zinc ores.
