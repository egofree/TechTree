# Regulatory signs

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Enforce traffic laws. Stop signs (octagonal, red), speed limits (circular with number), yield signs (inverted triangle). Made from painted timber or cast iron plates. Mount on timber posts at 2.0-2.5 m height at the road edge. Minimum letter height 100 mm for legibility at 50 m distance.
