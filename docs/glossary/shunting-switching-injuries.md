# Shunting (switching) injuries

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Yard crews coupling and uncoupling cars, operating hand-thrown points, and riding moving cars are at high risk for crushing, amputation, and fall injuries. The "hump man" who rides cars down the classification hump to apply hand brakes faces particular risk. All yard movements require positive communication between switch crew and engineer (hand signals or whistle codes). Never ride the leading car into a standing cut.
