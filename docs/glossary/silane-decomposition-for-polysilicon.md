# Silane Decomposition for Polysilicon

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

SiH4 decomposes exothermically at 600-700C on heated silicon seed rods (similar to Siemens TCS process but at lower temperature): SiH4 -> Si + 2H2, deltaH = -34 kJ/mol. Deposition rate 3-8 micrometers/min, energy consumption 30-50 kWh/kg polysilicon (significantly lower than Siemens TCS route at 100-200 kWh/kg).
