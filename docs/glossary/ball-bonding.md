# Ball bonding

> **Type**: process | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

(gold wire): Flame-off creates a small ball at the wire tip. Thermosonic bonding (heat + ultrasonic vibration + pressure) attaches the ball to the aluminum bond pad on the die. Wire is looped to the package lead, where a second bond (wedge) is made. Ball bonding is the dominant method for ICs and most discrete devices.
