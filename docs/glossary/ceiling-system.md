# Ceiling system

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

HEPA/ULPA fan-filter modules cover 60-100% of ceiling area. In ISO 5 (Class 100) areas, 100% coverage provides uniform laminar flow; ISO 7 (Class 10,000) service areas may use 60% coverage with blank ceiling panels between. LED lighting fixtures integrated into ceiling grid, providing 500-1000 lux at the work surface. UV-blocking lenses prevent accidental photoresist exposure below 450 nm.
