# Boron trifluoride (BF₃)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

p-type dopant gas. Molecular weight 67.81, boiling point -100.3°C. Non-flammable but highly corrosive — reacts violently with water producing HF and boric acid. Used in ion implantation for p-type wells and in borophosphosilicate glass (BPSG) reflow processes. BF₃ + 3H₂O → H₃BO₃ + 3HF. The HF byproduct is itself extremely hazardous.
