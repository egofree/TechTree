# Line insulation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Each insulator must present >100 MΩ resistance to ground in dry weather, >10 MΩ in rain. Wet glass insulators can leak enough current to drop the line below relay threshold. Double-petticoat insulators (two nested glass bells with an air gap) provide a longer leakage path and better wet-weather performance.
