# Spark-ignition engine

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Producer gas replaces gasoline or works in dual-fuel mode. Engine modifications: (1) Carburetor replaced with gas-air mixer (venturi-type or electronically controlled). (2) Compression ratio may be increased from 8:1 to 10-12:1 — producer gas has higher octane rating (resists knock) than gasoline. (3) Ignition timing advanced 5-15° — producer gas burns slower than gasoline.
