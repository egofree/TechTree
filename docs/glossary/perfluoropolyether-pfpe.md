# Perfluoropolyether (PFPE)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Fluorinated synthetic — the most chemically resistant lubricant. Inert to oxygen, fuels, solvents, acids, and bases. Temperature range: -80 to +300°C. Ultra-low vapor pressure — no outgassing in vacuum. Used in: spacecraft mechanisms, semiconductor manufacturing equipment, oxygen compressors (mineral oil + high-pressure O₂ = explosion), chemical processing valves.
