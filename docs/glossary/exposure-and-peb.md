# Exposure and PEB

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

UV exposure generates acid at each exposed location. During PEB (post-exposure bake, 95°C, 1-5 min), the acid catalyzes cross-linking of the epoxy groups. Each acid molecule participates in many cross-linking reactions (chemical amplification — one photon generates one acid, which catalyzes many bonds). This makes SU-8 extremely sensitive despite its thick films.
