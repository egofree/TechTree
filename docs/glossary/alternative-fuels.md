# Alternative fuels

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Producer gas (wood gasification — CO + H₂ + N₂ mixture, ~5-6 MJ/m³) can power gasoline engines with carburetor modification. Lower power output (40-60% of gasoline) but works without petroleum. Biodiesel from vegetable oil transesterification. Ethanol from fermentation.
