# Material range

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

Manual spinning handles sheet thicknesses of 0.5-6 mm (softer metals like copper and aluminum toward the thicker end, steel toward the thinner). Power spinning with hydraulic assistance extends the range to 25 mm in steel. Wall thickness remains approximately constant in conventional spinning because the metal flows rather than stretches.
