# Silk degumming

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Raw silk contains 20-30% sericin by weight. Sericin is a globular protein (amorphous, hydrophilic) coating the fibroin filaments (crystalline, hydrophobic, strong). Degumming removes sericin without damaging fibroin: - **Mild alkaline degumming**: Na₂CO₃ (sodium carbonate) at 5-10 g/L in water at 80-90°C for 1-2 hours.
