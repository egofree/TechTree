# Molding compound preparation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Blend novolac resin + hexamine hardener + wood flour filler (40-60% by weight — reduces cost and shrinkage) + hexamine (10-15%) + lime (CaO, 1-2% as acid scavenger) + lubricant (zinc stearate, 1%) + pigment (carbon black, iron oxide). Pelletize on a two-roll mill at 70-90°C (warm enough to soften resin but below cure temperature). Compression mold at 150-180°C, 15-30 MPa, 1-5 minutes per mm wall thickness.
