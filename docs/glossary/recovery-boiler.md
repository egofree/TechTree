# Recovery boiler

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Concentrated black liquor burned — organic material (lignin, hemicellulose) provides fuel energy (recovery boiler generates steam for the mill — modern kraft mills are energy self-sufficient), inorganic sodium salts melt and drain from the furnace bottom as smelt (Na₂CO₃ + Na₂S). Smelt dissolved in water → green liquor → causticized with lime → white liquor (NaOH + Na₂S) returned to digester.
