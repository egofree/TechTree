# Calcium soap (Ca(OH)₂ + fat)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Calcium stearate. Water-resistant (insoluble). THE most common grease thickener. "Lime soap" grease. Dropping point ~90°C (relatively low — grease softens and runs at high temperature). For: wheel bearings, water pumps, marine applications.
