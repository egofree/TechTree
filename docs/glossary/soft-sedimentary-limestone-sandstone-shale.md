# Soft sedimentary (limestone, sandstone, shale)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Powder factor 0.3-0.8 kg/m³. Rock breaks easily along bedding planes. Holes can be spaced further apart (1.0-1.5 m burden). Fragments tend to be blocky and well-sized for handling. Expect 5-10 m³ broken per kg of powder.
