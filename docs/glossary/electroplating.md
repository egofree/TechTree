# Electroplating

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, metals

Electroplating (10%) — nickel plating provides hard, corrosion-resistant, bright surface. Undercoat for chromium plating. Watts bath: NiSO₄ + NiCl₂ + H₃BO₃ at 45-65°C, pH 3.5-4.5. See: [Electrolysis](../chemistry/electrolysis.md) | [Non-Ferrous Metal Production](../metals/non-ferrous.md)
