# CO monitoring

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Producer gas contains 20-25% CO, making CO detectors mandatory in any gasification facility. Electrochemical CO sensors (0-500 ppm range, alarm at 50 ppm TLV-TWA and 100 ppm TLV-STEL). Sensors placed at breathing height (1.5 m) and at low points (CO density 0.97× air, nearly neutral but tends to accumulate in low areas).
