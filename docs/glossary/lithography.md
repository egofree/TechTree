# Lithography

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

(Alois Senefelder, 1796): Draw or write on a flat slab of fine-grained limestone (Bavarian Solnhofen limestone is the historical standard) with a grease-based crayon or tusche (liquid grease ink). The grease is absorbed into the stone pores. Dampen the stone surface with water — the water wets the bare stone but is repelled by the greasy drawn areas.
