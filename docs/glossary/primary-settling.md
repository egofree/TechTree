# Primary settling

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Rectangular or circular clarifier, 2-4 hour hydraulic retention time. Surface loading rate 25-50 m³/m²/day. Removes 50-70% of total suspended solids (TSS) and 25-40% of biochemical oxygen demand (BOD). Sludge scraped to hopper, pumped to sludge treatment.
