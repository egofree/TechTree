# Activated sludge

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Aeration tank (4-8 hour retention) blows air through diffusers at the bottom. Microbial floc consumes organic matter. Mixed liquor flows to secondary clarifier. Settled sludge returned to aeration tank (return activated sludge, RAS ratio 0.25-1.0). Excess sludge wasted to sludge treatment. BOD removal 85-95%. More energy-intensive than trickling filter but more compact and higher quality effluent.
