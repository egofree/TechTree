# Exclusion

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Seal all openings >6 mm (¼ inch) in building foundations, walls, and roofs. Steel wool or copper mesh packed into gaps, then sealed with mortar or caulk. Install door sweeps (metal or brush strips at the base of exterior doors). Vent openings screened with hardware cloth (6 mm mesh).
