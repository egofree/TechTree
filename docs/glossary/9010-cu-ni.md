# 90/10 Cu-Ni

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

90/10 Cu-Ni (C70600) — 90% Cu, 10% Ni, 1.5% Fe, 0.5% Mn. Excellent seawater corrosion resistance — the nickel stabilizes the protective oxide film. Biofouling resistance (copper ions inhibit marine organism attachment). Used for seawater desalination tubing, ship hull cladding, offshore platform piping.
