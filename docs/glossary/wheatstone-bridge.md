# Wheatstone bridge

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Measures unknown resistance by balancing two legs of a bridge circuit. Locate wire faults by measuring loop resistance from each end and comparing: fault distance = (R₁ / (R₁ + R₂)) × total line length, where R₁ and R₂ are the resistances measured from each end. For a 100 km line with a break at 40 km, the near end reads 40 × 40 = 1600 Ω and the far end reads 60 × 40 = 2400 Ω.
