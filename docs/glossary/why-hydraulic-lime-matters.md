# Why hydraulic lime matters

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

Sets underwater. Essential for hydraulic structures (canals, harbor walls, foundations in wet ground, cisterns). Ordinary (non-hydraulic) lime mortar never hardens if kept continuously wet because CO₂ cannot penetrate to carbonation sites. Hydraulic lime sets by internal chemical reaction with water — no CO₂ needed.
