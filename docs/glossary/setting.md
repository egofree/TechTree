# Setting

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Carbonation — Ca(OH)₂ slowly absorbs CO₂ from air → CaCO₃. Very slow (weeks-months to full harden). Softer than Portland cement mortar — flexible, self-healing (microcracks heal as CO₂ continues carbonating). Ideal for historic restoration and soft stone masonry.
