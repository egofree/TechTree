# Head-based selection

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

High head (>100 m), low flow: Pelton wheel, the only practical choice. Medium head (10-300 m), medium flow: Francis turbine, the dominant choice for most hydroelectric sites. Low head (1-15 m), high flow: Kaplan turbine, for large rivers and low dams. Very low head (<3 m): Bulb turbine (Kaplan variant) for run-of-river on flat rivers.
