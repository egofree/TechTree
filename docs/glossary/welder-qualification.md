# Welder qualification

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

Each welder performs a qualification test coupon (welding sample) that is destructively tested (bend test and macro-etch) to verify their technique before they are authorized to weld production joints. Qualification records document the process (SMAW, GTAW, etc.), position (flat, horizontal, vertical, overhead), material thickness range, and electrode type. A welder qualified in the flat position is not authorized to weld overhead without separate qualification testing.
