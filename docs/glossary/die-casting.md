# Die casting

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Die casting (high-pressure) — - Molten aluminum forced into steel die at 20-100 MPa. Two types: **hot chamber** (limited to lower-melting alloys, rarely used for aluminum due to attack on gooseneck components) and **cold chamber** (molten Al ladled into shot sleeve, then injected — standard for aluminum). - Very high production rates (100-1,000 shots/hour).
