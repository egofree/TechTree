# Electrical

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, polymers

4.0-5.0V per cell at 100-500 kA (modern cells). Cells connected in series (potline) — 200-300 cells per potline. Total potline voltage: 800-1200V DC. Current supplied by silicon diode rectifiers from AC power. Energy consumption: 13-15 kWh/kg Al (theoretical minimum: 6.3 kWh/kg for Al₂O₃ → Al, but carbon anode reduces electrical energy needed — total energy includes anode combustion heat).
