# Residual current device (RCD/GFCI)

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Compares current in the line (outgoing) and neutral (return) conductors. If the difference exceeds a threshold (typically 30 mA for personnel protection, 300 mA for equipment protection), the device trips within 30-40 ms. Any current difference means current is returning through an alternative path (ground, human body, equipment frame).
