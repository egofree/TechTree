# Dose

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography, plants

50-200 mJ/cm² for i-line (365 nm) with standard DNQ-novolac resist. Dose depends on resist thickness (thicker resist absorbs more light, needs higher dose), substrate reflectivity (highly reflective substrates increase effective dose by reflecting light back through the resist), and feature size (small features diffract light and may receive less dose than large open areas).
