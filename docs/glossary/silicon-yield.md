# Silicon yield

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Silicon yield refers to 75-85% of theoretical. Losses come from SiO gas escaping the furnace (SiO₂ + C → SiO + CO at intermediate temperatures), silicon dissolving into slag, and spillage during tapping. See: [Metallurgical-Grade Silicon Production](../silicon/mg-si-production.md)
