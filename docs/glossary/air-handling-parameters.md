# Air handling parameters

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Make-up air supplies 10-20% of total airflow volume, maintaining positive pressure at 10-25 Pa above ambient to prevent infiltration. Cooling load: 200-500 W/m² of cleanroom floor area from process equipment heat dissipation (vacuum pumps, RF generators, bake plates). Humidity control via chilled water cooling coils (dehumidification) and clean steam humidification (re-humidification), maintaining 43±3% RH.
