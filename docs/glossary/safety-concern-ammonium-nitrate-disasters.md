# Safety concern — ammonium nitrate disasters

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Pure NH₄NO₃ can decompose explosively under confinement and intense heat. Texas City disaster (1947): 2,300 tonnes AN on ship caught fire → detonation killed 581 people. Beirut explosion (2020): 2,750 tonnes AN stored unsafely → 218 dead. AN must be stored away from heat, fuels, and confined spaces. Contamination with organics or metals increases sensitivity.
