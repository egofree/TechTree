# Sewing thread

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals, textiles

Requires a smooth, strong, tightly twisted yarn. Use 3-ply construction: spin fine singles (Ne 60-80) with high twist (20-30 tpi), then ply with moderate twist. Glaze the finished thread by coating with starch or wax and polishing between rollers. Glazing fills the fiber crevices, producing a smooth surface that passes through fabric without snagging. Thread must be strong enough to survive needle tension (3-5 N typical) without breaking.
