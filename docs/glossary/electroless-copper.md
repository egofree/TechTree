# Electroless copper

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Used for metallizing non-conductors (plated-through holes in PCBs, plastic plating). Bath: CuSO₄ + formaldehyde + EDTA + NaOH. Temperature 25-45°C. Thin initial deposits (0.5-2 µm) followed by electroplating for thickness. Requires catalytic surface preparation (SnCl₂/PdCl₂ sensitization/activation).
