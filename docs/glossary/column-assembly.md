# Column assembly

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Electrodes are screwed together into columns 3-15 m long (depending on furnace size and consumption rate). Each column is lowered into the furnace as the lower end is consumed, with new electrodes added at the top. A pin wrench or power tong tightens the connection (torque: 500-3000 N·m depending on electrode size).
