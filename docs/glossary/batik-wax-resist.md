# Batik (wax resist)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: textiles

Textile dyeing technique where molten wax (beeswax, paraffin, or a blend) is applied to fabric to create patterned dye-resist areas. After dyeing, wax is removed by boiling or ironing, revealing undyed patterns. Allows multicolor designs through repeated wax-and-dye cycles.
