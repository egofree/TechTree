# Embankments

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Build from excavated soil, compacted in layers. Crest width 1-2 m for foot access. Freeboard (height above maximum water level) of 30-50 cm prevents wave overtopping in wind. Side slopes no steeper than 1:2 (vertical:horizontal) on the water side to prevent slumping. Grass the outer slopes immediately to prevent erosion.
