# Cold smoking

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Hang split or whole gutted fish in a smokehouse (small enclosed room or chimney structure) with a smoldering fire that produces smoke without significant heat. Temperature 25-35°C. Duration 12-48 hours. Cold-smoked fish retains a soft texture and must be cooked before eating. Keeps 1-3 weeks at cool temperatures.
