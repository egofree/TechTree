# Coke oven operation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: chemistry

Thermal decomposition of coal in sealed chambers at 1000-1100°C for 14-24 hours without air, producing coke (porous carbon fuel), coal gas (fuel), coal tar (chemical feedstock), and ammonia liquor. Beehive ovens are simpler but waste byproducts compared to byproduct recovery ovens.
