# Refracting telescopes

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: optics

Refracting telescopes (lens-based) — Objective lens**: Grind and polish a large plano-convex or biconvex lens (crown glass). Focal length f = R/(n-1) for plano-convex. Typical amateur: 50-80 mm diameter, f/10-f/15 (500-1200 mm focal length). - **Eyepiece**: Short-focus convex lens (Huygens: 2-element; later Ramsden, Kellner).
