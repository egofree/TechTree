# Coiling

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Strip is coiled at 550-750°C on downcoilers (mandrel diameter 760 mm). Coil weight: 15-35 tons. Coil outer diameter: 1.5-2.1 m. Coils are the feedstock for downstream cold rolling (see [Machine Tools Forming](../machine-tools/forming.md)).
