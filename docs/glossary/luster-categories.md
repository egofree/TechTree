# Luster categories

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Metallic (pyrite, galena, magnetite), vitreous/glassy (quartz, fluorite), resinous (sphalerite, zinc ore), adamantine (diamond, zircon), earthy/dull (kaolinite, limonite). Metallic luster in opaque minerals is the strongest single indicator of ore potential.
