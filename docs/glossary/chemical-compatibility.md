# Chemical compatibility

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Soda-lime glass dissolves slowly in strong alkalis (NaOH, KOH) above 60°C. Borosilicate resists alkalis better but still shows attack above 100°C. HF (hydrofluoric acid) dissolves all glass rapidly — never store or handle HF in glass containers. Use polyethylene or PTFE containers for HF.
