# CAE

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: critical | **Domains**: animals

Caprine Arthritis Encephalitis — lentiviral infection of goats causing chronic arthritis in adults and encephalitis in kids. Transmitted through colostrum and milk. Prevention: heat-treat colostrum (56°C/60 min), pasteurize milk, separate kids at birth. CAE-negative herd achievable in 3-5 years through test-and-cull.
