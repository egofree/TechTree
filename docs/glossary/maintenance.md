# Maintenance

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

When flow rate drops below acceptable levels (typically every 1-3 months), drain the filter and scrape off the top 1-2 cm of sand along with the schmutzdecke. Wash scraped sand and set aside for later replacement. Filter resumes full rate immediately. When sand depth drops below 0.5 m total, add back washed sand to restore depth.
