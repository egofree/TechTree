# Visualization

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Remove plate when solvent front reaches ~1 cm from the top. Mark the solvent front. Dry plate. Visualize under UV light (254 nm and 366 nm) or spray with detection reagent (Dragendorff's reagent for alkaloids → orange spots; ninhydrin for amino acids → purple; ferric chloride for phenolics → blue/black). Compare Rf values (distance traveled by compound ÷ distance traveled by solvent front) between sample and standard. Matching Rf confirms identity.
