# Density-based pre-concentration

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Use heavy media separation (HMS) to float off light waste rock. Prepare a heavy medium by suspending fine magnetite or ferrosilicon in water at a controlled specific gravity (2.6-3.3). Feed coarse ore (5-50 mm) into the medium. Light rock (gangue, SG 2.5-2.7) floats and is skimmed off. Heavy ore minerals (SG 3.0+) sink and are recovered from the bottom.
