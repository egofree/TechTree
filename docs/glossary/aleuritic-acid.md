# Aleuritic acid

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

(9,10,16-trihydroxypalmitic acid, ~35%): A C₁₆ hydroxy fatty acid providing the backbone of the polyester chain. The three hydroxyl groups (at C-9, C-10, and C-16) participate in ester bonds that form the cross-linked network.
