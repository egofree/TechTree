# Sheet

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Begin with a thick slab. Hammer flat with overlapping blows, working from center outward. Anneal frequently (every few minutes of hammering). Target: sheet of 0.5-2 mm thickness. Thinner sheet requires more annealing cycles. Support on a flat anvil or stake while planishing (smooth hammering for flat, uniform surface).
