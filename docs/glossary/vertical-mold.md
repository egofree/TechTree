# Vertical mold

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Copper mold (internally water-cooled at 5-15 m³/hr) oscillates vertically at 60-200 cycles/min with 2-8 mm stroke. Oscillation prevents sticking by breaking the friction bond between solidifying shell and mold wall. Mold length 700-1100 mm for slabs, 600-900 mm for billets.
