# Spring making

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Spring making (critical for mechanisms, locks, suspensions, valves) — Material**: High-carbon steel (0.6-1.0% C) or spring steel (silicon-manganese steel 0.5-0.7% C + 1.5-2% Si). Higher carbon = harder spring, but more brittle. - **Leaf spring**: Forge steel bar to flat taper. Heat to bright cherry red (~820°C). Bend over form or anvil horn to desired curve.
