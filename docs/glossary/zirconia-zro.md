# Zirconia (ZrO₂)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Melting point 2715°C. Stabilized with CaO, MgO, or Y₂O₃ to prevent destructive phase transformation (monoclinic → tetragonal → cubic) during heating. Used for continuous casting nozzles and tundish metering orifices in steelmaking — resists erosion by molten steel.
