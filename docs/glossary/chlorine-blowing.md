# Chlorine blowing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Pass Cl₂ gas through molten silicon. Chlorine reacts with Al, Ca, and Ti to form volatile chlorides (AlCl₃ boils at 180°C, CaCl₂ melts at 772°C). Extremely effective but produces toxic gas (HCl, Cl₂) requiring scrubbing. Used only when downstream purification demands lower starting impurity levels.
