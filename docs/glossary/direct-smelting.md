# Direct smelting

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Direct smelting (modern, ISASMELT/KIVCET) — oxygen-enriched smelting in a single vessel. Higher SO₂ capture (>95%), lower energy, less plant footprint. Replacing traditional sinter-blast furnace plants. See: [Non-Ferrous Metal Production](../metals/non-ferrous.md)
