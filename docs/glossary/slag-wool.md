# Slag wool

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Molten slag can be fiberized by spinning or steam jetting to produce mineral wool (rock wool / slag wool) insulation. The fibers (3-10 μm diameter, 20-100 mm long) are collected on a conveyor, bonded with phenolic resin, and formed into batts, boards, or loose-fill insulation. Slag wool insulation is non-combustible, provides both thermal and acoustic insulation, and is widely used in building construction and industrial insulation.
