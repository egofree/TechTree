# Glass manufacturing

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Silica crown (hot-face 1600°C), alumina-zirconia-silica (AZS) fused-cast blocks in glass contact areas — produced by melting raw materials in an electric arc furnace and casting into molds. AZS contains 30-40% ZrO₂ in an alumina-silica glass matrix — zirconia crystals resist dissolution by glass melt. Glass contact blocks slowly dissolve into the glass — refractory corrosion limits furnace campaign to 8-15 years.
