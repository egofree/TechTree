# Coreless induction furnace

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

A water-cooled copper coil surrounds a ceramic crucible containing the metal charge. Alternating current in the coil generates an oscillating magnetic field, inducing eddy currents in the conductive charge. The eddy currents heat the metal by I²R losses. No electrodes, no combustion gases, no contamination of the melt.
