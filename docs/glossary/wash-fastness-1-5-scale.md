# Wash fastness (1-5 scale)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Wash a dyed sample in warm water with mild soap (5 min, 40°C, mechanical agitation). Compare the washed sample and the wash water against a grey scale. Grade 5 = no color change, no staining of adjacent fabric in the wash. Grade 1 = severe fading or bleeding. Acceptable minimum for garment textiles: Grade 3-4. Indigo rates 4-5 (excellent). Most berry dyes rate 1-2 (poor — fades in 2-3 washes).
