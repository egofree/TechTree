# Diesel dual-fuel operation

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Producer gas replaces 80-95% of the diesel fuel. Pilot diesel injection (5-20% of full-load fuel) provides ignition, and the producer gas-air mixture burns as a lean premixed charge. Compression ratio 14-20:1 (standard diesel). Thermal efficiency 30-38%, higher than spark-ignition on producer gas because the higher compression ratio extracts more work.
