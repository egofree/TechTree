# Insect control

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Diatomaceous earth (abrasive to insect exoskeletons, causes dehydration). Plant-derived insecticides: pyrethrin (from chrysanthemum flowers, 0.1-0.5% spray), nicotine sulfate (tobacco extract, toxic to insects and humans — handle with gloves). Boric acid powder for cockroach control (ingestion poison, low mammalian toxicity). Remove standing water to control mosquito breeding.
