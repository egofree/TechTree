# Multi-effect evaporation

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Black liquor from the digester (15-18% solids) must be concentrated to 65-80% solids before firing in the recovery boiler. This is done in multiple-effect evaporators: a series of 5-7 steam-heated evaporator bodies operating at progressively lower pressures. First effect: live steam at 0.3-0.5 MPa heats the black liquor, boiling off water vapor.
