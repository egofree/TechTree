# Leaf harvesting (sustainable)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Collect fallen leaves from the forest floor, or climb trees and strip leaves from branches. Leaves contain 2-5% gutta-percha by weight (dry basis). This is labor-intensive but allows the tree to survive and continue producing. Yield is low — approximately 1-2 kg of raw gutta-percha per tree per year from leaf collection alone.
