# Centrifugal fan

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Centrifugal fan (industrial workhorse) — - Air enters the center of a spinning impeller (0.5-2 m diameter) and is thrown outward by centrifugal force into a spiral casing (volute). The impeller has backward-curved blades for efficiency or forward-curved blades for higher pressure at lower rotational speed. - Performance range: 5-50 m³/s airflow at 500-3000 Pa pressure.
