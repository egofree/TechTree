# Electrolysis of water

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, silicon

H₂O → H₂ + ½O₂. Most common source for high-purity H₂. Requires ~4.5-5.5 kWh per Nm³ H₂ (alkaline electrolysis at 70-80% efficiency). A polysilicon plant producing 1000 tonnes/year needs ~3,000-5,000 Nm³ H₂/hour — a dedicated electrolysis plant.
