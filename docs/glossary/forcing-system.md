# Forcing system

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

(fan on intake side): - Fan pushes fresh air into the mine. The mine is pressurized relative to atmosphere (positive pressure of 100-500 Pa inside). Leakage tends to be outward (mine air escaping to surface through cracks and old workings), which is safer than inward leakage of contaminated air. - The fan handles clean, cool air, reducing blade wear and corrosion. But the fan and its motor are near the mine entrance, where noise and vibration may be a nuisance. - Intake airway must be sealed ...
