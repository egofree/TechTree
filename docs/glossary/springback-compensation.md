# Springback compensation

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

After bending, elastic recovery causes the actual bend angle to spring back 2-5° from the formed angle in mild steel and 5-10° in aluminum alloys. Compensate by overbending past the target angle. For a 90° bend in aluminum, set the ram to approximately 97°. Higher yield strength materials exhibit more springback; the effect decreases with larger bend radius relative to sheet thickness.
