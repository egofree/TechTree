# Attach fabric

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Lay fabric over airframe. Tack edges with copper rivets or fabric nails (small nails with broad heads). Pull fabric taut (hand-tension — no wrinkles). Overlap seams by 3-5 cm. Stitch through fabric and rib caps with waxed linen thread (rib-stitching — prevents fabric from billowing in flight). Stitch spacing: 25-50 mm.
