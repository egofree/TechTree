# Bath additives

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

CaF₂ (4-8%, reduces liquidus), LiF (0-5%, increases conductivity, reduces temperature), MgF₂ (0-3%, reduces sodium penetration into cathode). Each addition is a trade-off between operating temperature, electrical conductivity, alumina solubility, and density (bath must be less dense than liquid aluminum — 2.1 vs. 2.3 g/cm³).
