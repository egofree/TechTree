# Moisture content effects

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Fresh-cut wood contains 30-60% moisture (wet basis). Air-dried wood reaches equilibrium at 15-20% moisture depending on climate. Each percentage point of moisture above 20% reduces gasifier efficiency by roughly 1% because combustion energy is wasted evaporating water instead of driving gasification reactions.
