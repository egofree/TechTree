# Music wire

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(0.8-1.0% C): Patent-drawn (austenitized, quenched in lead bath at ~500°C to form fine pearlite, then cold-drawn). Tensile strength up to 3,000 MPa in the finest gauges — among the strongest steel products. Used for precision springs, piano wire, valve springs.
