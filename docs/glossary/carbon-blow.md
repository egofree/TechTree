# Carbon blow

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Carbon blow (next 10-12 minutes) — c + ½O₂ → CO. The CO burns at the converter mouth with spectacular flame — "the blow" is visually dramatic. Carbon drops from ~4% to ~0.05%. Metal temperature reaches 1600-1650°C. See: [Modern Steelmaking](../metals/steelmaking.md)
