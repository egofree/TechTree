# Nitric acid storage

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Concentrated HNO₃ stored in aluminum tanks (aluminum passivates in HNO₃, forming a thin, inert Al₂O₃ film). Dilute HNO₃ stored in 304L or 316L stainless steel, or glass-lined steel. Never store in carbon steel (rapid, violent reaction). Tank design: vented to atmosphere through an acid mist eliminator and NOx scrubber. Color-coded labeling: red for HNO₃, yellow for HCl, white for H₂SO₄ (in most international systems).
