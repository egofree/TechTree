# Pressure

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, energy

Pressure: 15-30 MPa (150-300 bar). Higher pressure dramatically favors NH₃ formation (Le Chatelier). Early plants operated at 20 MPa; modern plants use 15-20 MPa with improved catalysts.
