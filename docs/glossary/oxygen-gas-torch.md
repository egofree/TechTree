# Oxygen-gas torch

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Propane-oxygen or natural gas-oxygen produces flame temperatures of 1800-2000°C, sufficient for borosilicate (softening point ~820°C). The flame is adjustable from reducing (fuel-rich, blue inner cone) to neutral to oxidizing (oxygen-rich, shorter cone). Different flame chemistry affects glass color and surface quality.
