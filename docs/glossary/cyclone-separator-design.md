# Cyclone separator design

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Gas enters tangentially at 10-20 m/s inlet velocity, creating a vortex. Centrifugal acceleration 100-1000× gravity forces particles to the wall. Collection efficiency follows the "50% cut diameter" (d₅₀): the particle size captured at 50% efficiency. For a well-designed cyclone: d₅₀ = 5-10 μm. Particles >20 μm captured at >95%.
