# Newcomen engine

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

(~1712, atmospheric engine): Steam in cylinder → condense → vacuum pulls piston down → pump rod lifted on other end of beam. Lift: 30-50+ m (pump at bottom of shaft, engine at surface). Power: 5-15 HP. THE KEY FEEDBACK LOOP — mine drainage drove steam engine development, which enabled all of the Energy stage.
