# Aleuritic acid

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Aleuritic acid (9,10,16-trihydroxypalmitic acid, ~35%) — a C₁₆ hydroxy fatty acid providing the backbone of the polyester chain. The three hydroxyl groups (at C-9, C-10, and C-16) participate in ester bonds that form the cross-linked network. See: [Shellac Production](../polymers/shellac.md)
