# Cylindricity

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement

Combination of roundness, straightness, and taper measured over the full length of a cylindrical feature. Measure roundness at multiple cross-sections along the cylinder length, then evaluate the envelope of all measurements. Cylindricity tolerance for precision bores (e.g., hydraulic cylinder bores): 5-10 μm total.
