# WARNING

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

Lead is a cumulative neurotoxin. Lead glazes on food-contact surfaces cause lead poisoning. **Never use lead-glazed vessels for food or drink.** Lead glaze production also hazards workers (inhalation of lead dust, fumes during firing). Use only for non-food decorative ware or industrial containers, with respiratory protection during manufacture.
