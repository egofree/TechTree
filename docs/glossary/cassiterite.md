# Cassiterite

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(SnO₂): The only economically important tin mineral. Black to brown, very high specific gravity (6.8-7.1). Placer deposits (stream tin) were the historical source — mined by panning and sluicing. Hard-rock veins in Bolivia, Indonesia. Ore grade: 0.5-2% Sn in hard rock; 0.01-0.1% in placers.
