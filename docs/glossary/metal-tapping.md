# Metal tapping

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Molten aluminum (density 2.3 g/cm³ at 960°C) pools beneath the bath (density ~2.1 g/cm³). Tap every 24-48 hours by inserting a steel or refractory siphon tube through the crust and vacuum-siphoning 1-5 tonnes of aluminum into a refractory-lined ladle. Typical cell holds 5-15 tonnes of molten aluminum at steady state.
