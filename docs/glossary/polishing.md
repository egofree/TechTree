# Polishing

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass, optics

Iron or pitch lap (pitch conforms to glass surface). Polishing compound: cerium oxide (CeO₂) slurry in water, or rouge (Fe₂O₃). Duration: 2-12 hours for optical quality. Test with laser or monochromatic light — interference fringes reveal surface errors.
