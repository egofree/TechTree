# Protein fibers

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Protein fibers (wool, silk) — Apply at 10-25% of fiber weight (owf). Dissolve in warm water at 60-80°C. Enter fiber, bring to 80-90°C over 30 minutes, hold 45-60 minutes. Wool and silk have amino and carboxyl groups in their protein structure that readily form coordination bonds with the aluminum ion (Al³⁺). The aluminum acts as a bridge: one coordination bond to the fiber surface, one to the dye molecule's hydroxyl or carbonyl groups. This three-way bond is the basis of color fastness.
