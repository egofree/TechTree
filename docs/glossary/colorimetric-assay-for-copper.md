# Colorimetric assay for copper

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Dissolve powdered sample in nitric acid. Add ammonia solution — copper produces an intense blue color (tetraamminecopper complex). Compare color intensity against standards of known concentration using a visual comparator or spectrophotometer (absorbance at 620 nm). Detection limit: 10-50 ppm copper. Fast and inexpensive, suitable for field screening of large sample sets.
