# Rectifier-charger

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

AC mains (or generator) → transformer → rectifier (full-wave bridge using silicon diodes) → filter (capacitor + inductor for ripple reduction) → battery. Output voltage regulated to ±1% of setpoint. Current limited to the maximum charge rate (C/10 for lead-acid, C/5 for nickel-iron). A 48V, 200 Ah battery bank requires a charger capable of 54-58 VDC output at 20 A (C/10), or about 1.2 kW.
