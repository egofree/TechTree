# Distillation recovery

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

The primary method. Fractionally distill spent solvent to separate it from dissolved oils, water, and contaminants. Simple pot still for gross separation; packed column for high-purity recovery. See [Distillation](distillation.md) for column design. Energy cost: 2-5 MJ per kg of recovered solvent.
