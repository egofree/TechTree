# RCA Clean

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

RCA Clean (standard wafer cleaning sequence) — 1. **SC-1 (Standard Clean 1)**: NH₄OH:H₂O₂:H₂O = 1:1:5 at 75-80°C for 10-15 min. Removes organic contaminants and particles. Leaves thin chemical oxide. 2. **DI water rinse**: Overflow rinse, 5-10 min. 3. **HF dip** (optional): Dilute HF (1-2%) for 15-30 seconds. Removes oxide (hydrogen-terminates surface). Skip if oxide desired. 4. **SC-2 (Standard Clean 2)**: HCl:H₂O₂:H₂O = 1:1:6 at 75-80°C for 10-15 min. Removes metallic contaminants (Fe, Au, Cu, Ni). 5....
