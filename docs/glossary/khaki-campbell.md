# Khaki Campbell

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Premier egg-laying duck breed, rivaling the best chicken layers. 250-340 eggs per year (white-shelled, 65-75 g). 1.5-2.0 kg adults. Feed consumption 120-150 g/day. Hardy, active foragers. The most productive egg-laying bird of any duck species.
