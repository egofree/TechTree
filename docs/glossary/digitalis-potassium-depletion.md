# Digitalis + potassium depletion

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Low serum potassium (hypokalemia, from diuretic use or severe diarrhea) potentiates digitalis toxicity. The heart becomes dangerously sensitive to digitalis at normal doses when potassium is low. Monitor serum potassium in patients on digitalis. Supplement potassium orally (KCl 20-40 mEq/day) if levels are low.
