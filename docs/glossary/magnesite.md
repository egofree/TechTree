# Magnesite

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, metals

(MgCO₃): Calcined to periclase (MgO) at 1700-2000°C. Dead-burned magnesite (dense, low-porosity) is the backbone of basic oxygen furnace (BOF) linings. MgO melting point: 2852°C — the highest of common refractory oxides. Also resistant to basic slags (CaO-based steelmaking slags).
