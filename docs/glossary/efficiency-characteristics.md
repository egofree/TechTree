# Efficiency characteristics

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Peak efficiency reaches 90% for well-designed Pelton wheels operating at design flow. The efficiency curve is relatively flat between 30-100% of rated flow when needle-nozzle regulation is used, because the jet velocity remains constant (full head is always available) and only the jet diameter changes. At very low flows, friction in the nozzle and bucket windage losses become proportionally larger.
