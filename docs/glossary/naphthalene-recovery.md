# Naphthalene recovery

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Crystallizes from cooled coal tar at ambient temperature. Recovered by centrifuging or pressing the tar. Purity >95% after washing. Used in phthalic anhydride production (precursor for dyes and plasticizers), mothballs, and concrete plasticizers. Yield: ~3-5 kg per tonne of coal carbonized.
