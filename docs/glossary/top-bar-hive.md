# Top-bar hive

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

(intermediate): Horizontal trough 80-120 cm long, 30-40 cm wide, with removable wooden bars 3-3.5 cm wide laid across the top as comb attachment points. Bees build comb downward from each bar. Allows non-destructive inspection and harvest by lifting individual bars. Constructed from planks (requires sawn lumber).
