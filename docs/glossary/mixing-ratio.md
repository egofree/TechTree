# Mixing ratio

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Approximately 2:1 SiO₂:C by weight (stoichiometric is 2.14:1 for SiO₂ + 2C → Si + 2CO, but excess carbon compensates for losses and incomplete reaction). The actual ratio is tuned to furnace conditions: too much carbon produces SiC (silicon carbide) crusts; too little leaves unreacted SiO₂.
