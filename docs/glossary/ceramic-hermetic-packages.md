# Ceramic hermetic packages

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

(high-reliability): Multi-layer ceramic (alumina) with tungsten metallization traces. Co-fired at 1500-1600°C. Seal lid to package with gold-tin eutectic solder (Au-20Sn, mp 280°C) in dry nitrogen atmosphere. Hermetic (gas-tight) — moisture cannot penetrate. For military, aerospace, high-temperature applications.
