# Essential oil extraction

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Plant material (lavender flowers, peppermint leaves, eucalyptus foliage) loaded into a still and steam injected directly. Water and the volatile oil form a heterogeneous mixture that boils at a temperature below either pure component. Example: eugenol (clove oil, bp 254°C) co-distills with water at 99.5°C.
