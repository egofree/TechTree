# Coke strength after handling

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Coke degrades during transport from oven to furnace. Typical degradation: 5-15% size reduction from oven discharge to blast furnace charging. The tumble test (ASTM D2930: 10 kg coke tumbled in a drum at 24 RPM for 1400 revolutions) simulates this degradation — stability factor >55 and hardness factor >60 are required for blast furnace use.
