# Ammonium sulfate production

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Coke oven gas is scrubbed with dilute sulfuric acid (5-10% H₂SO₄) in a saturator vessel at 50-60°C. Ammonia reacts to form (NH₄)₂SO₄ crystals, which are centrifuged, dried, and sold as fertilizer (21% N content). Typical yield: 8-12 kg (NH₄)₂SO₄ per tonne of coal. This was historically the primary source of nitrogen fertilizer before the Haber-Bosch process.
