# Slow ramp

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

Slow ramp (150-600°C) — Ramp ~100°C/hour. Chemically combined water evaporates (clay minerals dehydrate ~450-600°C). Organic matter (temper debris, carbon) begins burning out. Smoke from chimney is normal. See: [Kiln Firing](../ceramics/kiln-firing.md)
