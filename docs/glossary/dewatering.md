# Dewatering

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Filter cake from the drum filter contains 8-15% moisture. For smelter delivery, moisture must be below 10-12% (higher moisture causes steam explosions in the smelter). Additional drying in a rotary dryer (hot air at 200-300°C, 10-20 min residence time) reduces moisture to 5-8%. The dried concentrate is less prone to freezing in winter transport and generates less dust.
