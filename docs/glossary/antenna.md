# Antenna

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

A vertical wire or mast radiator. For wavelengths around 600 m (500 kHz), an efficient quarter-wave antenna would be 150 m tall, which is impractical for most installations. Short antennas (20-50 m) work but with reduced efficiency. Ground the antenna system with buried copper wire radials (10-20 wires, each at least 0.1 wavelength long) to provide a low-resistance return path.
