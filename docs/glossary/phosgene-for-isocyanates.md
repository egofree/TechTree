# Phosgene for isocyanates

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

CO + Cl₂ over activated carbon at 200-300°C — requires [chlor-alkali electrolysis](../chemistry/electrolysis.md) (for Cl₂) and CO from producer gas (C + H₂O → CO + H₂ at 800-1000°C). The phosgene synthesis is straightforward chemically but demands extreme safety precautions due to phosgene's extreme toxicity.
