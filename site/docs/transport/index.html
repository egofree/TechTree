Transportation & Logistics

Road construction, bridge building, railway systems, water transport, and aviation. Industrial-scale operations require moving materials, people, and information across distances.

Capabilities

  • Aviation

    Aircraft development, tube-and-fabric airframes, propellers, and flight testing. (Absorbed from former aircraft domain.)

  • Railways

    Steam locomotive railways, track construction, signaling, and freight/passenger operations.

  • Road & Bridge Construction

    Road construction from dirt tracks to paved highways, bridge building, and route planning.

  • Water Transport

    Water transport: boats, barges, canals, and maritime shipping infrastructure.
