# NaOH from lime-soda or Leblanc process → hard bar soap

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

NaOH from lime-soda or Leblanc process → hard bar soap (Years 10-20) — . Once Alkalis production is established, NaOH replaces KOH for hard, long-lasting bar soap. Kettle process with salt graining enables glycerol recovery. See: [Soap & Detergent Production](../chemistry/soap.md)
