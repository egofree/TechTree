# Cold box maintenance

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

The cold box is designed for zero maintenance access during operation. All internal equipment must last 2-4 years between major overhauls. When a warm-up is required (for column tray repair, heat exchanger inspection, or valve replacement), the entire cold box is brought to ambient temperature over 24-48 hours using controlled nitrogen heating.
