# Adhesion/barrier layer

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Deposit 20-50 nm TiN (titanium nitride) by sputtering or CVD as a diffusion barrier and adhesion layer. TiN prevents WF₆ (tungsten hexafluoride, the tungsten source gas) from reacting with the underlying SiO₂ or silicon.
