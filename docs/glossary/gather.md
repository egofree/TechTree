# Gather

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Heat the blowpipe tip to red heat. Dip into the molten glass with a smooth, steady rotation. Turn the pipe continuously as you lift — the gather forms a symmetric cylinder of molten glass. A typical gather is 50-150 g of glass. Multiple gathers (reheating and re-dipping) build up thicker walls for larger pieces.
