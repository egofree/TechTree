# Brine tank layout

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

A typical production tank measures 3 × 6 m with 100-200 cans arranged in rows. Agitators (propeller-type stirrers) circulate brine at 0.3-0.6 m/s around the cans to maintain uniform temperature. Without agitation, warm brine stratifies near the cans, slowing freezing.
