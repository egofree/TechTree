# Detergent plant (LAS)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

20,000-100,000 tonnes/year. Requires sulfonation unit, spray-drying tower, and blending/packaging lines. Capital cost: $10-50 million. Feedstock cost dominates (petroleum-derived alkenes, benzene). Co-located with petroleum refinery or chemical complex for feedstock access.
