# Securing cylinders

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Cylinders must be chained or strapped to a wall, bench, or cylinder cart at all times — both in storage and in use. An unsecured cylinder knocked over can snap the valve off, turning the cylinder into an unguided projectile (a 50 kg cylinder at 200 bar contains enough energy to penetrate a concrete wall). Use two chains: one at upper third, one at lower third of cylinder body.
