# Grading (RSS 1-5)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Visual inspection classifies sheets by color, cleanliness, and defect presence. RSS 1 (highest): uniform amber color, no mold, no oxidized spots, minimal bark fragments. RSS 5 (lowest): dark, oxidized, possible mold spots, bark inclusions. Price differential between grades can be 15-25%.
