# Vanadium Redox Flow Battery

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: critical | **Domains**: energy

Most mature flow battery chemistry using vanadium ions in sulfuric acid at different oxidation states (V2+/V3+ negative, V4+/V5+ positive), achieving 75-85% round-trip efficiency, 10,000+ cycle lifetime, and 1.15-1.4V nominal cell voltage.
