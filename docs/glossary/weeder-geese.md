# Weeder geese

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Geese selectively grazing grasses and broadleaf weeds while ignoring established crops. Used for biological weed control in cotton fields, strawberry beds, orchards, and vineyards. Stocking rate: 2-4 geese per 1,000 m². Must be removed before crop produces fruit or seeds.
