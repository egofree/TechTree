# Threshing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

Separate grain from stalks and chaff. Methods in order of mechanization: - Hand beating: Flail stalks against a hard surface or beat with a wooden flail (two sticks joined by leather thong). Throughput: 20-50 kg/hour - Treading: Drive oxen or horses in circles over spread stalks. Throughput: 100-200 kg/hour - Threshing sledge (tribulum): Wooden board with embedded flint chips, dragged by animal over grain. Used since Bronze Age in Mediterranean
