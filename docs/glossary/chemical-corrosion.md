# Chemical corrosion

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Slag attack is the dominant wear mechanism in steelmaking and non-ferrous metallurgy. Molten slag (CaO-SiO₂-FeO-MnO-Al₂O₃ system for steelmaking) dissolves refractory oxides by forming low-melting phases at the slag-refractory interface. Basic slags (high CaO) dissolve acidic refractories (silica, fireclay). Acidic slags (high SiO₂) dissolve basic refractories (magnesite).
