# Cellulose fibers

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Cellulose fibers (cotton, linen) — apply at 5-15% owf, but always after a tannin pre-mordant. Cellulose hydroxyl groups bond less readily with aluminum than protein amino groups. The tannin pre-treatment provides phenolic groups that bond strongly with both cellulose and alum, effectively extending the bonding sites on the fiber surface.
