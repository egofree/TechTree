# Raw material balance

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Per tonne of Na₂CO₃ produced, the Solvay process consumes approximately 2.2 tonnes of NaCl (at 72% conversion efficiency in the carbonation tower, accounting for recycle) and 2.0 tonnes of limestone (CaCO₃). Ammonia makeup is only 1-2 kg per tonne Na₂CO₃ due to efficient recovery. Steam consumption: 1.5-3.0 tonnes per tonne Na₂CO₃. Electrical power: 50-100 kWh per tonne. Total energy: 7-10 GJ per tonne Na₂CO₃.
