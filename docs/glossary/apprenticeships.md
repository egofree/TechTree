# Apprenticeships

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Apprenticeships (Foundations+) — - Duration: 4-7 years per skill. Apprentice works under master, learning by doing. Start with simple tasks → progress to complex. Master evaluates and certifies competency. - Key apprenticeship tracks: blacksmithing, foundry, machining, glassblowing, chemistry, electrical work, mining, construction. - Apprentice keeps notebook (hand-copied recipes, drawings, observations). This notebook becomes their reference for life. See: [Education & Training](../knowledge/education.md)
