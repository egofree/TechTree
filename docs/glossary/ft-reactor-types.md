# FT reactor types

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Multi-tubular fixed-bed reactor (Shell Middle Distillate Synthesis process): thousands of narrow tubes (25-50 mm diameter) packed with cobalt catalyst, surrounded by boiling water for temperature control. Slurry bubble column reactor (SASOL): catalyst particles suspended in liquid wax, syngas bubbled upward through the slurry.
