# Selection

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

Save seed from the healthiest, most productive plants — not from the bulk harvest. Mark superior plants in the field before harvest with colored cord. Select for disease resistance, yield, and adaptation to local conditions - **Drying**: Seeds must reach 8-12% moisture content for storage. Test by biting — a properly dry seed cracks rather than dents. Spread thin on screens in warm (30-35°C), dry, shaded location for 1-2 weeks - **Storage**: Cool, dry, dark.
