# Weighted pawl carry

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: computing

Each digit wheel carries a small weighted pawl (gravity-assisted catch). As the wheel rotates from 9 toward 0, the pawl reaches a protruding peg on its wheel. The pawl's weight carries it over the peg during normal rotation, but at the 9-to-0 transition the pawl drops into a slot on the adjacent (next-higher) wheel's carry lever, pulling it forward by one position. This is the carry.
