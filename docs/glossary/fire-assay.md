# Fire assay

> **Type**: process | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

(gold, silver): The definitive method for precious metals. Mix 15-30 g of pulverized ore sample with lead oxide (litharge), flour (reducing agent), and flux (borax, silica, soda ash). Heat in a crucible at 1000-1100°C for 45-60 minutes. Lead oxide reduces to metallic lead, which collects gold and silver into a lead button.
