# Hydrometallurgical alternative

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(SX-EW): Oxide ores (malachite, chrysocolla) unsuitable for froth flotation are leached with dilute sulfuric acid (50-100 g/L) in heap or dump leaching operations. Pregnant leach solution (1-6 g/L Cu²⁺) is concentrated by solvent extraction (LIX reagents in kerosene diluent) to 35-50 g/L Cu, then electrowon directly to cathode copper (99.99% Cu) in electrowinning cells at 180-250 A/m² and 1.8-2.2 V per cell.
