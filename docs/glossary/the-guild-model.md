# The guild model

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

(proven over centuries of European craft training): - **Apprentice** (years 1-7): Bound by contract to a master. Lives in the master's household or workshop. Performs menial tasks initially (sweeping, material preparation, tool maintenance) while observing the master at work. Gradually gains hands-on experience with supervised tasks.
