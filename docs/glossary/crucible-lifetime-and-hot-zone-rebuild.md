# Crucible lifetime and hot zone rebuild

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: silicon

Operational lifespan of quartz crucibles in CZ pulling (typically 1-4 pulls before devitrification causes failure) and the periodic rebuilding of the graphite hot zone assembly (heater, insulation, susceptor). Hot zone rebuilds are scheduled every 20-50 pulls to maintain thermal uniformity.
