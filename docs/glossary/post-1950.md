# Post-1950

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Hydraulic drills dominate new installations (higher efficiency: 40-50% vs. 15-20% energy efficiency for pneumatic, lower noise: 95-105 dB vs. 110-120 dB, no compressed air infrastructure). Down-the-hole (DTH) hammers for large-diameter surface blast holes. Top hammer drills for underground development. Modern hydraulic drills achieve 100-200 m/day in hard rock with a single operator.
