# Dross processing

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Dross (skimmed from the melt surface — a mixture of Al metal, Al₂O₃, salt, and impurities) contains 30-70% metallic aluminum. Processed in rotary salt furnaces or plasma arc furnaces to recover metal. Residual oxide residue (NMP — non-metallic product) goes to landfill or is reprocessed for alumina recovery.
