# Machine design

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Heated barrel with a reciprocating screw. The screw has a dual function — it rotates to melt and convey plastic forward (friction + external heater bands provide heat — ~70% of melting energy comes from mechanical shear, 30% from external heaters), then pushes forward axially to inject the accumulated melt into the mold.
