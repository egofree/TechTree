# Chopped strand mat (CSM)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

randomly oriented chopped fibers (25-50 mm long) held together by a powder binder (polyester resin, 3-7% by weight). Supplied in rolls 1-2 m wide. Used in hand lay-up composites. Fiber content in the final laminate: 20-35% by weight. CSM is the cheapest and easiest form of fiberglass to work with.
