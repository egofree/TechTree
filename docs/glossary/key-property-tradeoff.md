# Key property tradeoff

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Higher acrylonitrile content → better oil/fuel resistance (NBR swells <10% in IRM 903 oil at 100°C for High-ACN vs. >30% for Low-ACN), higher glass transition temperature (less flexible at low temperature — Tg increases from -50°C for Low-ACN to -15°C for High-ACN). Grades: Low-ACN (18-22%, Tg ≈ -50°C, best low-temperature flexibility), Medium-ACN (30-35%, Tg ≈ -35°C, general purpose), High-ACN (40-50%, Tg ≈ -15°C, best oil/fuel resistance).
