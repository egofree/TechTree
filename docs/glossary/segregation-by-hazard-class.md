# Segregation by hazard class

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Store flammable gases (H₂, CH₄, C₂H₂) at least 6 m from oxidizers (O₂, Cl₂) and 15 m from toxic gases. Incompatible gases must not share the same cabinet or manifold. Use physical barriers (concrete block walls) between hazard classes for quantities exceeding 1000 cubic feet.
