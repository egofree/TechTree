# Energy intensity

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals, silicon

Hot rolling consumes 1.5-2.5 GJ per ton of finished steel (primarily for reheating). This is 5-8% of the total energy used in steelmaking from iron ore. Continuous casting with hot charging (transfer slabs to the reheat furnace while still at 600-800°C from the caster) reduces reheating fuel consumption by 30-50%.
