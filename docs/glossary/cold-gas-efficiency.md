# Cold gas efficiency

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Chemical energy in the product gas / energy in the fuel input. Downdraft gasifier: 65-75%. Updraft gasifier: 70-80% (higher because more of the fuel's energy stays in the gas as tar and hydrocarbons). Fluidized bed: 70-80%. The remaining 20-35% is lost as sensible heat in the gas, radiation from the gasifier shell, and unburned carbon in the ash.
