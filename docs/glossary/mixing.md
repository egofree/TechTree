# Mixing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, energy, machine-tools, metals

Heated coke and liquid pitch are combined in a heated intensive mixer (sigma-blade or planetary type) at ~150-170°C. Mixing time: 20-60 minutes. The pitch must coat every coke particle uniformly. Under-mixing creates weak spots; over-mixing degrades pitch quality through oxidation.
