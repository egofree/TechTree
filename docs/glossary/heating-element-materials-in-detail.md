# Heating element materials in detail

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: energy

Materials for electric furnace heating elements: nichrome (Ni-Cr, 1100°C), Kanthal (Fe-Cr-Al, 1400°C), silicon carbide (1600°C), molybdenum disilicide (1700°C), tungsten (1900°C), and graphite (3000°C). Selection depends on operating temperature, atmosphere, element life, and cost.
