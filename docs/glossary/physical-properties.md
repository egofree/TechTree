# Physical properties

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Yellow crystalline solid, melting point 80.8°C, density 1.65 g/cm³. Can be safely melt-cast at 85-90°C into shells and shaped charges (one of TNT's great practical advantages). Detonation velocity 6,900 m/s at theoretical maximum density. Detonation pressure 21 GPa. Heat of explosion 4.6 MJ/kg. Oxygen balance: -74% (fuel-rich, produces CO and carbon soot).
