# Frass (insect)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: critical | **Domains**: animals

Insect waste (larval excrement + shed skins + undigested residue) from BSF farming. Approximately 3% nitrogen, 2% phosphorus, 1.5% potassium — similar to bat guano. Contains chitin from shed exoskeletons that stimulates plant immune responses and suppresses soil pathogens.
