# Magnetic particle inspection (MPI)

> **Type**: process | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Applicable only to ferromagnetic materials (iron and steel). Magnetize the part with a portable yoke or coil. Apply magnetic particles (dry powder or wet fluorescent suspension). Particles accumulate at surface and near-surface defects (cracks, seams, laps) where magnetic flux leaks out. Under UV light (fluorescent method), indications glow brightly. Fast, inexpensive — the standard method for inspecting welds, shafts, forgings, and castings in the field.
