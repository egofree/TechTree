# Outdoor storage preferred

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Gas cylinders are best stored outdoors under a roof or canopy (protection from rain and direct sunlight) with chain-link fencing for security. Ventilation is unlimited. Temperature stays below 50°C in most climates, avoiding pressure buildup from heating. Indoor storage requires mechanical ventilation at 1 CFM per square foot of floor area.
