# Marks must survive all process steps

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

from first lithography through final passivation. Choose mark structures in areas that will not be covered by opaque metal layers. Common design: a cross-in-box pattern (a cross etched into the wafer, surrounded by a rectangular frame, also etched) at the first lithography level. Subsequent layers have matching cross-in-box patterns that overlay on the original marks.
