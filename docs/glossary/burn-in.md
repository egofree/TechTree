# Burn-in

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, silicon

Operate devices at elevated temperature (125-150°C) and maximum rated voltage for 24-168 hours. Weeds out "infant mortality" failures (devices with latent defects that fail early in life). The bathtub curve: high failure rate in the first few hundred hours (infant mortality), low constant rate during operational life, rising rate at end of life (wearout).
