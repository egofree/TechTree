# Resources and References

## Practical Bootstrapping References

### Machine Tools and Foundry Work
- Dave Gingery, *Build Your Own Metal Working Shop from Scrap* (7-volume series)
  - Vol 1: The Charcoal Foundry
  - Vol 2: The Metal Lathe
  - Vol 3: The Metal Shaper
  - Vol 4: The Milling Machine
  - Vol 5: The Drill Press
  - Vol 6: The Dividing Head and Deluxe Accessories
  - Vol 7: Designing and Building the Sheet Metal Brake
- L. T. C. Rolt, *Tools for the Job* (history of machine tools)

### Metallurgy and Materials
- R. F. Tylecote, *A History of Metallurgy*
- Agricola, *De Re Metallica* (1556) — Renaissance mining and smelting

### Semiconductor History
- Michael Riordan and Lillian Hoddeson, *Crystal Fire: The Birth of the Information Age*
- Robert P. Feytis, "The Making of the First Silicon Solar Cell"
- Christophe Lécuyer, *Making Silicon Valley: Innovation and the Growth of High Tech*

### Chemistry and Chemical Engineering
- Trevor I. Williams, *A History of Chemical Technology*
- Ludwig Mond and the development of industrial chemistry

### Energy and Power
- Henry Dickenson, *A Short History of the Steam Engine*
- Thomas P. Hughes, *Networks of Power: Electrification in Western Society*

### Glass and Optics
- W. E. S. Turner, *Elements of Glass Technology*
- History of lens grinding from telescope and microscope development

### General Civilizational Development
- Vaclav Smil, *Creating the Twentieth Century* and *Energy and Civilization*
- Kenneth Pomeranz, *The Great Divergence*
