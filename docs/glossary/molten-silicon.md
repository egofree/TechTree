# Molten silicon

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Pours at ~1450-1600°C. Contact causes immediate deep burns. Molten silicon on wet surfaces or water causes steam explosion — catastrophic ejection of molten metal. All molds, ladles, and tools must be thoroughly pre-dried. No water in tapping area. Pouring ladle must be lined with dry refractory.
