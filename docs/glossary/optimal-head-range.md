# Optimal head range

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Optimal head range: 100-500 m. Balances civil works cost (lower heads require larger reservoirs and larger-diameter penstocks) against equipment cost (higher heads require stronger penstocks and higher-pressure pump-turbines). See: [Pumped Hydroelectric Storage](../energy/pumped-hydro.md)
