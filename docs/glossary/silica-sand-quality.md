# Silica sand quality

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

has a direct impact on glass clarity and color: - **Container glass and window glass**: >95% SiO₂, <0.3% Fe₂O₃. Iron imparts a green tint that increases with thickness. - **Optical and decorative glass**: >99% SiO₂, <0.01% Fe₂O₃. Achieved by washing sand in water with agitation, then acid leaching (HCl or H₂SO₄) to dissolve iron compounds, followed by rinsing and drying.
