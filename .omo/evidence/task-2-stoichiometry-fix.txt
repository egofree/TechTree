## Fix 2: Invalid Formula KPH₂O → KH₂PO₂ (dopant-etch-gases.md:11)
BEFORE: White phosphorus + KOH + H₂O → PH₃ + KPH₂O
AFTER:  White phosphorus + KOH + H₂O → PH₃ + KH₂PO₂

KPH₂O is not a valid chemical formula. KH₂PO₂ is potassium hypophosphite,
the correct product of boiling white phosphorus with KOH.

## Fix 3: H₂SO₄ Distillation Wording (acids.md:107)
BEFORE: H₂SO₄ distilled at 337°C under reduced pressure.
AFTER:  H₂SO₄ boils at 337°C at atmospheric pressure but decomposes near
        its boiling point; distilled under reduced pressure at lower
        temperatures to avoid decomposition.

The original wording implied distilling AT 337°C under reduced pressure,
which is misleading because 337°C is the normal (1 atm) boiling point.
Under reduced pressure, the bp is lower — that's the whole point of
vacuum distillation for H₂SO₄.
