# Plate mill

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(separate from strip mill): Produces plate 5-150 mm thick, 1500-5200 mm wide, up to 40 m long. Single-stand reversing mill with 4000-5500 mm work rolls. Roll force: 40,000-100,000 kN — the highest forces in any rolling application. Target applications: ship hull plate, pressure vessels, bridge girders, armored plate.
