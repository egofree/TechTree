# Purge gas

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Inerts (CH₄ from SMR, Ar from air separation) accumulate in the recycle loop. A small purge stream (~5-10% of recycle flow) is continuously withdrawn to prevent inert buildup above ~10-15% of the gas composition. The purge gas is burned as fuel or sent to a hydrogen recovery unit (cryogenic or membrane).
