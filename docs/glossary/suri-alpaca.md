# Suri alpaca

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Alpaca breed (10% of alpacas) with long, lustrous fleece hanging in pencil-like locks (dreadlocks). No crimp — straight fiber with silky handle. Fiber diameter 18-28 microns, often finer than Huacaya. More delicate constitution. Prized for weaving.
