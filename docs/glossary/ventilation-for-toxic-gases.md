# Ventilation for toxic gases

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Gases like CO, H₂S, NOx, and chlorine require use in ventilated enclosures or gas cabinets with continuous exhaust. Gas detection alarms for the specific gas must be installed. Scrubbers or absorbers on exhaust for toxic gas cylinders. Emergency shutoff valves accessible from outside the work area.
