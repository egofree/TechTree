# Operating cycle

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Operating cycle (each stove alternates between two phases) — 1. **On-gas (heating) phase** (20-45 minutes): Blast furnace gas (cleaned) is burned with combustion air in the combustion chamber. Hot combustion products (~1200-1400°C) pass downward through the checker bricks, heating them. Exhaust exits through the flue at the bottom at ~200-400°C. 2. **On-blast (blowing) phase** (20-45 minutes): Cold blast air from the blower enters at the bottom of the checker chamber and flows upward through the now-hot brick...
