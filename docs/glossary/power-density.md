# Power density

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Coreless furnaces operate at 200-800 kW per tonne of capacity. A 1-tonne steel furnace at 500 kW melts cold scrap to 1600°C in roughly 45-60 minutes. Electrical efficiency: 60-75% (coil losses, thermal losses account for the rest).
