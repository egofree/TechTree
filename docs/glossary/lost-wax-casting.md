# Lost-wax casting

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(complex shapes — tools, ornaments): 1. **Wax model**: Sculpt in beeswax or tallow. Attach wax sprue and vents. 2. **Clay investment**: Coat in thin clay slip, build up 2-4 layers of clay + grog (~1-3 cm thick). Dry thoroughly (days). 3. **Burnout**: Heat to 400-600°C to melt and drain wax.
