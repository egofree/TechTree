# Topcoat selection

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Polyurethane topcoats provide UV resistance, color retention, and gloss. Aliphatic polyurethane (HDI biuret or isocyanurate as crosslinker) has excellent weatherability and does not yellow. Aromatic polyurethane is cheaper but yellows and chalks in sunlight (limited to interior use). Fluoropolymer topcoats (PVDF or FEVE resin) provide the ultimate weatherability: 30+ year color and gloss retention. Used on architectural metal panels and stadium roofs.
