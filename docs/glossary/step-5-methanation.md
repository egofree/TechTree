# Step 5 — Methanation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Residual CO and CO₂ (0.2-0.5%) are hydrogenated back to CH₄ over Ni catalyst at 300-400°C. CO and CO₂ are severe catalyst poisons for the Haber-Bosch iron catalyst — must be reduced to <10 ppm total. The small amount of CH₄ formed is tolerated as an inert.
