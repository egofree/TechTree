# Reversible pump-turbine

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Modern installations use a single machine that operates as a turbine in one direction of rotation and as a pump in the opposite direction. This eliminates the need for separate pump and turbine units, reducing cost and civil works complexity. The motor-generator operates as a motor during pumping and as a generator during generation — the same electrical machine, different operating mode.
