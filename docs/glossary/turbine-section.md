# Turbine section

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Axial flow, 2-5 stages. Blade cooling via internal air passages (bleed air from compressor routed through blade interior) allows gas path temperatures 200-500°C above blade metal melting point. Turbine blade materials: single-crystal nickel-based superalloys (Inconel, CMSX-4) with ceramic thermal barrier coatings (yttria-stabilized zirconia). Each blade is a precision investment casting — tolerances of ±25 μm on airfoil surfaces.
