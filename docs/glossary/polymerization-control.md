# Polymerization control

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Chloroprene is highly reactive — it polymerizes spontaneously unless inhibited (stored with phenothiazine or hydroquinone inhibitor at 0-5°C). Emulsion polymerization at 40°C, n-dodecyl mercaptan as chain transfer agent (0.3-0.5%) to control molecular weight. Conversion limited to 70-80% — higher conversion causes excessive branching and gel formation. Short-stopped with hydroquinone.
