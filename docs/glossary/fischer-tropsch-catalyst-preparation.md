# Fischer-Tropsch catalyst preparation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Cobalt-based FT catalyst is prepared by impregnating γ-alumina or silica support with cobalt nitrate solution, drying at 110°C, and calcining at 300-400°C to convert cobalt nitrate to Co₃O₄. Typical cobalt loading: 15-25% by weight. A promoter (ruthenium at 0.1-1% or platinum at 0.05-0.5%) enhances reduction of Co₃O₄ to active metallic Co during hydrogen activation at 350-400°C. Iron-based catalyst for high-temperature FT: precipitated Fe₂O₃ with Cu (1-5%) as reduction promoter and K₂O (1-3%)...
