# Step 4 — CO₂ removal

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Absorb CO₂ in amine solution (MEA, MDEA) or physical solvent (Selexol, Rectisol). CO₂ is stripped from the solvent and either vented, used for urea production, or sequestered. CO₂ from SMR is the largest source of greenhouse gas emissions in ammonia production (~1.6 tonnes CO₂ per tonne NH₃).
