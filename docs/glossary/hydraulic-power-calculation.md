# Hydraulic power calculation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

(fundamental formula for all water power): - P = η × ρ × g × Q × H - Where: η = overall efficiency (0.20-0.90 depending on wheel/turbine type), ρ = water density (1000 kg/m³), g = gravitational acceleration (9.81 m/s²), Q = volumetric flow rate (m³/s), H = available head (m) - Example: Overshot wheel, η = 0.65, Q = 0.1 m³/s, H = 4 m → P = 0.65 × 1000 × 9.81 × 0.1 × 4 = ~2,551 W (~3.4 HP)
