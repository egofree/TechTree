# MIDREX process

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(dominant, ~60% of global DRI): - Uses natural gas as the reducing agent feedstock. Natural gas is reformed (partially oxidized with CO₂ or H₂O from the process) to produce a reducing gas mixture of ~55-65% H₂ and 25-35% CO. - The reducing gas is fed to a vertical shaft furnace at ~800-950°C. Iron oxide pellets or lump ore descends by gravity through the shaft countercurrent to the rising reducing gas. - **Reduction zone** (upper shaft): Fe₂O₃ + H₂/CO → Fe + H₂O/CO₂.
