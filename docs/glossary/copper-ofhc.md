# Copper (OFHC)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Oxygen-free high-conductivity copper is used for CF flange gaskets, internal heat sinks, and braze joints in vacuum assemblies. OFHC copper has very low outgassing (<10⁻⁹ Torr·L/s·cm² baked) and good thermal conductivity (391 W/m·K), making it the standard sealing material for UHV. Copper gaskets are single-use items.
