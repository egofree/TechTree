# Pyrophoric SiHCl₃

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Trichlorosilane ignites spontaneously on contact with air (autoignition ~185°C, but can flash at room temperature if warm or in fine mist). All handling must be under inert atmosphere (N₂ or Ar) in sealed systems. Leaks produce dense white HCl/SiO₂ smoke and fire. Firefighting: CO₂ or dry chemical. Do NOT use water — it accelerates decomposition and generates HCl.
