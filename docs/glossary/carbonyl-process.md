# Carbonyl process

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(iron, nickel): - Metal reacts with carbon monoxide to form volatile metal carbonyl: Fe + 5CO → Fe(CO)₅ at 150-200°C and 10-20 MPa (BASF process). The liquid carbonyl (boiling point 103°C) is distilled for purification, then decomposed by heating to 200-300°C at atmospheric pressure: Fe(CO)₅ → Fe + 5CO.
