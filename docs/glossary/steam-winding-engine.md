# Steam winding engine

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Horizontal steam engine drives winding drum. Speed 5-15 m/second. Depth 200-500+ m. Load 1-5 tonnes per skip. Guides (wooden or iron rails in shaft) prevent cage rotation. Safety brake (friction brake on drum, held off by steam pressure — loss of pressure = automatic brake application).
