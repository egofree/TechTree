# Butadiene

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Butadiene (C₄H₆) — co-product of ethylene production via steam cracking of naphtha (750-900°C, 0.1-0.5 s residence, ~4-5% butadiene yield). Also producible from ethanol via Lebedev process (2 C₂H₅OH → C₄H₆ + 2 H₂O + H₂ over ZnO/Al₂O₃ catalyst at 400-450°C) — the fermentation route, critical when petrochemicals are unavailable. Soviet Union produced butadiene from grain ethanol during WWII using the Lebedev process at industrial scale (~100,000 tonnes/year). See: [Synthetic Rubbers](../polymers/synthetic.md)
