# Ultra-pure water (UPW)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Used for all wafer rinsing steps. Resistivity: 18.2 MΩ·cm at 25°C (theoretical maximum for pure water). TOC (total organic carbon): <1 ppb. Particles: <1000 particles/L ≥0.05 μm. Dissolved oxygen: <10 ppb (oxygen in rinse water causes native oxide growth on silicon). Bacteria: <1 CFU/100 mL. Metals: <0.1 ppb each for Fe, Cu, Ni, Zn.
