# Mercury barometer

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement

Glass tube (~1 m) sealed at top, open at bottom, immersed in mercury reservoir. Atmospheric pressure supports mercury column. Height ≈ 760 mm at sea level. Read with vernier scale to ±0.1 mm Hg (±0.13 mbar). The absolute pressure reference. Mercury toxicity requires careful handling.
