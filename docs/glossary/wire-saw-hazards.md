# Wire saw hazards

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Wire saw hazards: 140-180 μm steel wire under tension (15-30 N) traveling at 5-15 m/s can snap and lash. Wire guard mandatory. Abrasive slurry (SiC or diamond) is an eye irritant — safety goggles required during slurry handling. See: [Crystal Growth & Wafering](../silicon/crystal-growth.md)
