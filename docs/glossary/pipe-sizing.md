# Pipe sizing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Size mains to maintain minimum 15 m head (1.5 bar) at the furthest point during peak demand. Maximum velocity 1.5-2.5 m/s (higher velocity causes erosion, water hammer, and noise). Minimum velocity 0.3 m/s (below this, sediment deposits). Hazen-Williams equation: Q = 0.278 × C × D²·⁶³ × S⁰·⁵⁴, where Q is flow (m³/s), C is roughness coefficient, D is diameter (m), and S is hydraulic gradient (m/m).
