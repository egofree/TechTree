# Stage 1: Puddled steel from pig iron

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Stage 1: Puddled steel from pig iron (Years 10-15) — Finery and chafery, or puddling furnace, refines pig iron by stirring molten iron in a reverberatory furnace with an iron bar, burning out carbon and silicon by exposure to air. Labor-intensive (one puddler per 100-200 kg heat) but requires no special equipment beyond the furnace and a strong back. Produces wrought iron with variable carbon content — early "steel" was made this way for tools and springs. See Iron & Steel for puddling detail. - Production: 1-5 ...
