# Dye extraction

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

Simmer dried, chopped roots in water at 70-80°C (do not boil — temperatures above 85°C degrade alizarin and produce duller browns) for 60-90 minutes. Strain. The dye bath is deep red-orange. Multiple extractions from the same roots (3-4 baths of decreasing intensity) maximize yield.
