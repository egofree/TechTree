# Shuttleless looms (1950s+)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Projectile (Gripper), rapier, and air-jet looms eliminate the shuttle entirely — weft is carried across by a projectile, flexible rapier, or puff of air. Higher speed (300-600 picks/minute vs. 150-200 for shuttle looms), quieter, less waste. The modern standard.
