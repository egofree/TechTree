# Extrusion blow molding

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Continuous parison extruded downward between two open mold halves. Mold closes around parison, pinching bottom. Compressed air (0.2-1.0 MPa) injected through blow pin at top inflates parison against mold walls. Mold cooled (water channels). Mold opens, part ejected. Trim flash (pinched excess at mold seam).
