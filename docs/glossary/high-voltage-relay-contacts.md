# High-voltage relay contacts

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: computing

Electromechanical relays switching inductive loads (motors, solenoids) produce arc flash across contact gaps — temperatures exceed 10,000°C at the arc root. Risk of electrocution and flash burns. Always disconnect and lock out power before servicing relay panels. Use insulated tools rated for the circuit voltage. Wear safety glasses when working near energized contacts.
