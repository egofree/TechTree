# Fire in telegraph offices

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Oil lamps for illumination, paper message forms stacked for filing, and wooden furniture create significant fire load. Telegraph offices have caused urban fires (the Great Chicago Fire of 1871 burned the telegraph office, cutting communications). Store message archives in fireproof vaults. Use metal desks and filing cabinets. Keep oil lamps away from paper. Install fire extinguishers (soda-acid type) at each operator position.
