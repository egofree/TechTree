# Steam generation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Recovery boiler steam pressure: 6-10 MPa, temperature 450-500°C. A modern kraft mill produces 2-5 tonnes of steam per tonne of pulp, sufficient to meet all mill energy needs with surplus for electricity generation via back-pressure turbines. Mills burning bark and other wood waste in auxiliary boilers produce additional power.
