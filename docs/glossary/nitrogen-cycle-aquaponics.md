# Nitrogen cycle (aquaponics)

> **Type**: process | **Tier**: critical | **Domains**: agriculture

Biological process in aquaponics where bacteria convert toxic fish ammonia waste to plant-available nitrate. Step 1: fish excrete ammonia (toxic above 1 mg/L). Step 2: Nitrosomonas oxidize ammonia to nitrite (toxic). Step 3: Nitrobacter oxidize nitrite to nitrate (safe, plant food). System cycling takes 4-6 weeks.
