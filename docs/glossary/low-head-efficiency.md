# Low-head efficiency

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Low-head efficiency refers to 85-92% at design flow for heads of 2-30 m. Flat efficiency curve: 85%+ from 30-100% of rated flow, critical for run-of-river installations with seasonal flow variation. See: [Water Turbines](../energy/water-turbines.md)
