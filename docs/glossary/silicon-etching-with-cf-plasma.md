# Silicon etching with CF₄ plasma

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

In a 13.56 MHz RF plasma, CF₄ dissociates: CF₄ + e⁻ → CF₃· + F· + e⁻. Fluorine radicals attack silicon: Si + 4F· → SiF₄ (volatile, bp -86°C). Base etch rate ~100 nm/min. Adding O₂ (10-30%) to CF₄ increases F· concentration by reacting with CF₃· to form COF₂ and CO₂, releasing additional fluorine. Etch rate increases 2-5×.
