# Dust catcher

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Dust catcher (primary) — large cyclonic vessel where the gas velocity drops and coarse dust settles out by gravity and centrifugal force. Removes ~60-80% of the dust. The collected dust (flue dust) contains 30-50% iron and can be recycled to the sinter plant. See: [Blast Furnace and Cast Iron](../metals/blast-furnace.md)
