# Apprentice

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

(years 1-7): Bound by contract to a master. Lives in the master's household or workshop. Performs menial tasks initially (sweeping, material preparation, tool maintenance) while observing the master at work. Gradually gains hands-on experience with supervised tasks. Keeps a notebook of observations, recipes, and measurements. The master provides food, lodging, basic literacy instruction, and trade training. The apprentice (or apprentice's family) may pay a premium (fee) for the training.
