# Die

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Steel plate with shaped orifice. Circle → rod or tube (pipe). Slot → sheet or film (a 1.5 m wide slot die produces flat film at 50-200 m/min). Complex profile → window frames, weather stripping, custom shapes.
