# Chemical pulping

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

(kraft process): - Cook wood chips in "white liquor" (NaOH + Na₂S solution) at 150-170°C, 0.8-1.0 MPa for 2-4 hours in pressure vessel (digester). Dissolves lignin, releases cellulose fibers. Wash, bleach (chlorine dioxide or hydrogen peroxide). Kraft pulp is strong and permanent (cellulose only, no lignin). Yield: ~45-55% of wood mass.
