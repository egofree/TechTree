# Cold NBR

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

(30-50°C): Superior properties — more linear chains, less branching, higher molecular weight (Mooney viscosity ML 1+4 at 100°C: 50-90). Batch time 6-12 hours to 70-80% conversion (stopped early to prevent excessive branching and gel formation — conversion above 85% produces cross-linked "microgel" particles that degrade properties). Short-stopped with hydroquinone or DMDTC (dimethyldithiocarbamate). Coagulated with CaCl₂ or Al₂(SO₄)₃ + dilute acid. Washed, dried on hot roll dryer (80-100°C), ...
