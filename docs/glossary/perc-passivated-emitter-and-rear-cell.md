# PERC (Passivated Emitter and Rear Cell)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: vlsi-scaling

Rear surface passivation (Al₂O₃ + SiNₓ stack) reflects unabsorbed light back through cell. Localized rear contacts (laser contact opening). Efficiency improvement: 2-3% absolute over standard cells. Target: 20-22% module efficiency.
