# Hermetic packages

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Ceramic or metal packages that are sealed against gas and moisture ingress. Used for high-reliability applications: military (MIL-STD-883), aerospace, medical implants, and high-temperature environments. CERDIP (ceramic dual in-line): ceramic body with glass-sealed ceramic lid. CQFP (ceramic quad flat pack): ceramic body with Kovar leads brazed to the side walls.
