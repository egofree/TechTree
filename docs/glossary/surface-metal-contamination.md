# Surface metal contamination

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

<10¹⁰ atoms/cm² for critical metals (Fe, Cu, Ni, Na). Measured by TXRF (total reflection X-ray fluorescence) or VPD-ICP-MS (vapor phase decomposition followed by ICP-MS). Metals at the surface degrade gate oxide integrity and minority carrier lifetime.
