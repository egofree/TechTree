# Aluminum etching

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Cl₂ and BCl₃ plasma etching of aluminum interconnects. Cl₂ at 200-500 W RF power generates Cl· radicals: 2Al + 6Cl· → 2AlCl₃ (volatile above 180°C). BCl₃ serves as both Cl source and oxide scavenger: BCl₃ + Al₂O₃ → AlCl₃ + B₂O₃ removes the native aluminum oxide that would otherwise block etching. Wafer chuck heated to 40-70°C to promote AlCl₃ volatilization.
