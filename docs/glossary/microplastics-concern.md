# Microplastics concern

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Washed-off paint flakes contribute to microplastic pollution in waterways. The EU is considering restrictions on intentionally added microplastics in coatings and paints under REACH. This affects texture additives (polyethylene beads), certain rheology modifiers, and some polymer dispersions. The industry response includes developing biodegradable polymer alternatives and improving coating durability to reduce flaking and weathering loss.
