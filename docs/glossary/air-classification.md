# Air classification

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

For fine powders (<100 μm), use an air classifier (centrifugal separator that separates particles by terminal velocity in an air stream). Lighter particles are carried upward by the air flow; heavier particles fall out. Adjustable cutoff size by varying air velocity. Used to remove fines (<50 μm) from the furnace charge (fines reduce permeability) and to produce uniform powder for the Siemens process.
