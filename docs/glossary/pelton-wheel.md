# Pelton wheel

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Pelton wheel (impulse turbine — high head, low flow) — Principle**: One or more water jets from a nozzle strike spoon-shaped buckets on the runner perimeter. The jet's kinetic energy transfers to the bucket — all pressure conversion happens at the nozzle, not in the runner. Runner operates in air (not submerged). - **Head range**: 30-1000+ m.
