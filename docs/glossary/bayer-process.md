# Bayer process

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

Bayer process (produces high-purity alumina powder) — 1. **Crush and grind** bauxite ore to <150 μm 2. **Digest** in concentrated NaOH (caustic soda) at 140-240°C under pressure in autoclave. Aluminum hydroxides dissolve as sodium aluminate: Al(OH)₃ + NaOH → NaAl(OH)₄ 3. **Settle and filter** to remove insoluble red mud (iron oxides, silica, titania) 4. **Precipitate** aluminum trihydrate by seeding with fine Al(OH)₃ crystals and cooling.
