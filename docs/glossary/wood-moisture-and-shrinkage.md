# Wood moisture and shrinkage

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

All wood shrinks as it dries. Tangential shrinkage (around growth rings): 6-12%. Radial shrinkage (across rings): 2-6%. Longitudinal (along grain): <0.3%. Differential shrinkage between tangential and radial planes causes warping and checking. Heartwood shrinks less than sapwood.
