### SIK Decision: NEW DOMAIN
Clean Room Technology is a distinct domain with:
- Unique knowledge base: HVAC engineering, particle physics, filtration science, ISO standards
- Unique practitioner profile: Cleanroom/HVAC engineers, contamination control specialists
- 4 nodes (1 domain + 3 capabilities), 6 outbound edges, 1 inbound edge
