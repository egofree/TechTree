# Gas quality

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

H₂ >99.5% pure (main impurity: traces of O₂ and KOH mist). O₂ >99.0% pure. Further purification by catalytic recombination (remove trace O₂ from H₂ by passing over Pt catalyst with excess H₂) and molecular sieve drying.
