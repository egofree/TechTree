# Pelletized slag (expanded slag)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Molten slag is treated with a limited amount of water on a rotating drum, producing partially expanded pellets that are then cooled in air. The resulting lightweight aggregate (bulk density 0.7-1.1 t/m³) is used in lightweight concrete blocks and structural concrete where weight reduction is desired. The pelletizing process uses less water than granulation and produces a product that requires no crushing.
