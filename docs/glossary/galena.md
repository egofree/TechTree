# Galena

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Galena (PbS) — the primary ore. Lead-gray metallic luster, cubic cleavage. Often carries silver (0.01-0.5% Ag) — historically the main source of silver. Ore grade: 3-8% Pb in vein deposits; lower in Mississippi Valley-type deposits. See: [Non-Ferrous Metal Production](../metals/non-ferrous.md)
