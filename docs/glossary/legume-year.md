# Legume year

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

Clovers, beans, peas, or vetch. Rhizobium bacteria in root nodules fix 50-200 kg N/ha. Plow under as green manure or harvest grain 2. **Cereal year**: Wheat, barley, or oats. Exploits nitrogen fixed by preceding legume 3. **Root crop year**: Turnips, potatoes, or beets. Deep rooting breaks plow pan; cultivation suppresses weeds 4. **Fallow or cereal**: Rest the land, or plant a second cereal (rye or oats) if fertility permits
