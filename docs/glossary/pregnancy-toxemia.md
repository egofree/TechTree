# Pregnancy toxemia

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: critical | **Domains**: animals

Metabolic disease in late-pregnancy ewes from inadequate energy intake. Body mobilizes fat producing ketone bodies faster than the liver can process. Symptoms: lethargy, blindness, staggering, sweet-smelling breath. Treat with oral propylene glycol. Prevent by increasing energy 2-3 weeks before lambing.
