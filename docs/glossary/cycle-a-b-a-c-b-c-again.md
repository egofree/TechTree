# Cycle A-B, A-C, B-C again

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

Each round of pairwise scraping eliminates residual error. After 3-5 full cycles, all three plates converge to true flatness, verified when all combinations show 80%+ bearing contact. A typical 300×450 mm cast iron plate requires 50-100 hours of scraping to reach this level.
