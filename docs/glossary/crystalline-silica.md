# Crystalline silica

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

Crystalline silica (quartz, cristobalite, tridymite) — causes silicosis — irreversible lung scarring. Exposure limit: 0.025 mg/m³ respirable (ACGIH TLV). Wet grinding wherever possible. Respiratory protection (P100 mask minimum) during all dry powder handling. See: [Advanced Ceramics & Refractories](../ceramics/advanced-ceramics.md)
