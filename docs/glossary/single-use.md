# Single-use

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

The crucible slowly dissolves into the silicon melt during growth. Dissolution rate ~0.01-0.03 mm/hour. This introduces 10-20 ppma oxygen into the crystal (from SiO₂ → Si + O). Cannot be reused — the thinning wall weakens and may fail catastrophically during a subsequent run.
