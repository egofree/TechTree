# Cryogenic separation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Not common for H₂ purification alone, but used in syngas plants to recover H₂ and CO separately. Hydrogen (bp -253°C) is the most volatile component — remains as gas while CO, CH₄, CO₂ condense. Purity: 95-98% H₂. Used in refinery and methanol plants.
