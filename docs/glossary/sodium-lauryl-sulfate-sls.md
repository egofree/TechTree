# Sodium lauryl sulfate (SLS)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Coconut or palm kernel fatty alcohol (C12-C14) + SO₃ or chlorosulfonic acid → alkyl sulfate. Excellent foaming and cleaning, used in shampoos, toothpaste, and bubble bath. Produced from oleochemical feedstocks (renewable route) or from petrochemical ethylene (Ziegler alcohol process).
