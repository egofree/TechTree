# Autoclave hazards

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Steam at 121°C causes immediate third-degree burns on contact. The chamber is a pressure vessel: a defective door seal or relief valve can cause explosive depressurization. Never open an autoclave until pressure reads zero. Wear heat-resistant gloves when unloading. Stand clear of the door during opening, as residual steam escapes under pressure.
