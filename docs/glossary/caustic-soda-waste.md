# Caustic soda waste

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Dilute NaOH rinse water and spent solutions must be neutralized with dilute hydrochloric acid to pH 6-9 before discharge to waterways. The neutralization reaction NaOH + HCl → NaCl + H₂O produces common salt, which is non-toxic at moderate concentrations but can cause freshwater salinity problems if discharged in large volumes.
