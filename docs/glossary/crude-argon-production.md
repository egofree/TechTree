# Crude argon production

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

A side-draw vapor stream from the upper column at the argon peak concentration point (~10-15% Ar, remainder O₂ and N₂) feeds the crude argon column. This column operates at ~1.2 bar and produces crude argon (~95-97% Ar, 2-3% O₂, 1-2% N₂) at the top and returns oxygen-enriched liquid to the upper column at the bottom. The crude argon column requires 60-80 theoretical stages, implemented with structured packing to minimize pressure drop.
