# Continuous saponification

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

(modern, large-scale): - Fat and concentrated NaOH solution (30-50%) fed continuously into a saponification reactor at controlled ratio. The Sharples or Mazzoni continuous process uses a high-shear mixing column — fat and lye enter at bottom, react during upward flow, and fully saponified soap exits at top.
