# Scribal education

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

(ancient model): - Training began in childhood (age 8-12). Student copied model texts repeatedly: first individual signs, then sign combinations, then complete compositions. Progressed from simple lists (vocabulary, metrological tables) to complex documents (legal contracts, mathematical problems, literary works). - **Materials**: Clay tablets for permanent exercises. Wax tablets for practice (erasable, reusable). Reference tablets: teacher writes model on left column, student copies on right...
