# Dimensional tolerances

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, glass

Standard brick (230×114×64 mm) is manufactured to ±1-2 mm tolerance. Larger shapes (arch bricks, wedges, keys) require tighter tolerance for proper fitting without excessive mortar joints. Refractory mortar joints are weak points in furnace linings — thin joints (<2 mm) are preferred and are achieved by grinding brick contact surfaces.
