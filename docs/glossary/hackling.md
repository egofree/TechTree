# Hackling

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

Draw scutched fiber through graduated combs (hackles) — coarse (8-12 teeth/dm), medium (15-20), fine (25-35). Longer fibers (line) pass through; shorter broken fibers (tow) are caught in the comb teeth. Hackling grades: - Rough hackle: Removes remaining shive, aligns fibers. Line fiber length: 30-90 cm - Fine hackle: Splits fiber bundles, produces smooth, aligned sliver.
