# Poling

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Green tree trunks (historically) or natural gas/reductant gas bubbled through the melt reduces dissolved Cu₂O back to Cu, lowering oxygen to 0.03-0.05%. "Tough pitch" copper at ~99.5% Cu is cast into anodes (800-1000 mm × 800-1000 mm × 40-50 mm, ~250-400 kg each) for electrolytic refining.
