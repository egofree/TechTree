# Gas panel integration

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Each etch process uses 4-8 gases, each with its own MFC and shutoff valve. The gas panel (gas box) is a separate enclosure, typically 0.5-1.0 m³ volume, housing all MFCs, valves, filters, and pressure transducers. Gas lines from the panel to the chamber are 1/4" OD electropolished 316L stainless steel, heated to 80-120°C for gases that condense at room temperature.
