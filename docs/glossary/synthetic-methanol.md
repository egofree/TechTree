# Synthetic methanol

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Synthetic methanol (preferred at scale) — React CO + 2H₂ over Cu/ZnO/Al₂O₃ catalyst at 250°C, 50-100 bar. Synthesis gas from coal gasification or natural gas reforming (see Petroleum). - Yield: nearly quantitative at proper conditions. Single-pass conversion 15-25%, recycle unreacted gas. - Older technology: ZnO/Cr₂O₃ catalyst at 300-400°C, 20-30 MPa — harsher conditions but robust.
