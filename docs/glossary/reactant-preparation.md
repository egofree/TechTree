# Reactant preparation

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Blend elemental powders in stoichiometric ratio (e.g., Ti + C for TiC, Ti + B for TiB₂, Mo + 2Si for MoSi₂). Press into a green compact at 50-200 MPa or fill into a graphite die. Green density: 50-70% theoretical.
