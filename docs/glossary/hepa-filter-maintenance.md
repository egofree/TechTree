# HEPA filter maintenance

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: photolithography

Scheduled upkeep of High-Efficiency Particulate Air filters in cleanrooms including regular DOP/PAO challenge testing to verify 99.97% efficiency at 0.3 μm, filter change-out when resistance exceeds limits, and inspection of filter frames and seals for bypass leaks. Proper maintenance ensures cleanroom classification compliance.
