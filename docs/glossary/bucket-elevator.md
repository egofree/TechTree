# Bucket elevator

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Vertical transport of coarse material. Steel buckets attached to a chain or belt, running between sprockets at top and bottom. Lift height: 10-30 m. Capacity: 10-200 tonnes/hour. Used for elevating crushed ore from below-ground crushers to surface processing. Enclose in a steel casing for dust control.
