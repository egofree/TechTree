# Airlock pressure cascades

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Personnel and material airlocks form pressure steps between the cleanroom and the outside. A typical cascade: outside (ambient) → gowning room (+5 Pa) → cleanroom (+15 Pa). Each step prevents backflow of contaminated air. Interlocked doors (only one open at a time) maintain the pressure barrier even during transit.
