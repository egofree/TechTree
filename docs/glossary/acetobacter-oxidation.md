# Acetobacter oxidation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Acetic acid bacteria (Acetobacter aceti, Gluconobacter oxydans) oxidize ethanol to acetic acid aerobically: CH₃CH₂OH + O₂ → CH₃COOH + H₂O. Surface culture (Orleans method): wine or cider in barrels with perforated covers, bacteria form a pellicle at the air-liquid interface. Slow (weeks to months), produces 3-6% acetic acid.
