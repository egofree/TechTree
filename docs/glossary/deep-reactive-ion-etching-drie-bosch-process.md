# Deep Reactive Ion Etching (DRIE) — Bosch process

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: photolithography

High-aspect-ratio etching technique alternating sulfur hexafluoride (SF₆) plasma etch cycles with octafluorocyclobutane (C₄F₈) fluorocarbon passivation deposition, achieving vertical sidewalls with scalloping of 50-200 nm. Produces features with aspect ratios >20:1 for MEMS and through-silicon vias.
