# Drying

> **Type**: process | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics, chemistry, energy, foundations, knowledge, mining, plants, polymers

Dries by solvent evaporation, not chemical reaction. Drying time: 15-30 minutes for thin coats at room temperature (20°C, 50% RH). At 30°C: 10-15 minutes. Fastest-drying natural finish — no other natural finish (oil varnish, lacquer, wax) approaches this speed. No curing time needed — fully hardened when solvent has evaporated.
