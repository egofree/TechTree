# Sulfuric acid plant scale

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

World production ~250 million tonnes/year. A single modern contact process plant produces 1,000-5,000 tonnes/day H₂SO₄. The plant is typically integrated with a smelter (capturing SO₂ from copper or nickel smelting) or a sulfur combustion source. Capital cost: $100-300 million for a 2000 t/d plant. Energy balance: the contact process is a net exporter of energy.
