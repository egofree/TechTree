# Heat resistant grade

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Novolac + hexamine + mineral filler (asbestos historically, now replaced by mineral wool, silica flour, or clay). Continuous service temperature to 200°C. Lower shrinkage (0.2-0.5%) and better dimensional stability at elevated temperature. Used for: motor commutator segments, high-temperature electrical connectors, heater components.
