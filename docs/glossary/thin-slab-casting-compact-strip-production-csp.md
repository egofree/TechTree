# Thin-slab casting (Compact Strip Production — CSP)

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Casts 50-80 mm slab directly into an in-line tunnel furnace (holding at 1100°C), then into a 5-6 stand hot strip mill. Eliminates the conventional slab reheating step entirely. Capital cost 40-50% lower than conventional thick-slab route. Product: hot-rolled coil down to 1.0 mm thickness.
