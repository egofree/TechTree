# Nitrogen

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

(99-99.99%): Used as inert blanket gas, carrier gas, purge gas. Store as compressed gas or liquid N₂ in vacuum-insulated dewar (Dewar flask — double-walled vessel, vacuum between walls, silvered inner wall for radiation shielding. Evaporation rate: 0.5-2% per day).
