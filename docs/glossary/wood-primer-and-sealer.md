# Wood primer and sealer

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Shellac is an excellent universal sealer. Applied as a thin coat (1 lb cut) before paint or varnish, it seals porous surfaces, prevents tannin bleed-through (especially on oak, mahogany, and cherry — tannins are water-soluble phenolic compounds that leach through water-based paints, causing brown staining), and bonds to almost any substrate.
