# Sulfide ores

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Sulfide ores (~30% of production) — Pentlandite ((Fe,Ni)₉S₈) with pyrrhotite (Fe₁₋ₓS) and chalcopyrite (CuFeS₂). Major deposits: Sudbury (Canada), Norilsk (Russia), Kambalda (Australia). Nickel grade: 1-3% Ni. These ores also carry Cu, Co, and platinum-group metals as valuable byproducts. See: [Non-Ferrous Metal Production](../metals/non-ferrous.md)
