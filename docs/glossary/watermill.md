# Watermill

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Watermill (if water power available) — Overshot water wheel 2-4 m diameter drives a horizontal millstone 0.8-1.2 m diameter through a gear train. Output: 50-100 kg flour/hour, feeding a whole settlement from one mill. Requires Machine Tools-level gearing and precision construction. Water flow of 100-300 liters/second with 2-4 m head drives the wheel. The watermill frees enormous human labor and is one of the highest-return investments in a growing community.
