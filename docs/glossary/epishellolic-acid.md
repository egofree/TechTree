# epishellolic acid

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

epishellolic acid (~25%) — sesquiterpene carboxylic acids (C₁₅) that contribute rigidity and hydrophobic character. The aldehyde group of shellolic acid can undergo esterification and acetal formation, contributing to the aging (insolubilization) of shellac. See: [Shellac Production](../polymers/shellac.md)
