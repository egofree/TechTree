# Cathode lining

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Bottom and lower sides lined with prebaked carbon cathode blocks (anthracite + 25-30% coal tar pitch, baked at 1000-1200°C). Blocks typically 400-700 mm × 400-600 mm × 1200-2500 mm, joined with carbonaceous ramming paste. Steel current-collector bars (100-200 mm diameter round or rectangular) embedded in grooves in the cathode blocks, sealed with cast iron or carbonaceous paste.
