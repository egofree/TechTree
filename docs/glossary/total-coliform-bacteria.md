# Total coliform bacteria

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Presence/absence test using lauryl tryptose broth in a presence-absence (P/A) bottle. Add 100 mL water sample, incubate at 35°C for 24-48 hours. Gas production (bubbles in inverted Durham tube) or turbidity indicates coliform bacteria present. Coliforms indicate fecal contamination. Any positive result means the water is unsafe for drinking without further treatment.
