# Mirror coating

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass, optics

(aluminum): - Evaporate high-purity aluminum in vacuum at 10⁻⁴ Pa. Tungsten filament or boat, heated to melt aluminum (660°C) then to evaporation (~1200°C). Vapor deposits on mirror substrate above. - Reflectivity: ~92% for fresh aluminum (vs ~85% for silver, which tarnishes). Protective overcoat of SiO₂ (quarter-wave) extends lifetime. - **Requires**: same vacuum infrastructure as anti-reflection coating, plus aluminum source material.
