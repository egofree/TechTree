# Reef knot (square knot)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Joins two ropes of equal diameter. Flat and secure under moderate load. Used for reefing sails. Never use for joining ropes under heavy load (can capsize and slip). For critical load-bearing joins, use a sheet bend (for unequal diameters) or a double fisherman's knot.
