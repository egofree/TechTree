# Peirce-Smith converter

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(matte → blister copper): A horizontal cylindrical vessel (3-5 m dia × 9-12 m long), lined with basic refractory (magnesite-chrome), mounted on trunnions for rotation. Molten matte (50-70% Cu) is charged, and air is blown through submerged tuyeres (30-60 mm dia, 40-50 tuyeres along the cylinder length) at 1-2 bar.
