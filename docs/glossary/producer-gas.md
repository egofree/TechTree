# Producer gas

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, energy

(CO + H₂ + N₂): - **Gas producer**: Cylindrical shaft 1-3 m diameter × 2-5 m tall, lined with refractory brick. Fill with lump charcoal or coke. Ignite bottom. Blow limited air upward through bed. Incomplete combustion produces CO (carbon monoxide) instead of CO₂. - C + ½O₂ → CO (exothermic, provides heat) - C + H₂O → CO + H₂ (endothermic water-gas reaction — add steam to enrich gas) - **Gas composition**: ~25% CO, ~10% H₂, ~60% N₂, ~5% CO₂.
