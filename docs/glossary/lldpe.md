# LLDPE

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

LLDPE (linear low-density polyethylene) — Density 0.915-0.925 g/cm³. Produced with Ziegler-Natta or metallocene catalysts at low pressure, copolymerizing ethylene with butene, hexene, or octene (2-8% comonomer). Combines the strength of HDPE with the flexibility of LDPE. Metallocene-catalyzed LLDPE (mLLDPE) has narrower molecular weight distribution (Mw/Mn ~2 vs. 4-6 for Ziegler-Natta), giving better optical clarity and more consistent sealing performance.
