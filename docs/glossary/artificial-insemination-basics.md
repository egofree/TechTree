# Artificial insemination basics

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Collecting semen from a superior male and depositing it in the female reproductive tract allows wider distribution of desirable genetics without moving live animals. Requires knowledge of female estrus cycles (cattle cycle every 21 days, sheep every 16-17 days during breeding season, goats every 18-21 days, pigs every 21 days, mares every 21 days from spring through autumn).
