# Toxicity ranges

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

Plant compounds do not have pharmaceutical-grade dose uniformity. The same species can vary 3-10x in potency depending on growing conditions, harvest time, and preparation. Species with narrow therapeutic windows (e.g., cardiac glycosides from Digitalis/foxglove — not in the tech tree but widely known) can be lethal at just 2-3x the therapeutic dose.
