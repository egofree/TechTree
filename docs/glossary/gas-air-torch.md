# Gas-air torch

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

(moderate temperature, ~1200°C): - Town gas or propane mixed with ambient air. Simple construction — single tube with gas orifice, air drawn in by Venturi effect. - Suitable for soft glass (soda-lime, lead glass). NOT hot enough for borosilicate (softening point ~820°C — needs higher flame temperature than glass can achieve in gas-air). - Advantages: Simple, cheap, no oxygen supply needed.
