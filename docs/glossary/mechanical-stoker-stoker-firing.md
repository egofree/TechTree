# Mechanical stoker (stoker firing)

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Auger, ram, or chain grate feeds coal onto the grate automatically. Chain grate moves the entire fuel bed slowly through the furnace — coal enters at one end, ash falls off the other. More uniform combustion than hand-firing, lower labor. Requires power (steam or electric) to drive the mechanism. Thermal efficiency ~65-75%. Suitable for medium-sized boilers (industrial, institutional heating).
