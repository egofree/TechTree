# Crawling

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

Glaze pulls back from the surface during firing, leaving bare patches. Causes: glaze applied too thick, dusty or oily bisque surface, glaze shrinkage too high during drying. Fix: thin the glaze (lower SG to 1.3-1.4), clean bisque with a damp sponge before glazing, ensure bisque is fully fired (underfired bisque is dusty).
