# Continuous casting rod systems

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

The Southwire process casts 8-12 mm rod continuously through a graphite-coated copper whee mold, then hot-rolls it through 8-14 rolling stands to final rod diameter. Line speed: 5-15 m/min. Annual output per line: 50,000-200,000 tonnes. The Properzi process uses a rotating ring mold instead of a wheel. Both produce rod with <0.04% oxygen (tough-pitch grade) suitable for direct wire drawing without additional processing.
