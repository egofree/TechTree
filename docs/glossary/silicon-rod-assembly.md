# Silicon rod assembly

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Thin silicon seed rods (~5 mm diameter, inverted U-shape) mounted on electrode feedthroughs at the base of the bell jar. Typical configurations: 2-12 rods per reactor. Each rod pair forms a U-shape with the base of the U at the top. Electrical current passes through the rods to heat them.
