# Inspection sequence

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

For a critical structural weld, apply methods in order: (1) visual inspection of every weld, no exceptions; (2) magnetic particle or dye penetrant for all surface-breaking defect detection on steel; (3) radiographic or ultrasonic testing for internal defects on designated critical joints (pressure vessel seams, structural column splices, crane hook welds).
