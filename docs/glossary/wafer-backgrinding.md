# Wafer backgrinding

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Grind back of completed wafer from ~725 μm to 200-350 μm (thinner die fit in thinner packages, better heat dissipation). Diamond-grit grinding wheel, water coolant. Multi-step: coarse grind (30 μm grit, ~600 μm/min removal) → fine grind (5 μm grit, ~100 μm/min) → polish (1 μm grit). Stress relief etch (brief HF dip to remove grinding damage).
