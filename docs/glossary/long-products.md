# Long products

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(from billets and blooms): - **Bar**: Round, square, hexagonal, or flat cross-sections, 8-100 mm diameter, rolled on bar mills (10-18 stands in continuous train). Used for shafts, fasteners, reinforcing bar, and machining stock. Reinforcing bar (rebar) is the largest single long product by volume. - **Rod**: 5.5-20 mm diameter, coiled at high speed (wire rod mill, finishing speeds up to 100-120 m/s).
