# Trichlorosilane Purification Detail

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Raw TCS from the fluidized bed reactor contains impurities: methyltrichlorosilane (bp 66.4C), dichlorosilane (bp 8.2C), silicon tetrachloride (bp 57.6C), and boron/phosphorus chlorides (BCI3 bp 12.5C, PCl3 bp 76.1C). Fractional distillation in a multi-column system separates these: Column 1 removes lights (DCS, BCl3) at overhead temperature 8-30C, Column 2 removes heavies (SiCl4, PCl3) at bottom temperature 60-80C, Column 3 produces specification TCS at overhead 31.8C with purity >99.9%.
