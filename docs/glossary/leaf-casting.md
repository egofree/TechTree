# Leaf-casting

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

(for losses — holes and missing areas): Suspend the damaged leaf in a leaf-casting tank filled with paper pulp slurry. Vacuum draws pulp through the hole, depositing fibers that interlock with the original paper edges. The resulting fill matches the thickness and texture of the surrounding paper. Requires specialized equipment but produces nearly invisible repairs.
