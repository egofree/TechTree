# Intel 1103 DRAM

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: computing

Intel 1103 DRAM (1970) — 1 Kbit organized as 1,024 × 1 bit. Three-transistor cell design. Access time 300-600 ns. Replaced magnetic core in most new designs by 1974. See: [Electronic Computing](../computing/electronic.md)
