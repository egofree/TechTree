# Chilled water system

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Temperature control (22±0.5°C) requires chilled water at ~7°C supplied to air handling units. Chiller capacity: 50-200 W per m² of cleanroom floor area (depending on internal heat load from process equipment). Redundant chillers (N+1) ensure continuous cooling during maintenance.
