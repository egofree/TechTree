# Rotary kiln DRI

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(coal-based, used in India): - Uses non-coking coal as both the reductant and the fuel. Iron ore and coal are fed into a slowly rotating inclined kiln (3-5 rpm, 2-5° inclination). - Air is injected along the kiln length to burn the volatile matter and partially combust the coal, providing heat. The coal char provides the reducing agent (C + CO₂ → 2CO; CO reduces the ore). - Operating temperature: 950-1050°C.
