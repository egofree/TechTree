# Accuracy limits

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: computing

A 25 cm rule resolves about 3 significant figures. Reading error comes from visual interpolation between graduations, which at 1 mm spacing on the C scale corresponds to roughly 0.2-0.5% of reading. A 50 cm rule improves to 4 figures. Circular slide rules (effective scale length 50-125 cm) achieve 4-5 figures.
