# Construction sequence

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals, chemistry

Compressor → aftercooler (initial air cooling) → molecular sieve traps (remove H₂O, CO₂) → main heat exchanger (cool to near-liquefaction) → expansion valve or turbine (final cooling to liquid) → lower distillation column → upper distillation column → product storage (compressed gas cylinders or vacuum-insulated dewars).
