# Energy storage

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

E = ρ × g × h × V × η, where ρ = water density (1000 kg/m³), g = 9.81 m/s², h = head (m), V = volume (m³), η = efficiency. Example: 100 m head, 10,000 m³ upper reservoir = 1000 × 9.81 × 100 × 10000 × 0.75 / 3.6 × 10⁶ = ~2044 kWh. This is substantial.
