# Straightedge verification

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

Check flatness of long surfaces (lathe beds) with a straightedge. Generate the first straightedge by scraping a cast iron bar against the surface plate, then verifying straightness by the three-bar method (analogous to three plates). A straightedge accurate to 0.02 mm over 500 mm length is achievable by hand scraping.
