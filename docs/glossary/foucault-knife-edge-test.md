# Foucault knife-edge test

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

The definitive test for mirror figure. Place a point light source (pinhole illuminated by LED or candle) at the mirror's center of curvature. The reflected light returns to a focus near the source. Intercept the returning cone with a razor blade (knife-edge) mounted on a micrometer stage. Moving the blade laterally across the beam: a perfect sphere darkens uniformly across the entire mirror face. Any zone that appears bright or shadowed ahead of the rest indicates a surface error.
