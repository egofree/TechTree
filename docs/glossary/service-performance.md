# Service performance

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

PCE 31-33. Maximum service temperature 1650-1700°C. Load-bearing capacity at 1600°C is exceptional — the brick softens very little even within 50°C of its failure point. Bulk density 1.75-1.85 g/cm³ (lighter than fireclay). Thermal conductivity 1.5-2.0 W/m·K at 1000°C.
