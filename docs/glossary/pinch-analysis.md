# Pinch analysis

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Plot composite heating and cooling curves (temperature vs. enthalpy) for all process streams. The pinch point is where hot and cold curves are closest (minimum ΔT). Design heat exchanger network: match hot streams above the pinch with cold streams above the pinch; match cold streams below with cold below.
