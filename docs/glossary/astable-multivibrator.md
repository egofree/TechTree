# Astable multivibrator

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Two transistors, two collector resistors, two base resistors, and two coupling capacitors. Each transistor alternately turns on and off. Frequency: f = 1/(1.4·R·C) where R is the base resistor and C is the coupling capacitor (symmetric circuit). Used as a simple clock oscillator. Output: square wave at the collector of either transistor. Duty cycle ~50% with equal R and C values.
