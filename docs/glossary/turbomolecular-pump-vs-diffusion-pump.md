# Turbomolecular pump vs. diffusion pump

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Turbo pump — fast startup (1-3 min), clean (no oil vapor), expensive to build (precision rotor at 30,000-90,000 RPM). Diffusion pump — slower startup (15-30 min), requires cold trap to prevent oil backstreaming, simpler construction, lower cost.
