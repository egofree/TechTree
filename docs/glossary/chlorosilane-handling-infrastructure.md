# Chlorosilane Handling Infrastructure

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

The TCS route generates byproduct SiCl4 (1.0-1.5 kg per kg SiH4). SiCl4 is corrosive liquid (bp 57.6C, density 1.48 g/cm3) fuming in moist air. Storage: glass-lined or PTFE-lined tanks, N2 blanketed, secondary containment 110% tank volume. SiCl4 recycling: react with H2 at 1000-1200C to regenerate TCS, or hydrolyze to fumed silica (SiCl4 + 2H2O -> SiO2 + 4HCl). HCl byproduct absorbed in water for reuse in TCS synthesis.
