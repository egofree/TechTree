# Calibration Management

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement

Maintain calibration records for every instrument with serial number, calibration date, due date, standard used (traceable to national standards), and as-found/as-left data. Calibration intervals: DMMs 6-12 months, oscilloscopes 12 months, meggers 12 months. Field calibration checks between formal calibrations using reference standards (voltage reference: 10.000V ±0.01% for DMM spot-check, precision resistor 100.00 Ω ±0.01% for ohmmeter verification).
