# Personal protective equipment (PPE)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Acid splash suit (PVC or neoprene), chemical splash goggles (not safety glasses — goggles seal around the eyes), face shield over goggles for high-splash-risk operations, acid-resistant gloves (nitrile or neoprene, inspected for holes before each use), chemical apron for additional torso protection, rubber boots with pant legs outside the boots (acid running into boots is a severe burn hazard).
