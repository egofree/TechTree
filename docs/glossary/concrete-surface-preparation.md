# Concrete surface preparation

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Remove laitance (weak surface layer of fine particles), curing compound, and form release agent. Methods: abrasive blast (shot blast or sand blast), acid etch (10% HCl or muriatic acid, 1:4 dilution with water, applied at 0.1-0.2 L/m², scrubbed, and thoroughly rinsed), or mechanical scarification (rotary milling machine with tungsten carbide teeth).
