# Flammability and explosion

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

All liquid and gaseous fuels form explosive mixtures with air. Gasoline vapor ignites at 1.4-7.6% concentration. Natural gas (methane) at 5-15%. Never store fuels near ignition sources. Use in well-ventilated areas. Bond and ground containers during transfer to prevent static discharge ignition.
