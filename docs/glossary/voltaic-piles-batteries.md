# Voltaic piles / batteries

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

(first electrical source): - **Construction**: Stack alternating disks: copper plate → cardboard soaked in brine (or sulfuric acid) → zinc plate → repeat. 10-20 cells produce 10-20V DC at low current. - **Output**: ~1V per cell, ~50-100 mA. Sufficient for early electroplating experiments, telegraph operation, electrochemistry exploration. Not sufficient for industrial power. - **Daniell cell** (improved): Copper electrode in CuSO₄ solution, separated by porous pot from zinc electrode in ZnSO₄...
