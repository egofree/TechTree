# Settle and filter

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

to remove insoluble red mud (iron oxides, silica, titania) 4. **Precipitate** aluminum trihydrate by seeding with fine Al(OH)₃ crystals and cooling. Pure Al(OH)₃ crystals form. 5. **Calcine** at 1100-1200°C to drive off water: 2 Al(OH)₃ → Al₂O₃ + 3 H₂O. Produces α-alumina (corundum) powder, 99.0-99.99% pure.
