# Spinning mounts

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

When 5th instar larvae stop feeding and begin wandering, transfer them to spinning mounts. Traditional mounts are straw bundles, corrugated cardboard frames, or paper cones arranged on trays. Each larva needs a separate cell or space roughly 3 x 3 cm. Spinning density: 400-500 cocoons per m² of mount surface.
