# Boundary scan (JTAG)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: vlsi-scaling

IEEE 1149.1 standard. Adds scan cells at every I/O pin for board-level interconnect testing. TAP (Test Access Port) controller with 4 mandatory pins (TCK, TMS, TDI, TDO). Used for in-system programming, debug, and board-level connectivity verification.
