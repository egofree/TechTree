# 1870-1902

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Global submarine cable network expanded to connect all inhabited continents. All used gutta-percha insulation. By 1900, over 300,000 km of submarine cable had been laid worldwide, all insulated with gutta-percha - **1940s**: Polyethylene replaced gutta-percha for new cable installations
