# Deoxidation and finishing

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Deoxidation and finishing (30-60 min) — stop ore additions when target carbon is reached. Add ferromanganese (Mn 70-80%, 8-12 kg/tonne steel) and aluminum (0.3-0.8 kg/tonne) to deoxidize. Take samples, adjust composition with ferroalloys. See: [Modern Steelmaking](../metals/steelmaking.md)
