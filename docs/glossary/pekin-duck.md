# Pekin duck

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

The most common meat duck worldwide. White plumage, 3.5-5 kg adults. Market weight 3.0-3.5 kg at 7-9 weeks on 2.0-2.5 kg feed per kg live weight. Moderate egg layer (140-200/year). Nervous temperament but hardy. Poor broody mother — artificial incubation typically required.
