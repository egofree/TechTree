# Straight cutting oil

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Mineral oil or lard oil, undiluted. Best lubrication, poorest cooling (low heat capacity). For: tapping, threading, broaching, heavy turning (where lubrication matters more than cooling). Add sulfur or chlorine compounds (extreme pressure additives — react with metal surface at high temperature → solid lubricating film that prevents welding of chip to tool).
