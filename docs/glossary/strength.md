# Strength

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, textiles

Strength refers to 12 mm steel wire rope (6×19) breaking strength ~80 kN (vs. ~10 kN for 12 mm hemp). Weight: ~0.5 kg/m vs. ~0.14 kg/m for hemp. The dramatic strength advantage makes wire rope essential for mine hoisting, bridge cables, crane ropes, and elevator suspension.
