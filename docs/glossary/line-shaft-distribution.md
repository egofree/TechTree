# Line shaft distribution

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

A single water wheel of 10 HP can drive an entire small workshop of 5-8 machines simultaneously via a horizontal line shaft. The iron shaft (50-100 mm diameter) runs the length of the workshop, supported by hung bearings from ceiling joists. Each machine connects via its own belt drive from a countershaft, with cone pulleys providing 2-4 speed ranges.
