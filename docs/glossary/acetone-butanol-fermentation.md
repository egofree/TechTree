# Acetone-butanol fermentation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

(Weizmann process): - **Organism**: Clostridium acetobutylicum (anaerobic bacterium). Can be isolated from soil or preserved culture. - **Process**: Ferment starch (corn, potato, or grain mash) at 35-37°C for 48-72 hours. Products: acetone (~30%), n-butanol (~60%), ethanol (~10%) by volume, plus CO₂ and H₂ gases. - **Distillation**: Separate by fractional distillation.
