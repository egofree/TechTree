# Dopant gases

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Arsine (AsH₃) and phosphine (PH₃) — extremely toxic (IDLH 3 ppm and 50 ppm respectively). Used in ion implantation and epitaxial doping. Stored in cylinder cabinets with gas detectors, automatic shutoff valves, and scrubbers (thermal oxidation or wet chemical). Boron trifluoride (BF₃) — p-type dopant, less toxic but corrosive.
