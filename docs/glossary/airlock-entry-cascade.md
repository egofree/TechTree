# Airlock entry cascade

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Three-stage progression — pre-change room (ambient pressure) → gowning room (+5 Pa) → cleanroom (+15 Pa). Interlocked doors prevent two adjacent doors from opening simultaneously, maintaining the pressure barrier. Sticky adhesive mat at each threshold removes particles from shoe covers and cart wheels (peel off top layer when visibly soiled).
