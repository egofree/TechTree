# Boron

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

This means boron distributes almost equally between solid and liquid phases during crystallization, making segregation-based methods nearly useless for boron. Since boron is a compensating dopant that degrades solar cell efficiency, dedicated removal is essential for solar-grade silicon.
