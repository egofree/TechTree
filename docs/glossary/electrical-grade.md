# Electrical grade

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Novolac + hexamine + mica powder (15-30%) or glass fiber (20-30%). Highest dielectric strength (16-20 kV/mm) and arc resistance (60-180 seconds — the mineral filler prevents carbon tracking). Tracking resistance is critical for high-voltage switchgear — phenolic compounds that track (form conductive carbon paths) across the surface under arcing conditions cause catastrophic flashover.
