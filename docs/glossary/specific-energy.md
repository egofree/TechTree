# Specific energy

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Specific energy refers to 30-40 Wh/kg (flooded), 25-35 Wh/kg (deep-cycle with thicker plates). A 12V, 100 Ah automotive battery weighs approximately 25-30 kg. See: [Energy Storage & Diversification](../energy/storage.md)
