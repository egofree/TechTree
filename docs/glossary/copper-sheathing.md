# Copper sheathing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Copper sheathing (later stage) — thin copper sheets (0.5-1.0 mm) nailed to hull below waterline. Prevents fouling (barnacles, weed growth) and boring worm attack. Reduces drag and increases speed 1-2 knots. Requires copper available from metallurgy. See: [Water Transport](../transport/shipping.md)
