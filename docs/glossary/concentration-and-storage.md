# Concentration and storage

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Strain the liquid through fine cloth to remove solids. Evaporate in shallow pans to 30-50% concentration. Cool to a firm gel. Slice into sheets or break into granules. Dry in a warm, ventilated area (not in direct sun, which can melt the gel). Store in a dry place. Properly dried hide glue keeps indefinitely.
