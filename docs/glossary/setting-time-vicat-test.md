# Setting time (Vicat test)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

A 1 mm diameter needle penetrates a cement paste sample (500 g cement + water at standard consistency, typically 25-30% water by weight). Initial set: needle penetration ≤ 5 mm from the base plate (typically 45-120 minutes for OPC). Final set: needle makes no visible impression on the surface (typically 2-5 hours).
