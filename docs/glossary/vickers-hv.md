# Vickers (HV)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Diamond pyramid indenter, any load from 10 gf (microhardness) to 100 kgf. Measure diagonal of the square indentation. HV = 1.854 × load / diagonal². One continuous scale covers all metals. Ideal for microstructural analysis (hardness of individual phases, case depth measurement, heat-affected zone mapping). Range: 5-2000 HV.
