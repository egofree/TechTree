# DC (direct current)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

Generator or rectified AC. Smoother arc, easier to strike, directional heat control. Two polarity options: DCEN (electrode negative / straight polarity) — 70% heat in workpiece, deeper penetration. DCEP (electrode positive / reverse polarity) — 70% heat in electrode, faster deposition, shallower penetration. Typical: 50-200 A, 20-30 V arc voltage.
