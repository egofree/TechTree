# Architectural latex paint

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

(typical interior wall paint): Water 35%, TiO₂ 20%, calcium carbonate (extender) 15%, acrylic emulsion (binder, 50% solids) 25%, additives 5%. PVC (pigment volume concentration) = 55-70% — above CPVC (critical PVC, ~55%) the film is porous (matte finish). Below CPVC: glossy, washable.
