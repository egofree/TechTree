# Passivation stacks

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: vlsi-scaling

Thermal SiO₂ + SiNₓ (front, SiNₓ also serves as anti-reflection coating). Al₂O₃ for p-type surfaces (surface recombination velocity < 10 cm/s achievable). Hydrogen passivation from SiNₓ anneal passivates bulk defects.
