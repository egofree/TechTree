# Amine hardeners

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Amine hardeners (DETA, TETA) — room temperature to 80°C cure, fast pot life (30-60 minutes). Aliphatic amines (DETA, TETA) cure at room temperature; cycloaliphatic amines (isophorone diamine, IPDA) require elevated temperature (60-80°C) but produce higher Tg. See: [Thermosetting Polymers](../polymers/thermosets.md)
