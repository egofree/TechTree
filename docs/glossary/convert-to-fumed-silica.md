# Convert to fumed silica

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Burn SiCl₄ in H₂/O₂ flame: SiCl₄ + 2H₂ + O₂ → SiO₂ + 4HCl. Produces fumed silica (Aerosil) — extremely fine amorphous SiO₂ particles (7-40 nm). Used as thickening agent, desiccant, and CMP slurry component. Valuable co-product.
