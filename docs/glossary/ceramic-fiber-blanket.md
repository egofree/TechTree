# Ceramic fiber blanket

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics, chemistry

Alumina-silica fiber (45-60% Al₂O₃, balance SiO₂) produced by melting raw materials at 2000°C and attenuating the melt into fibers (diameter 2-4 μm, length 50-200 mm) by blowing or spinning. Formed into blankets (density 64-160 kg/m³, thickness 13-50 mm) needled for dimensional stability. Classification temperature: 1260°C (standard), 1400°C (high-alumina), 1600°C (zirconia-modified).
