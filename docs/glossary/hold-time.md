# Hold time

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Maintain peak temperature for 30-60 minutes, depending on the dye and fiber type. Short holds (30 min) suit high-affinity combinations (wool + madder). Long holds (60 min) suit low-affinity combinations (cotton + weld). For the deepest shades, turn off the heat after the hold and let the fiber cool in the bath overnight (exhaust dyeing). The fiber continues to absorb dye as the bath cools.
