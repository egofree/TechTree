# HCl neutralization

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Scrubber systems (packed column, caustic recirculation) neutralize HCl gas from CVD reactor exhaust. NaOH or Ca(OH)₂ scrubbing solution absorbs HCl → NaCl or CaCl₂ brine. Brine is disposed as industrial wastewater or evaporated to dry salt.
