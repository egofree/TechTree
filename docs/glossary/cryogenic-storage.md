# Cryogenic storage

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Double-walled vacuum-insulated Dewar flasks. Inner vessel: stainless steel. Outer vessel: carbon steel. Vacuum space filled with perlite or multi-layer insulation (MLI — alternating aluminum foil and fiberglass). Evaporation losses: 0.5-2%/day depending on tank size and insulation quality.
