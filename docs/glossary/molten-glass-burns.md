# Molten glass burns

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Glass batch melts at 1100-1400°C and working temperatures reach 1500-1600°C during fining. Molten glass adheres to skin and causes severe deep burns that are extremely difficult to remove. Wear heat-resistant gloves, arm protection, face shield, and closed-toe boots at all times near the furnace. Use properly sized gathering irons and blowing pipes — a dropped gather of molten glass splatters widely. Never use wet or damp tools to contact molten glass; trapped moisture causes steam explosions.
