# Cold box

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

The entire cryogenic section (heat exchangers, distillation columns, expansion turbines, valves, and piping) is enclosed in a single rectangular steel enclosure (the "cold box") typically 3-5 m wide × 3-5 m deep × 20-40 m tall. The cold box is filled with perlite insulation under a slight nitrogen purge atmosphere.
