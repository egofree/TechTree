# Raschig rings

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Simple hollow cylinders (length ≈ diameter), 25-50 mm size. Ceramic or metal. Oldest packing type. HETP: 0.4-0.6 m. Cheap but inefficient by modern standards. Liquid distribution degrades with height — requires redistribution every 3-4 m.
