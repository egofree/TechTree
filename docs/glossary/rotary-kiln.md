# Rotary kiln

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Steel tube 30-60 m long, 2-4 m diameter, lined with refractory brick (magnesia or high-alumina). Rotates at 1-3 RPM, inclined 3-4°. Material moves counter to hot gas flow. Peak temperature **1450°C** in the burning zone. Fuel: coal, natural gas, or oil — 3-5 kg fuel per kg cement.
