# COD and BOD in wash water

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

The effluent from pulp washing contains dissolved organic material measured as chemical oxygen demand (COD, 1000-5000 mg/L) and biochemical oxygen demand (BOD₅, 300-1500 mg/L). Modern mills treat this effluent in biological wastewater treatment (activated sludge at 0.5-2.0 kg BOD/kg MLSS/day, retention time 4-8 hours) before discharge. Treatment efficiency: 90-95% BOD removal.
