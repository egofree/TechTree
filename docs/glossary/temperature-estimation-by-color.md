# Temperature estimation by color

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Temperature estimation by color (no instruments) — Dark red ~550°C, cherry red ~650°C, bright cherry ~750°C, dark orange ~850°C, orange ~950°C, light yellow ~1050°C, white ~1150°C+. See: [Copper & Bronze Production](../metals/copper-bronze.md)
