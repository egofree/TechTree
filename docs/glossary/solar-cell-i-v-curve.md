# Solar cell I-V curve

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Illuminate cell with standard solar spectrum (AM1.5, 1000 W/m²). Measure open-circuit voltage (Voc), short-circuit current (Isc), and maximum power point (Pmax). Fill factor FF = Pmax/(Voc × Isc). Efficiency η = Pmax/(incident power × cell area). A good 156×156 mm cell: Voc ~0.62V, Isc ~9A, FF ~0.78, η ~17%.
