# Grasserie

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Grasserie (nuclear polyhedrosis) — viral infection causing larvae to become swollen, glossy, and lethargic. Spreads through frass and body fluids. No treatment. Destroy infected larvae and disinfect trays. See: [Sericulture (Silkworm Rearing)](../animals/sericulture.md)
