# Excavation collapse

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Trenches deeper than 1.2 m must be shored (timber or hydraulic shoring) or sloped (1:1 slope for most soils) before workers enter. Unshored trenches collapse without warning. Soil weighs 1600-2000 kg/m³. A cubic meter of soil on a buried worker is not survivable.
