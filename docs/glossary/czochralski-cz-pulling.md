# Czochralski (CZ) pulling

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

The dominant method, producing >90% of all single crystal silicon. Crystal is pulled from a melt in a fused silica crucible. See detailed CZ puller construction in [CZ Pulling](../silicon/cz-pulling.md) and the process description below. Key limitation: oxygen incorporation from the dissolving quartz crucible (10-20 ppma O). This oxygen is manageable for most devices but limits certain high-power and radiation-hard applications.
