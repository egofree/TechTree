# RF power delivery

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

13.56 MHz is the standard industrial, scientific, and medical (ISM) frequency for plasma generation. An RF generator (300-3000 W for single-wafer etch tools) delivers power through an impedance matching network (automatically tuned to maintain <5% reflected power) to the cathode (wafer chuck). The matching network contains a variable capacitor and inductor that transform the plasma impedance to the 50 Ω output impedance of the RF generator.
