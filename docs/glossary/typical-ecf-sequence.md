# Typical ECF sequence

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

(D₀EopD₁EPD₂): (1) **D₀** — ClO₂ at pH 2.5-3.5, 50-70°C, 30-60 min. Oxidizes lignin (electron transfer — ClO₂ reduced to ClO₂⁻) but does not chlorinate (unlike Cl₂, which forms chlorinated organics including dioxins). (2) **Eop** — NaOH extraction with oxygen and hydrogen peroxide reinforcement at 70-80°C. Dissolves oxidized lignin fragments. O₂ and H₂O₂ provide additional delignification without chlorine.
