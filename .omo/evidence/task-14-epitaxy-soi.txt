Task 14: Add epitaxy and SOI process nodes to silicon domain
Date: 2026-05-24

## Changes Made

### nodes.json — 2 new process nodes added

1. silicon.wafering.epitaxy
   - level: process (2 dots)
   - parent: silicon.wafering
   - description: CVD epitaxial growth of single-crystal silicon layers on polished wafer substrates using trichlorosilane or silane at 900-1200°C. Enables independently doped device layers (p/p+ or n/n+ configurations) with autodoping control via reduced pressure and buffer layers.
   - outputs: epitaxial_wafers, epitaxial_layers
   - tags: material=[silicon, gases], capability=[], era=semiconductor

2. silicon.wafering.soi
   - level: process (2 dots)
   - parent: silicon.wafering
   - description: Silicon-on-insulator wafer fabrication via SIMOX (oxygen implantation), Bonded SOI (wafer bonding and back-thinning), and Smart Cut (hydrogen-induced splitting) processes. Produces wafers with a buried SiO₂ layer for reduced parasitic capacitance and radiation-hard applications.
   - outputs: soi_wafers, buried_oxide_layers
   - tags: material=[silicon], capability=[], era=semiconductor

## Learnings
- Process nodes under silicon.wafering use era=semiconductor (not electronic like the parent capability)
- Epitaxy uses both silicon and gases material tags (trichlorosilane/silane precursor gases + dopant gases)
- SOI only needs silicon tag (the processes transform silicon wafers, even though they use implantation/bonding)
- The dopant-etch-gases edge for epitaxy captures the PH₃/B₂H₆ dopant gas and HCl etch gas dependency
