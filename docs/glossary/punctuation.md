# Punctuation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Period (end of sentence), comma (pause/clause boundary), colon (introduction or ratio), semicolon (related clauses), parentheses (aside), quotation marks. Standardize all of these — ambiguous punctuation causes misreading of technical instructions. Decimal point vs. comma: choose one convention and enforce it (the cost of switching is trivial compared to the cost of confusion).
