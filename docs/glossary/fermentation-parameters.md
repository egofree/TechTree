# Fermentation parameters

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Saccharomyces cerevisiae ferments glucose at 25-35°C (optimum 30-32°C), pH 4.0-5.0 (maintained with CaCO₃ or Ca(OH)₂ addition). Sugar concentration 15-25% w/v in the mash. Yeast pitching rate 10-30 million cells/mL. Fermentation duration 48-72 hours to reach terminal gravity (8-12% ABV in batch, up to 15-18% in very high gravity fermentations with adapted yeast strains).
