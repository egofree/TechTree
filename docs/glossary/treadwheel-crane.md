# Treadwheel crane

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations, transport

Human-powered. Large wooden wheel (3-4 m diameter) with internal treads. One or two workers walk inside the wheel, turning a windlass that hoists via rope and pulley. Lift capacity 2-5 tonnes. Hoisting speed 0.3-0.5 m/s. Used in harbors and construction from Roman times through the 18th century.
