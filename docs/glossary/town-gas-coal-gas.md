# Town gas (coal gas)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Produced by destructive distillation of coal in coke ovens or retorts (heating coal in absence of air at 900-1100°C). Gas composition: ~50% H₂, ~30% CH₄, ~10% CO, with minor CO₂, C₂H₄, N₂. Calorific value ~18 MJ/m³. Byproducts: coke (solid), coal tar (source of chemicals: benzene, toluene, naphthalene, creosote), ammonia liquor.
