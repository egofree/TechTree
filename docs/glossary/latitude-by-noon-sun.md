# Latitude by noon sun

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Measure sun's altitude at local noon (highest point of the sun's daily arc). Declination of sun (angle above/below celestial equator) varies from +23.45° (summer solstice) to -23.45° (winter solstice). Latitude = 90° - observed altitude + declination. Requires an almanac of daily solar declination values. Accuracy with a sextant: ±15 arcminutes (±15 nautical miles).
