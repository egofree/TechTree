# Kappa number

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Measures residual lignin content in unbleached pulp. Determined by oxidizing a pulp sample with KMnO₄ under standardized conditions (ISO 302). Softwood kraft: kappa 25-35 for bleachable grades. Hardwood kraft: kappa 15-25. Higher kappa means more lignin remaining, requiring more bleaching chemicals but higher yield. Lower kappa means more delignification in the digester (lower yield) but less bleaching chemical needed.
