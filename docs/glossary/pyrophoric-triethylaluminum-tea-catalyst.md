# Pyrophoric triethylaluminum (TEA) catalyst

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

The Ziegler-Natta catalyst system (TiCl₄/AlEt₃) for HDPE uses triethylaluminum as a co-catalyst. TEA ignites spontaneously on contact with air — even trace moisture causes violent ignition. Handle only under inert atmosphere (nitrogen or argon blanket) in sealed, purged systems. Never expose to air or water.
