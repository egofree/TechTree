# Hydrochloric acid pickling

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

(modern, dominant): - 15-20% HCl at 35-40°C. Faster than sulfuric (2-10 minutes). Less base metal attack, less hydrogen embrittlement, better surface finish. Scale dissolves: Fe₂O₃ + 6HCl → 2FeCl₃ + 3H₂O. - Spent acid recovery: spray roaster produces Fe₂O₃ (sellable pigment) and HCl gas (absorbed in water → regenerated acid). Closed-loop acid recovery is standard in modern steel plants.
