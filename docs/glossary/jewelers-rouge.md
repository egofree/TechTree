# Jeweler's rouge

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

Jeweler's rouge (iron oxide, Fe₂O₃) — Fine polishing compound. Heat steel wool or iron filings in open air until red-hot. Grind resulting oxide to fine powder. Produces mirror-like finish on metals. See: [Bearings, Abrasives & Cutting Tools](../machine-tools/bearings-abrasives.md)
