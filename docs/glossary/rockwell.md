# Rockwell

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Minor load (10 kgf) seats the indenter, then major load (60, 100, or 150 kgf depending on scale) is applied. Hardness read directly from the dial or display — measures depth of penetration, not area. Fast, no optical measurement needed. Common scales: HRC (150 kgf, diamond cone, for hardened steel, range 20-70), HRB (100 kgf, 1/16" ball, for soft steel, range 60-100), HRA (60 kgf, diamond cone, for carbides and thin hard cases).
