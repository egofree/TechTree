# Ultrasonic testing (UT)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools, metals

A piezoelectric transducer (1-10 MHz probe, typically 2-5 MHz for steel welds) sends high-frequency sound waves into the weld. Sound reflects from internal boundaries (cracks, lack of fusion, porosity) and returns to the transducer. The time-of-flight and amplitude of reflected signals reveal flaw depth, size, and orientation.
