# Reeling equipment

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

A hand-turned reel consisting of a wheel 0.5-1.0 m in diameter, a traversing guide that spreads the thread evenly across the reel width, and a cocoon basin (pot of hot water where cocoons float as their filaments are drawn off). Reeling speed: 100-200 m/min. One skilled worker reels 500-800 g of raw silk per day, processing roughly 2,000-3,000 cocoons.
