# Virtual leaks

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Not all leaks are holes in the chamber wall. A virtual leak is a trapped volume of gas inside the vacuum system that slowly releases into the chamber over time. Common sources: screw threads with trapped air pockets, blind holes that are not vented, gaps between stacked components, and cracks in welds that communicate with the interior through a long, narrow path.
