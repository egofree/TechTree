# Self-potential (SP) method

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Sulfide ore bodies generate natural electrical potentials of 100-1000 mV due to electrochemical reactions between the oxidized upper portion and reduced lower portion of the body. Measure voltage between a fixed reference electrode and a roving electrode along survey lines. Negative SP anomalies centered over the ore body are diagnostic.
