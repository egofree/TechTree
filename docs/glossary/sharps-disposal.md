# Sharps disposal

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Used needles, scalpel blades, and broken glass must be placed in rigid, puncture-resistant containers (polypropylene, marked with biohazard symbol) before disposal. Never recap needles (the most common cause of needlestick injuries). Never overfill sharps containers past the fill line. Full containers are sealed and incinerated or autoclaved before disposal.
