# Critical diameter

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Minimum diameter of explosive charge that sustains stable detonation. Below this diameter, the shockwave loses energy to the charge surface faster than the chemical reaction generates it — detonation fails. ANFO: 50-100 mm (large boreholes). TNT: 5-10 mm. Lead azide: <1 mm (used in blasting caps).
