# Roundness measurement

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement

(out-of-roundness): - Rotate the part on a precision spindle (radial error ≤0.1 μm) while a displacement transducer (LVDT or capacitance probe) contacts the surface. Record the radial deviation at hundreds of points around one complete revolution. Plot the polar profile — deviations from a perfect circle appear as lobing or irregularity.
