# Rod mill

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Similar to ball mill but uses long steel rods instead of balls. Produces less slime (over-ground fines) than ball mills. Preferred for coarse grinding (feed 5-20 mm, product 0.5-5 mm). Rods cascade rather than cataract, giving more uniform grinding with less over-grinding of already-fine particles.
