# Recovered fats

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Restaurant fryer oil, waste cooking grease, and animal processing waste are all viable soap feedstocks after filtering and water removal. Waste grease requires pre-treatment: heat to 80-90°C to melt, filter through 50-100 µm screen, settle to remove water and food particles. Free fatty acid (FFA) content determines lye quantity — high-FFA waste oils require more lye (each gram of FFA consumes NaOH: RCOOH + NaOH → RCOONa + H₂O).
