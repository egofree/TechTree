# Grinding to diameter

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Grind the cylindrical body to the exact target diameter (e.g., 150.00 ±0.25 mm) on a centerless grinding machine. Diamond grinding wheel removes 0.5-2 mm from the surface. This also removes the outer layer of the crystal, which has higher impurity concentration from crucible contact and surface defects.
