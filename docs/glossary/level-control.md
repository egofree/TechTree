# Level control

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Liquid levels must be maintained in the reboiler (to prevent dry firing or flooding the bottom trays) and the reflux drum (to provide adequate residence time for phase separation and pump suction). Level controllers adjust bottom product draw rate (reboiler level) and distillate draw rate (reflux drum level). Cascade control: level controller output becomes the setpoint for the flow controller on the draw valve.
