# Thermostat

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: computing

Bimetallic strip (brass + steel laminated strip — brass expands more than steel when heated, causing strip to bend). At set temperature, bent strip opens electrical contacts → heater off. As temperature drops, strip straightens → contacts close → heater on. Hysteresis: small gap between on and off temperatures (1-3°C). For furnaces, ovens, incubators.
