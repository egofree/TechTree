# Surface passivation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

The SiNₓ anti-reflection coating deposited by PECVD also provides surface passivation. Hydrogen atoms in the SiNₓ film diffuse to the silicon surface during the firing step, terminating dangling bonds that would otherwise act as recombination centers. This passivation is critical for achieving >16% efficiency.
