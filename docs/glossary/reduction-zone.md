# Reduction zone

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, metals

Reduction zone (below tuyeres) — 800-1000°C. Hot char reacts with CO₂ and H₂O to produce CO and H₂. This is where the useful fuel gas is generated. See: [Wood Gasification](../chemistry/wood-gasification.md) | [Blast Furnace and Cast Iron](../metals/blast-furnace.md)
