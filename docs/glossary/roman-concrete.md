# Roman concrete

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Lime + volcanic ash (pozzolana from Pozzuoli, Italy) + aggregate. Pumice for lightweight. Structures still standing after 2000 years (Pantheon dome, aqueducts). Seawater exposure actually strengthens it (alkali-silica reaction forms additional C-A-S-H gel).
