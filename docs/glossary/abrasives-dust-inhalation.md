# Abrasives dust inhalation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

Grinding and polishing generates fine dust from both the abrasive and the workpiece. Silica dust from sandstone wheels causes silicosis. Aluminum oxide and silicon carbide dust are respiratory irritants. Wear respirators during dry grinding. Wet grinding preferred — eliminates airborne dust and improves surface finish.
