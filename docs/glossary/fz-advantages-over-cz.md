# FZ advantages over CZ

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: silicon

Benefits of float-zone (FZ) crystal growth over Czochralski: no crucible contact means no oxygen contamination (carbon and oxygen both < 10¹⁵ cm⁻³), enabling higher resistivity and lower defect density. FZ silicon is preferred for power devices and radiation detectors but is limited to smaller diameters.
