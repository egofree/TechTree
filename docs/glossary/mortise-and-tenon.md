# Mortise-and-tenon

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations, plants

Cut rectangular hole (mortise) in one piece with chisel and auger; shape protruding tenon on mating piece. Peg through both for permanent joint. Foundation of timber framing. A tight fit should require light mallet taps to seat the tenon. Tenon width is roughly one-third of the timber thickness.
