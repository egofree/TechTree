# 10 μm → 3 μm (1970s)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: vlsi-scaling

The first shrink. Contact printing gives way to 1:1 projection — a lens images the mask onto the wafer without physical contact, eliminating mask damage. Overlay tolerance relaxes from ±1 μm to ±0.5 μm with basic mechanical alignment stages. Wet etching still adequate. Yield challenge: defect density dominates — even 1 defect/cm² kills most die. Cleanroom discipline (Class 10,000) becomes essential. Equipment: mercury arc lamp sources (g-line 436 nm), basic optical projection lenses.
