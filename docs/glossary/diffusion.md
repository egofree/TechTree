# Diffusion

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Expose wafer to dopant source at high temperature - n-type: phosphorus (POCl₃ gas, or solid P₂O₅) - p-type: boron (BBr₃ gas, or solid B₂O₃) - Dopant atoms diffuse into silicon from surface - **Ion implantation** (later, more precise): Accelerate dopant ions into wafer - Requires: high-voltage accelerator, vacuum, mass separator (magnets), beam scanning
