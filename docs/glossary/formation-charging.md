# Formation charging

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Cured plates are assembled into cells with dilute electrolyte (SG 1.05-1.10) and connected to a DC power supply. The formation process is a controlled first charge that electrochemically converts the paste: positive plates oxidize to PbO₂ (chocolate brown), negative plates reduce to spongy lead (gray).
