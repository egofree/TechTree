# Form the Bourdon tube

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement

Start with oval-cross-section tube (flatten round tube between rollers, or draw through oval die). Bend into C-shape (arc ~270°) around a hardwood or steel form. Radius depends on pressure range: 25-40 mm for low pressure (0-10 bar), 15-25 mm for high pressure (0-100+ bar). The oval cross-section is the active element — internal pressure acts on the flat sides, generating a net unbending force that straightens the tube proportionally to applied pressure.
