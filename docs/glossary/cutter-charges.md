# Cutter charges

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Linear shaped charges with a V-shaped liner, producing a planar cutting jet. Used for demolition of steel structures (bridges, offshore platforms, buildings). Cutting thickness: up to 100 mm steel plate with a single charge. Detonation velocity of the explosive filling: >7,000 m/s for effective jet formation. The cutting jet severs structural steel members in milliseconds, enabling controlled collapse of the structure.
