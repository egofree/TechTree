# Steel shell construction

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

The outer gasifier shell is mild steel plate (4-8 mm thick), welded cylindrical construction with bolted top and bottom caps. The shell must be airtight (all seams welded or gasketed). Access doors for fuel loading, ash removal, and grate maintenance are bolted with high-temperature gaskets (ceramic fiber or compressed mineral wool).
