# Söderberg self-baking electrodes

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

A steel casing (1-2 mm thick) is continuously filled with paste of calcined anthracite, coal tar pitch, and petroleum coke. As the electrode descends through the furnace roof, heat bakes the paste into solid carbon in place. Diameter: 600-1500 mm. Electrode consumption: 3-5 kg/MWh of electrical input for ferroalloy production. This avoids the expensive separate graphitization step required for prebaked electrodes.
