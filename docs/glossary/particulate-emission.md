# Particulate emission

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Uncontrolled coal combustion emits 10-50 kg of fly ash per tonne of coal burned. Cyclone collectors reduce this to 2-10 kg/t, bag filters to <0.05 kg/t, and electrostatic precipitators to <0.02 kg/t. Without particulate control, surrounding areas suffer severe air quality degradation.
