# Rotary vane pump

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

(workhorse — achieves medium vacuum): - **Principle**: Eccentric rotor in cylindrical stator. Spring-loaded vanes slide in rotor slots, maintaining contact with stator wall. Gas enters inlet port, trapped between vanes and stator, compressed, expelled through exhaust valve. - **Construction**: Cast iron stator (bored to ~0.01 mm tolerance on Machine Tools lathe/boring machine).
