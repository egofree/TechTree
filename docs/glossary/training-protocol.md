# Training protocol

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

puppies are placed with the flock at 8-12 weeks of age. They grow up identifying with the sheep as their social group. The bonding period is critical: the puppy must spend 24 hours a day with the livestock, not in the house or with humans. Supervision during months 4-12 corrects play-chasing and establishes boundaries. Full effectiveness is reached at 18-24 months.
