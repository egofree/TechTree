# Electronic grade silicon (9-11N)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: silicon

Ultra-high purity silicon (99.9999999% to 99.999999999%) required for semiconductor device manufacturing, containing less than one part per billion of most impurities. Achieved through chemical purification of metallurgical-grade silicon via Siemens process or fluidized bed reactor decomposition of silane.
