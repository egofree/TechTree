# Fining

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

1500-1600°C. Raise temperature to reduce glass viscosity, allowing bubbles to rise and escape. Add fining agents (arsenic trioxide or antimony trioxide, 0.1-0.5%) to generate large bubbles that sweep up small ones. Continue until glass is clear and bubble-free (examine with backlight).
