# Product compression and storage

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Gaseous O₂ and N₂ are compressed by centrifugal or reciprocating compressors to pipeline pressure (15-40 bar) or cylinder filling pressure (150-200 bar). Liquid products are stored in vacuum-insulated tanks: LOX tank (100-5000 tonnes capacity), LIN tank (50-2000 tonnes), LAR tank (10-100 tonnes). Ambient vaporizers (finned-tube heat exchangers, no energy input required) convert liquid to gas on demand.
