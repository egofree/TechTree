# Pigment particle size

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

TiO₂ white pigment: 0.2-0.3 μm optimum diameter for visible light scattering (Mie scattering theory). Iron oxide red (Fe₂O₃): 0.1-1.0 μm. Carbon black: primary particle size 10-100 nm, but aggregates to 100-500 nm structures with surface area 15-25 m²/g (for furnace black grades). Smaller carbon black particles provide deeper black but are harder to disperse.
