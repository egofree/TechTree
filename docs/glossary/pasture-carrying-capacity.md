# Pasture carrying capacity

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Fertile lowland pasture supports 2-3 cattle or 10-15 sheep per hectare during the growing season. Hill pasture supports half that. Rotational grazing (move animals every 3-5 days to fresh paddock) increases utilization 20-30% compared to continuous grazing and reduces parasite loads.
