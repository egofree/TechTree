# Firing (sintering)

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Tunnel kiln at 1300-1800°C (temperature depends on composition). 12-48 hours. Sintering bonds particles by partial melting (liquid-phase sintering) or solid-state diffusion. Fireclay: 1300-1400°C. High-alumina: 1500-1600°C. Magnesite: 1700-1800°C. Shrinkage: 2-8% (controlled by raw material selection and pressing density).
