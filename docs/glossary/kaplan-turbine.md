# Kaplan turbine

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

(axial-flow reaction turbine — low head, high flow): - **Principle**: Propeller-type runner with adjustable blade pitch (like an outboard motor running in reverse). Water flows axially through the runner — no radial component. Adjustable blades allow efficient operation over a wide flow range. - **Head range**: 1-15 m. Designed for large rivers with low dams. - **Efficiency**: 80-92% at design flow, maintains good efficiency over a wide flow range due to adjustable blades. - **Construction**:...
