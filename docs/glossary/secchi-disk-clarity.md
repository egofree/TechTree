# Secchi disk clarity

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Lower a white disk 20 cm in diameter into the water on a calibrated cord. The depth at which it disappears from view is the Secchi depth. Readings below 1.5 m indicate turbidity too high for drinking without treatment. Clear water bodies register 4-10 m Secchi depth. This simple test requires only a white-painted disk and a marked rope.
