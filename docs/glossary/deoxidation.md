# Deoxidation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Dissolved oxygen in liquid steel (~200-600 ppm after BOF, ~50-200 ppm after EAF) must be reduced to <10 ppm (ideally <5 ppm) to prevent blowholes and inclusion formation during solidification. Deoxidizers: aluminum (most common, 0.3-1.0 kg/t, forms Al₂O₃ clusters), silicon (as FeSi, 1-3 kg/t), manganese (as FeMn, 5-15 kg/t).
