# Ash glaze

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

Ash glaze (simplest, earliest — wood ash + clay) — - **Composition**: Mix 50-70% wood ash (sieved) with 30-50% clay (same clay body or kaolin). Ash provides flux (K₂O, CaO, MgO from dissolved minerals). Add water to creamy consistency. - **Application**: Dip pot or brush on. Fire to 1100-1250°C. Ash glazes produce earthy greens, browns, and glossy surfaces depending on ash source. - **Variation**: Different tree species produce different glazes.
