# Wind Power

> **Node ID**: energy.wind
> **Domain**: Energy
> **Timeline**: Years 5-15
> **Outputs**: wind_power

**Overshot wheel (higher efficiency)**:
- Requires head (vertical drop) of 2-5+ meters. Water flows over top of wheel, filling buckets. Gravity drives wheel. 2-3x more efficient than undershot.
- Construction similar but with buckets instead of flat paddles. Needs sluice/flume to deliver water to top.
