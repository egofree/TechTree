# Baiting and attracting swarms

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: animals

Techniques for luring honey bee swarms to bait hives using lemongrass oil (mimics Nasanov pheromone), drawn comb, or propolis-scented wood. Effective swarm traps are placed 3-5 m high, shaded, and sized ~40 liters (volume of a natural cavity).
