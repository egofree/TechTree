# Vehicle integration

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Gasifier mounted on a trailer behind the car or on a platform over the hood/bumper. Gas passes through a cyclone separator (removes ash and soot), a cooling tube (finned pipe, reduces gas from 400°C to ~30°C — cooler gas is denser, improving volumetric efficiency), and a cloth or sawdust filter (final particulate removal).
