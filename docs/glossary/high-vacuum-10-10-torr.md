# High vacuum (10⁻³-10⁻⁸ Torr)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Penning ionization gauge (cold cathode ionizes gas, ion current proportional to pressure). Bayard-Alpert ion gauge (hot cathode version, more accurate). Calibration against McLeod gauge (absolute pressure measurement using compressed gas column — primary standard).
