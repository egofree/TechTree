# Zone refining

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Zone refining (for highest purity physical route) — Process**: Pass molten zone (narrow band of liquid) along solid silicon rod, repeatedly. Each pass sweeps impurities toward one end. 10-20 passes achieve ~8-9N purity. - **Equipment**: RF induction coil or focused IR lamp creates moving molten zone. Rod rotates slowly. Under inert atmosphere (Ar) or vacuum. Very slow — ~1-5 cm/hour travel speed. - **Limitation**: Still cannot remove boron effectively (k ≈ 0.8). Combine with another boron removal step (...
