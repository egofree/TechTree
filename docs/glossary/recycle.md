# Recycle

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Separate materials (metals, glass, paper, certain plastics) for reprocessing. Metals are the highest-value recyclable (aluminum recycling uses 5% of the energy of primary production). Glass is indefinitely recyclable (crush, melt, reform). Paper fiber degrades after 5-7 recycling cycles (shorter fibers = weaker paper).
