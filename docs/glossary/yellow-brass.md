# Yellow brass

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Yellow brass (60% Cu / 40% Zn) — Melting range 880-920°C. Tensile strength ~340 MPa (as-cast). Good castability, moderate strength, golden color. Used for fittings, valves, hardware, decorative castings. See: [Copper & Bronze Production](../metals/copper-bronze.md)
