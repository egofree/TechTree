# Pattern-following milling

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: computing

Pattern-following milling (tracing) — Tracer mill**: Stylus follows a master template (wood, plaster, or metal pattern). Hydraulic or electrical linkage drives milling cutter to duplicate pattern in workpiece. Enables production of complex 3D shapes (dies, molds, turbine blades) without direct CNC capability. Accuracy: ±0.05-0.1 mm depending on tracer mechanism. See: [Electromechanical Computing](../computing/electromechanical.md)
