# Journeyman-level overall competency

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Journeyman-level overall competency: 10,000 hours (approximately 5 years full-time) across multiple disciplines. This figure, sometimes called the "10,000-hour rule," reflects the time required to develop intuitive pattern recognition for diagnosing problems, adapting to unexpected situations, and producing consistent quality under varying conditions.
