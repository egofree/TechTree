# Tincture preparation

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Combine plant material with 45-70% ethanol at 1:5 or 1:10 ratio (plant weight:solvent volume). Seal in glass container. Shake daily for 14-28 days. Decant and press residue. Filter through paper or cloth. The ethanol preserves the preparation for years. Standardize by comparing color, odor, and specific chemical tests to a reference batch.
