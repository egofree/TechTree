# Stage 3: Full-scale Haber-Bosch with SMR

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

(Years 30-50) - Requires: natural gas (or coal gasification), steam reforming furnace, shift converters, CO₂ removal, air separation for nitrogen, centrifugal synthesis gas compressors. - Plant scale: 500-3,000 tonnes NH₃/day. Energy consumption: 28-35 GJ per tonne NH₃ (modern plants). This is the mature industry. - Enables massive fertilizer production → agricultural expansion → population growth support.
