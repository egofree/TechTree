# Francis turbine

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

(reaction turbine — medium head, medium flow): - **Principle**: Water enters the runner radially from a spiral casing (volute), passes through adjustable guide vanes (wicket gates) that direct flow at the optimal angle, then flows through the runner blades and exits axially via the draft tube. Both pressure and velocity drop across the runner — a true reaction machine. - **Head range**: 3-300 m.
