# Tears and splits

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Use Japanese tissue (kozo fiber, long-fibered and strong) applied with wheat starch paste (cooked 1:5 starch:water, cooled to thin gel). The tissue bridges the tear with minimal bulk — Japanese tissue can be split to match the thickness of the damaged paper. Feather the edges of the tissue patch to avoid a visible ridge.
