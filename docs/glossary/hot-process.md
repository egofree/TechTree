# Hot process

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

(faster cure, used with KOH for soft soap): - Heat fat to 70-80°C. Add KOH solution (from [Alkalis](alkalis.md)) gradually with constant stirring. Maintain temperature for 2-4 hours with periodic stirring until mixture reaches "trace" and then thickens further into a translucent paste (KOH soaps remain paste-like).
