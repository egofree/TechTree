# Modifier

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Modifier (lime, cyanide, zinc sulfate) — Adjusts pH and suppresses unwanted minerals. Lime raises pH to 9-11 (depresses pyrite). Cyanide depresses sphalerite (zinc sulfide) in copper-zinc ores. See: [Ore Processing](../mining/processing.md)
