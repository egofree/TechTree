# Electroless nickel (Ni-P alloy)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Electroless nickel-phosphorus (Ni-P) alloy is a coating deposited by autocatalytic chemical reduction without an external electrical current. It is the most commercially important electroless plating process, producing a uniform, hard, corrosion-resistant layer of nickel alloyed with 3-12% phosphorus on complex geometries.
