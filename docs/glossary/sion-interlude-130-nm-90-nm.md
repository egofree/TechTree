# SiON interlude (130 nm → 90 nm)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: vlsi-scaling

Nitrogen-doped SiO₂ (silicon oxynitride) slightly raises dielectric constant (κ ≈ 5.0 vs 3.9 for SiO₂), allowing a physically thicker (lower leakage) film with equivalent electrical oxide thickness. A stopgap measure.
