# Decoction

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health, plants

Boil coarse plant material (roots, bark, seeds, woody stems) in water for 15-30 minutes. Strain. Heat breaks down tough cell walls that infusion cannot penetrate. Suitable for Agrimonia pilosa roots, Artemisia bark preparations. Use within 24 hours or preserve with ethanol (≥15% by volume).
