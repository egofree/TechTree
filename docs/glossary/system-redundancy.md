# System redundancy

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Never depend on a single water source or single storage structure. A community water plan should include at least two independent sources (well + rainwater catchment, or spring + river with treatment) and distribute storage across multiple cisterns. If one cistern cracks or one source becomes contaminated, the remaining infrastructure covers essential needs at reduced consumption.
