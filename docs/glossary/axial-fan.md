# Axial fan

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

(compact, high volume): - Propeller-like blades spinning inside a cylindrical housing. Air flows parallel to the shaft axis. Blade pitch adjustable (some designs allow pitch change while running, enabling airflow adjustment without changing motor speed). - Performance range: 10-200 m³/s at 200-2000 Pa.
