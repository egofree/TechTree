# Log hive

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Log hive (simplest) — Hollow section of tree trunk 60-100 cm long, 30-50 cm diameter. Close both ends with fitted wooden discs secured with wedges or clay. Bore a single entrance hole 2-3 cm diameter near one end. Harvest by removing one end disc and cutting out comb — destructive to the colony. Requires no tools beyond axe, auger, and knife. Suitable for capturing wild swarms in forested areas. Yield: 5-10 kg honey per year. See: [Apiculture (Beekeeping)](../animals/beekeeping.md)
