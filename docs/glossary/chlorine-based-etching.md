# Chlorine-based etching

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Cl₂, BCl₃, HCl used for aluminum metallization etching. Chlorine radicals etch aluminum: 2Al + 6Cl· → 2AlCl₃ (volatile above 180°C). BCl₃ serves dual purpose: Cl source and native oxide (Al₂O₃) removal (BCl₃ + Al₂O₃ → AlCl₃ + B₂O₃). Aluminum etch requires heating the wafer chuck to 40-70°C to volatilize AlCl₃. Corrosion concern: residual Cl on etched surfaces attacks aluminum — post-etch passivation with CHF₃ or H₂O rinse required.
