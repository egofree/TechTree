# Room temperature to 100°C

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

Room temperature to 100°C (2 hours) — Very slow. All physically adsorbed water must evaporate before the temperature rises further. If the kiln is loaded with thick-walled pieces, extend this phase to 3 hours. See: [Pottery & Clay Products](../ceramics/pottery.md)
