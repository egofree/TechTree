# Shelf life of mixed solution

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

250-350 g/L shellac in ethanol begins esterifying within weeks. The esterification reaction (shellac's hydroxyl and carboxyl groups react with ethanol to form ethyl esters) progressively makes the shellac insoluble. Signs of degraded solution: cloudy appearance, slow drying, gummy or rubbery film. Discard solution older than 3-6 months. Mix only what will be used within 2-3 months.
