# C/N ratio

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: critical | **Domains**: agriculture

Carbon-to-nitrogen ratio by weight — the critical parameter in composting. Target 25-30:1. Too much carbon (>40:1) = slow decomposition. Too much nitrogen (<15:1) = ammonia loss and odors. Mix 'browns' (straw 80-120:1, sawdust 200-500:1) with 'greens' (manure 10-25:1, grass clippings 15-20:1).
