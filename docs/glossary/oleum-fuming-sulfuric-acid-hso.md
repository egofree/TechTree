# Oleum (fuming sulfuric acid, H₂S₂O₇)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: chemistry

Solution of sulfur trioxide (SO₃) in concentrated sulfuric acid (H₂SO₄), containing free SO₃ that releases toxic fumes. Extremely corrosive; used in sulfonation reactions and as a more concentrated form of sulfuric acid for industrial processes.
