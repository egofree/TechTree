# Wood pulp paper

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Debark logs, chip to 15-25 mm pieces, then either grind mechanically (groundwood) or digest chemically (kraft). 1 m³ of softwood (spruce, pine) yields 150-200 kg of dry pulp. This pulp produces 40-50 reams of 80 gsm A4 paper (500 sheets/ream = 20,000-25,000 sheets). Wood pulp paper is 10-20× cheaper than rag paper per sheet, enabling mass-circulation printing.
