# Absorber

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Ammonia vapor from evaporator is absorbed by weak solution returning from generator. Absorption is exothermic — requires cooling water (20-30°C). The strong solution is pumped back to generator by a small liquid pump (the ONLY moving part in the refrigerant circuit, requiring ~0.5-2 kW for a 100 kW cooling capacity unit).
