# Causticizing

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Green liquor is reacted with slaked lime (Ca(OH)₂) in a series of agitated tanks at 80-90°C. Na₂CO₃ + Ca(OH)₂ → 2NaOH + CaCO₃. The resulting CaCO₃ (lime mud) is filtered, washed, and sent to the lime kiln for reburning (CaCO₃ → CaO + CO₂ at 900-1000°C). The regenerated white liquor (NaOH + Na₂S) returns to the digester, completing the chemical cycle. Causticizing efficiency: 80-85% per pass.
