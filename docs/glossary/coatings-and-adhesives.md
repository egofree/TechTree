# Coatings and adhesives

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Two-component PU coatings (polyol + isocyanate) cure to tough, chemical-resistant films with excellent weatherability and UV resistance (aliphatic isocyanates like HDI and IPDI provide superior UV stability vs. aromatic MDI/TDI which yellow on UV exposure).
