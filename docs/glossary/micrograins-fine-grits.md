# Micrograins (fine grits)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

FEPA F230 through F1200, graded by sedimentation or laser diffraction rather than sieving. F230 ≈ 53 μm, F320 ≈ 29 μm, F400 ≈ 17 μm, F600 ≈ 9 μm, F800 ≈ 6.5 μm, F1200 ≈ 3 μm. These ultra-fine grades are used for lapping compounds, super-finishing stones, and honing sticks. F600-F1200 produce surface finishes below 0.5 μm Ra.
