# Iron (Fe₂O₃/FeO)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Green or blue-green. Even natural sand impurities (0.1% Fe₂O₃) produce a noticeable green tint in thick sections. Ancient Roman and medieval glass is typically greenish for this reason. Deliberate addition at 1-3% produces dark green (bottle glass). Iron is the cheapest and most common colorant.
