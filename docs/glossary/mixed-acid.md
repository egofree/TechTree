# Mixed acid

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Mixed acid (HF + HNO₃) — The standard pickle for stainless steel. Typical bath: 8-15% HNO₃ + 1-5% HF at 20-50°C. Nitric acid dissolves iron and chromium oxides; hydrofluoric acid dissolves silicon oxides and complex chromium oxides. Immersion: 10-30 minutes. Produces a uniform, white, pickled finish.
