# Wool-cotton (virgin blend)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

50-70% wool, 30-50% cotton. The wool provides warmth, elasticity, and moisture wicking. The cotton adds softness against the skin and improves washability (wool felts less when blended with cotton). Card the two fibers together on a drum carder, making 2-3 passes for uniform distribution. The different staple lengths (wool 5-15 cm, cotton 2-3 cm) mean cotton tends to migrate to the yarn core and wool to the surface during spinning.
