# Smelt formation

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Inorganic sodium salts (Na₂CO₃ from the kraft cycle, plus Na₂SO₄ reduced to Na₂S) melt at the furnace bottom, forming a molten smelt pool at 800-1000°C. The smelt flows continuously through a water-cooled spout into a dissolving tank, where it is dissolved in weak wash liquor to form green liquor (Na₂CO₃ + Na₂S solution).
