# Induration (firing)

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Green pellets are too weak for blast furnace use. They must be fired at high temperature to develop permanent bonding between particles. Three furnace types are used: - **Straight-grate (traveling grate)**: Pellets travel on a moving grate through drying, preheating, firing (1250-1350°C), and cooling zones.
