# Flax and hemp line fiber

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Store in dry, well-ventilated conditions. Line fibers (long, combed) are bundled into stricks (twisted hanks, 200-400 g each) and packed in burlap sacks. Tow (short fibers) is baled. Bast fibers are less vulnerable to insects than wool but will mildew if stored damp.
