# Flattening horn sheets

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Soak horn in boiling water for 15-30 minutes until pliable. Remove, split along its natural seam with a knife. Press flat between two smooth boards with weights or a clamp. Let cool and dry under pressure. The resulting horn sheet is translucent, tough, and can be cut, drilled, carved, and polished.
