# Charge scrap

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Charge scrap (20-30% of total charge, 50-100 tonnes). Scrap acts as coolant — the exothermic reactions generate more heat than needed, and scrap absorbs the excess. Larger scrap pieces are placed at the bottom; light scrap on top. See: [Modern Steelmaking](../metals/steelmaking.md)
