# Mohair

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: critical | **Domains**: animals

Long, lustrous, curly fiber produced by Angora goats (not sheep). Fiber diameter 23-38 microns, staple length 15-25 cm, growing ~2.5 cm/month. Shear twice per year. Yield: 4-6 kg per shearing for mature animals. Takes dye beautifully, silk-like sheen.
