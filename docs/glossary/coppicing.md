# Coppicing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

Cut deciduous hardwoods (oak, ash, chestnut, acacia) at ground level on a 7-15 year cycle. Stumps regrow multiple straight poles from the stool. Sustainable indefinitely — medieval coppice stools are documented at 500+ years old. Produces straight poles ideal for wattle, fencing, and tool handles.
