# Gunning repair

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Pneumatically applied repair material extends furnace campaign life without full relining. Dry gunning: pre-damped refractory mix is fed through a gunning machine, conveyed by compressed air through a hose (25-40 mm ID, 10-30 m length), and projected onto the hot furnace surface at 20-40 m/s impact velocity.
