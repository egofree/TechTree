# Simple clay crucible

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

Simple clay crucible (for lower temperatures) — Composition**: Fireclay + 30% grog. Form by coil or press-mold. Fire to 1200°C. - **Adequate for**: Copper (1085°C), bronze (950°C), tin (232°C), lead (327°C). Not suitable for iron (1538°C — softens clay). See: [Pottery & Clay Products](../ceramics/pottery.md)
