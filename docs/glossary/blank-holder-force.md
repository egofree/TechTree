# Blank holder force

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

Applied at 10-30% of the drawing force by a hydraulic cushion, pneumatic cushion, or die spring mechanism. The blank holder prevents wrinkling of the flange as it draws inward through the die opening. Too little force and circumferential compressive stress buckles the flange into wrinkles. Too much force and the material stretches thin and tears at the punch nose. Optimal force is found empirically for each material thickness and geometry combination.
