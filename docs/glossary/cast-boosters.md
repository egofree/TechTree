# Cast boosters

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Pentolite (50% PETN + 50% TNT) or Composition B (60% RDX + 40% TNT) cast into cylindrical charges (25-500 g). Detonation velocity 7,000-7,800 m/s. Required to initiate ANFO and other insensitive explosives. The booster is placed at the bottom of the borehole, with the detonator inserted into a molded cap well.
