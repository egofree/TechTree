# Gas distribution

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

TCS (SiHCl₃) mixed with H₂ enters the bell jar through inlet ports at the base. Gas flows upward around the heated rods. Unreacted gas and HCl byproduct exit through ports at the top. Flow rate: 50-200 g/h TCS per rod, with H₂ carrier at 2-10× stoichiometric excess.
