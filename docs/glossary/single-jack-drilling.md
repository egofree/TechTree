# Single jack drilling

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Single jack drilling (one person, one hammer) — One hand holds and rotates the drill steel, the other swings a 2-3 kg hammer. The drill steel is typically 600-1200 mm long, 19-25 mm diameter, with a forged chisel point at the working end and a slightly widened head (to prevent mushrooming over the holder's hand). - Advance rate: 0.3-0.5 m/hour in granite.
