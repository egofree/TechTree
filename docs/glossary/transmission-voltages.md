# Transmission voltages

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

11 kV for short local distribution (<5 km), 33 kV for regional distribution (5-30 km), 66-110 kV for sub-transmission (20-80 km), 220-400 kV for long-distance bulk transmission (>50 km). Higher voltage requires taller towers, larger insulators, and greater clearance from vegetation and structures, but the reduction in conductor losses is dramatic.
