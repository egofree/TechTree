# Underground utility strikes

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Before excavating, survey for buried utilities (water, gas, electric, telegraph lines). Hand-dig within 1 m of known utility locations. A gas line strike can cause an explosion. An electric cable strike causes electrocution. A water main strike floods the excavation. Utility markings (colored paint on the road surface) indicate horizontal location only, not depth.
