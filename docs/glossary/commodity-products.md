# Commodity products

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Commodity products (production >1 million tonnes/year) — ethanol (fuel and beverage, ~100 million tonnes/year), lactic acid (~2 million tonnes/year, PLA precursor), citric acid (~2 million tonnes/year, food and detergent use), monosodium glutamate (~3 million tonnes/year, flavor enhancer), lysine (~2 million tonnes/year, animal feed additive). These are high-volume, low-margin products where raw material cost and fermentation yield are the primary competitive factors. See: [Fermentation Chemistry](../chemistry/fermentation.md)
