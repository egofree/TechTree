# Heavy metal restrictions

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Lead: banned in consumer paints since the 1970s in most countries (US: 90 ppm limit on dried paint film). Hexavalent chromium (Cr⁶⁺): banned in the EU under REACH for most applications (chromate conversion coatings replaced by trivalent chromium or zirconium/titanium-based alternatives). Cadmium: banned as a pigment in most applications.
