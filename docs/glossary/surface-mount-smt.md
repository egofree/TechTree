# Surface mount (SMT)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: computing

Components soldered directly to surface pads (no through-holes). Smaller components, higher density. Solder paste (solder powder + flux, printed onto pads through a stainless steel stencil). Components placed by pick-and-place machine. Reflow soldering: board passes through a conveyor oven with temperature profile (preheat 150°C, ramp to 250°C peak, cool). The solder paste melts, wets the pads and component leads, and solidifies on cooling.
