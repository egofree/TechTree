# Optical and decorative glass

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

>99% SiO₂, <0.01% Fe₂O₃. Achieved by washing sand in water with agitation, then acid leaching (HCl or H₂SO₄) to dissolve iron compounds, followed by rinsing and drying. Magnetic separation removes iron-bearing mineral grains (magnetite, ilmenite).
