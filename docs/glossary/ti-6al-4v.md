# Ti-6Al-4V

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(Grade 5, "workhorse alloy" — 50% of titanium consumption): 900-950 MPa UTS, 830-880 MPa yield, 10-14% elongation, 4.43 g/cm³. α+β alloy — aluminum stabilizes α phase, vanadium stabilizes β phase. Used in annealed or solution-treated-and-aged condition.
