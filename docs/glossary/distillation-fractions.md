# Distillation fractions

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: energy

The distinct product streams obtained from coke oven tar distillation, sorted by boiling range: light oil (up to 170°C), carbolic oil (170–210°C), creosote oil (210–270°C), anthracene oil (270–360°C), and residual pitch. Each fraction contains valuable chemical feedstocks.
