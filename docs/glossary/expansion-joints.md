# Expansion joints

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Refractory linings expand when heated. Fireclay bricks expand 0.5-0.7% (5-7 mm per meter of lining length). Without expansion joints, the lining buckles and collapses. Expansion joints (5-10 mm wide, filled with ceramic fiber blanket or cardboard that burns out) placed every 1-2 meters of linear length. The exact spacing depends on the brick type, service temperature, and furnace geometry.
