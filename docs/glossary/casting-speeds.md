# Casting speeds

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Slabs (200-250 mm thick × 800-2100 mm wide): 1.0-2.5 m/min. Blooms (200-400 mm square): 0.5-1.5 m/min. Billets (100-200 mm square): 2.0-5.0 m/min. Throughput: a single-strand slab caster produces 0.5-1.5 million tonnes/year.
