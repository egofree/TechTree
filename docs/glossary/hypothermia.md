# Hypothermia

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Water draws body heat 25× faster than air at the same temperature. A person in 10°C water loses consciousness in 30-60 minutes. In 0°C water, exhaustion and unconsciousness in 15 minutes. Wear warm layers under waterproof outer garments. Get wet crew members out of the water and into dry clothes immediately.
