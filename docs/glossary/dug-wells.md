# Dug wells

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Hand-dug 1-2 m diameter, 5-20 m deep. Line with stone or brick (dry-laid, mortar at top to prevent surface contamination). Cover with wooden or stone cap. Rope and bucket for drawing. Locate uphill and ≥30 m from latrines, graves, animal pens.
