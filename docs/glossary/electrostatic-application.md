# Electrostatic application

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Powder is fluidized (suspended in an air stream) and conveyed to a spray gun where a high-voltage electrode (60-100 kV) imparts a negative charge to each particle. The charged particles are attracted to the grounded workpiece, wrapping around edges and reaching recessed areas. Transfer efficiency: 95-99% (overspray collected by a cartridge filter system and reprocessed).
