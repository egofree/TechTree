# Crops benefitting from bee pollination

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: animals

Agricultural plants that rely on or significantly benefit from honey bee pollination, including fruits (apples, cherries, berries), vegetables (cucumbers, squash, melons), nuts (almonds depend entirely on bees), and oilseeds (canola, sunflower). Bee pollination increases yields by 20-300% depending on crop.
