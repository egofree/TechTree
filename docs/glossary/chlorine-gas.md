# Chlorine gas

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Toxic (IDLH 10 ppm). Fatal at 1000 ppm. Chlor-alkali plants require gas detection, ventilation, emergency scrubbers (NaOH solution absorbs Cl₂), and full-face supplied-air respirators for emergency response. Chlorine is heavier than air — accumulates in low points.
