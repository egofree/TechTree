# Nitrogen asphyxiation

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

LN₂ evaporates to 700× volume of gas. In confined spaces, N₂ displaces O₂ rapidly. Unconsciousness within seconds at <10% O₂, death within minutes. Continuous O₂ monitoring in all ASU enclosed areas. Ventilate before entry.
