# Catalytic bead (pellistor) sensor

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Two platinum wire coils embedded in ceramic beads, one coated with a catalyst (active bead) and one without (reference). Both heated to ~500°C. Combustible gas oxidizes on the active bead, raising its temperature and resistance. The resistance difference between active and reference beads is proportional to gas concentration. Measures 0-100% LEL (lower explosive limit). Responds to all combustible gases but with varying sensitivity. Poisoned by silicones, lead, and halogenated compounds.
