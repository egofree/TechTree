# Tubing specifications

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Electropolished 316L stainless steel tubing, 1/4 inch OD (6.35 mm) standard for gas distribution. Internal surface electropolished to Ra <0.25 μm to minimize particle generation and gas adsorption. Orbital-welded joints (automated TIG welding in argon purge) ensure smooth, crevice-free internal surfaces with no weld beads.
