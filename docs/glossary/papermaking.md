# Papermaking

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

(hand process): 1. **Beat pulp**: Beat pulp fibers in water with wooden mallets or mechanical beater (Hollander beater — rotating cylindrical knife against bed plate, water-powered). Fiber length 1-3 mm for writing paper, 3-5 mm for heavy paper. Beating fibrillates fiber surfaces → better bonding → stronger paper. 2. **Vat preparation**: Suspend pulp in water (0.5-1% consistency).
