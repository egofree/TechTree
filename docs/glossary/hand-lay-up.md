# Hand lay-up

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

(simplest composite method): - A mold (wood, metal, or existing plug) is coated with mold release wax. A gel coat (pigmented polyester resin, 0.5-0.8 mm thick) is brushed onto the mold surface to create a smooth, colored exterior. - Layers of CSM or woven roving are laid onto the wet gel coat. Polyester resin (with 1-2% methyl ethyl ketone peroxide catalyst) is brushed or rolled into the fiberglass, saturating the fabric and expelling air bubbles.
