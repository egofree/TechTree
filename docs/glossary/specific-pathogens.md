# Specific pathogens

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Absence of *E. coli*, *Salmonella*, *S. aureus*, and *P. aeruginosa* in 1 g (oral products) or 10 g (topical products). Test by enrichment culture in selective broth, then subculture on selective agar (MacConkey for *E. coli*, bismuth sulfite for *Salmonella*).
