# Tonnage calculation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

for V-die bending: P = 650 × S² × L / V, where P = bending force (kN), S = sheet thickness (mm), L = bend length (mm), V = V-die opening width (mm). For example, bending 3 mm mild steel sheet 1000 mm long in a 24 mm V-die requires P = 650 × 9 × 1000 / 24 = 243.75 kN (~25 tonnes). The V-die opening is selected as V = 6-12 × S for mild steel; wider openings reduce tonnage but increase the inside bend radius.
