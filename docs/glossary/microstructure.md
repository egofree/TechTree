# Microstructure

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Dual-phase, with the α/β ratio controlled by thermomechanical processing. Two common microstructural conditions: - **Mill-annealed** (α + dispersed β): Equiaxed α grains in a β matrix. Good ductility, moderate strength. Tensile strength ~900-950 MPa, yield ~830 MPa, elongation 13-16%. - **β-annealed** (lenticular α in prior-β grains): Slow cooling from above the β transus (~995°C) produces a Widmanstätten (basketweave) α+β structure.
