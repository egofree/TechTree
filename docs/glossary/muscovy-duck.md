# Muscovy duck

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: critical | **Domains**: animals

Cairina moschata — a distinct duck species unrelated to mallard-derived breeds. Native to tropical America. Drakes reach 5-7 kg. Distinguished by red caruncles on the face. Incubation period 35 days (vs 28 for mallard-derived). Goes broody readily and makes excellent mother. Quiet — males hiss rather than quack.
