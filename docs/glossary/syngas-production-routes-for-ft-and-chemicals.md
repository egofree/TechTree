# Syngas production routes for FT and chemicals

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Coal gasification (Lurgi fixed-bed or Texaco entrained-flow gasifier): C + H₂O → CO + H₂ at 1200-1500°C, 20-40 bar. Raw syngas cleaned of sulfur (H₂S removed by Rectisol process using chilled methanol at -40°C, reducing sulfur to <0.1 ppm) because sulfur poisons FT catalysts and downstream refining catalysts within hours.
