# Concentration

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics, chemistry

Concentration: 68% azeotrope from simple distillation. Higher concentrations (90-100% "fuming nitric acid") require: (a) distill with concentrated H₂SO₄ as dehydrating agent, or (b) absorb NO₂ gas in water at high pressure and low temperature. See: [Lime Production](../ceramics/lime.md) | [Mineral Acid Production](../chemistry/acids.md)
