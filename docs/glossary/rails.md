# Rails

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals, transport

Initially iron straps on wooden stringers (strap rail — iron bar 2.5-5 cm wide × 1-1.5 cm thick nailed to timber). By the Energy stage: wrought iron or steel T-rails (inverted-T cross section, 15-30 kg/m, rolled in iron rolling mill). Rail length: 5-10 m sections, joined by fish plates (steel plates bolted through rail web holes).
