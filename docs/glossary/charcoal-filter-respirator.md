# Charcoal filter respirator

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Canister filled with activated charcoal granules (wood charcoal heated to 800-900°C in steam → porous, enormous surface area). Charcoal adsorbs organic vapors and many toxic gases. Rubber or leather face mask with exhale valve. Replace canister when breathing becomes difficult or odor detected inside mask.
