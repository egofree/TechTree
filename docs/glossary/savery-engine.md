# Savery engine

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

(~1698, first practical steam-powered pump): Boil water → fill chamber with steam → condense with cold water injection → vacuum → atmospheric pressure forces water up suction pipe. Lift: ~6-9 m suction + ~10-15 m pressure head. Efficiency ~0.5%. Simpler than Newcomen but limited lift and very wasteful.
