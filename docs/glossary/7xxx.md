# 7xxx

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(Al-Zn-Mg-Cu, e.g., 7075): Highest strength aluminum alloy (~570 MPa for 7075-T6). Zinc and magnesium form MgZn₂ precipitates (η phase). Used for aircraft frames, high-strength sporting goods. Susceptible to stress corrosion cracking in the peak-aged condition — overaged tempers (T73) sacrifice ~10-15% strength for full SCC resistance.
