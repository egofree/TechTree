# Scarf joint

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

The strongest joint for bar-to-bar welding. Taper both pieces to a long diagonal (scarf angle ~10-15°, overlap length ~2-3× bar thickness). The long taper distributes the forge weld over a large area. Position the scarfs offset so the thin ends overlap — this prevents a cold shut (unwelded gap) at the edges.
