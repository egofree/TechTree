# Photostability

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Expose to 1.2 million lux hours of visible light and 200 W·h/m² of UV (ICH Q1B). Determines if the product is photosensitive and requires light-protective packaging. The light source is a cool white fluorescent lamp (visible) plus a near-UV fluorescent lamp (320-400 nm). Samples compared to dark controls for color, assay, degradation products, and dissolution.
