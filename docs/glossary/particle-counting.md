# Particle counting

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Laser scattering particle counters measure airborne particles (in cleanroom monitoring) or on wafer surfaces (bare wafer or patterned wafer inspection). Defect density (particles/cm² per process step) directly predicts yield: Yield = (1 - D·A)ⁿ where D = defect density, A = die area, n = process steps.
