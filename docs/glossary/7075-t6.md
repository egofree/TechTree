# 7075-T6

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

High-strength aerospace alloy. 5.6% Zn, 2.5% Mg, 1.6% Cu. UTS 572 MPa — comparable to many steels at 1/3 the density. Difficult to weld (use 7xxx filler or friction stir welding). Used for aircraft frames, gears, high-stress components.
