# Dust collectors

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Dust collectors (secondary defense) — - Dry-type dust collector: dusty air drawn through a fabric filter bag (cotton or synthetic felt, 0.5-2 m² filter area per m³/s of airflow). Filter captures particles > 1 μm. Bags are shaken or pulse-cleaned with compressed air periodically to maintain airflow. - Wet scrubber: dusty air passes through a water spray chamber or venturi scrubber.
