# Specific speed and application

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Kaplan turbines span specific speeds Ns = 300-800, the highest of any reaction turbine. This makes them suitable for the lowest heads and highest flow rates. At Ns = 800, the runner resembles a ship propeller with broad, widely spaced blades. The high specific speed means high rotational speed for a given head and power, which is advantageous for direct coupling to generators without gearing.
