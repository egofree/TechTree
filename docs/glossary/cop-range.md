# COP range

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Single-effect ammonia absorption achieves COP of 0.5-0.7 under design conditions. This means 100 kW of cooling requires 140-200 kW of heat input. Lower than vapor-compression, but the heat can come from waste sources: engine exhaust, boiler flue gas, solar thermal collectors, or process steam.
