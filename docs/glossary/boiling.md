# Boiling

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations, health

Bring water to a rolling boil for 1 minute at sea level (100°C) to kill bacteria, viruses, and protozoan cysts. At altitudes above 2,000 m, extend to 3 minutes because water boils at lower temperatures (~93°C at 2,000 m). Boiling is the most reliable field disinfection method and requires no chemicals, only fuel and a container.
