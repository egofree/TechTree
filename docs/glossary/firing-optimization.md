# Firing optimization

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

The rapid thermal processing (belt furnace, 700-800°C peak, ~30 seconds) serves three purposes simultaneously: (1) Ag paste etches through SiNₓ to contact the n+ emitter, (2) Al alloys with silicon to form the p+ back surface field, and (3) hydrogen from SiNₓ passivates surface and bulk defects. Temperature must be precisely controlled: too low → poor contact, too high → junction degradation.
