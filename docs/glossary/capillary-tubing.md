# Capillary tubing

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

For very fine bore (0.1-1.0 mm), draw tubing vertically from a molten glass reservoir through a precision die. The draw speed (1-50 m/min) controls the outer diameter; internal pressure controls the bore. Diameter tolerance: ±5%. Used for thermometer capillaries, microfluidic devices, and gas chromatography columns.
