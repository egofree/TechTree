# Target residual

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Target residual: 0.2-0.5 mg/L free chlorine at the far end of the distribution system after 30 minutes contact time. This ensures disinfection throughout the network. Higher residuals (>1 mg/L) cause taste complaints and may form disinfection byproducts. See: [Water Supply & Sanitation](../health/sanitation.md)
