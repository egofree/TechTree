# Example 3: Petroleum crude distillation (atmospheric)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: chemistry

Atmospheric distillation of crude oil in a fractionating tower separating it into cuts by boiling range: gases (C₁–C₄), gasoline (30–200°C), kerosene (150–280°C), diesel (200–350°C), and atmospheric residue (>350°C). The foundational process of petroleum refining.
