# Cover crop

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: agriculture

Plants grown specifically to benefit soil rather than for harvest. Protect from erosion, add organic matter, fix nitrogen (legumes), suppress weeds, break pest cycles. Examples: clover (fixes 80-150 kg N/ha/yr), winter rye (hardy to -30°C), buckwheat (phosphorus scavenger, 40-50 days to flower).
