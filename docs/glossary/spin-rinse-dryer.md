# Spin rinse dryer

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

wafers are spun at 3000-5000 RPM while rinsed with ultra-pure water, then blown dry with filtered hot nitrogen (60-80°C). Centrifugal force removes water from the surface. Fast (3-5 minutes per batch) but can generate particles from water droplets hitting the wafer surface at high speed.
