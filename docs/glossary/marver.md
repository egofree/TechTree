# Marver

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Flat steel or cast iron plate, 30 × 40 cm minimum, polished smooth. Used to roll and shape the hot gather before and during blowing. The marver cools the glass surface slightly, stiffening the outer skin to control shape. A warm marver (heated on the furnace rim) shapes without excessive cooling. Keep the marver clean — any grit or debris imprints into the soft glass.
