# Global pulp production

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

~190 million tonnes/year (2020s). Chemical pulp (kraft + sulfite): ~140 million tonnes. Mechanical pulp: ~30 million tonnes. Recovered paper (recycled): ~250 million tonnes collected, representing ~50-60% of global papermaking furnish. The largest kraft pulp mills produce 3,000-6,000 tonnes/day of air-dry pulp (90% consistency). A world-scale mill consumes 10,000-20,000 m³ of wood per day, requiring a secure wood supply from managed forest plantations within 100-200 km transport radius.
