# Replacement procedure

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Power down and cool lamp housing (lamp surface reaches >600°C during operation; wait 30+ minutes). Remove old lamp, clean housing interior with IPA. Install new lamp with cotton gloves (skin oils on the quartz envelope create hot spots that cause premature failure). Align lamp in the reflector (eccentricity <0.5 mm). Power up and re-calibrate intensity. Log lamp serial number and installation date.
