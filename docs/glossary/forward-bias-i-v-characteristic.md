# Forward bias I-V characteristic

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

I = I₀(e^(qV/nkT) - 1), where I₀ is the reverse saturation current (typically 10⁻¹² to 10⁻⁹ A for small diodes), n is the ideality factor (1.0-2.0, where n=1 is ideal diffusion current and n=2 indicates recombination current), k is Boltzmann's constant (1.38×10⁻²³ J/K), and T is absolute temperature. At room temperature, kT/q ≈ 26 mV.
