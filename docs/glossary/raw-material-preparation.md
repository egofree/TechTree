# Raw material preparation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Crush and grind to target particle size distribution. Grading: coarse (3-6 mm) for skeleton, medium (1-3 mm) for packing, fine (<0.1 mm) for bonding and sintering. The particle size distribution is critical — optimized for maximum packing density (minimum porosity → maximum strength and slag resistance).
