# High-alumina refractories

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

(1500-1700°C): 1. Ramp 30-50°C/hour to 200°C 2. Ramp 60-80°C/hour to 1000°C 3. Ramp 100°C/hour to peak 4. Soak 2-4 hours (mullite formation requires extended hold above 1400°C) 5. Controlled cool at 30-50°C/hour through 600°C
