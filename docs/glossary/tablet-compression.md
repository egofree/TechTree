# Tablet compression

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Mix active ingredient with binder (starch paste, 5-10% by weight), filler (lactose or calcium phosphate), and lubricant (talc or magnesium stearate, 1-2%). Compress in a steel die at 5-20 kN using a single-punch or rotary tablet press. Target hardness: 4-8 kgf (too soft → crumbles in handling; too hard → does not disintegrate in stomach). Disintegration time: <15 minutes in water at 37°C (test by dropping tablet into warm water and timing).
