# Fuel injection timing

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Injection begins 5-15° of crankshaft rotation before TDC. The timing is critical: too early and peak cylinder pressure rises excessively (mechanical stress, knocking); too late and the fuel burns late in the expansion stroke, reducing efficiency and raising exhaust temperature. Injection duration: 20-40° of crankshaft rotation at full load. At 1500 RPM, 40° of rotation takes roughly 4.4 milliseconds.
