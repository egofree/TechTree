# Product characteristics

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

DRI typically contains 90-95% total Fe (of which 80-95% is metallic iron, the remainder is unreduced FeO). It also contains 1-4% carbon (from the reducing gas) and the gangue minerals from the original ore. DRI is highly porous (~50-70% porosity) and has a large surface area — making it ideal as feedstock for electric arc furnaces (EAF), where it melts readily and dilutes residuals in scrap-based charges.
