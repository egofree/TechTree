# Assembly

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: critical | **Domains**: computing, energy, gas-handling, machine-tools, measurement

Stack alternating positive and negative plates with separators between (porous rubber, PVC, or glass fiber mat — prevents electrical contact while allowing ion flow). Connect all positives in parallel (lead bus bar), all negatives in parallel. Insert into cell container (hard rubber or polypropylene box, acid-resistant). 3 cells in series = 6V battery, 6 cells = 12V.

## Context in the Tech Tree

Assembly is the process of combining components into a functional whole. In [Energy Storage](../energy/storage.md), it describes lead-acid battery construction — stacking plates, connecting bus bars, and sealing cell containers. In [Electromechanical Computing](../computing/electronic.md), assembly covers component integration. In [Vacuum Technology](../gas-handling/vacuum.md), it refers to chamber and pump system integration. The term marks the transition from individual parts to operational systems.

## Technical Details

Lead-acid battery assembly illustrates the assembly process at its most concrete: alternating positive (PbO₂) and negative (Pb) plates are stacked with porous separators between them. The separators prevent electrical short circuits while allowing ion flow through the sulfuric acid electrolyte. All positive plates connect in parallel via a lead bus bar; all negatives connect similarly. The plate assembly inserts into an acid-resistant container (hard rubber or polypropylene). Three cells in series produce 6V; six cells produce 12V.

Assembly quality directly determines system performance and longevity. In batteries, plate spacing uniformity affects current distribution and charging behavior. In vacuum systems, assembly cleanliness (two-step solvent wash with acetone and IPA) determines ultimate vacuum level — a single fingerprint on an internal surface can outgas for days. In mechanical computing devices, assembly precision (gear mesh, shaft alignment, bearing preload) determines calculation accuracy.

Assembly processes in the tech tree range from hand-fit (bloomery furnace construction) to semi-precision (lead-acid batteries) to high-precision (turbomolecular pump rotor balancing). Each level builds on the metrology and machining capabilities established in earlier stages.
