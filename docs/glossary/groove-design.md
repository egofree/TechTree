# Groove design

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

O-ring sits in a rectangular groove machined into one flange face. Groove depth = 70-80% of O-ring cross-section diameter (compresses the ring 20-30% to create seal). Groove width = 120-140% of cross-section (room for the ring to expand when compressed). Surface finish on groove walls: Ra <1.6 μm (smooth enough to prevent leaks past the ring).
