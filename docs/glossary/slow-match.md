# Slow match

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

A hemp or cotton rope, 1-2 cm in diameter, soaked in a solution of saltpeter (potassium nitrate, 10-15% by weight in water) and dried. One end is lit and the match smolders at roughly 10 cm per hour. It can be carried in a closed metal tube or wrapped in leather to prevent accidental ignition of surroundings. A 50 cm slow match lasts about 5 hours. To start a fire, blow on the glowing tip until bright, touch to prepared tinder.
