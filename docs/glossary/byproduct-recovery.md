# Byproduct recovery

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

All volatile gases are drawn off through an offtake pipe to a chemical recovery plant. The gas is cooled, scrubbed, and fractionated to recover coal tar, benzol, ammonia, and sulfur. Cleaned gas (coke oven gas) is burned to heat the ovens — the process is self-sustaining.
