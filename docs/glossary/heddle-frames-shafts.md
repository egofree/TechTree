# Heddle frames (shafts)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Heddle frames (shafts) refers to 2-8 lightweight wooden frames (2 × 2 cm stock), each holding metal or string heddles. Suspended from the loom's top by cords running over rollers or pulleys. Connected to treadles below by lever cords. See: [Weaving](../textiles/weaving.md)
