# Typical producer gas composition

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Typical producer gas composition (downdraft gasifier, dry wood) — CO 18-22%, H₂ 15-20%, CH₄ 2-4%, CO₂ 8-12%, N₂ 45-55% (from air), trace tars. Heating value: 4.5-6.0 MJ/Nm³ (vs 35-40 MJ/Nm³ for natural gas — producer gas is low-energy but adequate for internal combustion engines). See: [Wood Gasification](../chemistry/wood-gasification.md)
