# Numerical aperture (NA)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

NA = n·sin(θ) where n is medium refractive index (air: 1.0, immersion fluid: 1.44 for water) and θ is half-angle of light cone. Higher NA = better resolution but shallower depth of focus. Typical projection lenses: NA 0.28-0.60 for i-line steppers.
