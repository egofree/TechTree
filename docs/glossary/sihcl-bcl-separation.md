# SiHCl₃ / BCl₃ separation

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

BCl₃ boils at 12.5°C, only 19.3°C below SiHCl₃ at 31.8°C. This close boiling point difference requires a distillation column with many theoretical plates (>50) and high reflux ratio (>50:1) to achieve the separation needed for electronic-grade purity. The column height for this separation alone can exceed 30 m.
