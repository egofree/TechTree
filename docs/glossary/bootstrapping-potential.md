# Bootstrapping potential

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Can be built with moderate machining capability. A simple beta-configuration Stirling engine with air as working gas and cast-iron cylinder is achievable with a lathe and basic foundry work. Power output will be low (tens of watts to a few kW) but useful for small-scale solar thermal power generation or biomass combined heat and power (CHP). The critical path is sealing the power piston at elevated temperature without excessive friction or leakage.
