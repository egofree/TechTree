# Crushing and milling

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Calcined coke is crushed in jaw crushers and then ground in ball mills or rod mills to the target particle size distribution. A controlled mix of coarse (2-8 mm), medium (0.5-2 mm), and fine (fines, <0.15 mm) particles achieves maximum packing density in the final electrode. Typical distribution: 30-40% coarse, 20-30% medium, 30-40% fines.
