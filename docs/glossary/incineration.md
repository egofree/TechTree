# Incineration

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Last resort for solvents that cannot be recovered economically. Burn in a high-temperature incinerator (>1000°C, 2-second residence time) with afterburner to ensure complete destruction. Waste solvents with adequate heating value (>20 MJ/kg) support combustion without supplemental fuel. Flue gas must be scrubbed (chlorinated solvents produce HCl; sulfur-containing solvents produce SO₂).
