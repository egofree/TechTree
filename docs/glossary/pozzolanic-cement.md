# Pozzolanic cement

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

(Roman concrete): - **Principle**: Mix slaked lime (Ca(OH)₂) with pozzolan (reactive silica — volcanic ash, diatomaceous earth, calcined clay). Silica reacts with lime + water → calcium silicate hydrate (same binding gel as Portland cement). Sets underwater — hydraulic cement. - **Roman concrete**: Lime + volcanic ash (pozzolana from Pozzuoli, Italy) + aggregate. Pumice for lightweight.
