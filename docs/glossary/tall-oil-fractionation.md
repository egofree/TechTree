# Tall oil fractionation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Crude tall oil is distilled under vacuum (5-20 mmHg) to separate fractions. The distillation column (8-12 m tall, packed with structured packing) operates at 200-260°C bottom temperature.
