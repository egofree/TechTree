# Eutectoid composition

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

At 0.76-0.83% C, the steel is exactly at the eutectoid point. Slow cooling produces 100% pearlite (alternating lamellae of ferrite and cementite, spacing ~0.1-1 μm). This composition has the finest pearlite and the most uniform response to heat treatment — preferred for blades and springs.
