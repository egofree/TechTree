# Dry aggregate

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Crush, grind, and size petroleum coke to a defined particle size distribution (coarse 3-10 mm, medium 0.5-3 mm, fines <0.5 mm, ball-mill fines <75 μm). The packing density and porosity of the final anode depend critically on this blend.
