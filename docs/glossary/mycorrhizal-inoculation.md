# Mycorrhizal inoculation

> **Type**: process | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: agriculture

Introducing symbiotic fungi to plant roots to extend the effective root network 10-100x. Arbuscular mycorrhizae associate with 80% of crops, essential for phosphorus uptake in low-P soils. Collect inoculant from healthy forest or pasture soil. Minimize tillage to preserve hyphal networks.
