# Martensitic stainless steels

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Martensitic stainless steels (400 series, magnetic, hardenable) — Structure**: Body-centered tetragonal (BCT, distorted martensite) after quenching. Higher carbon (0.1-1.2% C) with moderate chromium (11.5-18%). - **Type 410** (12% Cr, 0.15% C): General-purpose hardenable stainless. Quench from 950-1000°C, temper at 200-700°C depending on desired hardness/toughness balance.
