# Dolomitic lime

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

Dolomitic lime (CaO·MgO, from dolomite CaCO₃·MgCO₃ fired at 1600-1800°C) — lines the refractory vessels themselves. Dolomitic refractory withstands the basic slag environment that would dissolve acidic (silica-based) refractories. See: [Lime Production](../ceramics/lime.md)
