# Hide glue (animal glue)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Boil animal hides, bones, or connective tissue in water → collagen denatures → gelatin. Concentrate by evaporation → solid glue cakes or liquid glue. Reversible with heat and water — a advantage for furniture repair (joints can be disassembled by applying hot water and steam). Used for millennia in woodworking, musical instrument construction, bookbinding, and sizing paper. Strong bond on wood but poor water resistance.
