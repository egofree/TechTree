# Warp yarn

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

The lengthwise threads on a loom must withstand sustained tension (0.5-2 N per thread for medium-weight cloth) and the abrasion of the heddles and reed during weaving. Spin warp yarn with high twist (20-30 tpi for cotton, 15-25 tpi for wool) to maximize strength and reduce hairiness. Use a 2-ply construction for reliability — a weak spot in one single is backed up by the other.
