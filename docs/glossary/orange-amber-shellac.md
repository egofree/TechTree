# Orange (amber) shellac

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

The natural product after melt-filtering. Warm amber color. Contains natural wax (3-5% by weight). Available in grades from TN (thin, lightest) to M (medium) to T (thick, darkest). The most common and versatile grade. Wax content gives a slightly cloudy appearance but also provides water resistance.
