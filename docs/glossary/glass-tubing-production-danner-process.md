# Glass tubing production — Danner process

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

(continuous): - Molten glass flows from furnace forehearth onto a rotating inclined refractory mandrel (hollow ceramic cylinder, tilted ~15° from horizontal, rotating at 5-20 RPM). Glass wraps around mandrel forming a ribbon. - Air blown through hollow mandrel center inflates the glass ribbon into a tube. Air pressure and draw speed control inner diameter.
