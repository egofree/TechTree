# Spin coating

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Dispense ~2-5 ml resist on wafer center. Spin at 1000-6000 RPM for 30-60 seconds. Centrifugal force spreads resist uniformly. Thickness: 0.5-3 μm (depends on viscosity and spin speed — roughly t ∝ 1/√ω). Edge bead removal (EBR): spray solvent at wafer edge while spinning to remove thick bead that forms at edge.
