# Hot process

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Hot process (faster cure, used with KOH for soft soap) — Heat fat to 70-80°C. Add KOH solution (from Alkalis) gradually with constant stirring. Maintain temperature for 2-4 hours with periodic stirring until mixture reaches "trace" and then thickens further into a translucent paste (KOH soaps remain paste-like).
