# Turnover rate

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Loans ÷ collection size. A healthy circulating collection turns over 0.5-2.0 times per year (each book is borrowed once every 6-24 months on average). Below 0.3 suggests the collection does not meet user needs; above 3.0 suggests the collection is too small for the demand.
