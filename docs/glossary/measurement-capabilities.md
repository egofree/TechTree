# Measurement capabilities

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: optics

Length (X-Y stage micrometers), angle (rotary stage + protractor reticle), thread pitch and profile, tool cutting edge angles, radius gauges, small hole diameters (using the double-image method with a birefringent prism). Accuracy: ±0.003 mm for linear measurements, ±1 arcminute for angles.
