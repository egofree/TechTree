# Colpitts oscillator

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Single transistor with capacitive voltage divider feedback. Two capacitors in series form the resonant circuit with an inductor. Oscillation frequency: f = 1/(2π√(L·Cseries)), where Cseries = C1·C2/(C1+C2). Feedback ratio: C1/C2 sets the loop gain. Typical values: C1 = C2 = 100 pF, L = 10 μH → f ≈ 7 MHz. Used for RF oscillators and local oscillators in radio receivers.
