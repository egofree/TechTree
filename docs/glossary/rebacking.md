# Rebacking

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

(restoring a deteriorated spine): - Remove the old spine material carefully with a thin lifting knife, preserving any spine lettering or decoration. Clean the exposed spine and sewing supports. Reinforce the spine with muslin cloth adhered with pH-neutral PVA (polyvinyl acetate) adhesive, extending the muslin 25-30 mm onto each board to create new hinge material.
