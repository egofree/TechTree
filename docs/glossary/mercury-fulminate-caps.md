# Mercury fulminate caps

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Mercury fulminate caps (1860s-1920s) — Hg(ONC)₂ primary explosive pressed into copper or aluminum shell. Initiated by safety fuse. Unstable in hot climates (decomposes above 50°C). Mercury toxicity. See: [Explosives and Propellants](../chemistry/explosives.md)
