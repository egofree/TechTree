# Why steelmaking matters for bootstrapping

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Steel is the structural material of industrial civilization. Without it: no rails, no bridges, no pressure vessels, no machine tools, no automobiles, no ships, no high-rise buildings, no pipelines. Each stage of steelmaking development unlocks progressively more demanding applications — puddled steel for hand tools, Bessemer steel for rails and boilers, BOF + continuous casting for mass production, EAF + ladle refining for clean specialty grades.
