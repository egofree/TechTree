# Coal data summary

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Coal data summary (typical bituminous, washed) — - Calorific value: 26-32 MJ/kg - Carbon: 65-85% | Hydrogen: 4-6% | Oxygen: 5-15% | Sulfur: 0.5-2% | Ash: 5-12% - Bulk density: 700-900 kg/m³ (loose), 1000-1200 kg/m³ (compacted) - Stoichiometric air: ~8 kg air/kg coal - Ash fusion temperature: 1100-1400°C. See: [Coal Mining & Combustion](../energy/coal.md)
