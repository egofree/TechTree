# Short runs (<1,000 copies)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Typesetting dominates total cost. A 200-page book requires ~200 hours of compositor time at 1-2 pages/hour. This labor cost is fixed regardless of edition size — it is amortized across all copies. For 500 copies, typesetting may represent 60-70% of total production cost. Paper and press time are relatively minor contributors.
