# Roof and charging

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

The roof is either a swing-away or lift-off design to allow scrap charging via overhead crane and drop-bottom charging basket. A typical charge involves 2-3 basket loads to fill the furnace. The roof contains three electrode ports (water-cooled copper or steel rings) and a fourth port for off-gas extraction. Roof life: 500-2000 heats for water-cooled panel roofs.
