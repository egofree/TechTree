# Stage 3: BOF and continuous casting

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(Years 20-25) - Requires: tonnage oxygen plant (air separation, see [Air Separation](../chemistry/air-separation.md)), water-cooled copper molds, precision hydraulics for caster. The BOF replaces Bessemer with faster cycles (20 min vs. 20 min, but 250+ tonnes per heat vs. 15-30 t) and vastly better quality (no nitrogen pickup). - Continuous casting eliminates ingot soaking and blooming mill — direct slab/billet production with 95-98% yield.
