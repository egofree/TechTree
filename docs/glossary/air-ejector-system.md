# Air ejector system

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Non-condensable gases (air leaking through shaft seals, gases dissolved in feedwater) accumulate in the condenser and degrade vacuum. A two-stage steam jet air ejector is the standard removal method. The first stage pulls the air-steam mixture from the condenser, compresses it, and condenses the steam in an inter-aftercondenser.
