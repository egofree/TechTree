# Pressure flaking

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Antler tine or copper-tipped tool pressed against edge to remove tiny flakes for final shaping and sharpening. Produces edges sharper than surgical steel scalpels (obsidian ~3 nm edge width vs steel ~500 nm). Work slowly along the edge, removing flakes 2-5 mm wide. Pressure flaking is how you get the final cutting edge and symmetrical form.
