# Overspeed trip

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Independent mechanical bolt (emergency trip) mounted on the rotor shaft. At 110-115% of rated speed, centrifugal force flings the bolt outward, tripping a latch that dumps hydraulic oil from the steam valve actuators — valves slam shut under spring force. This is the last line of defense against runaway. Test the overspeed trip regularly by tripping it electrically.
