# Lithium thermal runaway

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

(later chemistries): Lithium-ion cells can enter self-sustaining overheating if overcharged, punctured, or shorted. Fire burns without external oxygen (self-oxidizing). Requires Class D fire extinguisher or sand — water may worsen lithium fires. Not applicable to lead-acid/nickel-iron bootstrap phase.
