# Secondary containment

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

All acid storage tanks must have secondary containment sized for 110% of the largest tank volume. Concrete dike or bund with acid-resistant coating (epoxy novolac or vinyl ester). Containment floor sloped to a collection sump with level alarm. Rainwater that accumulates within the containment must be tested for pH before discharge (contaminated rainwater is treated as waste acid). Tank truck unloading areas must also have containment and spill collection.
