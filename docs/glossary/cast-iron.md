# Cast iron

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Standard for municipal water distribution since the 19th century. Available in diameters 50-1200 mm. Joint types: bell-and-spigot with lead or rubber ring gasket, or flanged. Working pressure: 10-25 bar. Lifespan: 50-100+ years if soil conditions are non-corrosive. Internal cement-mortar lining prevents tuberculation (iron oxide nodules that reduce flow capacity).
