# Prepare bath

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Add enough water to submerge fiber freely — minimum 10:1 water-to-fiber ratio by weight (e.g., 10 liters water per 1 kg fiber). Less water produces uneven dyeing; more water wastes fuel. Dissolve mordant (if applying in same bath — "one-bath" method) or use pre-mordanted fiber.
