# Tap-to-tap cycle

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

A complete heat cycle (tap-to-tap time) runs 45-90 minutes. Breakdown: charging (5-10 min), melting (20-40 min at full power), refining and temperature adjustment (5-15 min), tapping (3-5 min). Productivity: 20-30 heats per day for a well-run furnace. Modern high-power EAFs with scrap preheating and oxygen lancing approach 45-minute cycles.
