# Cultivation attempts

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Several colonial-era plantations were established in Java and Malaya (1880s-1920s), but the long maturation period (15-30 years vs. 5-7 years for rubber trees) made gutta-percha plantation economics unattractive. Trees can be propagated from seed or air-layered cuttings. Optimal growing conditions: annual rainfall 2000-3500 mm, mean temperature 25-28°C, well-drained volcanic or loam soils, altitude below 500 m.
