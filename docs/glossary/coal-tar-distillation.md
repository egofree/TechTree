# Coal tar distillation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: chemistry

Fractional separation of coal tar into usable fractions by boiling range: light oil (<180°C), middle oil/creosote (180-240°C), heavy oil (240-300°C), anthracene oil (300-360°C), and pitch residue (>360°C). Produces benzene, naphthalene, phenol, and creosote as primary products.
