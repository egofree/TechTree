# Oil diffusion pump fire hazard

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Diffusion pump oil is heated to 150-200°C in the boiler. If the cooling water fails (or was never turned on), oil temperature rises uncontrollably — silicone oil decomposes above ~250-300°C, producing flammable decomposition gases. If air leaks into a hot diffusion pump, the mixture of hot oil vapor and oxygen can ignite.
