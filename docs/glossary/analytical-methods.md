# Analytical methods

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Gas chromatography with thermal conductivity detection quantifies chlorosilanes and higher silanes to ppm levels. Fourier transform infrared spectroscopy (FTIR) detects moisture and oxygen-containing impurities to ppb levels in real time. Atmospheric pressure ionization mass spectrometry (APIMS) provides the ultimate sensitivity: sub-ppb detection of trace metallic and organic contaminants in silane gas streams. For bootstrap production, GC-TCD is sufficient for process control.
