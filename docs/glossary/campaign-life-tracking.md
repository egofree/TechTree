# Campaign life tracking

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Furnace lining wear is monitored by: (1) infrared thermography of the external steel shell (hot spots indicate lining thinning or failure), (2) laser profilometry of the hot face during shutdown inspections (maps remaining lining thickness), (3) historical tracking of refractory consumption per tonne of product (typical steelmaking: 4-8 kg refractory per tonne of steel for BOF linings, 2-4 kg/t for ladles).
