# ANFO properties

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

ANFO properties: 94% ammonium nitrate (NH₄NO₃) prills + 6% fuel oil (diesel). Density 0.8-0.85 g/cm³ (very low, limits energy density per unit volume). Detonation velocity 2,500-4,000 m/s depending on confinement, prill size, and density. Critical diameter 50-100 mm (below this, detonation dies out). Requires a booster (pentolite or cast TNT/RDX booster, 100-500 g) to initiate because ANFO is too insensitive for a standard blasting cap. Detonation energy 3.7 MJ/kg.
