# Vacuum evaporation

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

(simplest deposition method): - **Process**: Place metal source (Al, Au, Ti) in tungsten or molybdenum boat or basket in vacuum chamber. Pump to 10⁻⁵ to 10⁻⁶ Torr. Heat source resistively (current through boat) to metal's evaporation temperature (Al: 1200°C, Au: 1500°C). Metal vapor travels in straight lines to substrate.
