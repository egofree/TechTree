# Deep silicon etching (Bosch process)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Alternating etch/passivate cycles for high-aspect-ratio structures (MEMS, through-silicon vias). Etch step: SF₆ plasma generates F· radicals for isotropic Si etch (etch rate 5-20 μm/min). Passivation step: C₄F₈ plasma deposits a thin (~10 nm) fluorocarbon polymer (CF₂)ₙ on all surfaces. Next etch step's ion bombardment removes polymer from horizontal surfaces while protecting sidewalls. Cycle frequency 5-20 Hz. Result: vertical sidewalls with periodic scallops (50-200 nm roughness per cycle)....
