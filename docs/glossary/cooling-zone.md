# Cooling zone

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics, chemistry

Clinker exits kiln at ~1300°C into a clinker cooler (grate cooler — ambient air blows upward through falling clinker bed). Clinker cooled to 100-200°C in 20-30 minutes. The heated air (800-1000°C) is recovered as combustion air for the kiln burner — essential for fuel efficiency.
