# Vitamins and growth factors

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Biotin (0.01-0.1 mg/L) is essential for S. cerevisiae growth (biotin-deficient molasses must be supplemented). Thiamine (vitamin B₁), riboflavin (B₂), and pantothenic acid (B₅) are required by many bacterial species. Corn steep liquor and yeast extract provide these as natural complexes, avoiding the need for pure vitamin supplements.
