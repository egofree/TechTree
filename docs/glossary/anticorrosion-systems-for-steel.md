# Anticorrosion systems for steel

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Multi-layer system: (1) **Abrasive blast** to Sa 2.5 (near-white metal, ISO 8501-1). (2) **Zinc-rich primer** (organic or inorganic) — galvanic protection (zinc corrodes preferentially, protecting steel). 50-75 µm. (3) **Epoxy intermediate coat** — barrier protection. 100-200 µm. (4) **Polyurethane topcoat** — UV resistance, color/gloss retention, weatherability. 50-75 µm. Total system: 200-350 µm. Service life: 15-25 years to first maintenance in moderate environment (ISO 12944 C3).
