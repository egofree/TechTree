# Flatness measurement

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement, silicon

Place optical flat (quartz or glass, flat to λ/10) on surface to be measured. Illuminate with monochromatic light (sodium lamp, λ = 589 nm). Interference fringes appear between flat and test surface. Straight, equally-spaced fringes = flat surface. Curved fringes = surface deviation. One fringe deviation = λ/2 = 295 nm flatness error.
