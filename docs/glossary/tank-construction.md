# Tank construction

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Concrete or polymer-lined (PVC, polyethylene) rectangular tank, typically 3-5 m long × 1-1.2 m wide × 1.2-1.5 m deep. Multiple anodes and cathodes hang in parallel, alternating, with 80-120 mm gap between anode and cathode surfaces. A single tank holds 30-50 anode-cathode pairs.
