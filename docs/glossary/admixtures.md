# Admixtures

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Admixtures (chemical additives mixed into concrete to modify properties) — - **Plasticizers** (water reducers): Lignosulfonates or polycarboxylate ethers. Reduce water content 5-15% while maintaining workability → higher strength (lower water/cement ratio). - **Superplasticizers** (high-range water reducers): Polycarboxylate ethers.
