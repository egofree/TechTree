# Dovetail

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Splay-cut interlocking pins and tails. Resists pull-out without fasteners. Used for chests, boxes, drawer joints. The angle of the splay is typically 1:6 to 1:8 along the thickness. Too steep and the joint weakens the pins; too shallow and it doesn't lock.
