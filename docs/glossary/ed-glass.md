# ED glass

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

(extra-low dispersion): Borosilicate with rare earth oxides (lanthanum, La₂O₃), RI 1.65-1.80, Abbe number 45-55. Further reduces chromatic aberration in apochromatic lens designs. Requires rare earth element extraction from monazite or bastnäsite ores.
