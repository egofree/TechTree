# Screen printing inks

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Used for PCB solder paste, thick-film circuits, textile printing, signage. Heavy paste consistency (50-200 Pa·s). Printed through a mesh screen (40-150 threads/cm) with a squeegee. Wet film thickness: 20-100 µm. Thick-film conductive inks: silver or copper particles in glass frit binder — fired at 600-900°C to sinter metal and fuse glass.
