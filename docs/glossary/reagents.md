# Reagents

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health, mining

Three categories. (1) **Collector** (xanthate, 20-100 g/tonne): Adsorbs on sulfide mineral surfaces, making them hydrophobic (water-repellent). Sodium ethyl xanthate is the most common. (2) **Frother** (MIBC or pine oil, 20-50 g/tonne): Stabilizes air bubbles into a persistent froth. Without frother, bubbles coalesce and pop too quickly.
