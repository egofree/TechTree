# Castable refractories

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Calcium aluminate cement (CAC, 15-25% of mix) bonds refractory aggregate (tabular alumina, bauxite, or magnesite) when mixed with water on site. Installation by pouring into forms (casting), pumping (shotcreting), or pneumatic spraying (gunning).
