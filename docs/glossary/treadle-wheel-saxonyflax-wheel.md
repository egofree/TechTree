# Treadle wheel (Saxony/flax wheel)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Foot treadle drives wheel via crank, leaving both hands free for fiber control. Flyer and bobbin assembly: flyer (U-shaped bracket with hooks) adds twist, bobbin winds yarn at slightly different speed (controlled by Scotch tension or double-drive band). Ratios 8:1 to 20:1 (spindle revolutions per wheel revolution). Fine linen requires high ratio. Production: 300-800 m/hour. Requires precision woodwork and iron fittings for bearings.
