# Acid rock drainage

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Sulfide-bearing tailings exposed to air and water generate sulfuric acid (pyrite oxidation: FeS₂ + O₂ + H₂O → Fe(OH)₃ + H₂SO₄). The acidic water dissolves heavy metals from the tailings, creating a toxic effluent. Prevention: submerge sulfide tailings under water (limits oxygen access), or blend with limestone to neutralize acid as it forms. Monitor discharge water pH and metal concentrations continuously.
