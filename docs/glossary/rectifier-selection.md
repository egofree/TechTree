# Rectifier selection

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Silicon diode rectifiers are standard for modern electrolysis plants. Input: 3-phase AC at 6-35 kV. Output: DC at the required voltage and current. Rectifier transformer steps down to the cell operating voltage (200-1200V DC depending on process). Rectifier efficiency: 97-99%. Cooling: water-cooled diode heat sinks.
