# Latex flow

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Each cut yields 30-100 ml of latex per tapping, flowing for 2-4 hours before the wound seals through latex coagulation at the cut surface (a natural wound-healing mechanism called "plugging"). Flow rate is highest in the first 30 minutes and declines exponentially. Annual tapping days: 200-300 depending on climate and management intensity.
