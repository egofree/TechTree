# Rough grinding

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass, optics

Fix glass blank to convex or concave cast iron tool (curve matched to desired radius). Add coarse abrasive (60-120 grit SiC or emery) and water. Stroke blank across tool in random pattern. Check radius with spherometer or template. Duration: 2-8 hours depending on size and depth.
