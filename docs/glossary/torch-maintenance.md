# Torch maintenance

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Clean torch tips regularly. Carbon buildup in the gas orifice causes uneven flame and popping. Replace O-rings on gas fittings when they show cracking. Check hose connections for leaks with a soap solution before each session. Flashback arrestors on both gas and oxygen lines are mandatory — a flashback into the fuel supply causes an explosion.
