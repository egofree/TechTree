# Chemical exposure

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Coal tar and bitumen contain polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons (PAHs), some of which are carcinogenic with prolonged skin contact. Workers handling hot tar or bitumen should wear impermeable gloves and wash exposed skin at the end of each shift. Cold tar workers (macadam surfacing) face lower but non-zero exposure.
