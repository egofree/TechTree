# From tokens to tablets

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

From tokens to tablets (~3400 BCE) — - The impressions on bullae evolved into systematic marks on flat clay tablets — the beginning of proto-cuneiform. The transition from three-dimensional tokens to two-dimensional impressed marks is the origin of abstract written numbers and commodity symbols.
