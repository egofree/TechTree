# Toxic fining agents

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Arsenic trioxide and antimony trioxide are listed as fining agents (0.1-0.5% addition) used during the fining stage at 1500-1600°C. Both are toxic by inhalation and ingestion. Arsenic trioxide is a known carcinogen. Handle as sealed, pre-weighed additions whenever possible. Avoid creating dust. Use gloves and respiratory protection when measuring and adding fining agents to the melt.
