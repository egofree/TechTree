# Tetracycline + calcium/iron/dairy

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Divalent and trivalent cations (Ca²⁺, Fe²⁺/³⁺, Mg²⁺, Al³⁺) chelate tetracycline in the gut, preventing absorption. Take tetracycline on an empty stomach, 2 hours before or after calcium/iron supplements or dairy products.
