# Fuel options

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Coal (pulverized, most common globally), natural gas, heavy fuel oil, petroleum coke, waste tires, municipal solid waste-derived fuel. Fuel consumption: 3.0-3.5 GJ/tonne clinker (modern dry process), 5.0-6.5 GJ/tonne (wet process). Kiln burner: 40-120 MW thermal input for a 3000 tonnes/day kiln.
