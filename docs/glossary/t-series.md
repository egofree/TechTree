# T-series

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(Tungsten HSS, e.g., T1): 0.75% C, 4% Cr, 18% W, 1% V. The original HSS composition (Taylor and White, 1901). Tungsten provides the same secondary hardening as molybdenum but at higher cost. M-series has largely replaced T-series except for special applications.
