# Pigments

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, metals

— provide color, opacity, and corrosion inhibition: - **Titanium dioxide (TiO₂)**: The most important white pigment — extreme opacity (refractive index 2.7, vs 1.5 for most binders) from light scattering. Rutile form preferred (higher RI than anatase). Global production: 7 million tonnes/year. Used in virtually every white and light-colored paint.
