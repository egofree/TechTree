# Gelatin production

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Hydrolyze animal collagen (skin, bone, connective tissue) in dilute acid (Type A, porcine) or dilute alkali (Type B, bovine) for 12-48 hours. Extract gelatin by heating the treated collagen in water at 60-80°C. Filter, concentrate, dry to sheets or powder.
