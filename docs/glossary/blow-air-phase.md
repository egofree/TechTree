# Blow (air) phase

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Air replaces steam. Exothermic C + O₂ reactions reheat the bed to 1000-1200°C. Blow gas (mostly N₂ + CO₂, low heating value) is either vented or burned in a waste heat boiler for steam generation. Phase duration: 1-3 minutes.
