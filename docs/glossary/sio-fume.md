# SiO fume

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Silicon monoxide vapor condenses to ultrafine SiO₂ particles (<1 μm) in the off-gas. Known as "silica fume." Collected in baghouse filters. ~200-400 kg per tonne Si. Valuable byproduct — sold as pozzolanic additive for high-strength concrete, or reprocessed.
