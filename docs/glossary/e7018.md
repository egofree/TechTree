# E7018

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

Low-hydrogen coating (limestone/CaCO₃ base). Iron powder in coating increases deposition rate. High tensile strength (70 ksi / 480 MPa minimum). The structural welding electrode — required for bridges, buildings, pressure vessels. Resists hydrogen-induced cracking. MUST be stored dry (bake at 120-200°C if exposed to humidity). DC preferred, AC usable.
