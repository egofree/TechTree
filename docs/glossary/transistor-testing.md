# Transistor testing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement, silicon

A simple curve tracer displays the I-V characteristics of a transistor on an oscilloscope screen. A staircase generator drives the base current in steps while a sawtooth sweeps the collector voltage. The resulting family of curves reveals gain (β = ΔIc/ΔIb), saturation voltage, breakdown voltage, and leakage current.
