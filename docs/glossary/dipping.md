# Dipping

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics, textiles

Submerge the bisqued pot in glaze slurry, lift out in one smooth motion, drain for 3-5 seconds, then set aside. Specific gravity of the glaze slurry: 1.4-1.6 (measured with a hydrometer). A 3-second dip in a glaze at SG 1.5 typically deposits a 0.3-0.5 mm glaze layer, which fires to a smooth coating. Fast, consistent, good for production.
