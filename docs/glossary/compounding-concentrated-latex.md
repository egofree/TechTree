# Compounding concentrated latex

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Add sulfur (1-2 phr as aqueous dispersion), zinc oxide (1-3 phr), accelerators (ZDEC, ZMBT at 0.5-2 phr as dispersion), and antioxidants directly to concentrated latex with stirring. The compounded latex is matured (aged) for 6-24 hours at ambient temperature to allow pre-vulcanization — partial crosslinking occurs in the latex particle before the product is formed. This "pre-vulcanized latex" produces stronger dipped goods.
