# Typical operations

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Seal one end of a tube (rotate in flame, let surface tension close the opening). Make a T-seal (heat a hole in the wall of a tube, butt-join a perpendicular tube, flame-polish). Bend tubing (heat narrow zone, bend to desired angle, blow lightly to prevent collapse of the cross-section). Make a ring seal (join two tubes of different diameter end-to-end with a smooth tapered transition).
