# Extraction — Sulfide ores

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: metals

Metal extraction from sulfide ore minerals (chalcopyrite, sphalerite, galena, pentlandite) through crushing, grinding, flotation concentration, smelting, and refining. Sulfide ores are the primary source of copper, zinc, lead, and nickel; their processing produces sulfur dioxide requiring capture.
