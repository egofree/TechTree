# Fish sauce

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Layer small fish (or fish scraps) with salt at a ratio of 3-4:1 fish to salt by weight (20-30% salt by total weight). Pack into earthenware jars or wooden barrels. Weigh down with a stone to submerge. Ferment in a warm place for 6-12 months. Liquid drawn off is fish sauce: rich in amino acids and umami flavor. The remaining paste can be used as a condiment or fertilizer.
