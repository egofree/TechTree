# Belt drive addition

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Flat leather or canvas belts (50-200 mm wide) connecting pulleys of different diameters provide additional speed changes with built-in slip protection. Speed ratio equals driven pulley diameter divided by driver pulley diameter. Slip of 2-5% is normal and acts as a safety clutch, preventing catastrophic damage if the driven machine jams. Minimum pulley diameter is roughly 30× belt thickness to avoid cracking the leather.
