# Weather minimums

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Fly only in visual meteorological conditions (VMC): visibility ≥1.5 km, clear of clouds, in sight of the ground. Cloud base above 300 m for safe terrain clearance. Do not launch into fog, low overcast, heavy rain, or thunderstorms. Ultralight aircraft have no de-icing capability and minimal structural margin for turbulence. A cumulonimbus (thunderstorm) can produce updrafts and downdrafts exceeding 20 m/s, far beyond the structural capability of a light airframe.
