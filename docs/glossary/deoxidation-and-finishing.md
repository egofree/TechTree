# Deoxidation and finishing

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(30-60 min): Stop ore additions when target carbon is reached. Add ferromanganese (Mn 70-80%, 8-12 kg/tonne steel) and aluminum (0.3-0.8 kg/tonne) to deoxidize. Take samples, adjust composition with ferroalloys.
