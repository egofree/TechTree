# Electrical properties

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Good electrical insulator. Volume resistivity ~10¹⁴-10¹⁵ Ω·cm. Dielectric strength 300-500 V/mil (12-20 kV/mm). Dielectric constant ~3.5 at 1 kHz. Used historically for coil insulation and early electronics — adequate for low-voltage (<1000 V) applications but replaced by synthetic varnishes for high-temperature and high-voltage service.
