# Phosphating

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Zinc or iron phosphate conversion coating applied by immersion or spray. Steel surfaces are cleaned, then exposed to phosphoric acid solution containing zinc dihydrogen phosphate and accelerators (nitrite or chlorate). Fe dissolution at the surface raises local pH, causing insoluble zinc phosphate crystals to precipitate on the steel surface. Coating weight 1-5 g/m².
