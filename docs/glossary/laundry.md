# Laundry

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Specialized cleanroom laundry — ultra-pure water wash, non-ionic detergent, drying in HEPA-filtered environment. Garments tested for particle shedding after each wash (Helmke drum test or body box test). Typically replaced after 30-50 wash cycles when particle shedding exceeds specification.
