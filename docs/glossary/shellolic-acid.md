# Shellolic acid

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

and **epishellolic acid** (~25%): Sesquiterpene carboxylic acids (C₁₅) that contribute rigidity and hydrophobic character. The aldehyde group of shellolic acid can undergo esterification and acetal formation, contributing to the aging (insolubilization) of shellac.
