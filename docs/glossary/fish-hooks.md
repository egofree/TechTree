# Fish hooks

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Carved from bone or antler with stone flakes. Barbed designs carved with a burin. Gorge hooks (straight double-pointed pieces lashed at midpoint) are the simplest form. Bait the gorge, swallow it lengthwise, and it turns sideways in the fish's throat. Composite hooks, with a bone point lashed to a wooden shank, allow larger hooks than a single bone piece permits.
