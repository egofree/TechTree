# Saggars

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

Saggars (for glaze firing) — Fired clay boxes that enclose individual pots. Protect glaze from direct flame contact and ash deposit. Stack saggar towers inside kiln. Essential for clean glazed ware in wood-fired kilns. Used extensively in porcelain production. See: [Kiln Construction](../ceramics/kilns.md)
