# Nitroglycerin synthesis

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Glycerol + mixed acid (HNO₃ + H₂SO₄) at 10-15°C. Temperature must stay below 15°C — above 50°C, runaway decomposition produces massive explosion. Carefully separate nitroglycerin from acid layer. Wash with water, then sodium bicarbonate solution (neutralize residual acid). Nitroglycerin is extremely shock-sensitive — never handle in quantities >10 mL without remote facilities.
