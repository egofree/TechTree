# Sugar sources

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Sugarcane juice (12-18% fermentable sugar, predominantly sucrose) provides the simplest route, requiring only pressing and clarification before fermentation. Grain starch requires enzymatic hydrolysis: barley amylase (α-amylase and β-amylase produced during malting) converts starch to maltose and glucose at 60-65°C.
