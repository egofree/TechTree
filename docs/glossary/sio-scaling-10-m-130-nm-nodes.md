# SiO₂ scaling (10 μm → 130 nm nodes)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: vlsi-scaling

Gate oxide scales from ~100 nm down to ~1.2 nm (about 5 atomic layers) over ~30 years. Each node thins the oxide by ~30%. Below ~3 nm, quantum tunneling through the oxide produces gate leakage current that increases ~10x for every 0.2 nm of thinning. At 1.2 nm SiO₂ (130 nm node), gate leakage reaches ~100 A/cm² — unacceptable for power consumption.
