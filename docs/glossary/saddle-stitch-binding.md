# Saddle-stitch binding

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Fold signatures and nest them one inside another, then staple through the fold with two wire staples (stitching wire: 24-26 gauge). Suitable for booklets of 80 pages or fewer. Fast and cheap: a saddle-stitcher binds 3,000-8,000 booklets per hour. Limitation: pages pull out under heavy use because the wire is the sole attachment.
