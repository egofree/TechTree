# Insulation and heat loss

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

All distillation columns and associated piping must be insulated to minimize heat loss. Mineral wool or calcium silicate insulation, 50-100 mm thickness, with aluminum sheet metal cladding for weather protection. Heat loss from an insulated column: 50-200 W/m² of external surface area, depending on operating temperature and ambient conditions.
