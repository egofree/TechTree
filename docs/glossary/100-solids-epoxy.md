# 100% solids epoxy

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

No solvent — all applied material becomes the cured film. Applied by airless spray or plural-component spray equipment (resin and hardener mixed at the spray gun). Film thickness: 200-500 μm in single coat. Used for: secondary containment floors (chemical spill resistance), food processing plants (hygienic, seamless surface), pharmaceutical clean rooms.
