# Sodium hydroxide and sodium sulfide

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

White liquor causes severe chemical burns. PPE: acid-resistant gloves, face shield, chemical suit. Emergency showers in all pulping areas. Na₂S releases H₂S (rotten egg gas, toxic — IDLH 100 ppm, deadens olfactory nerve at higher concentrations making it impossible to smell) on contact with acid.
