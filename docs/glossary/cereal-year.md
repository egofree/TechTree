# Cereal year

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

Wheat, barley, or oats. Exploits nitrogen fixed by preceding legume 3. **Root crop year**: Turnips, potatoes, or beets. Deep rooting breaks plow pan; cultivation suppresses weeds 4. **Fallow or cereal**: Rest the land, or plant a second cereal (rye or oats) if fertility permits
