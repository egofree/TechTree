# Resistivity mapping

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Four-point probe measurement at multiple positions along the ingot length (every 10-25 mm) before slicing. Identifies regions where resistivity is out of specification (high at the seed end due to initial dopant fluctuations, and at the tail end where impurity segregation concentrates dopants). These regions are cropped and discarded or recycled.
