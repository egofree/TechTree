# Advanced control

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Model predictive control (MPC) optimizes the entire column operation by predicting the effect of feed composition changes, ambient temperature variations, and other disturbances on product quality. MPC adjusts multiple manipulated variables (reboiler heat, reflux ratio, feed tray position) simultaneously to maintain product specifications while minimizing energy consumption.
