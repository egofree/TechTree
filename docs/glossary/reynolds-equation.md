# Reynolds equation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

governs film pressure distribution. Simplified: for a plain journal bearing, minimum film thickness h_min ≈ (μ × U × d²) / (4 × W), where μ = viscosity, U = surface speed, d = bearing diameter, W = load. Maintain h_min > 3× surface roughness for full film separation.
