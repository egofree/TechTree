# Filtration rate

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

0.3-0.5 m/h (0.1-0.4 m/h in some references depending on raw water quality). At this rate, a 50 m² filter produces 15-25 m³/hour of treated water, enough for a small community of 500-1,000 people at 25-50 L/person/day.
