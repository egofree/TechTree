# Gas cabinet ventilation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Enclose toxic gas cylinders in a ventilated gas cabinet. Face velocity 100-200 fpm (0.5-1.0 m/s) at the cabinet access opening. Exhaust to outside, never recirculate. Install leak detection interlock: if the gas detector alarms, automatic shutoff valve closes the cylinder, ventilation runs at maximum, and audible/visual alarms activate. Emergency shutoff accessible from outside the cabinet.
