# Fish traps

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

funnel-shaped woven traps with an inverted cone entrance. Fish swim in but can't find the exit. Anchor with stones in flowing water. A trap 60-80 cm long, 20-30 cm in diameter at the wide end, takes 3-5 hours to weave. Bait with scraps or grain. Check traps daily. Fish traps are one of the highest-return tools for labor invested: a single well-placed trap in a stream can catch several fish per day with no ongoing effort beyond checking and rebaiting.
