# Lime mortar setting

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Ca(OH)₂ + CO₂ (from air) → CaCO₃ + H₂O. Sets by carbonation — absorbs CO₂ from atmosphere over weeks to months. Hydraulic lime (containing clay impurities) also sets by hydration of calcium silicates — sets underwater. Roman concrete (opus caecementicium) used hydraulic lime + volcanic ash (pozzolana) — 2000-year durability.
