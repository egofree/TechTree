# Electric caps

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Electric caps (1900s-present) — bridge wire heated by electric current ignites primary charge. Precise timing — delay elements (pyrotechnic delay compositions) provide 25 ms to 1000 ms delays for sequential blasting. Enables controlled blasts with dozens of holes detonated in precise sequence. See: [Explosives and Propellants](../chemistry/explosives.md)
