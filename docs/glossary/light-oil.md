# Light oil

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Light oil (up to 170°C) — ~5% of tar. Contains benzene (C₆H₆), toluene (C₇H₈), xylene (C₈H₁₀) — KEY organic chemistry feedstocks. Further fractionated by re-distillation. Benzene boils at 80°C, toluene at 111°C, xylene at 139°C. See: [Petroleum & Alternative Chemistry](../chemistry/petroleum-alternatives.md)
