# Structural spalling

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Penetration of slag or vapor into the refractory creates a chemically altered zone (the "reaction zone") with different thermal expansion and density from the original brick. During temperature cycling, the boundary between altered and unaltered zones becomes a stress concentration point, causing the altered layer to detach.
