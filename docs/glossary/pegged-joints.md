# Pegged joints

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Drill holes through overlapping members, drive hardwood pegs (oak, ash). Pegs swell with moisture and lock tight. Replaces nails in timber construction. Offset the peg hole slightly (1-2 mm) toward the joint line to draw the pieces together as the peg is driven, creating a clamped effect called drawboring.
