# Roughing mill

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Roughing mill (reversing) — A single high-capacity stand (or 2-stand tandem) that reduces slab from 200-300 mm to 30-50 mm in 3-7 reversing passes. Roll force: 20,000-40,000 kN. Roll torque: 1,000-3,000 kN·m. Work rolls 1000-1350 mm diameter, driven by 5,000-10,000 kW motors through pinion gears and spindles. Slab passes back and forth, with roll gap adjusted between passes (screw-down mechanism, 0.1 mm positioning accuracy). Edger rolls control width to ±2-5 mm.
