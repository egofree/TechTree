# Filter production

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Glass fiber production (melt borosilicate glass, attenuate into fine fibers by flame attenuation or rotary spinning). Form mat on moving belt with vacuum forming. Bond with acrylic resin binder (5-10% by weight). Cut, pleat, frame, test (challenge with DOP or PAO aerosol particles, measure penetration with photometer).
