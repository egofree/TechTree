# High-power laser mirrors

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: optics

Optical mirrors designed to withstand intense laser irradiation without damage, using high-reflectivity dielectric coatings (alternating high/low refractive index layers) on thermally conductive substrates (copper, silicon carbide). Coating design must minimize absorption and provide thermal management for CW or pulsed laser power.
