# Online measurements

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Modern fermenters are equipped with probes for continuous monitoring of: dissolved oxygen (DO, polarographic or optical sensor, 0-100% saturation), pH (steam-sterilizable glass electrode), temperature (Pt100 RTD in a thermowell), off-gas composition (CO₂ and O₂ by infrared and paramagnetic analyzers), foam level (capacitance probe), and redox potential (for anaerobic fermentations).
