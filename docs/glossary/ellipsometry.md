# Ellipsometry

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Ellipsometry (film thickness) — monochromatic or spectroscopic light (632.8 nm HeNe laser, or broad spectrum 250-1000 nm) reflects off the thin film surface at a known angle (70-75°). Measures change in polarization (Ψ, Δ) upon reflection. Fits to optical model (Cauchy or Sellmeier dispersion) to extract film thickness and refractive index.
