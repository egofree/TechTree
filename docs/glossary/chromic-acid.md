# Chromic acid

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(Type I): 30-50 g/L CrO₃, 40°C, 40-50 V. Thin coating (2-10 µm), excellent for fatigue-critical aerospace parts (minimal stress concentration from the thin, non-porous coating). Also used as a pre-prime treatment for aluminum aircraft skins. Environmental concern: Cr⁶⁺ in bath and rinse water.
