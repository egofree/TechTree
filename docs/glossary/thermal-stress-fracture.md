# Thermal stress fracture

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Unannealed or improperly annealed glass can shatter spontaneously, sometimes hours or days after cooling, sending sharp fragments at high velocity. Always follow the annealing schedule (500°C hold, cool at 1-5°C/min through strain point). Never skip annealing for any glass item intended for use or handling.
