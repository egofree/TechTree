# Herringbone

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

A twill variant where the diagonal direction reverses at regular intervals (every 8-16 threads), creating a V-pattern resembling fish bones. The reversal point creates a characteristic "break" line where the twill changes direction. Herringbone is one of the most common suiting fabric patterns. Requires 4 shafts for basic versions.
