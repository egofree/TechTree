# Acetylene handling

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Acetylene handling (exceptionally dangerous gas) — - **Properties**: Acetylene (C₂H₂) is unstable and can decompose explosively without oxygen at pressures above ~2 bar. It cannot be stored in plain steel cylinders like other gases. - **Cylinder design**: Acetylene cylinders are filled with a porous material (calcium silicate or diatomaceous earth) soaked with acetone.
