# NaOH / KOH (caustic etchants)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

20-40% solutions at 80°C for saw-damage removal and texturing. Causes deep caustic burns on skin and permanent eye damage. Heat increases severity. PPE: chemical splash goggles (not just safety glasses), nitrile gloves, apron. Eye wash station within 10 seconds travel. If splashed in eyes, flush with water for 15+ minutes immediately.
