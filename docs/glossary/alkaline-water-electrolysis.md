# Alkaline Water Electrolysis

> **Type**: process | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Mature water electrolysis technology using 20-40% KOH liquid electrolyte with a porous diaphragm separator, nickel-based electrodes, operating at 60-90°C with 200-500 mA/cm2 current density and 60,000-90,000 hour stack lifetime.
