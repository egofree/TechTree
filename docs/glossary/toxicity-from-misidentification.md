# Toxicity from misidentification

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

Many edible plants have toxic lookalikes. Never eat a plant you cannot identify with certainty. Learn 10-20 local species thoroughly before expanding your foraging range. When uncertain, apply the universal edibility test: fast for 8 hours, then rub the plant on inner wrist, wait 15 minutes for irritation. Place on lips, wait 3 minutes.
