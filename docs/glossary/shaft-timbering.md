# Shaft timbering

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Line shafts with timber frames at 1.5-2 m intervals. Each frame consists of two vertical posts, two horizontal caps, and sometimes sills at the base. The compartment dividers (brattice) are horizontal planks separating the winding, ventilation, and pumping compartments. For permanent shafts in stable ground, brick or stone lining replaces timber (longer lifespan, fire-resistant). In wet ground, use concrete lining with waterproofing.
