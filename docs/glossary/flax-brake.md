# Flax brake

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Wooden frame (~80 cm tall) with two sets of interlocking wooden blades. Upper blades are hinged and raised by a lever arm. Place retted, dried flax stalks across the lower blades. Slam upper blades down — the narrow gap (2-3 mm) between upper and lower blades crushes the woody shives without cutting the fibers.
