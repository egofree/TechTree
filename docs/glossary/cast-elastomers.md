# Cast elastomers

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Prepolymer (isocyanate-terminated — typically MDI prepolymer, 6-9% NCO content) + chain extender (1,4-butanediol, BDO, at stoichiometric ratio), mixed by hand or machine and poured into a heated mold (metal or silicone, preheated to 80-100°C). Cure 100-120°C, 30-60 minutes in the mold, then post-cure at 100°C for 16-24 hours for complete reaction.
