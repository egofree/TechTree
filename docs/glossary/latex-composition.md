# Latex composition

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

(Hevea latex, typical): - 30-40% rubber hydrocarbon (cis-1,4-polyisoprene) — the useful polymer - 55-65% water — the continuous phase of the emulsion - 2-5% proteins — natural stabilizers (also cause latex allergies) - 1-2% resins — contribute to tack and aging behavior - 0.5-1% minerals (K, Mg, Cu, Fe trace elements)
