# Asphalt road

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Asphalt road (bituminous pavement) — - **Hot mix asphalt (HMA)**: Aggregate (crushed stone 0-20 mm graded) mixed with bitumen binder (5-6% by weight) at 150-180°C in a batch plant. Laid with a paving machine at 135-165°C. Compact with steel-wheel roller (initial pass at 135°C, finish at 90°C minimum). - Compaction target: 92-95% of theoretical maximum density. Under-compacted asphalt ruts under heavy loads; over-compacted crushes the aggregate. - Layer thickness: 50-75 mm wearing course on 75-150 mm binder...
