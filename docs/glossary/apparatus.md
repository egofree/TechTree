# Apparatus

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Electrolytic cell with nickel or platinum electrodes. Electrolyte: 20-30% KOH solution (better conductivity than pure water, prevents corrosion of electrodes). Steel cell body. Asbestos or polymer diaphragm separates anode and cathode compartments (prevents H₂ and O₂ mixing).
