# Multi-die drawing

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Rod passes through a series of tungsten carbide or diamond drawing dies of decreasing bore diameter. Each die reduces cross-section by 15-25%. Lubrication: soap solution or oil-based drawing compound. A 12-die machine draws 8 mm rod to 1.6 mm wire at ~30 m/s.
