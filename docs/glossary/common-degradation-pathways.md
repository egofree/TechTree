# Common degradation pathways

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: health

Chemical and physical processes that reduce drug potency and safety over time: hydrolysis (aspirin → salicylic acid), oxidation (epinephrine browns in air), photolysis (light-sensitive drugs), and microbial contamination of aqueous preparations. Proper storage (cool, dark, dry) slows degradation.
