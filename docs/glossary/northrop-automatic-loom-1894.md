# Northrop automatic loom (1894)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Automatically replaced empty bobbins in the shuttle without stopping the loom. Eliminated the need for a skilled weaver to stop and reload. One weaver could tend 12-16 Northrop looms. The basis of 20th-century weaving. Required precision-manufactured bobbins and shuttle boxes.
