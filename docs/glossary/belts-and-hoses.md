# Belts and hoses

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Drive belts (V-belts, timing belts) use tension members (polyester or fiberglass cord) embedded in rubber compound. Hoses are layered constructions — inner tube (oil-resistant compound, NBR or CR), reinforcement (textile braid, spiral wire, or aramid), outer cover (weather-resistant CR or EPDM). Working pressure: 1-30 MPa depending on reinforcement.
