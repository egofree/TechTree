# Collating and folding

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Fold printed sheets into gatherings (signatures — typically 4, 8, or 16 pages per gathering). A full sheet printed on both sides, folded once, makes a folio (4 pages). Folded again makes a quarto (8 pages). Folded again makes an octavo (16 pages). Collate gatherings in correct order.
