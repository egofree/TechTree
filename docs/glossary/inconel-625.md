# Inconel 625

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Inconel 625 (Ni-22Cr-9Mo-3.5Nb) — Solid-solution strengthened (no precipitation hardening). Exceptional corrosion resistance in seawater and acidic environments. Used for chemical processing, marine, nuclear. Tensile strength ~830 MPa. Service temperature to ~980°C for oxidation resistance. See: [Specialty Alloys](../metals/alloys.md)
