# Hydrogen from steam methane reforming (SMR)

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

(dominant route, ~80% of global production): - **Step 1 — Primary reformer**: CH₄ + H₂O → CO + 3H₂ at 700-850°C, 3-4 MPa, over Ni/Al₂O₃ catalyst in vertically suspended tubes inside a gas-fired furnace. Endothermic (ΔH = +206 kJ/mol). The furnace is the largest single equipment item in an ammonia plant. - **Step 2 — Secondary reformer**: Residual CH₄ (5-10% from primary) is reformed with air (which also introduces the N₂ needed for ammonia synthesis).
