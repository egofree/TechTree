# Brass brazing (spelter brazing)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools, metals

Filler = brass alloy (Cu + Zn, typically 60/40, melts ~900°C). Flux = borax (sodium borate) paste. Heat joint to bright red (~950°C). Apply brazing rod — brass melts, flows into joint by capillary action. Strong joint, higher temperature than soldering.
