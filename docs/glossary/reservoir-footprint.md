# Reservoir footprint

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Upper and lower reservoirs inundate land. A 1 million m³ reservoir covering 10 hectares displaces that land from other use. Reservoir banks must be stabilized against erosion from daily water level fluctuations (the upper reservoir cycles through its full drawdown range daily, creating a 5-15 m "bathtub ring" of exposed shoreline susceptible to wave erosion).
