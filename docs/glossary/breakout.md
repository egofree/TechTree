# Breakout

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

The catastrophic failure mode — the solid shell ruptures, releasing liquid steel into the machine. Caused by sticker (shell sticking in mold), thin shell from insufficient mold cooling, or mold level fluctuation. Prevented by mold level control, mold thermal monitoring (detecting hot spots indicating thin shell), and breakout prediction systems using mold thermocouple data. Consequences: production stoppage of 4-12 hours, equipment damage, safety hazard.
