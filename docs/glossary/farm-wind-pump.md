# Farm wind pump

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

(American water-pumping windmill — 1854 onward, but buildable with iron-age technology): - **Rotor**: Multi-bladed (12-24) steel or wooden vanes on 3-8 m diameter wheel. High starting torque (many blades) — essential for starting a loaded pump. Low tip-speed ratio (~1.0) optimized for torque, not speed. - **Pump**: Reciprocating piston pump in borehole or well.
