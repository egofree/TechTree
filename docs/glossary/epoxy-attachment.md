# Epoxy attachment

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Epoxy attachment (more common, lower temperature) — silver-filled epoxy (80% Ag flakes by weight in epoxy resin — provides electrical and thermal conductivity). Dispense epoxy on substrate. Place die (pick-and-place tool — vacuum pickup needle, automated XY positioning). Cure at 150-175°C for 1-2 hours. Epoxy: bisphenol-A + hardener (amine or anhydride), loaded with silver flake 5-20 μm particle size. See: [Semiconductor Packaging & Testing](../chemistry/packaging-testing.md)
