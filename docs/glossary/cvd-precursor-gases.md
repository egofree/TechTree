# CVD precursor gases

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Tungsten hexafluoride (WF₆) for tungsten metallization. Tetraethylorthosilicate (TEOS, Si(OC₂H₅)₄) for oxide deposition. Trimethylaluminum (TMA, Al(CH₃)₃) for Al₂O₃ dielectric — pyrophoric. Tetramethylammonium hydroxide (TMAH) for silicon etching.
