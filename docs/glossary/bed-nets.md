# Bed nets

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Insecticide-treated bed nets (ITNs) reduce malaria transmission by 50-70% in endemic areas. Net treated with pyrethroid insecticide (permethrin or deltamethrin, 20-50 mg/m²). Retreat every 6-12 months (or use long-lasting insecticidal nets, LLINs, rated for 3+ years). Nets must be tucked under the mattress and used consistently.
