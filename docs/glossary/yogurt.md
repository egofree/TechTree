# Yogurt

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Heat milk to 85°C for 30 minutes to pasteurize and denature proteins for a thicker texture. Cool to 42-45°C. Add starter culture (2-3 tablespoons from a previous batch). Incubate 4-8 hours at 40-45°C in a warm spot (near a hearth, in a hay-lined box). The milk thickens as lactic acid bacteria multiply and acidify it. Keeps 1-2 weeks if kept cool.
