# 90/10 cupronickel

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(C70600: 88.5% Cu, 10% Ni, 1.5% Fe, 0.5% Mn): Tensile strength ~320 MPa (annealed), ~440 MPa (cold-worked). Excellent resistance to seawater corrosion, biofouling, and stress corrosion cracking. Used for seawater piping, condenser tubes, heat exchangers, desalination plant tubing. Service life: 20-40 years in flowing seawater. Thermal conductivity: 45 W/m·K (vs. 52 for brass, 390 for pure copper).
