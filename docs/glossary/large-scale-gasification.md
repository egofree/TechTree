# Large-scale gasification

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Fluidized bed gasifiers at 5-50 MWth scale achieve better economics than small downdraft units but require more complex operation. Biomass feed rate: 1-10 tonnes/hour. Gas production: 2000-20,000 m³/hour. Applications: district heating, industrial process heat, and power generation (1-10 MWe via gas engine or gas turbine).
