# Steam bending

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Place green wood strip (1-3 cm thick) inside a sealed wooden steam box with a steam inlet from a boiling water pot. Steam for 1-2 hours per cm of thickness. Pull the softened strip out, bend it around a wooden form, clamp it in place, and let it dry for 24-48 hours. The lignin in the wood resets around the new shape.
