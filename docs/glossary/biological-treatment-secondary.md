# Biological treatment (secondary)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Trickling filter — wastewater sprinkled over bed of rocks (1-3 m deep). Biofilm on rocks consumes organic matter. Or activated sludge — aerate wastewater in tank (blow air through diffusers). Microorganisms consume organics. Settle, return some sludge to tank, discharge clear effluent.
