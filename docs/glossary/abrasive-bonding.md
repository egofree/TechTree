# Abrasive bonding

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Shellac is used as a binder for abrasive grains on grinding wheels and sandpaper. The shellac holds abrasive particles (aluminum oxide, silicon carbide) firmly to the backing material while being heat-resistant enough for moderate grinding operations. Shellac-bonded grinding wheels are still manufactured for specific applications requiring cool cutting.
