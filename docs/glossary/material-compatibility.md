# Material compatibility

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

316L stainless steel is standard for most gases. Hastelloy C-276 (nickel-molybdenum-chromium alloy) for halogen gases (Cl₂, HCl, HF, HBr) that cause pitting corrosion in stainless steel. Monel (nickel-copper alloy) for fluorine gas. PTFE or PFA (perfluoroalkoxy) gaskets and diaphragms for corrosive service. Nickel or nickel-plated components for silane service (silane can react with copper).
