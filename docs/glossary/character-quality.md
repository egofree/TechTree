# Character (quality)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Bounding pulse (strong, full) in fever, hyperthyroidism, anxiety. Thready pulse (weak, difficult to palpate) in shock, severe blood loss, heart failure. Wiry pulse (tense, narrow) in hypertension. Asymmetrical pulses between arms suggest aortic dissection (emergency).
