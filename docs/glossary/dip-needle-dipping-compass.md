# Dip needle (dipping compass)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Freely pivoting magnetic needle that measures inclination (vertical angle) of Earth's magnetic field. Anomalies (deviations from expected inclination) indicate subsurface magnetic bodies — magnetite ore bodies, buried iron objects. Survey on a grid (10-50 m spacing). Contour the anomaly values. Positive anomaly = magnetic mass below.
