# Mass spectrometry

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

For the most critical purity verification (especially SiH₄), quadrupole mass spectrometry detects impurities at ppb (parts per billion) levels. Can distinguish SiH₄ (mass 32) from O₂ (also mass 32) by fragmentation pattern. Required for semiconductor-grade qualification of silane batches.
