# Digitization for redundancy

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Photograph or scan critical documents at 300-600 DPI minimum for text, 600-1200 DPI for illustrations and technical drawings. Store in lossless formats (TIFF for archival masters, PDF/A for access copies). Maintain at least two geographically separate copies. Digitization does not replace physical preservation — digital media degrade (bit rot, format obsolescence) — but provides access and insurance against catastrophic physical loss.
