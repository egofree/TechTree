# Gas production

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: machine-tools

Generation of shielding gases (argon, CO₂, or argon-CO₂ mixtures) for welding processes. Gas production involves air separation (cryogenic distillation for argon), combustion or fermentation (for CO₂), and gas mixing equipment. Proper shielding gas prevents weld oxidation and porosity.
