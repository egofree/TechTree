# High-voltage laser discharge (15-30 kV)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: vlsi-scaling

Excimer laser discharge circuits store lethal energy in capacitor banks. Interlock the laser power supply so capacitors discharge through a bleeder resistor when panels are opened. Wait ≥5 × the RC time constant before servicing. Post "DANGER — HIGH VOLTAGE" signage. Only trained, authorized personnel may service laser power supplies.
