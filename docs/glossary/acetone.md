# Acetone

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

For non-crosslinked resist (resist that has not been severely hard-baked or plasma-damaged). Soak 5-10 min at room temperature or 50°C. Acetone swells and dissolves the resin. Follow with IPA rinse (acetone leaves residue) and N₂ dry. Cannot remove hard-baked or plasma-crosslinked resist.
