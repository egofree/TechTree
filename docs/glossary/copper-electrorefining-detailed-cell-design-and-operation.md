# Copper electrorefining — detailed cell design and operation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: chemistry

Process where impure copper anodes dissolve electrolytically in CuSO₄-H₂SO₄ electrolyte, and pure copper deposits on starter cathodes at 99.99% purity. Current density 200-300 A/m², cell voltage 0.3V, energy consumption 250-350 kWh/tonne. Precious metals (Ag, Au, Pt) collect in anode slimes for recovery.
