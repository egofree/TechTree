# Refining — Mond carbonyl process

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Ni + 4CO → Ni(CO)₄ at 50-60°C, atmospheric pressure. The volatile nickel carbonyl gas is distilled and decomposed at 200°C on nickel pellets (decomposition: Ni(CO)₄ → Ni + 4CO). CO is recycled. Produces 99.97% pure nickel. Highly toxic process — Ni(CO)₄ is one of the most poisonous industrial gases (TLV 0.001 ppm).
