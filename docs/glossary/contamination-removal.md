# Contamination removal

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass

Ceramics, stones, and metals in cullet cause defects (stones, cords) in the melt. Hand-sort cullet on a picking belt before crushing. Pass crushed cullet over a magnetic separator to remove iron and steel contaminants. Density separation removes ceramics and stones (glass SG ~2.5, ceramics 2.0-2.8, metals 7-8).
