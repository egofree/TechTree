# Displacement calculation

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Δ = L × B × D × Cb × ρ, where L = waterline length, B = beam, D = draft (all in meters), Cb = block coefficient (0.50-0.85; lower for fine-lined vessels, higher for full-bodied cargo ships), and ρ = 1025 kg/m³ for seawater. A 30 m × 7 m × 3 m vessel with Cb 0.65 displaces 30 × 7 × 3 × 0.65 × 1025 = 420,225 kg ≈ 420 tonnes.
