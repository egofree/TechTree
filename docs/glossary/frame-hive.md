# Frame hive

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

(advanced — Langstroth design): Stackable rectangular boxes (supers) with precisely spaced removable frames. Each frame holds a sheet of wax or plastic foundation embossed with hexagonal cell pattern (5.2-5.4 mm cell diameter) that guides comb construction. Critical dimension: 7-9 mm bee space between frames and walls — bees respect this gap and do not glue surfaces together with propolis or comb.
