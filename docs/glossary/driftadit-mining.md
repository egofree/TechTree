# Drift/adit mining

> **Type**: process | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Drift/adit mining (horizontal entry from hillside) — - Drive horizontal tunnel into hillside at or slightly above valley floor level. Gravity drainage — no pumping needed. Most economical method for hillslope deposits. Minimum 1.5 m wide × 2 m tall. Timber supports every 1-2 m in unstable ground. See: [Mining Extraction](../mining/extraction.md)
