# Blade profile grinding

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

The airfoil section of each blade must match the designed aerodynamic profile within ±0.01 mm over its entire length. Deviations cause flow separation, reduced efficiency, and local stress concentrations. Profile grinding uses formed grinding wheels or continuous-path CNC grinding. Surface finish requirement: Ra 0.4-1.6 μm. After grinding, each blade is measured on a coordinate measuring machine (CMM) or optical comparator against the master profile.
