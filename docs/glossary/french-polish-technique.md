# French polish technique

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

The premier wood finishing technique. Equipment: a "rubber" (pad) made from a core of clean cotton wadding wrapped in a clean, lint-free cloth (linen or cotton). Charge the rubber with 2 lb cut shellac solution and a few drops of linseed oil (lubricant — allows the rubber to glide smoothly). Apply with rapid, sweeping strokes in a figure-8 pattern across the wood surface. Each pass deposits a microscopically thin film.
