# Edible Plants

> **Node ID**: plants.edible-plants
> **Domain**: [Plants & Botanical Resources](./)
> **Timeline**: Years 0-10
> **Outputs**: edible_plants, food_crops, vegetables, fruits, seeds

### Overview

Foraging and cultivation of plant species for human consumption including vegetables, fruits, seeds, roots, and herbs. Provides caloric intake and essential nutrients from wild and domesticated sources. Wild edible plants provide immediate food security before agriculture is established.
