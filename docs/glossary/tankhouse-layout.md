# Tankhouse layout

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Hundreds to thousands of tanks arranged in rows. Electrical connection: tanks in series, anodes and cathodes within each tank in parallel. Total tankhouse voltage: 100-200V DC at 10,000-30,000 A. Current per cathode: 200-350 A/m² × cathode area (~0.8-1 m²) = 160-350 A per cathode.
