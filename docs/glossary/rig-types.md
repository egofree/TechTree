# Rig types

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Single mast (simplest, 5-15 m boats), ketch (two masts, mizzen shorter and stepped aft of rudder), schooner (two+ masts, foremast equal or shorter than main). Sail area to displacement ratio ~15-25 m²/tonne for reasonable speed (5-8 knots).
