# Chimney design

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

Draft pressure (Pa) ≈ 0.0342 × chimney height (m) × (ambient temperature K⁻¹ − flue gas temperature K⁻¹). Practical rule: 3-5 m chimney height for temperatures up to 1000°C; 5-8 m for 1200°C+. Chimney cross-section: 1/15 to 1/20 of kiln floor area. Round chimneys draft more evenly than square.
