# Rope maintenance

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Keep natural fiber rope dry when not in use. Rot and mildew weaken rope rapidly in damp storage. Coil properly (following the natural lay of the rope) to prevent kinks. Inspect ropes before critical use: look for chafed spots, broken strands, or discoloration indicating rot. Retire suspect rope to non-critical tasks.
