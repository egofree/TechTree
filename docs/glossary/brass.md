# Brass

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools, metals

(copper-zinc alloy, typically 60-70% Cu, 30-40% Zn): Melts at ~900-940°C. Zinc boils at 907°C — generates zinc vapor (toxic, causes "brass founder's ague"). Requires forced ventilation. Used for valves, fittings, decorative hardware.
