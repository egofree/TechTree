# Primary use

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy, polymers

Thermoset molding (phenolic, epoxy, melamine — heat + pressure cures crosslinks). Also used for thermoplastic sheet (heated sheet compressed between mold halves) and rubber vulcanization. Products: electrical insulators, appliance handles, composite panels, brake pads, large flat components, melamine dinnerware, rubber O-rings and gaskets.
