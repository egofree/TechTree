# Air filtration

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

HEPA filters (99.97% efficient at 0.3 μm) in ceiling. Laminar flow (air flows in parallel streamlines from ceiling to floor perforations). 20-60 air changes per hour for Class 100. ULPA filters (99.999% at 0.12 μm) for Class 10 and below.
