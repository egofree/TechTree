# Water temperature and quality

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Pumped storage operation mixes water between reservoirs, potentially transferring warm surface water to deeper, cooler zones. Stratification patterns in the lower reservoir may be disrupted, affecting aquatic ecosystems. In tropical or temperate climates, the mixing can increase dissolved oxygen in deeper water (beneficial) or redistribute nutrients and sediment (potentially harmful).
