# Mass flow controllers (MFCs)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, gas-handling

For process gas delivery in semiconductor and chemical manufacturing, the gas flow rate must be precisely controlled. An MFC measures the mass flow rate (not volume flow rate, which varies with temperature and pressure) using a thermal sensor. A portion of the gas stream passes through a capillary tube with a heated element and two temperature sensors upstream and downstream.
