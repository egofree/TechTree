# Direct chill (DC) casting

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Direct chill (DC) casting (for wrought products — ingots for rolling/extrusion) — - Semi-continuous vertical casting. Molten Al flows into a water-cooled mold with a moving bottom block. As metal solidifies, the bottom block descends. - Produces rectangular ingots (for rolling to sheet/plate) or cylindrical billets (for extrusion). Ingot size: up to 9 m long, 0.5 × 1.5 m cross-section. Billet diameter: 150-800 mm. - Water spray directly onto the solidifying shell provides intense cooling (solidification rate 10-100 mm/...
