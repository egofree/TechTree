# Magnesium silicide route

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Magnesium silicide route (laboratory) — Mg₂Si + 4HCl → 2MgCl₂ + SiH₄. Produces impure silane with phosphine (PH₃) and arsine (AsH₃) contaminants — dangerous and unsuitable for semiconductor-grade material. Only for small-scale/non-electronic applications. See: [Hydrogen & Silane Production](../chemistry/hydrogen-silane.md)
