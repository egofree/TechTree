# SEM Tech membrane

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Homogeneous ion exchange membrane formed from pulverized commercial pre-functionalized ion exchange resin particles dispersed in PVC or CPVC matrix. Manufactured without post-functionalization. Cost less than $1/sq ft vs $100-400/sq ft for commercial membranes. Particle size <200μm, resin loading 10-70% by volume.
