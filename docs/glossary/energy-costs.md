# Energy costs

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Distillation is the most energy-intensive separation method in the chemical industry, accounting for 40-60% of a typical plant's energy consumption. Reboiler duty is the dominant cost. For a typical binary column separating 10 tonnes/hour of feed with reflux ratio 3:1, reboiler steam consumption is approximately 15-25 tonnes/hour.
