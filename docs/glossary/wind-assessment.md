# Wind assessment

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Measure wind speed distribution over months (anemometer — rotating cups driving counter or generator). Average wind speed >5 m/s at hub height for economic viability. Power proportional to wind speed cubed: P = ½ρAv³Cp, where ρ = air density, A = swept area, Cp = power coefficient (max 0.593, Betz limit; practical 0.3-0.45).
