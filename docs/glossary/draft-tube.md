# Draft tube

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Conical tube extending from the runner exit downward to the tailrace. Its purpose is twofold: recover kinetic energy from the exiting water (acts as a diffuser, slowing the water and converting velocity to pressure), and allow the runner to be set above tailwater level for maintenance access while maintaining a pressure head at the runner exit. Draft tube must be carefully designed — poorly shaped tubes cause flow separation and energy loss.
