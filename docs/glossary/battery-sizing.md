# Battery sizing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

(lead-acid, see Energy Storage): - **Capacity**: C = P × t / V, where P = load (W), t = backup time (hours), V = system voltage. Example: 500W load, 4-hour backup, 48V system: C = 500 × 4 / 48 = 41.7 Ah. Add 50% margin for depth-of- discharge limit: 62.5 Ah bank. - **Charging current**: C/10 rate (capacity ÷ 10 hours) for maximum battery life. For 62.5 Ah bank: charge at ~6.25A, 48V = 300W charger needed. Charge time: ~12-14 hours from fully discharged.
