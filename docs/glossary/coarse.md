# Coarse

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Coarse (31-40 μm) — longwool breeds (Lincoln, Romney). Long staple (15-30 cm), strong, lustrous. Used for carpets, upholstery, heavy outerwear. Low crimp (1-3 waves per cm). Spinning count: 36s-48s. See: [Fiber Preparation](../textiles/fibers.md)
