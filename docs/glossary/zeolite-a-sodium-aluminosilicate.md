# Zeolite A (sodium aluminosilicate)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Primary phosphate replacement. Ion-exchange builder — swaps Na⁺ for Ca²⁺ in its crystal structure. Produced from sodium silicate + sodium aluminate. Insoluble — suspended in wash water, rinsed away. Effective but slower than STPP. Often combined with polycarboxylate co-builder (polyacrylic acid) for performance matching STPP.
