# Silicone vacuum oil

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Polydimethylsiloxane (PDMS) — from silicon + methyl chloride chemistry. Very low vapor pressure (~10⁻⁶ Pa at 25°C). Chemically inert. For diffusion pumps (see Vacuum & Optics) — achieves ultimate vacuum ~10⁻⁶ to 10⁻⁸ Pa.
