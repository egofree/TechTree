# Liquid nitrogen (LIN)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Stored at -196°C, density 0.81 g/mL. Expansion ratio 1:694 (liquid to gas). Used for inerting, food freezing, shrink-fitting metal parts, cryogenic grinding of plastics and spices, and as a coolant for superconducting magnets and vacuum cold traps. Transportation: vacuum-insulated road tankers (10,000-20,000 L capacity) or portable dewars (25-500 L). Fill losses: 5-15% of transferred volume due to flash evaporation when warm equipment contacts cryogenic liquid.
