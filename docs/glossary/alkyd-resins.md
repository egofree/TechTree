# Alkyd resins

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Polyester modified with fatty acids — the workhorse of architectural paints. Synthesized from phthalic anhydride, glycerol/pentaerythritol, and drying oil fatty acids at 230-250°C. Air-drying (oxidative cross-linking). Good adhesion, gloss, and weatherability. Solvent-borne alkyds being replaced by water-borne (emulsion) versions due to VOC regulations.
