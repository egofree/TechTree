# Foaming

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Some systems (amine gas treating, caustic washing, fermentation products) generate stable foam that traps vapor and liquid, reducing tray efficiency and capacity. Antifoam agents (silicone oil or polyglycol, 5-50 ppm) injected continuously or as needed. Mechanical foam breakers (centrifugal or impact type) in the vapor space above the top tray.
