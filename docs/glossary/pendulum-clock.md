# Pendulum clock

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement

(Machine Tools): - **Principle**: Pendulum period T = 2π√(L/g), where L = pendulum length, g = gravitational acceleration. A 1-meter pendulum has period ~2.000 seconds (each swing = 1 second). Period is independent of amplitude (for small angles <5°). - **Escapement**: Anchor escapement or dead-beat escapement converts continuous pendulum swing into discrete tick-tock while maintaining pendulum oscillation.
