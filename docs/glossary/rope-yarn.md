# Rope yarn

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

For rope-making, spin coarse singles (Ne 2-5, or 200-500 tex) from hemp, manila, or sisal. Twist direction: Z-twist for the singles. Ply multiple singles into a strand (S-twist). Then lay multiple strands into the final rope (Z-twist). The alternating twist directions (Z-S-Z) balance the rope and prevent it from kinking or unlaying under load.
