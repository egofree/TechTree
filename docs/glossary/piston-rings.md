# Piston rings

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Cast iron split rings (expand against cylinder wall). Compression rings (top 1-2): rectangular or barrel-faced cross-section, 2-3 mm thick, gap 0.3-0.5 mm when installed. Seal combustion pressure. Oil control ring (bottom): slotted, scrapes excess oil off cylinder wall, vents oil back to crankcase through holes in piston.
