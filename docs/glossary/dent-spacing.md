# Dent spacing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Measured in dents per inch (dpi) or dents per 10 cm. Common sizes: 8, 10, 12, 15, 20 dpi. Finer cloth = higher dpi. A 12 dpi reed with 1 warp thread per dent produces 12 ends per inch (epi). Two threads per dent → 24 epi (used for fine cloth).
