# Preservation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health, polymers

Add ammonia (NH₃) to 0.5-0.7% concentration within hours of tapping. Ammonia raises pH to 10-11, inhibiting bacterial growth and preventing premature coagulation. Secondary preservatives (zinc diethyldithiocarbamate, ZDEC, at 0.02-0.05%) provide additional protection for long-term storage.
