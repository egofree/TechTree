# Gauge blocks (Johansson blocks)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

Precision-ground rectangular blocks of hardened steel (or tungsten carbide for maximum stability). Manufacturing sequence: rough-grind to near dimension, heat-treat to 64-68 HRC, finish-grind on surface grinder, then hand-lap to final size. Flatness: ±0.05 μm across the measuring face. Surface finish: 0.025 μm Ra (mirror-like).
