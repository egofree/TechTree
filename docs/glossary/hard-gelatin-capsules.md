# Hard gelatin capsules

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Two-piece cylindrical shells (cap and body) made from gelatin, water, and plasticizer. Fill body with powdered drug mixture by hand or semi-automatic machine. Cap slips over body to seal. Available in sizes 000 (largest, ~1.4 mL) to 5 (smallest, ~0.13 mL). For doses too large for one capsule, divide into multiple capsules.
