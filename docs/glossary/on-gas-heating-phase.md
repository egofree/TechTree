# On-gas (heating) phase

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(20-45 minutes): Blast furnace gas (cleaned) is burned with combustion air in the combustion chamber. Hot combustion products (~1200-1400°C) pass downward through the checker bricks, heating them. Exhaust exits through the flue at the bottom at ~200-400°C.
