# Type 2205

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(22Cr-5Ni-3Mo-0.15N): Roughly twice the yield strength of austenitic grades (450 MPa vs. 205 MPa annealed). Excellent stress corrosion cracking resistance and pitting resistance (PREN > 34). Used in offshore, chemical tankers, heat exchangers. Nitrogen acts as a potent austenite stabilizer and strengthens both phases.
