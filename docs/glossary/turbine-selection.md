# Turbine selection

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

At 50 m head and 2 MW, the specific speed Ns is roughly 150-200 for an optimal Francis runner. This dictates a runner diameter of approximately 1 m and a rotational speed near 500 RPM (for 50 Hz generation, this would couple to a 12-pole generator or use a speed-increasing gearbox to reach 750 or 1000 RPM).
