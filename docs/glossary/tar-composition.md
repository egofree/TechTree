# Tar composition

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Wood gasifier tar is a complex mixture of >100 organic compounds. Major components: benzene (5-15%), toluene (3-8%), naphthalene (2-10%), phenol (1-5%), cresols (1-3%), polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons (PAHs: pyrene, anthracene, fluorene, 0.1-1%), and oxygenated aromatics (guaiacol, syringol from lignin depolymerization, 1-5%).
