# TETA hardener formulation

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Triethylenetetramine (TETA) at 10-14 phr (parts per hundred resin, by weight — calculated from amine hydrogen equivalent weight vs. epoxy equivalent weight). Pot life at 25°C: 30-60 minutes (working time before viscosity increases unworkably). Cure schedule: 24 hours at 25°C (room temperature cure, reaches ~60-70% of ultimate strength) or 2 hours at 80°C (elevated temperature cure, reaches >95% of ultimate strength).
