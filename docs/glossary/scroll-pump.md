# Scroll pump

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Scroll pump (oil-free, medium vacuum) — Principle**: Two interleaving spiral scrolls — one fixed, one orbiting. The orbiting motion creates crescent-shaped gas pockets that progressively decrease in volume, compressing gas from inlet to outlet. No sliding contacts, no oil seal. - **Performance**: Ultimate vacuum ~10⁻² mbar. Pumping speed 5-60 m³/h.
