# DO-41 (axial lead)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Glass-sealed cylindrical package for small-signal and rectifier diodes. Glass body (2-4 mm diameter, 4-6 mm long) with axial metal leads (copper or steel, 0.5-0.8 mm diameter). Glass-to-metal seal hermetically seals the die. Rated to 500V-1000V, 1A average forward current. The simplest and cheapest diode package. Color band on cathode end indicates polarity.
