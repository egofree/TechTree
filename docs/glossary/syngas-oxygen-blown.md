# Syngas (oxygen-blown)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

When pure oxygen replaces air as the gasifying agent, nitrogen dilution is eliminated. Composition: CO 35-45%, H₂ 25-35%, CO₂ 15-25%, CH₄ 0-5%, small amounts of H₂O and trace contaminants. Calorific value 10-15 MJ/Nm³, comparable to low-quality natural gas.
