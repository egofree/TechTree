# Combustible gas detection

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: gas-handling

Methods and instruments for detecting flammable gas concentrations (H₂, CH₄, CO) in air, including catalytic bead sensors (oxidize gas on a heated filament), infrared sensors, and flame ionization detectors. Alarm thresholds typically set at 10% and 25% of the lower explosive limit (LEL).
