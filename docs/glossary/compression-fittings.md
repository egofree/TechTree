# Compression fittings

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

(Swagelok-type): Three-piece design: body, front ferrule (stainless or brass), back ferrule. Tighten nut → ferrules compress onto tube outer surface, creating a metal-to-metal seal. Removable and re-sealable. Tube must be fully inserted and nut tightened to the specified number of turns past finger-tight (typically 1-1/4 turns for new fittings). Do not mix ferrules from different manufacturers.
