# Ester oils

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Diesters and polyol esters — synthesized from organic acids and alcohols. Excellent lubricity (polar ester groups adsorb strongly to metal surfaces), good thermal stability (200-250°C continuous), biodegradable. Used in: jet engine oils (MIL-PRF-23699), compressor oils, biodegradable hydraulic fluids (forestry, marine), two-stroke engine oils.
