# Hot-rolled strip

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Slab reheated to 1200-1300°C, rolled in a continuous hot strip mill (roughing stands + 5-7 finishing stands) to 1.5-12 mm thickness at 850-950°C exit temperature. Coil weight: 20-30 tonnes. The dominant steel product by volume — used for automotive, construction, pipe, tube, and as feedstock for cold rolling.
