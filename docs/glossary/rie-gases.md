# RIE gases

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: vlsi-scaling

SF₆ (GWP 23,900× CO₂), CF₄ (GWP 6,630× CO₂), and NF₃ (GWP 17,200× CO₂) are among the most potent greenhouse gases. Point-of-use abatement (thermal or plasma destruct units with >99 % DRE) is mandatory on all RIE exhaust lines. Plasma byproducts include HF and COF₂ — downstream wet scrubbing required.
