# Chlorination

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: health

Water disinfection method using chlorine gas (Cl₂), sodium hypochlorite (bleach), or calcium hypochlorite to inactivate pathogens. Effective residual concentration of 0.2-0.5 mg/L free chlorine; contact time of 30+ minutes required. Most cost-effective large-scale water treatment.
