# S-glass

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: glass, polymers

(structural grade, higher strength): 65% SiO₂, 25% Al₂O₃, 10% MgO. Tensile strength 40-50% higher than E-glass, but requires higher melting temperature (~1500°C) and more energy to produce. Used in aerospace and ballistic armor.
