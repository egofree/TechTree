# Coal tar pitch

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

(binder): - Byproduct of coke oven gas condensation in coal carbonization. Black, brittle, thermoplastic solid at room temperature, softens at ~80-120°C, melts at ~120-150°C. - **Function**: Binds the coke particles together into a coherent mass during forming, then carbonizes during baking to form a solid carbon matrix. The pitch must wet the coke surface adequately and provide sufficient green strength after forming. - **Types**: Soft pitch (low softening point, 60-80°C) gives better mixing...
