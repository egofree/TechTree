# Centrifugal flyball governor

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

(Watt): Two rotating balls on hinged arms spin with engine output shaft. As speed increases, balls swing outward (centrifugal force), arms raise a sleeve that closes the steam throttle valve slightly. As speed decreases, balls drop, valve opens. Proportional control — simple, reliable, self-contained.
