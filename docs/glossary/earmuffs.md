# Earmuffs

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Hard plastic cups with foam-filled cushions that seal around the ear. NRR: 15-30 dB. More consistent fit than earplugs. Can be combined with earplugs for 5-10 dB additional protection (not additive: 33 dB plugs + 25 dB muffs ≈ 38 dB, not 58 dB).
