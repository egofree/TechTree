# Bar rolling

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Bar rolling (round, square, hexagonal, flat) — - Billets 100-150 mm square → 10-50 mm rounds in 10-18 passes through a continuous bar mill. - Two types: horizontal-vertical stands (alternate orientation) or no-twist mills (angled stands that turn the bar without requiring twist).
