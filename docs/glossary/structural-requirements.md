# Structural requirements

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Load-bearing masonry walls (stone or brick, minimum 300 mm thick). Reinforced concrete or vaulted ceiling for fire resistance. Iron or steel framing for multi-story stacks. Floor loading: books average ~1 kg each; full shelving runs 200-300 kg per linear meter — design floors for 5-7 kN/m² minimum.
