# Retting tank construction

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Line a pit (2 × 3 × 1 m deep) with clay puddle or wooden planks to hold water. Fill with water, add flax/hemp bundles weighted down with stones. A tank this size rets ~200 kg of stalk per batch. Change water between batches — spent retting water is foul-smelling and depleted of oxygen. Stagnant retting water generates hydrogen sulfide (H₂S, rotten egg gas — toxic at high concentrations; ventilate or work upwind).
