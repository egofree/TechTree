# Retting water management

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Change retting water every 2-3 days for consistent quality. Spent retting water contains organic acids (butyric acid, acetic acid), dissolved pectin, and anaerobic bacteria. It has a strong, unpleasant odor and is polluting to waterways. In a retting tank operation, drain the spent water to a settling pond before releasing it.
