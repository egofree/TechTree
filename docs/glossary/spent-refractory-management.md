# Spent refractory management

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

When furnace linings are replaced, the spent refractory material (typically 60-90% unchanged, only the hot-face 10-40% being degraded) can be partially recycled. Chrome-bearing refractories (containing Cr⁶⁺ after service) require specialized disposal as hazardous waste in many jurisdictions. MgO-C bricks can be reclaimed: the carbon is burned off, the MgO grains are reprocessed into new refractory aggregates.
