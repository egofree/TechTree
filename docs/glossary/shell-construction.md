# Shell construction

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Column shells are fabricated from rolled steel plate (carbon steel for non-corrosive service, 304L or 316L stainless steel for corrosive chemicals). Plate thickness: 6-25 mm depending on diameter and design pressure. Shells with diameter >1 m typically require post-weld heat treatment to relieve residual stresses.
