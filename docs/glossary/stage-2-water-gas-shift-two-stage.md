# Stage 2: Water-gas shift (two-stage)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

- Converts CO to additional H₂: CO + H₂O → CO₂ + H₂ (mildly exothermic, ΔH = -41 kJ/mol) - **High-temperature shift (HTS)**: 350-450°C, Fe₂O₃/Cr₂O₃ catalyst. Converts bulk of CO. Exit: ~3% CO remaining. Iron-chrome catalyst is robust and tolerant of impurities. - **Low-temperature shift (LTS)**: 200-250°C, Cu/ZnO/Al₂O₃ catalyst. Converts most remaining CO. Exit: ~0.2-0.3% CO.
