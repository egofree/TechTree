# Alkaline etching

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Alkaline etching (alternative, safer) — - Sodium hydroxide (NaOH) solution, 10-20% by weight, heated to 60-80°C. Etch rate: 5-15 μm/min. The alkaline etch is anisotropic (etches different crystal planes at different rates: {100} planes etch ~30× faster than {111} planes), producing a textured surface. - For <100> oriented silicon, the alkaline etch reveals pyramidal textures (4-10 μm feature size) across the entire surface.
