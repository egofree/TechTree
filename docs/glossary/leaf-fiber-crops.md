# Leaf fiber crops

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

Leaf fiber crops (sisal, abacá) — Perennial plantations — plant once, harvest for 7-15 years. Sisal produces ~25 leaves/year after maturity; each leaf yields 2-3% dry fiber by weight. See: [Fiber Plants](../plants/fiber-plants.md)
