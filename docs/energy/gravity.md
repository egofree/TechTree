### Water &amp; Wind Power

**Water wheel construction (undershot — simplest)**:
- **Site requirements**: Stream or river with reliable flow, ~0.5 m/s+ water velocity. Can be enhanced with dam and sluice to concentrate flow.
- **Wheel construction**: Wooden paddles (oak, 5-10 cm thick) mounted on wooden axle (30-50 cm diameter, 2-4 m long). Wheel diameter 2-4 m, width 0.5-1.5 m. Paddles dip into flowing water, which pushes them.
- **Power output**: 1-5 HP depending on water flow and wheel diameter. Sufficient for grain grinding, bellows operation, wood sawing.
- **Bearing lubrication**: Tallow or animal fat on wooden bearing surfaces. Water lubrication works for some designs.
