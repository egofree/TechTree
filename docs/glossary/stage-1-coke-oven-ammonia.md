# Stage 1: Coke-oven ammonia

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Stage 1: Coke-oven ammonia (Years 15-25) — Coal destructive distillation (coke ovens at 1000-1200°C) produces coke oven gas containing ~1-2% NH₃ by volume. Scrub with water or dilute H₂SO₄ to recover ammonium sulfate ((NH₄)₂SO₄) or ammonia liquor. - A medium coke plant (1,000 tonnes coal/day) yields ~5-10 tonnes NH₃/day. Enough for early Solvay plants and limited fertilizer production. - This is pre-Haber-Bosch ammonia — limited by coal supply but requires no high-pressure equipment.
