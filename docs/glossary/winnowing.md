# Winnowing

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

Separate grain from chaff using air. Toss threshed grain mixture into a light breeze from a basket or flat tray. Chaff blows away; heavy grain falls straight down. Repeat 3-5 times until grain is clean. Requires a steady breeze (2-5 m/s). Winnowing trays are wide, shallow baskets ~60-80 cm diameter.
