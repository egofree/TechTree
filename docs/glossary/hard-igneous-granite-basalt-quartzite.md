# Hard igneous (granite, basalt, quartzite)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Powder factor 1.0-2.0 kg/m³. Requires closely spaced, deep holes. Black powder is less effective than dynamite in hard rock because it lacks the shattering (detonation) force needed to break tough, crystalline rock. Expect 1-3 m³ per kg. Consider fire-setting to pre-weaken the rock before blasting.
