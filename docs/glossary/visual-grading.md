# Visual grading

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Spread a thin layer of fibers on a dark cloth. Assess: uniformity (even color and crimp), vegetable matter content (VM — seed heads, burrs, straw), staple length, and strength (break test: pull staple between hands — should not break easily). Grade affects price by 2-5× from coarse to fine.
