# Mechanical

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, polymers

Hard and rigid at room temperature (Shore D ~60-80). Tensile strength ~20-30 MPa. Elongation at break ~500% when warm (above transition temperature), ~3-8% at room temperature (brittle). Density ~0.97-1.06 g/cm³ (slightly less than water — gutta-percha floats). Impact strength: low — cracks if struck sharply at room temperature, but remarkably tough when warm (above 70°C).
