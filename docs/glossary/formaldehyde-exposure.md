# Formaldehyde exposure

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

OSHA PEL (permissible exposure limit) 0.75 ppm 8-hour TWA (time-weighted average). STEL (short-term exposure limit) 2 ppm for 15-minute exposure. IARC Group 1 carcinogen (nasopharyngeal cancer). Immediately detectable odor at 0.5-1.0 ppm (warning property — if you can smell it, you're near or above the TWA). Monitor with colorimetric detector tubes or electrochemical sensors.
