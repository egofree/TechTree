# Combustor

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Annular or can-annular design. Fuel injected as atomized spray into compressed air stream. Continuous combustion (no intermittent explosions like reciprocating engines). Combustion temperature 1200-1500°C. Turbine inlet temperature (TIT) limited by blade materials — nickel superalloys with air cooling allow TIT up to 1500-1700°C in advanced engines.
