Mining Engineering & Extractive Metallurgy

Surface and underground mining methods, rock breaking (hand tools, fire-setting, black powder blasting), mine ventilation, water pumping (manual → water wheel → Newcomen engine), hoisting and transport, and ore dressing (crushing, gravity separation, magnetic separation). Ore access is the hard constraint on every metallurgical process.

Capabilities

  • Mining Extraction

    Surface and underground mining methods for extracting ore from deposits.

  • Ore Processing

    Ore crushing, grinding, gravity separation, and concentration to produce marketable ore products.

  • Prospecting & Surveying

    Geological survey, ore body identification, and deposit evaluation techniques.
