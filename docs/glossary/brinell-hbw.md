# Brinell (HBW)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

10 mm tungsten carbide ball pressed into the surface at 3000 kgf (for steel). Measure the indentation diameter with a low-power microscope. HBW = load / indentation area. Good for castings and forgings (large indentation averages out local variations). Range: 50-600 HBW.
