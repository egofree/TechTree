# Sterilization

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Critical for pure-culture fermentations (contaminants compete for substrate and may produce toxins). Methods: (1) **Heat** — steam at 121°C for 15-30 minutes (batch sterilization of medium in the vessel). (2) **Continuous HTST** — 140°C for 5-10 seconds in a heat exchanger, then flash-cooled. More energy-efficient for large volumes. (3) **Filter sterilization** — 0.2 µm membrane filters for heat-sensitive components (vitamins, some growth factors).
