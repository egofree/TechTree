# Organometallic sources

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Metal-organic CVD (MOCVD) for compound semiconductors uses liquid or solid organometallic precursors stored in temperature-controlled bubblers. Trimethylgallium (TMGa, (CH₃)₃Ga) for GaAs and GaN. Trimethylaluminum (TMAl, (CH₃)₃Al) for AlGaAs and AlN. Trimethylindium (TMIn, (CH₃)₃In) for InGaAsP. MOCVD growth of GaN for LEDs: TMGa + NH₃ at 1000-1100°C on sapphire or SiC substrate.
