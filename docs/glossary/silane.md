# Silane

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Electropolished stainless steel (316L or 304L) with PTFE-lined valves. VCR-type face-seal fittings — no elastomer O-rings (silane attacks many organics). All lines purged with N₂ or Ar before introducing silane. Minimum dead legs (pockets where gas can stagnate).
