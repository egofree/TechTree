# Zinc plating

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Corrosion protection for fasteners and small parts. Acid zinc (ZnSO₄ + brighteners) or alkaline zinc (Zn(CN)₂ + NaOH, cyanide-based — being replaced by non-cyanide alkaline zincate baths). Thickness: 5-25 µm. Chromate passivation (clear, blue, yellow, olive drab) adds 50-200+ hours salt spray resistance. Hexavalent chromium passivates are being phased out (RoHS) in favor of trivalent chromium.
