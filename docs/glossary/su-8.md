# SU-8

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

SU-8 (epoxy-based, modern) — Epoxy novolac resin + triarylsulfonium salt photoacid generator (PAG). UV exposure generates acid → PEB causes acid-catalyzed epoxy cross-linking. Highly cross-linked, excellent chemical resistance. Can pattern thick films (5-500 μm) in a single coat. Used for MEMS structures, thick passivation, electroplating molds. Resolution limited by swelling during development (~2-5 μm minimum for thin films). See: [Photoresists, Masks & Lithography](../photolithography/resists-masks.md)
