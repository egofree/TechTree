# Integrity testing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

DOP (dioctyl phthalate) or PAO (polyalphaolefin) challenge test annually. Generate a uniform aerosol upstream of the filter and scan the downstream face with a photometer. Any leak >0.01% of the upstream concentration indicates a defect (pinhole, gasket leak, damaged media). Repair or replace.
