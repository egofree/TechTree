# Hackle (hatchel) comb

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Flat wooden board (15 × 30 cm) with rows of sharp iron teeth (nails or specially drawn wire) driven through and bent to a slight upward angle. Three grades: coarse (teeth 8-10 cm long, 3-5 mm spacing), medium (6-8 cm, 2-3 mm spacing), fine (4-6 cm, 1-2 mm spacing). Draw flax fibers through each grade in sequence.
