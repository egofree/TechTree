# Voltage and current

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Different processes need different supplies: - Chlor-alkali: 200-400V DC at 50-200 kA - Aluminum potline: 800-1200V DC at 100-500 kA - Water electrolysis: 100-400V DC at 2-20 kA - Copper refining: 100-200V DC at 10,000-30,000 A - **Bus bar design**: Massive copper or aluminum bus bars carry current between rectifier and cells. At 200 kA, even 0.001 Ω resistance dissipates 40 kW as heat. Bus bars must be short, thick, and well-cooled. Typical cross-section: 200-1000 mm² per 1000 A.
