# D-series

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(High carbon/high chromium, e.g., D2): 1.5% C, 12% Cr, 1.0% Mo. Massive volume of chromium carbides (Cr₇C₃) provides exceptional wear resistance. Air-hardening — minimal distortion. Used for stamping dies, forming rolls, blanking dies, shear blades. Hardness: HRC 58-62. Limited toughness — not for impact applications.
