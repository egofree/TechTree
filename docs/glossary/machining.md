# Machining

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge, machine-tools

Turn a steel shaft to 25.00 ±0.02 mm diameter × 100 mm length in 30 minutes. Evaluate with micrometer at three points along the length — all three readings must fall within tolerance. Surface finish: visual and tactile comparison to 1.6 μm Ra standard sample. Concentricity between two diameters: dial indicator reading ≤0.03 mm total indicator runout.
