# Nitrating conditions

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Glycerol (99%+ purity, no water) is slowly added to a mixture of concentrated nitric acid (90-98%) and sulfuric acid (96-98%) at 10-15°C. The mixed acid composition: ~50% HNO₃, ~50% H₂SO₄ by weight. The ratio is approximately 1 part glycerol to 6 parts mixed acid. Temperature control is critical: the reaction is exothermic (ΔH ≈ -370 kJ/mol of glycerol).
