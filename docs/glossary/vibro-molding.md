# Vibro-molding

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

(alternative for very large diameters): - The green paste is placed in a mold on a vibrating table. Vibration compacts the paste into the mold shape. Used for diameters >700 mm where extrusion forces become impractically high. Lower particle alignment (more isotropic properties) but suitable for applications where this is acceptable.
