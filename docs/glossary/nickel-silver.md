# Nickel silver

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(German silver, 55-65% Cu / 10-30% Ni / 20-30% Zn): Contains no silver — named for its silvery appearance. Resistivity ~25-30 μΩ·cm. Used for tableware, musical instrument bodies (flutes, saxophones), resistor wire, and decorative hardware. The Zn addition lowers cost and improves castability but reduces seawater corrosion resistance compared to binary cupronickel. Melting range: 960-1120°C.
