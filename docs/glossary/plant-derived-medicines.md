# Plant-derived medicines

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Plant-derived medicines (Foundations+) — Willow bark (salicin → salicylic acid → aspirin)**: Harvest bark from willow (Salix species) in spring when sap flows. Dry bark. Extract by boiling 30 g bark in 500 mL water for 15 minutes. Strain. Dose: 1 cup of decoction for pain/fever. Contains salicin (prodrug of salicylic acid).
