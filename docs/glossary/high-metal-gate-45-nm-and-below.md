# High-κ + metal gate (45 nm and below)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: vlsi-scaling

Hafnium oxide (HfO₂, κ ≈ 20-25) deposited by ALD replaces SiO₂. A 2 nm HfO₂ film has the same gate capacitance as ~0.4 nm SiO₂ ("equivalent oxide thickness" or EOT) but is physically thick enough to suppress tunneling leakage by orders of magnitude. Deposited by ALD for Angstrom-level thickness control (see [Advanced Processes](advanced-processes.md)).
