# Knowledge multiplier

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

A single technical manual in a scribe-based system might have 10-50 copies across a civilization (each copy takes weeks of skilled labor). The same manual printed in an edition of 500 copies can be distributed to every workshop, school, and library in the civilization within months. This acceleration of knowledge transmission is the single most important consequence of printing technology.
