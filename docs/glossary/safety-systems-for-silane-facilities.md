# Safety Systems for Silane Facilities

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Continuous monitoring: catalytic bead sensor (0-100% LEL, LEL = 1.4% SiH4 in air) plus infrared (0-1000 ppm), alarm at 25% LEL. Emergency shutdown: automatic valve closure, ventilation boost to 10x normal flow. Fire suppression: dry chemical Purple K (potassium bicarbonate) - do NOT use water on silane fires. Abatement: burn box at 800-1000C decomposes SiH4 to Si + 2H2 then oxidizes H2. Emergency response: evacuate 100m radius for release, 300m for cylinder rupture.
