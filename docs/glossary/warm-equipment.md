# Warm equipment

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Air compressors, molecular sieve adsorbers, product compressors, and control room are located outside the cold box at grade level. The main air compressor (reciprocating or centrifugal, 5-10 MW for a medium-size plant) is typically housed in a separate compressor building with noise attenuation. Molecular sieve adsorbers (two vertical pressure vessels, 3-5 m diameter × 5-10 m tall) switch on 4-8 hour cycles, one adsorbing while the other regenerates with heated nitrogen.
