# Series resistor

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

R = (Vin - Vz) / (Iz + Iload). Choose Iz to be at least 5-10 mA to keep the Zener in its regulation region. For Vin = 12V, Vz = 5.1V, Iload = 20 mA, Iz = 10 mA: R = (12 - 5.1)/(0.03) = 230 Ω. Use 220 Ω standard value.
