# Cell operating conditions

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Cell voltage 2.9-3.5 V (decomposition voltage 2.2 V + overpotentials + membrane resistance + solution resistance + hardware resistance). Current density 2-5 kA/m². Operating temperature 85-95°C. Current efficiency 95-97% (3-5% of current produces O₂ at the anode instead of Cl₂, and some OH⁻ back-migrates through the membrane). Energy consumption: 2,100-2,400 kWh per tonne Cl₂. Individual cell area 1-4 m². Electrolyzer with 50-150 cells in series, drawing 5-30 kA total current at 200-400 V DC.
