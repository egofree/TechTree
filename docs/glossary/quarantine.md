# Quarantine

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Isolate all incoming animals for 30 days minimum in a separate enclosure at least 15 m from the main herd. Observe for coughing, diarrhea, lameness, discharge from eyes or nose, and abnormal behavior. Test for brucellosis and tuberculosis where testing is available. Never mix new stock with the herd until the quarantine period ends with no symptoms.
