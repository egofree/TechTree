# Quartz crystal oscillator

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: measurement

AT-cut quartz crystal resonates at precise frequency (32,768 Hz for watches, 1-10 MHz for instrumentation). Frequency stability: ±10-50 ppm over -40°C to +85°C (uncompensated), ±0.1-1 ppm with temperature compensation (TCXO), ±0.001-0.01 ppm in oven-controlled enclosure (OCXO). Requires quartz crystal production from the Silicon stage.
