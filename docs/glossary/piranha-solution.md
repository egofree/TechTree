# Piranha solution

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

H₂SO₄:H₂O₂ at 3:1 ratio (add peroxide slowly to sulfuric acid — vigorous exotherm, temperature reaches 100-130°C). Attacks organic material aggressively. Dissolves resist and removes metallic contaminants. Use immediately after mixing (peroxide decomposes rapidly). Extremely dangerous — burns skin on contact, can cause explosions if mixed with organics. Always add peroxide TO acid, never reverse.
