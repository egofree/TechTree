# Viscose rayon

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Dissolving pulp steeped in 18% NaOH → alkali cellulose. Pressed, shredded, aged (depolymerized to target molecular weight). Reacted with CS₂ (carbon disulfide) → sodium cellulose xanthate (orange crumb). Dissolved in dilute NaOH → viscose solution. Filtered, deaerated, ripened. Extruded through spinneret into H₂SO₄/Na₂SO₄ coagulation bath → regenerated cellulose filaments.
