# Transmission

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

High voltage (10-100+ kV) reduces line losses (P_loss = I²R — higher voltage = lower current = lower loss for same power). Step up at generator with transformer, step down at load. Three-phase AC transmission (3 conductors, 120° phase separation — most efficient wire utilization). Copper or aluminum conductors on wooden or steel towers.
