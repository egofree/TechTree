# Delayed primary closure

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Delayed primary closure (4-5 days after injury) — for contaminated wounds that have been cleaned and observed for signs of infection. If no infection develops by day 4-5, close the wound. Combines safety of open wound management with cosmetic/functional benefit of closure. See: [Medicine & Surgery](../health/medicine.md)
