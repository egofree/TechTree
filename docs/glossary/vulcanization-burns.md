# Vulcanization burns

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Autoclave steam at 3-10 bar causes severe scalds on contact. Press platens at 140-180°C cause thermal burns. Use thermal gloves and face shields when loading/unloading presses. Depressurize autoclaves fully before opening. Steam burns are particularly severe because the latent heat of condensation (2260 kJ/kg at 1 atm) transfers far more energy than an equivalent mass of hot water.
