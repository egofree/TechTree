# Surface preparation for electroless

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

The substrate must be catalytically active. Steel and nickel are self-catalytic for electroless nickel. Non-conductors (plastics, ceramics) require etch → sensitize → activate sequence: chromic acid etch (creates micro-roughness), SnCl₂ sensitization, PdCl₂ activation (palladium nuclei serve as catalytic sites). Aluminum requires zincate treatment (double zincate for best adhesion).
