# Humanure

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: agriculture

Human waste composted through thermophilic process at 55°C+ for pathogen destruction. Contains valuable nutrients (~4-5 kg N, 1.5 kg P, 1.5 kg K per person per year). Must maintain 55°C for 3 consecutive days with minimum 5 turnings over 15 days. Never apply inadequately composted material to food crops.
