# Lens centering

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: optics

A lens must be mounted so that its mechanical axis coincides with its optical axis. Mount the lens in a precision cell on a lathe. Shine a laser through the lens while rotating the cell. If the reflected or transmitted beam wobbles, the lens is decentered. Adjust until the beam is stationary during rotation. Centration tolerance: 0.01-0.1 mm depending on the application.
