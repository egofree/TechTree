# Layup

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Cut fiberglass mat or fabric to shape. Place on mold. Apply resin by brush or roller. Work resin into the fiber with a consolidation roller (serrated aluminum or nylon roller) to wet out the fiber and remove entrapped air. Add additional layers, alternating fiber direction (0°/90°/±45° orientations typical), with resin between each layer.
