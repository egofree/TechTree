# Typical yield

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

First runs 30-50%, improving to 80-95% with process optimization. Key yield limiters: contamination (particles on wafer surface kill cells), uniformity (non-uniform diffusion creates shunts), and mechanical breakage (thin wafers are fragile). Each step requires process control and monitoring to maintain yield. For integrated circuits, typical IC yield at the 180nm node runs 80-90% for simple logic and 50-70% for large SoC designs.
