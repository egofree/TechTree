# Hot carrier injection

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

High electric fields near the drain end of short-channel MOSFETs accelerate electrons to high energy. These "hot carriers" can be injected into the gate oxide, causing threshold voltage shift and transconductance degradation over time. Worse at higher drain voltages and lower temperatures. Mitigated by lightly-doped drain (LDD) structures that reduce the peak electric field.
