# Small cryogenic ASU

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

— requires compressor, countercurrent heat exchanger (copper or stainless), insulated cold box (perlite-filled), and a double distillation column (copper or stainless sieve trays). Produces liquid O₂/N₂ and, with an additional column, crude argon. This is the entry point for semiconductor-capable gas production.
