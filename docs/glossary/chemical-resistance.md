# Chemical resistance

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Resistant to water, seawater, dilute acids (HCl, H₂SO₄ up to 10%), dilute alkalis, and most organic solvents at room temperature. Attacked by concentrated oxidizing acids (conc. HNO₃, hot conc. H₂SO₄), chlorinated hydrocarbons (chloroform, carbon tetrachloride), and hot turpentine. Does not rot, mold, or support microbial growth in soil or seawater — a crucial property for submarine cable longevity.
