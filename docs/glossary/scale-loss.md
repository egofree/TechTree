# Scale loss

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Steel oxidizes rapidly at rolling temperatures. Total scale loss from reheat furnace through hot rolling: 1.5-3% of original weight. Scale is recovered and recycled to the sinter plant in integrated steelworks. Oxide scale on the finished product is removed by pickling (HCl or H₂SO₄) before cold rolling.
