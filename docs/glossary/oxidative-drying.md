# Oxidative drying

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

(air-drying oils, alkyds): Unsaturated fatty acid chains (from linseed, soybean, or tung oil) cross-link by reaction with atmospheric oxygen, catalyzed by metal driers. Cobalt naphthenate or octoate (0.05-0.2% metal on resin) is the primary surface drier (promotes skin formation). Manganese naphthenate assists through-drying.
