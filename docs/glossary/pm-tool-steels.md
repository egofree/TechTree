# PM tool steels

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Gas-atomized tool steel powder (M2, M4, T15, CPM 10V) sealed in steel cans, HIPped at 1100-1200°C, 150 MPa for 4-6 hours. Produces fully dense billets with ultra-fine, uniformly distributed carbide microstructure (carbide size 1-5 µm vs. 10-30 µm in conventional ingot tool steel). Superior wear resistance and toughness — used for cold forming dies, powder compaction tooling, and high-performance cutting tools.
