# Steel shelving

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Angle-iron uprights with clip-in shelf brackets. Thinner shelves (1.5 mm sheet steel) hold more weight per unit than wood. Corrosion-resistant paint or galvanized finish. Steel conducts heat faster than wood — avoid placing steel shelves against exterior walls in cold climates (condensation risk on books).
