# Crystal oscillator

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: computing, silicon

Piezoelectric quartz crystal resonates at a precise frequency determined by its physical dimensions (thickness for AT-cut crystals). Frequency stability: ±10-50 ppm over temperature (-40°C to +85°C). Used for all precision timing: CPU clocks, real-time clocks, communication system frequency references.
