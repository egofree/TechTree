# Secondary crushing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Jaw crusher reduces chunks to 10-50 mm lumps. Jaw crusher gap adjusted to control output size. The crusher liners must be hardened steel or manganese steel (silicon is abrasive). Liner wear introduces iron contamination (~0.01-0.05% Fe from crusher wear per pass). Use magnetic separation after crushing to remove steel fragments.
