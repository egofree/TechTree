# Exhausting system

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

(fan on return side): - Fan pulls air through the mine. The mine is under slight negative pressure relative to atmosphere. Leakage tends to be inward (surface air drawn in through cracks), which can bring contaminants from old workings or waste areas into the intake stream. - The fan handles contaminated, humid, warm return air.
