# Vinyl chloride monomer (VCM) for PVC

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

A confirmed human carcinogen (IARC Group 1) causing angiosarcoma of the liver — a rare cancer with a specific association to VCM exposure. Latency period: 10-40 years. Also flammable/explosive (LEL 3.6% in air, UEL 33%). Monomer handling requires closed systems with leak detection, continuous air monitoring, and explosion-proof electrical equipment.
