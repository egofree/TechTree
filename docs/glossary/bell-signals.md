# Bell signals

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

The oldest method. A system of bell codes (bell pulls on a wire rope running down the shaft) between the banksman (surface) and the onsetter (underground shaft station). Standard signals: 1 bell = stop, 2 bells = lower, 3 bells = raise, 4 bells = men riding (slow and careful), emergency = continuous ringing. Still used as backup in modern mines.
