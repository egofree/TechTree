# Wool grading

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

(Blood System, US; or Micron System): - **Fine** (<22.5 μm fiber diameter): Merino and similar breeds. Softest, warmest, most crimp (6-12 waves per cm). Used for fine clothing, knitting yarns. Premium price — Merino fleece typically 18-24 μm. Spinning count: 64s-80s (yields very fine yarn). - **Medium** (22.5-31 μm): Down breeds, crossbreeds. Versatile — clothing, blankets, worsted cloth.
