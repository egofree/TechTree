# Total impurities

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

<1 ppb for all metallic species. Individual element limits: Fe <0.1 ppb, Cu <0.1 ppb, Na <0.05 ppb, Ni <0.1 ppb, Cr <0.1 ppb. These metals diffuse rapidly in silicon and contaminate device junctions even at ppb levels.
