# Electrochemical cells

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

(gas-specific): Each cell is designed for one target gas. H₂S cell: 0-50 ppm or 0-100 ppm range, alarm at 10 ppm (TWA) and 15 ppm (STEL). CO cell: 0-500 ppm, alarm at 25 ppm (TWA) and 100 ppm (STEL). Cl₂ cell: 0-10 ppm. Cell life 1-3 years. Cross-sensitivity exists (H₂S sensors may respond to SO₂, etc.) — review manufacturer data.
