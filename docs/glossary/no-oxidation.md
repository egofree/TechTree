# NO oxidation

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

2NO + O₂ → 2NO₂. This reaction is unique: it is third-order (second order in NO, first order in O₂) and is favored by low temperature and high pressure. It is the rate-limiting step in the overall process. The gas is cooled to 40-60°C and given residence time in large absorption towers. The brown NO₂ gas is clearly visible in process equipment.
