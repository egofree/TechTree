# Hand layup procedure (step-by-step)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: glass

Manual composite fabrication process: apply release agent to mold, lay dry glass fiber reinforcement, brush or roll resin (polyester or epoxy) into the fibers, work out air bubbles with consolidation rollers, repeat for additional layers, and cure at room temperature or elevated temperature. Labor-intensive but requires minimal equipment.
