# Silica (SiO₂)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics, glass

Silica (SiO₂) refers to 70-74% — glass former, provides network structure - **Soda (Na₂O)**: 12-16% — flux, lowers melting point from ~1700°C to ~1000°C - **Lime (CaO)**: 10-12% — stabilizer, prevents water solubility of the glass - **Alumina (Al₂O₃)**: 1-3% — improves chemical durability and hardness - **Magnesia (MgO)**: 0.1-1% — prevents devitrification (crystal formation during cooling).
