# Latex allergies

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Natural rubber latex proteins sensitize some individuals, causing allergic reactions ranging from skin irritation to anaphylactic shock. Use nitrile alternatives for allergic workers. Monitor for sensitization in repeated-exposure settings. Prevalence: 1-6% of general population, up to 17% in healthcare workers with frequent latex glove use.
