# Top gas composition

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Top gas composition (typical by volume) — 20-30% CO (carbon monoxide — the combustible component) - 1-5% H₂ (hydrogen — also combustible) - 18-24% CO₂ (carbon dioxide — inert diluent) - 48-55% N₂ (nitrogen — inert, from the blast air) - Trace CH₄. See: [Blast Furnace and Cast Iron](../metals/blast-furnace.md)
