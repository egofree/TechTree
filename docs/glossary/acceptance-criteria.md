# Acceptance criteria

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

Define defect acceptance limits before inspection begins. Common standards: porosity shall not exceed 2% of weld cross-sectional area; any single pore shall not exceed 3 mm diameter; cracks are unacceptable in structural welds; incomplete penetration limited to 10% of weld depth for non-critical joints, zero for pressure-containing welds. slag inclusions shall not exceed 10 mm length or 2 mm width.
