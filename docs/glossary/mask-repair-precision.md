# Mask repair precision

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

FIB systems using Ga⁺ ions at 30 keV sputter-remove chrome opaque defects with 10 nm precision. For clear defects, electron beam induced deposition (EBID) builds up a carbon-containing patch from a precursor gas to fill the gap. Post-repair inspection verifies each repair site meets quality requirements with ≤10 nm placement error relative to the intended mask geometry.
