# Air-lift pump

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Inject compressed air into the bottom of a water-filled pipe. The air-water mixture is less dense than the surrounding water column, so it rises by buoyancy. Simple, no moving parts underground (compressor is at surface). Lift: 20-50 m per stage. Efficiency: 20-40%. Useful for sumps where installing a mechanical pump is impractical.
