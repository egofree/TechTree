# Resource assessment

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Measure stream/river flow rate (m³/s) and available head (vertical drop, m). Power potential: P = ρ × g × Q × h × η, where Q = flow rate, η = overall efficiency (0.6-0.85). Example: 1 m³/s flow, 10 m head = 1000 × 9.81 × 1 × 10 × 0.75 = ~73.6 kW. Reliable, continuous baseload power.
