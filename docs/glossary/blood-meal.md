# Blood meal

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Collect fresh blood, stir to prevent coagulation, boil to coagulate the proteins, press the solid mass, dry, and grind to powder. Blood meal is 10-12% nitrogen by weight, making it a potent fertilizer (applied at 100-200 kg/hectare). Also used as an adhesive additive in plywood and particle board (blood albumin adhesive).
