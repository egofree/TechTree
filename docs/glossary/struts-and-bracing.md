# Struts and bracing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Steel tube lift struts (20-25 mm diameter) from fuselage to wing spar — carry wing loads to fuselage. Flying wires (steel piano wire, 3-5 mm diameter, in tension) brace wings against positive and negative g-loads. Landing wires brace against negative loads. Turnbuckles on all wires for tension adjustment and rigging (set wing incidence and dihedral angle).
