# Downdraft kiln

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

(highest temperature, 1200-1400°C): - **Design**: Fireboxes on sides. Hot gases rise to crown (dome), deflect downward through ware chamber, exit through floor-level flue (damper-controlled). Longer gas path = more heat transfer to ware. - **Advantage**: More uniform temperature throughout ware chamber.
