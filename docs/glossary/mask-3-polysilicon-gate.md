# Mask 3 — Polysilicon gate

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Deposit 300-500 nm poly-Si by LPCVD (SiH₄ at 620°C, ~10 nm/min). Dope n+ (POCl₃ diffusion or ion implant). Spin resist, expose Mask 3, develop, dry etch poly-Si (CF₄/O₂ plasma). Strip resist. Result: polysilicon gate electrodes, self-aligned to source/drain.
