# Outgassing

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Plastics (cable insulation, wafer carriers, gaskets) release volatile organic compounds (VOCs) under vacuum or elevated temperature. These VOCs deposit on wafer surfaces as thin organic films. Use low-outgassing materials (PTFE, PFA, stainless steel) for all components in contact with wafers or vacuum.
