# Ring spinning

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Ring spinning (Thorpe, 1828) — The traveler, a small C-shaped piece of spring steel wire, rides around a ring (steel circle, 40-55 mm inner diameter) at high speed. The traveler inserts twist and winds yarn onto the bobbin simultaneously, without any carriage stroke. Continuous motion, simpler mechanism, higher speed.
