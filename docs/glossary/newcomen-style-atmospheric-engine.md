# Newcomen-style atmospheric engine

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

(first practical steam engine, ~1712): - **Principle**: Steam at atmospheric pressure fills cylinder. Cold water injected into cylinder condenses steam → vacuum → atmospheric pressure pushes piston down → work stroke. - **Critical component**: Cylinder, bored to close tolerance (~0.5 mm clearance over piston). Requires the Machine Tools stage (Wilkinson boring machine). Cylinder diameter 30-80 cm, stroke 1.5-3 m. - **Construction**: - **Cylinder**: Cast iron, bored true. Brass or lead sealing...
