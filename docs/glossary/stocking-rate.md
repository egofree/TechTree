# Stocking rate

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

Stocking rate: 1-2 dogs per 50-200 head of sheep or goats. Terrain difficulty and predator pressure determine the upper end. Open pasture with moderate predator pressure: 1 dog per 100 head. Mountainous terrain with heavy wolf or bear pressure: 2 dogs per 50 head. See: [Biological Pest Management](../animals/pest-management.md)
