# Skin contact

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Remove contaminated clothing immediately (clothing holds chemical against the skin, extending exposure). Flush with copious water for a minimum of 15 minutes. For hydrofluoric acid, apply calcium gluconate gel (2.5%) to the affected area after flushing: the calcium ions bind free fluoride ions, preventing deep tissue destruction.
