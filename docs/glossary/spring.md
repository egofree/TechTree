(colony buildup): First inspection when temperature exceeds 15°C. Check for queen presence (eggs and young larvae present). Assess food stores — feed sugar syrup (1:1 sugar:water by weight) if stores are low before nectar flow begins. Add supers or bars as population expands. Monitor for swarm cells (queen cups on comb bottom edges) and split colonies or add space to prevent swarming. Replace old or damaged comb.
