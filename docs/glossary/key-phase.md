# Key phase

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: ceramics

Mullite (3Al₂O₃·2SiO₂) forms above 1600°C — excellent high-temperature strength - **Service temperature**: 1500-1800°C - **Advantage over fireclay**: Higher strength, better slag resistance, lower porosity - **Applications**: Steel furnace linings, glass tank furnaces, CZ crystal growth furnace insulation, high-fire ceramic kilns
