# Hastelloy C-276

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(Ni-16Cr-16Mo-5Fe-4W): Outstanding resistance to both oxidizing and reducing acids, including hydrochloric and sulfuric acid at concentrations and temperatures that destroy stainless steel. The tungsten and molybdenum provide pitting and crevice corrosion resistance. Used for flue gas desulfurization, chemical reactors, pollution control.
