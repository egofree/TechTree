# Electrical design rules

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: vlsi-scaling

Maximum gate oxide voltage (prevents time-dependent dielectric breakdown), maximum transistor width per finger (prevents gate resistance from degrading performance), minimum coupling capacitance between signal nets (prevents crosstalk-induced failures), maximum simultaneous switching current per power pin (prevents IR drop and ground bounce). These rules come from the foundry's process characterization data and are non-negotiable for design signoff.
