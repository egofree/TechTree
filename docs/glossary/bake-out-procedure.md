# Bake-out procedure

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Heat the entire vacuum system to 150-400°C while pumping continuously. This accelerates desorption by orders of magnitude. A system that takes 2 weeks to reach 10⁻⁶ Pa without bake-out reaches it in 24-48 hours with bake-out. Stainless steel chambers with copper CF gaskets tolerate the highest bake temperatures (300-400°C). Viton-sealed systems limited to 150°C.
