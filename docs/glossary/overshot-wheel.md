# Overshot wheel

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Overshot wheel (highest efficiency water wheel — requires significant head) — Principle**: Water is delivered to the top of the wheel via a headrace and sluice, filling buckets at the crown. Water's weight in descending buckets drives the wheel — primarily a gravity machine, not an impulse machine. Buckets empty at the bottom into the tailrace. - **Efficiency**: ~60-70%.
