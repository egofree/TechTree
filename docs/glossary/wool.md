# Wool

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Shear fleece → sort by quality (shoulder and back = finest, breech and belly = coarsest) → wash in warm water (40-50°C) with mild alkali (soap or ammonia) to remove lanolin (wool grease, ~10-30% of raw fleece weight) and dirt → rinse thoroughly → dry → tease (pull apart by hand to open locks). Lanolin recovered from wash water is useful for waterproofing and lubrication.
