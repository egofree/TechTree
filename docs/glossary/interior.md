# Interior

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Wooden shelves for powder containers, at least 15 cm above the floor. Non-sparking tools only (copper, brass, wood). Good ventilation (louvered openings) to prevent moisture buildup and temperature extremes. Thermometer mounted inside. Temperature indicator: if the interior exceeds 30°C, improve ventilation or insulate the magazine.
