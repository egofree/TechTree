# Roughing pump

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Roughing pump (rotary vane or diaphragm) — Pulls system from atmospheric pressure (~10⁵ Pa) down to ~0.1-1 Pa. This is the "rough vacuum" phase. The roughing pump handles the bulk of the gas load — most of the air molecules are removed in this stage. See: [Vacuum Technology](../gas-handling/vacuum.md)
