# Dektak / profilometer

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

(step height): Diamond-tipped stylus (12.5-50 μm radius, 1-15 mg force) scans across a step in the film surface (e.g., where resist or oxide was etched away). Measures vertical displacement with sub-nm resolution (typically ±1-5 nm over 1 μm step range). Used for: etch depth verification, film thickness (after patterning a step), planarization uniformity.
