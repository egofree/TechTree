# Cement production

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Rotary kiln burning zone: magnesite-spinel or dolomite bricks (resist CaO-SiO₂-Al₂O₃ clinker melt attack). Transition zones: high-alumina or fireclay. Preheater tower: alkali-resistant castables (alkali vapor condensation attacks alumina-silicate refractories — causes hot-phase formation and spalling).
