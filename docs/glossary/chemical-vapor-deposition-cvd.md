# Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Gas-phase reaction deposits thin films - Poly-Si: SiH₄ decomposition - SiO₂: SiH₄ + O₂ or TEOS decomposition - Si₃N₄: SiH₄ + NH₃ - Requires: gas handling, temperature control, vacuum/flow control - **Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD)**: Sputtering, evaporation (from the Silicon stage) - **Epitaxy**: Growing single-crystal layer on single-crystal substrate
