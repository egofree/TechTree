# Worker visibility

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: transport

Road construction crews working alongside live traffic must wear high-visibility garments (fluorescent orange or yellow vests with retroreflective stripes). At night, each worker needs at least 0.1 m² of retroreflective material visible from all directions. Without this, drivers at 60 km/h may not see a worker until 30 m away, leaving less than 2 seconds to react.
