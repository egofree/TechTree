# Column management

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

In the furnace, the electrode column is consumed from the bottom and new electrodes are screwed on at the top. Track column length and consumption rate to plan electrode changes during scheduled furnace downtime rather than mid-heat. A typical EAF consumes 0.5-1.5 m of electrode column per heat (depending on furnace size and steel grade).
