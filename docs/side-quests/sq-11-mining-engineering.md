# SQ 11: Mining Engineering &amp; Extractive Metallurgy

**Starts**: Phase 1  
**Priority**: High — ore access is the hard constraint on every metallurgical phase.

## Problem

The tech tree needs copper, iron, tin, coal, sulfur, fluorite, and quartz in industrial quantities. Surface deposits exhaust fast. Going deeper demands engineered mines with ventilation, drainage, hoisting, and structural support. Mining engineering is not optional. It is the gateway to every material the civilization needs.

## Technologies &amp; Systems

### Mining Methods
- Surface mining (open pits, placer) for initial ore supply
- Shaft mining with timber shoring and cribbing for deeper deposits
- Drainage adits driven from valley walls to gravity-drain water

### Ventilation
- Natural draft ventilation (single-shaft convection)
- Bellows-forced air for deeper workings
- Mechanical fans (water-powered, later steam-powered)

### Water Pumping (critical escalation path)
- Bailing with buckets and hand windlasses
- Chain pumps and bucket elevators (water-powered)
- **Savery engine** (early steam, limited head)
- **Newcomen engine** (atmospheric engine, mines unlock deep coal) — this is the KEY FEEDBACK LOOP to Phase 4

### Hoisting &amp; Transport
- Windlass → whim → horse whim → steam winding engine
- Underground tramways (wooden rails, iron rails later)
- Skip hoists for bulk ore

### Ore Dressing
- Hand sorting and cobbing
- Crushing (stamp mills, later jaw crushers)
- Washing and gravity separation (sluices, buddles)

### Mine Safety
- Candle and oil-lamp gas detection (fire damp, choke damp)
- Canaries for carbon monoxide
- Timber quality standards for shoring
- Firesetting with controlled ventilation

## Integration Points

| Phase | Contribution |
|-------|-------------|
| Phase 1 | Surface mining, basic prospecting, charcoal for smelting |
| Phase 2 | Shaft mining, timber shoring, hand-powered drainage |
| Phase 3 | Machine-driven pumps, precision shafts, iron rails |
| Phase 4 | **Newcomen engine for mine drainage** — mines DRIVE steam development |
| Phase 5 | Chemical ore processing, flotation, acid leaching |
| Phase 6 | Ventilation fans, vacuum-assisted processes |
| Phase 7 | High-purity quartz mining, silicon-grade feedstock |

## Key Deliverables

- Prospected ore bodies with assay data
- Engineered mine shafts with timber shoring and ventilation
- Water pumping progression from manual to steam-powered
- Ore dressing workflow from raw rock to concentrated feedstock
- The Newcomen engine, born from mine drainage need, enabling all of Phase 4
