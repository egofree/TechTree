# Slag and spark injuries

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

During hot forging and slag skimming, hot slag and metal particles spray out as bright sparks at 700-900°C. Eye protection (safety glasses or face shield) is essential when hammering hot metal. Slag skimmed from molten copper retains dangerous heat for extended periods — set aside on dry sand or refractory surface, never on combustible material.
