# Power estimate

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

P = η × ρ × g × Q × H. Assuming 85% overall efficiency for a well-designed Francis installation: P = 0.85 × 1000 × 9.81 × 5 × 50 = 2,084,625 W ≈ 2.08 MW. This falls squarely in the medium-head range, confirming the Francis turbine as the correct choice.
