# Safety notes

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: photolithography

Piranha solution is the most dangerous wet chemical in the fab. Mix only in dedicated fume hood with acid-resistant PPE (face shield, acid gloves, apron). Never store piranha — decompose with excess water before disposal. Oxygen plasma equipment requires proper RF grounding and O₂ leak detection. Acetone and IPA are fire hazards — no ignition sources, use in ventilated areas.
