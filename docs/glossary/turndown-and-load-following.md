# Turndown and load following

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

ASUs operate most efficiently at 90-100% of design capacity. Below 70% load, column reflux ratios change, product purity may drop, and the expansion turbine may operate off-design. Some ASUs can turndown to 50% while maintaining product purity by reducing air flow, reducing expansion turbine output, and adjusting liquid product draw rates. Rapid load changes (<30 minutes) are not possible due to the large thermal mass of the cold box and the time required for column re-equilibration.
