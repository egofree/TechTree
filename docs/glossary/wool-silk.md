# Wool-silk

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

70-80% wool, 20-30% silk. Silk adds luster, drape, and strength to the wool. The blend produces a luxury knitting and weaving yarn with a soft halo from the wool and a subtle sheen from the silk. Card together; silk fibers (continuous filament) must be cut or broken to staple length (5-10 cm) before blending with the wool.
