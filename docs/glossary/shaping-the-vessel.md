# Shaping the vessel

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

As the walls grow, shape the profile by pressing outward for a belly or inward for a neck. Use a wooden paddle on the outside and a stone anvil held inside to thin and shape the walls. This paddle-and-anvil technique can produce thin, even walls on large vessels. Work on one section at a time, letting lower coils stiffen slightly before adding weight above. If the walls start to sag, stop and let the clay firm up before continuing.
