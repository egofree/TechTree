# Coppicing for withies

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: plants

Cut willow stools annually to produce long, straight, flexible rods (1.5-3 m). *Salix viminalis* (basket willow) is the preferred species, producing 20-40 rods per stool per year. Plant 40-60 cm cuttings at 30-50 cm spacing in damp ground. First harvestable crop in year 2.
