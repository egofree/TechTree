# Non-electric (shock tube)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

(1970s-present): Hollow plastic tube (3 mm OD) with thin coating of explosive dust (HMX + aluminum) on inner wall. Initiated by starter, propagates shock at ~2,000 m/s to detonator. Immune to radio frequency, static electricity, and stray currents. Safer than electric caps near power lines.
