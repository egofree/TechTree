# Chrome (K₂Cr₂O₇, potassium dichromate)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Brightens and deepens colors at 1-3% owf. Produces especially rich reds and yellows on wool. Chrome mordanting was standard in professional dyeing for centuries. However, hexavalent chromium (Cr⁶⁺) is a known carcinogen, causes skin ulcers on contact, and damages kidneys and liver. Handle with nitrile gloves in a well-ventilated area.
