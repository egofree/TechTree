# RF coil

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Water-cooled copper induction coil (single-turn or multi-turn) wrapped around the polysilicon rod. RF power (50-500 kHz, 20-100 kW) induces eddy currents in the silicon, heating the narrow zone to >1414°C. The coil geometry defines the molten zone width (~10-20 mm).
