# BSF prepupae

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: critical | **Domains**: animals

Final larval instar of Black Soldier Fly before pupation. Ceases feeding, empties gut, changes from cream to dark brown-black. Self-harvesting stage — instinctively climbs upward seeking dry crevices, exploited by ramp-based harvesting systems. Optimal harvest point for protein and fat content.
