# Beneficiation

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Hand-sort ore from gangue (waste rock). Crush with stone hammer on stone anvil to ~1-3 cm pieces. Wash to remove clay and fines. Roast sulfide ores in open fire (400-600°C, 4-8 hours) to convert sulfides to oxides and drive off sulfur — essential step for chalcopyrite.
