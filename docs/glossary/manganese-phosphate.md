# Manganese phosphate

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(Parkerizing): - Bath: MnO or MnCO₃ dissolved in H₃PO₄ + Fe(H₂PO₄)₂. Temperature: 90-98°C (near-boiling). Immersion: 5-20 minutes. - Produces a dark gray to black coating of hureaulite ((Mn,Fe)₅H₂(PO₄)₄·4H₂O) crystals. Coating weight: 5-30 g/m². Crystal structure is larger and more absorbent than zinc phosphate — retains oil effectively. - Applications: Gun barrels, engine components, gears, fasteners.
