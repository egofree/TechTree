# Quality control

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Test finished bars for total fatty matter (TFM, minimum 60% for grade 1 soap), free alkali (maximum 0.1% — higher causes skin irritation), moisture content (10-15%), and lather volume. pH test: dissolve 1 g soap in 100 mL distilled water, measure pH. Acceptable range: 9.0-10.5.
