# SO₃ sulfonation

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

SO₃ sulfonation (synthetic detergents) — Sulfur trioxide is extremely corrosive and reacts violently with water. Sulfonation reactors must be water-free. SO₃ fumes cause severe respiratory damage. Full respiratory protection, acid-resistant clothing, and emergency shower required. See: [Soap & Detergent Production](../chemistry/soap.md)
