# Cracking and sieving

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

Break the pressed cake with corrugated rollers or a light impact mill. Sieve the broken grains through standard mesh screens to sort by size. Oversize grains are re-cracked; undersize fines are re-pressed. The sieving operation must be done with non-sparking equipment (brass or copper sieves, wooden frames) in a well-ventilated area with humidity above 50%.
