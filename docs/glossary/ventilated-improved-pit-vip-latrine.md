# Ventilated improved pit (VIP) latrine

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Adds a vent pipe (minimum 150 mm diameter PVC or brick) extending at least 0.5 m above the roofline, topped with a fly screen (stainless steel mesh, 1-2 mm openings). Dark interior + bright vent top creates updraft. Flies attracted to light fly up the pipe and are trapped by the screen. Reduces fly breeding by 90-95% and significantly reduces odor. Pit should be at least 3 m deep to allow adequate air volume.
