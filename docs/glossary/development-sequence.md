for an underground mine: 1. **Decline or shaft**: Access from surface to the ore body. A decline (inclined tunnel, 15-20% grade) allows rubber-tired equipment access. A vertical shaft is cheaper per vertical meter but requires winding equipment for all transport. 2. **Level development**: Drive horizontal tunnels (drifts) along the ore body at regular vertical intervals (40-80 m between levels).
