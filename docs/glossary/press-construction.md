# Press construction

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

The Gutenberg press was adapted from the wooden screw press used for wine and olive pressing. Key components: - **Housing**: Heavy wooden frame (elm or oak timbers, mortise-and-tenon joints, bolted with iron rods for rigidity). Approximately 1.8 m tall, 1.2 m wide, 0.6 m deep. Weight: 200-300 kg.
