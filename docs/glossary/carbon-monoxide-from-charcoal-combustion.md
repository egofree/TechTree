# Carbon monoxide from charcoal combustion

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Bloomery smelting consumes charcoal at high rates, producing large volumes of carbon monoxide. CO is colorless, odorless, and causes headache, confusion, loss of consciousness, and death. Never operate a bloomery or forge in an enclosed space. Ensure cross-ventilation. If an operator becomes confused or lethargic, move them to fresh air immediately and monitor breathing.
