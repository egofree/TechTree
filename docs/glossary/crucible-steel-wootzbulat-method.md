# Crucible steel (Wootz/Bulat method):

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Place wrought iron + 1-3% carbon (charcoal powder or cast iron) in sealed clay crucible. Heat to 1450-1550°C (requires very good furnace + forced air) for 2-4 hours. Cool slowly. Produces homogeneous medium-to-high carbon steel (~0.8-1.5% C). This is the path to quality cutting tools and springs.
