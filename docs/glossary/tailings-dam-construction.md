# Tailings dam construction

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

The most common method is upstream construction — the dam is built incrementally by depositing coarse tailings on the downstream face and raising the dam crest as the tailings pond fills. Dam height: 20-100+ m for large mines. The dam must be engineered for stability (slope angle, drainage, seismic resistance). Tailings dam failures are catastrophic — they release massive volumes of water and mud downstream. The 2019 Brumadinho dam failure in Brazil killed 270 people.
