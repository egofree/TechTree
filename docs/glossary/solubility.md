# Solubility

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Dissolves readily in ethanol (95% ethyl alcohol — the primary industrial solvent for shellac; dissolution rate is fastest with 95% ethanol vs. 100% anhydrous ethanol, because a small amount of water aids in breaking the intermolecular hydrogen bonds within the shellac) and isopropyl alcohol (2-propanol — slightly slower dissolution than ethanol but less expensive and less regulated).
