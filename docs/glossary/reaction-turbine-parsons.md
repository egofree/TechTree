# Reaction turbine (Parsons)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: energy

Steam expands through both stationary guide blades (nozzles) AND rotating blades. Blades act as moving nozzles — pressure drops across both fixed and moving rows. Multiple stages extract energy gradually. Runs at lower speed (1,500-3,600 RPM) — direct drive to generator possible.
