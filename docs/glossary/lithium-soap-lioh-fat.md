# Lithium soap (LiOH + fat)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Lithium stearate. Water-resistant AND higher dropping point (~190°C). Multi-purpose grease — single grease type for most applications. THE modern standard. Requires LiOH (from lithium ore — lepidolite or spodumene, roasted with CaO, leached with water, electrolyzed → LiOH). Lithium is less common than sodium/calcium — save for applications requiring both water resistance and high temperature.
