# Seed pressing

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Fill a wooden screw press with oil seeds (flax/linseed, sesame, rapeseed, sunflower). Apply pressure via a screw mechanism that crushes the seeds against a solid base. Oil drains through gaps or a filter into a collection vessel. Yield: 25-35% oil by weight for most seeds. The remaining seed cake is high-protein animal feed.
