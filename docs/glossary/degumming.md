# Degumming

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

Boil cocoons in water with sodium carbonate (Na₂CO₃, 5-10 g/L) at 80-90°C for 1-2 hours. Removes sericin (gummy protein binding filaments, ~20-30% of raw weight). Fibers become lustrous and soft. Degumming waste (sericin) recoverable as adhesive.
