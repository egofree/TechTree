# Birch tar adhesive

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: foundations

Heat birch bark in sealed container (similar to char cloth method) at 300-400°C. Black, sticky tar forms. Roll into balls. Soften with heat to apply. Strongest natural adhesive available. Birch tar is waterproof and remains slightly flexible, so it absorbs shock without cracking. Store tar balls wrapped in leaves in a cool, dry place. They keep indefinitely.
