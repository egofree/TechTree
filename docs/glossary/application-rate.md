# Application rate

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: animals

10-20 kg fresh manure per 100 m² per week. Cattle, pig, or poultry manure all work. Poultry manure is richest in nitrogen but must be composted 2-4 weeks before adding to the pond to avoid ammonia spikes that kill fish. Distribute manure from a boat or by tossing from the bank around the pond margins.
