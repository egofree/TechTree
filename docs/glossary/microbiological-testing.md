# Microbiological testing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Total aerobic microbial count (TAMC): <100 CFU/g for oral solid dosage forms, <10³ CFU/g for oral liquids. Total yeast and mold count (TYMC): <10 CFU/g solids, <10² CFU/g liquids. Absence of specified organisms: E. coli, Salmonella, S. aureus, P. aeruginosa (USP <62>). Sterile products (injectables, ophthalmics): must pass sterility test (USP <71>, membrane filtration, incubate 14 days in thioglycollate and soybean-casein digest media).
