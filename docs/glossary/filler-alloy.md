# Filler alloy

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: machine-tools

Brass (copper-zinc). Typical composition: 60% Cu / 40% Zn (melts ~900°C, flows at ~950°C). Produce by alloying copper and zinc in a crucible — see [Iron & Steel](../metals/iron-steel.md) for copper and zinc sourcing.
