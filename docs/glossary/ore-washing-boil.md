# Ore washing /沸腾 (boil)

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

(1-2 hours): Add iron ore (Fe₂O₃) to the bath to provide oxygen for decarburization. Fe₂O₃ + 3C → 2Fe + 3CO. The CO evolution causes the bath to "boil" — stirring the metal and floating out inclusions. Carbon drops 0.3-0.5% per hour. The boil also homogenizes temperature and composition throughout the shallow bath.
