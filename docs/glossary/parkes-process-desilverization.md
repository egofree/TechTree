# Parkes process (desilverization)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Add zinc at 450-480°C. Silver (and gold) dissolve preferentially in zinc, which is immiscible in lead. Zinc-silver crust floats and is skimmed. Zinc is distilled off under vacuum; silver is refined electrolytically. Recovers >98% of silver.
