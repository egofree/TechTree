# G-10

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

Epoxy-glass laminate, non-flame-retardant grade. Continuous glass fiber fabric (style 7628, 0.18 mm thick, 18 × 14 ends/picks per cm) impregnated with epoxy resin (resin content 35-45% by weight) to form "prepreg" (pre-impregnated fabric — partially cured B-stage resin that is dry and tacky). Multiple prepreg plies stacked to desired thickness (standard PCB: 1.6 mm = ~8 plies of 7628 fabric). Cured in heated platen press.
