# Working pressure

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Working pressure: 150-200 bar at 15°C (standard industrial). High-pressure cylinders: 250-300 bar for hydrogen and helium (these gases have high compressibility). Cylinder expands slightly under pressure — this is normal elastic deformation. See: [Basic Gas Handling](../gas-handling/basic.md)
