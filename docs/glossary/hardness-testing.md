# Hardness testing

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: metals

Mechanical testing methods for measuring metal hardness: Brinell (steel ball indenter, for castings), Rockwell (diamond cone or ball, most common), Vickers (diamond pyramid, for thin sections), and Knoop (elongated diamond, for coatings). Hardness correlates with strength and is non-destructive.
