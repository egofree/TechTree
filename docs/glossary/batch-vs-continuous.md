# Batch vs continuous

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Batch fermentation (fill, sterilize, inoculate, ferment, harvest, clean, repeat) is the most common mode. Cycle time: 24-120 hours per batch. Equipment utilization: 60-80% (remaining time for cleaning, sterilization, and turnaround).
