# Particles in gas lines

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Particles generated by valve actuation (metal wear from diaphragm valves), gas-phase nucleation (silane decomposition forming Si particles), and corrosion products can transport to the process chamber and cause device defects. Point-of-use filtration: 0.003 μm sintered metal filter as the last component before the chamber. Filter integrity verified by bubble point test.
