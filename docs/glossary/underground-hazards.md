# Underground hazards

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: mining

If test pits or adits are dug, ensure roof support. Bad air (low oxygen, methane, CO₂) in enclosed spaces — ventilate before entering. Never enter alone. Test pits deeper than 1.2 m must be shored or sloped to prevent collapse. Excavated material must be placed at least 1 m from the pit edge. Atmospheric oxygen below 19.5% causes impaired judgment; below 16% is immediately dangerous to life.
