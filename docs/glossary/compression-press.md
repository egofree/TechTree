# Compression press

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

The workhorse of thermoset molding. Hydraulic press with heated platens (electric cartridge heaters or steam). Capacity: 10-500+ tons clamping force. Platen temperature control: ±5°C uniformity across the platen surface (critical for consistent cure — hot spots cause overcure, cold spots cause undercure).
