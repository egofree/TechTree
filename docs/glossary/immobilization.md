# Immobilization

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: health

Wooden splints + cloth wrapping, or plaster cast (gypsum plaster (CaSO₄·½H₂O) + water → sets hard in 10-15 minutes, full strength in 24-48 hours). Pad bony prominences with cotton before plaster application. Immobilize joint above and below fracture. X-ray verification unavailable — rely on clinical alignment and repeat examination.
