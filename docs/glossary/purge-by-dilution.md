# Purge by dilution

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: gas-handling

Flow inert gas through the vessel at a steady rate. The oxygen concentration decays exponentially as the incoming inert gas dilutes the remaining air. After 3-5 volume changes (where one volume change equals flowing a volume of inert gas equal to the vessel volume), the oxygen concentration drops below 1%.
