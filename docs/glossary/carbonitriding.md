# Carbonitriding

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: supporting | **Domains**: metals

Surface hardening process that diffuses both carbon and nitrogen into the surface layer of low-alloy steel at 700-900°C in a carbon-nitrogen bearing atmosphere. Produces a hard, wear-resistant case (0.1-0.8 mm deep) with improved fatigue and corrosion resistance compared to carburizing alone.
