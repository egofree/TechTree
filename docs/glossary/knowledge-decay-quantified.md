# Knowledge decay quantified

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: knowledge

Studies of industrial skills show that complex manual skills (precision welding, instrument-grade machining) degrade noticeably after 6 months of inactivity, falling to 70-80% of peak performance after 12 months without practice. Theoretical knowledge (mathematics, physics) decays more slowly but benefits from periodic review exercises. Schedule mandatory refresher sessions for critical skills at intervals no longer than the demonstrated decay half-life for each skill category.
