# MoSi₂

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Intermetallic with excellent oxidation resistance (forms protective SiO₂ layer at 1000-1700°C). Used as heating elements in high-temperature furnaces (operating temperature up to 1700°C in air — higher than any metallic element). Brittle at room temperature (ductile above ~1000°C).
