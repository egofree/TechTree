# Pharmaceutical and food

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: polymers

"Confectioner's glaze" — purified, de-waxed shellac used as a coating on candies (jelly beans, candy corn, chocolate-covered almonds — provides gloss and prevents sticking), pills, and tablets. Provides gloss, moisture barrier (extends shelf life of hygroscopic products by 2-5×), and controlled release.
