# Step 1 (Salt cake)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

NaCl + H₂SO₄ → NaHSO₄ + HCl (150-200°C), then NaHSO₄ + NaCl → Na₂SO₄ + HCl (550-600°C). Product: sodium sulfate (salt cake). HCl gas captured as hydrochloric acid (useful byproduct for pickling steel, chemical synthesis).
