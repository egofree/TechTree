# Extreme ultraviolet (EUV) resist

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

13.5 nm exposure wavelength. Challenges: (1) Photon shot noise — at 13.5 nm, photon density is low enough that statistical variations cause line-edge roughness. (2) Outgassing — resist decomposition products contaminate EUV optics ($200M+ replacement cost).
