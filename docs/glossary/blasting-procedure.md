# Blasting procedure

> **Type**: material | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry, mining

Drill hole (2.5-4 cm diameter, 0.5-2 m deep) with jumper drill (steel bar, 1-3 m long, struck with 4-8 kg hammer). Clean hole with scraper. Fill bottom 1/3 with powder. Insert safety fuse (black powder core in tarred cotton sheath) — cut to length for 30-60 second delay. Tamp remaining hole with clay or damp sand (NOT dry sand — sparks from tamping rod ignite powder). Light fuse, retreat. 1-2 kg powder breaks 2-10 m³ rock depending on placement.
