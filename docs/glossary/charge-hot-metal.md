# Charge hot metal

> **Type**: equipment | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Charge hot metal (70-80% of charge). Pour molten pig iron from blast furnace torpedo car or hot metal ladle at 1300-1350°C. Typical hot metal composition: 4.0-4.5% C, 0.3-0.8% Si, 0.3-0.8% Mn, 0.05-0.15% P, 0.02-0.05% S. See: [Modern Steelmaking](../metals/steelmaking.md)
