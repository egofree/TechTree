# Output drive

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: silicon

Output impedance <100 Ω at DC (with feedback), rising at higher frequencies. Maximum output current: 20-40 mA for general-purpose op-amps. Output voltage swing: within 1-2V of the supply rails (0-5V swing from ±15V supplies). Rail-to-rail output op-amps swing to within millivolts of the supply rails.
