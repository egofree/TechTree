# Black

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: textiles

The hardest natural color to achieve. Most reliable sequence: (1) Tannin pre-mordant (oak galls, 25% WOF), (2) Iron after-treatment (5% WOF) → gray-black base, (3) Indigo overdye (2-3 dips) → deep blue-black. Alternative: madder (50% WOF) + logwood (30% WOF) + iron (3% WOF).
