# Curved-mold (bow-type)

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: metals

Most common configuration. Mold is curved (~1.5-2.5 m radius of curvature). Strand bends from vertical to horizontal through a guided arc, allowing a lower machine height (6-12 m vs 25-40 m for vertical). Splitting the mold into curved segments simplifies maintenance.
