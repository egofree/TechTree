# Abrasion and erosion

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

Moving molten metal, slag, and process gases physically erode refractory surfaces. Areas of high velocity (tapholes, pour pads, burner ports, slag line) wear faster. Hot strength (modulus of rupture at 1300-1500°C) is the best predictor of erosion resistance. Direct-bonded bricks (sintered at high temperature to form direct ceramic-ceramic bonds between grains, without glassy intergranular phase) have superior hot strength and erosion resistance.
