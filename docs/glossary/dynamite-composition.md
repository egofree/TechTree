# Dynamite composition

> **Type**: noun | **Tier**: important | **Domains**: chemistry

75% nitroglycerin absorbed in 25% diatomaceous earth (inert absorbent). Press into cardboard cartridges (2-5 cm diameter × 10-30 cm long). Stable to shock and friction — can be dropped, thrown, and transported safely. Detonated with blasting cap (small charge of mercury fulminate or lead azide, initiated by safety fuse or electric current).
